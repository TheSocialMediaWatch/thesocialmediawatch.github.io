10 Hilarious YouTube Channels You Should Subscribe To


YouTube is a treasure trove of content, offering everything from educational videos to DIY projects and vlogs. But among all the categories, there is one that stands out for its ability to make us laugh until we’re in tears—comedy! Whether you’re looking for quick giggles or deep belly laughs, YouTube has some of the most talented and funny creators that will never fail to entertain. In this post, we’ll take a look at 10 hilarious YouTube channels that will add some humor to your daily routine.

1. Ryan George

If you love quick, witty humor with a touch of sarcasm, Ryan George is a must-watch. Known for his “First Guy to Ever” series, Ryan creates hilarious sketches where he imagines the first person to experience something in history. From “The First Guy to Ever Play Golf” to “The First Guy to Ever Buy a Cellphone,” Ryan’s over-the-top scenarios and witty dialogue are bound to have you in stitches. His dry humor and clever editing make each video a rollercoaster of laughs, and his ability to parody everyday situations is uncanny.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Quick, bite-sized sketches that deliver laughs without wasting time.
  • Unique perspective on everyday scenarios that make them feel fresh and funny.
  • Ryan’s delivery and expressions add extra charm to each video.

2. Lilly Singh (aka Superwoman)

Lilly Singh, a global comedy powerhouse, has built a huge following with her diverse comedic style. From exaggerated parodies to relatable sketches, Lilly has an incredible ability to blend humor with important messages about life, self-love, and acceptance. While she is best known for her viral videos and social media posts, her YouTube channel is a must-subscribe for anyone who loves sharp humor with a hint of heart.

Lilly’s “Types of” videos (e.g., Types of People at the Gym) showcase her creativity in portraying everyday characters that we all know. Whether she’s poking fun at common social situations or showing off her witty side in vlogs, Lilly is the epitome of funny.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Hilarious sketches that bring relatable content to the forefront.
  • Fun and inspiring personality that shines through in every video.
  • Fantastic parody videos that never fail to entertain.

3. Key & Peele

For a dose of clever and often satirical humor, Key & Peele’s channel is an essential one. Originally a popular sketch comedy show on Comedy Central, Key & Peele is now on YouTube, bringing their hilarious skits to the masses. The duo, comprised of Keegan-Michael Key and Jordan Peele, masterfully tackles everything from race relations to absurd life scenarios. Their ability to blend absurdity with social commentary is both thought-provoking and laugh-out-loud funny.

Their skits range from the popular “Substitute Teacher” to “Anger Translator,” which features Peele as the translator for Barack Obama’s more passionate moments. With impeccable timing and well-crafted writing, their videos keep you entertained while offering some deeper reflection on society.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Iconic sketches that blend humor with social commentary.
  • Unmatched chemistry between Keegan-Michael Key and Jordan Peele.
  • Timeless content that remains relevant and hilarious with each re-watch.

4. The Try Guys

The Try Guys are four best friends who take on the most bizarre, fun, and challenging experiences, often with hilarious results. From trying weird foods to participating in extreme activities like skydiving or making pottery, The Try Guys are masters of pushing boundaries and doing things that normal people would never attempt. Their chemistry, quick wit, and good-natured humor make their channel a top contender for anyone in need of a good laugh.

Each video showcases a unique adventure, and the guys’ reactions to their experiences (and each other) are absolutely priceless. Whether they’re trying their hands at makeup tutorials or battling it out in physical challenges, their sense of humor makes the most ordinary tasks extraordinary.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • An incredible mix of humor and challenges that is always fresh.
  • Great chemistry and banter between the hosts.
  • Fun and diverse content that keeps you entertained from start to finish.

5. James Charles

While James Charles is primarily known for his makeup tutorials, his channel also features some seriously funny content. As one of YouTube’s most popular beauty influencers, James doesn’t shy away from infusing his content with comedy and over-the-top humor. Whether he’s participating in challenges or collaborating with other influencers, his wit and vibrant personality shine through.

James is also known for his “Instant Influencer” competition show, which highlights aspiring beauty gurus while showcasing James’ comedic chops. His ability to mix glam and humor makes his channel a unique blend of entertainment and style.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Hilarious collaborations with celebrities and other YouTubers.
  • A balance of makeup artistry and comedy that keeps things light and entertaining.
  • His infectious humor and personality make each video a joy to watch.

6. Gus Johnson

Gus Johnson has an ability to turn the most mundane situations into laugh-out-loud comedy. Whether he’s turning an awkward interaction at a party into a series of funny sketches or riffing on a random idea, Gus’s ability to find humor in everyday life is unparalleled. His deadpan delivery and absurd scenarios make for a unique comedic style that feels both fresh and relatable.

Gus’s ability to mix absurdity with clever punchlines keeps you guessing. You never know what kind of weird and wonderful content he’ll come up with next, but it’s sure to keep you laughing the whole way through.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Absurd humor that will keep you guessing and laughing.
  • Short and sweet videos that make it easy to catch up on his content.
  • Relatable humor that finds comedy in everyday life.

7. Honest Trailers

Do you love movies? Do you love a good roast? Honest Trailers combines the two in the most hilarious way possible. This channel takes popular films and TV shows and gives them the “honest” treatment by pointing out their flaws, tropes, and ridiculous moments. While some might call it criticism, Honest Trailers delivers it with such wit and sarcasm that you’ll be laughing the entire time.

Whether they’re roasting a blockbuster film like “The Avengers” or a cult classic like “The Matrix,” the Honest Trailers team delivers savage but hilarious commentary that every movie lover can appreciate.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Hilarious and witty takes on popular films and TV shows.
  • Great way to rewatch your favorite movies from a different perspective.
  • Perfect for movie buffs who enjoy a good laugh.

8. Julian Smith

Julian Smith is one of the original YouTubers who has kept the comedy going with his absurd and often bizarre sketches. From pretending to be a walking, talking refrigerator to mocking popular internet trends, Julian’s outlandish sense of humor has remained a fan favorite. His ability to create entirely new worlds filled with quirky characters makes his content unique and endlessly entertaining.

Julian’s videos often push the limits of absurdity, making them hilarious for those who love the weird and unexpected. His comedic timing and commitment to the bit are second to none, making each sketch an unforgettable experience.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Outlandish and absurd comedy that’s sure to surprise you.
  • Unique approach to sketch comedy that pushes boundaries.
  • Perfect for fans of quirky humor that doesn’t take itself seriously.

9. Markiplier

While Markiplier is primarily known for his gaming videos, his hilarious commentary and over-the-top reactions to horror games are just as entertaining as his gameplay. With a mix of energetic humor and sarcastic commentary, Markiplier knows how to keep his audience laughing. His “Try Not To Laugh” challenges, collaborations, and charity livestreams are all filled with comedic moments that prove Markiplier is as much of a comedian as a gamer.

His “Funny Moments” compilation videos feature his most outrageous and hilarious reactions, which will have you in stitches even if you’re not a fan of gaming.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Energetic and humorous gaming commentary that’s impossible to resist.
  • Hilarious collaborations with other popular YouTubers.
  • A mix of gaming and comedy that’s both entertaining and funny.

10. Smosh

Smosh is one of the oldest and most beloved comedy channels on YouTube. With a range of sketches, parodies, and absurd humor, Smosh has been making people laugh for years. From recreating funny moments from popular culture to creating their own hilarious scenarios, Smosh’s content is always fresh and energetic. Their humor ranges from light-hearted to absurd, with a focus on relatable situations and hilarious observations.

With various series and recurring characters, Smosh’s channel is packed with content that will keep you entertained for hours. If you’re looking for comedy that doesn’t take itself seriously, Smosh is the perfect choice.

Why You Should Subscribe:

  • Classic YouTube humor that’s been entertaining audiences for years.
  • A huge variety of sketches, parodies, and series that appeal to different tastes.
  • Consistently funny content that will leave you in stitches.

Conclusion

With so much content available on YouTube, it’s easy to get lost in the sea of channels, but these 10 hilarious YouTube channels are sure to bring laughter to your day. Whether you’re into quick sketches, clever parodies, or absurd humor, there’s something here for everyone. So, the next time you need a good laugh, check out these channels, hit the subscribe button, and get ready for some serious entertainment.
