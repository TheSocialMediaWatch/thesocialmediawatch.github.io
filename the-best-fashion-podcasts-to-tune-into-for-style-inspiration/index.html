The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune into for Style Inspiration


In today’s digital age, podcasts have become a popular medium for learning and entertainment. Whether you’re commuting to work, hitting the gym, or simply relaxing at home, tuning into a fashion podcast can be a great way to stay up-to-date with the latest trends, gain style inspiration, and delve into the fascinating world of fashion.

To help you navigate the vast array of fashion podcasts available, we have curated a list of the best ones that are guaranteed to keep you hooked and inspire your personal style journey. So grab your headphones and get ready to immerse yourself in the world of fashion!

1. “Fashion No Filter” by Monica Ainley and Camille Charriere

Monica Ainley and Camille Charriere, two fashion insiders with a wealth of experience, bring you “Fashion No Filter.” This podcast takes you behind the scenes of the fashion industry, exploring the stories and personalities that shape the world of fashion. From interviews with renowned designers to discussions about sustainability and inclusivity, this podcast offers a fresh and unfiltered perspective on the fashion industry.

2. “The Business of Fashion” by Imran Amed

If you’re interested in the business side of fashion, “The Business of Fashion” podcast by Imran Amed is a must-listen. Imran Amed, the founder and CEO of Business of Fashion, interviews industry leaders, designers, and entrepreneurs to provide valuable insights into the inner workings of the fashion world. This podcast covers a wide range of topics, including retail, marketing, and the future of fashion.

3. “Wardrobe Crisis” by Clare Press

For those passionate about sustainable fashion, “Wardrobe Crisis” hosted by Clare Press is a treasure trove of knowledge. Clare Press, a renowned journalist and sustainability advocate, explores the environmental and social impact of the fashion industry. Through thought-provoking interviews with designers, activists, and experts, this podcast encourages listeners to rethink their relationship with fashion and make more conscious choices.

4. “Dressed: The History of Fashion” by April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary

If you’re fascinated by the history of fashion, “Dressed: The History of Fashion” is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast takes you on a journey through time, exploring the cultural, social, and political influences that have shaped fashion throughout history. Each episode is a captivating blend of storytelling, analysis, and fascinating anecdotes.

5. “Fashion Revolution Podcast”

The “Fashion Revolution Podcast” is a powerful platform that aims to promote transparency and sustainability in the fashion industry. Hosted by a team of passionate activists, this podcast delves into the pressing issues of the fashion industry, such as labor rights, ethical production, and the need for a more sustainable future. Through interviews with industry experts, the podcast encourages listeners to question the status quo and demand change.

6. “UnStyled” by Christene Barberich

Hosted by Christene Barberich, the co-founder and global editor-in-chief of Refinery29, “UnStyled” is a podcast that celebrates personal style and individuality. Through engaging interviews with influential figures in the fashion and entertainment industries, Barberich explores the connection between style and identity. This podcast is a source of inspiration for those looking to embrace their unique fashion journey.

7. “Pop Fashion” by Lisa Rowan and Kaarin Vembar

For a fun and informative take on the fashion industry, tune into “Pop Fashion” hosted by Lisa Rowan and Kaarin Vembar. This podcast covers a wide range of fashion-related topics, from industry news to trend analysis and personal anecdotes. The hosts’ witty banter and deep knowledge of the fashion world make this podcast an enjoyable and educational listen.

8. “Conscious Chatter” by Kestrel Jenkins

“Conscious Chatter” is a podcast dedicated to exploring the intersection of fashion, sustainability, and social consciousness. Hosted by Kestrel Jenkins, this podcast features conversations with designers, activists, and thought leaders who are driving positive change in the fashion industry. From slow fashion to ethical manufacturing, “Conscious Chatter” provides valuable insights and inspires listeners to make more conscious fashion choices.

9. “The American Fashion Podcast” by Charles Beckwith

“The American Fashion Podcast” offers a unique perspective on the American fashion industry. Hosted by fashion industry veterans Charles Beckwith and Cathy Schepis, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including fashion week coverage, interviews with designers, and discussions about the challenges and opportunities facing the American fashion scene.

10. “Fashion is Your Business” by Pavan Bahl

Last but not least, “Fashion is Your Business” is a podcast that explores the intersection of fashion, technology, and innovation. Hosted by Pavan Bahl, this podcast features conversations with industry experts, entrepreneurs, and innovators who are redefining the fashion landscape. From e-commerce to wearable technology, “Fashion is Your Business” offers valuable insights into the future of fashion.

These ten fashion podcasts are just the tip of the iceberg. Exploring the world of fashion through podcasts is not only informative but also a great way to discover new perspectives, gain inspiration, and deepen your understanding of the ever-evolving fashion industry. So why not tune in and let these podcasts take your style game to new heights? Happy listening!

Note: This blog post is not affiliated with any of the mentioned podcasts. The recommendations are based on personal opinions and research.
