Simple and Delicious Chicken Dinner Recipes


When it comes to cooking dinner, chicken is a versatile and delicious protein that can be used in a wide variety of recipes. Whether you’re cooking for yourself, your family, or hosting a dinner party, chicken dishes are always a crowd-pleaser. In this blog post, we’ll explore some simple and mouthwatering chicken dinner recipes that are easy to make and sure to impress.

1. Lemon Herb Roast Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 lemon
  • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, and parsley)
  • Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Cut the lemon into wedges and roughly chop the fresh herbs and garlic.
  4. Stuff the chicken cavity with the lemon wedges, herbs, and garlic.
  5. Rub the chicken with olive oil, then season generously with salt and pepper.
  6. Place the chicken in a roasting pan and roast for about 1.5 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  7. Let the chicken rest for 10-15 minutes before carving and serving.

This simple roast chicken is bursting with flavor and is perfect for a cozy family dinner.

2. Creamy Garlic Parmesan Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 6-7 minutes per side.
  4. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add the minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until the sauce thickens.
  8. Return the chicken to the skillet and simmer for an additional 10 minutes, until the chicken is cooked through.
  9. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.

This creamy and indulgent chicken dish pairs perfectly with pasta or a side of crispy roasted potatoes.

3. Honey Mustard Baked Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 8 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1/4 cup Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Fresh thyme leaves
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the Dijon mustard, honey, minced garlic, olive oil, and thyme leaves.
  3. Season the chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then brush the honey mustard mixture over each piece.
  4. Place the chicken thighs in a baking dish and bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the skin is golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve the honey mustard baked chicken with a fresh green salad or steamed vegetables for a light and satisfying meal.

These three chicken dinner recipes are simple to prepare and offer a delicious mix of flavors that are sure to please everyone at the table. Whether you’re a seasoned home cook or just starting out in the kitchen, these recipes are perfect for adding a touch of culinary flair to your dinner routine. Enjoy!
