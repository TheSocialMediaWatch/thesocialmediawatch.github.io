The Legend of Skinwalkers: Paranormal Shapeshifters of Navajo Legend

The Skinwalker legend originates from the Navajo Nation, where it is believed that these creatures were once ordinary humans who had made a pact with the devil or had been possessed by evil spirits. Skinwalkers are said to be characterized by their ability to shapeshift, which allows them to change their physical form to suit their needs.
