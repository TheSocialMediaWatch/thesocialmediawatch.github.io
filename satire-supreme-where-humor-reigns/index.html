The Art of Satire

Satire is more than just comedy; it is a form of social criticism wrapped in humor. Satirical works aim to expose and ridicule societal issues, politics, and human behavior in a way that prompts reflection and introspection. Through clever wordplay, exaggerated scenarios, and sharp wit, satire offers a unique lens through which we can view the world around us.
