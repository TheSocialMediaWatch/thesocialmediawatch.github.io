Crafter's Favorites: Top 10 DIY Tools and Supplies


Embarking on a creative journey can be both an invigorating and challenging experience, especially when it comes to having the right tools and supplies at your fingertips. Each tool and material has its unique role in shaping your masterpiece, and knowing which ones to use can transform any crafting novice into a crafting aficionado. Let’s dive into our top 10 DIY tools and supplies that every crafter should have in their arsenal.

  1. Multitool

Starting off strong, a versatile multitool is an absolute lifesaver, especially for the impromptu crafter. From opening stubborn packages to cutting and shaping materials, a multitool can handle a range of tasks. Brands like Leatherman and Gerber offer various options to suit your needs and budget.

  1. Rotating Cutting Mat

Creating a safe and organized workspace is essential for fostering creativity and reducing the risk of accidents. A rotating cutting mat serves this purpose by providing both a durable surface and convenient measurements for precision cuts. Additionally, these mats often have guidelines for different cutting angles, making it a must-have for any crafter.

  1. Fabric Shears

Crafting often involves cutting a variety of materials, from paper to fabric. Fabric shears excel in this area, as they are designed for precise and sharp cuts without damaging the materials. Invest in a quality pair from a manufacturer like Fiskars, and they will be your go-to for any cutting need.

  1. Easel

Versatility is key when it comes to crafting tools, and a sturdy easel provides just that. A reliable easel can support your work surface and accommodate various projects like painting, drawing, and crafting. From adjustable models to foldable ones, there are endless options from brands such as US Art Supply and Mont Marte.

  1. Craft Boxes and Totes

Keeping your supplies organized and easily accessible is vital for a functional and efficient crafting experience. Craft boxes and totes come in various sizes and designs to suit your specific needs, allowing you to neatly store and transport your essentials with ease. Brands like Prange and MindReader offer top-notch organizers for all of your crafting requirements.

  1. Permanent andFine-Tip Marker Set

When working with a range of materials, having a trusty marker set is crucial. A permanent marker set like the Sharpie offers exceptional durability and staying power, while a fine-tip marker set like the Tombow Monomeros Waterproof provides precise detail work. Having these two versatile sets enables endless possibilities for your projects.

  1. Craft Scissors

Grab a pair of high-quality craft scissors for all your cutting needs, like Gosum Craft Scissors. These scissors are designed for various materials and boast sharp, precise blades for clean cuts on all your crafting endeavors.

  1. Glue Gun and Adhesives

Being able to securely attach pieces without causing damage or discoloration is essential. A trustworthy glue gun like the PiccoCraft Ladybug is perfect for crafting projects, while adhesive solutions such as E-6000 offer a robust hold for a variety of surfaces.

  1. Sewing Kit

For those who enjoy sewing or wish to try their hand at it, having a comprehensive sewing kit on hand is essential. With everything from fabric scissors to sewing needles, a sewing kit by Dritz will help you tackle any sewing-related project confidently.

  1. Portable Light

And finally, no crafting experience is complete without a portable light source. Whether it’s for illuminating intricate details or maintaining visibility during late-night crafting sessions, a portable light like the LED Headlamp is an absolute game-changer.

Crafting is an exhilarating journey, and having the right tools and supplies along for the ride can make all the difference in achieving your desired results. Invest in these versatile tools and supplies, and you’ll be well on your way to crafting success. Happy creating!
