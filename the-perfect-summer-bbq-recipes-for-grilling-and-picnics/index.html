The Perfect Summer BBQ: Recipes for Grilling and Picnics


Summer is here, and that means it’s time to fire up the grill and enjoy some delicious outdoor meals with family and friends. Whether you’re planning a backyard BBQ or a picnic at the park, we’ve got you covered with a selection of mouthwatering recipes that are sure to impress.

Grilled Chicken Skewers with Lemon and Herbs

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, combine the lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, parsley, thyme, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Pour the marinade over the chicken pieces and toss to coat evenly. Let it marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
  4. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Thread the marinated chicken onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  6. Grill the skewers for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  7. Remove from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.

Grilled Corn on the Cob with Chili Lime Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husks removed
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon lime zest
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium heat.
  2. In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, chili powder, lime zest, lime juice, and salt. Mix well.
  3. Brush the corn with the chili lime butter, making sure to coat all sides.
  4. Place the corn directly on the grill grates and cook for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are tender and lightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and let them cool slightly before serving.

Grilled Vegetable Skewers with Balsamic Glaze

Ingredients:

  • 1 zucchini, sliced into rounds
  • 1 yellow squash, sliced into rounds
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 8-10 cherry tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomatoes, olive oil, salt, and pepper until well coated.
  3. Thread the vegetables onto the soaked wooden skewers, alternating between different types of vegetables.
  4. Grill the skewers for about 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.
  5. Remove from the grill and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Picnic Potato Salad with Dill Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs baby potatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Place the halved baby potatoes in a large pot of salted water. Bring to a boil and cook for about 10-12 minutes, or until fork-tender. Drain and let them cool slightly.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, fresh dill, green onions, salt, and pepper.
  3. Transfer the cooked potatoes to a large bowl and pour the dressing over them. Toss gently until the potatoes are well coated.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving, allowing the flavors to meld together.

Conclusion

With these delicious recipes, your summer BBQ or picnic is sure to be a hit. From the juicy grilled chicken skewers to the flavorful grilled vegetables, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So gather your loved ones, soak up the sun, and savor the flavors of the perfect summer BBQ. Happy grilling!
