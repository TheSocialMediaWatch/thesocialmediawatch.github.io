Grilling and BBQ: Recipes for Summertime Fun


Summer is the perfect time to fire up the grill and indulge in some mouthwatering barbecued delights. Whether you’re hosting a backyard party or simply enjoying a sunny day with your loved ones, grilling and BBQ recipes are a fantastic way to elevate your summertime fun. In this article, we’ll explore some delicious recipes that are sure to impress your guests and leave them craving for more.

1. Smoky Grilled Chicken Skewers

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of boneless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the olive oil, smoked paprika, brown sugar, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken chunks to the bowl and toss until they are evenly coated with the marinade. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Thread the marinated chicken chunks onto the soaked wooden skewers.
  5. Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.
  6. Serve the smoky grilled chicken skewers hot with your favorite dipping sauce.

2. Grilled Shrimp Tacos with Mango Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 small flour tortillas
  • 1 cup of shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup of diced mango
  • 1/2 cup of diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss until they are coated with the marinade. Let it marinate for about 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat your grill to medium heat.
  4. Grill the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes per side, until they are pink and cooked through.
  5. Warm the flour tortillas on the grill for a minute on each side.
  6. To make the mango salsa, combine the diced mango, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice in a separate bowl.
  7. Assemble the grilled shrimp tacos by placing some shredded lettuce, grilled shrimp, and mango salsa on each tortilla.
  8. Serve the tacos immediately with a squeeze of lime juice.

3. Grilled Corn on the Cob with Herb Butter

Ingredients:

  • 4 ears of corn, husked
  • 4 tablespoons of unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley, thyme, or cilantro)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, chopped fresh herbs, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  3. Grill the corn on the cob for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the kernels are slightly charred.
  4. Remove the corn from the grill and immediately brush each ear with the herb butter mixture.
  5. Serve the grilled corn on the cob hot as a delightful side dish to your grilled meats and veggies.

Conclusion

Grilling and BBQ recipes are a fantastic way to make the most of your summertime fun. From smoky grilled chicken skewers to grilled shrimp tacos with mango salsa, these recipes are sure to impress your guests and satisfy your cravings. So, fire up that grill, gather your loved ones, and get ready to indulge in some delicious flavors under the warm summer sun. Happy grilling!
