The Best Personal Finance Podcasts to Listen to in 2023


In today’s fast-paced world, managing personal finances has become more important than ever. With inflation, changing economic conditions, and the increasing cost of living, it’s essential to stay informed about smart money management strategies. One of the most effective ways to learn and stay updated on personal finance is by listening to podcasts.

Podcasts provide a convenient way to absorb financial knowledge while on the go. Whether you’re commuting, exercising, or relaxing at home, podcasts allow you to learn valuable financial lessons without taking time away from your daily activities. In this post, we’ll cover some of the best personal finance podcasts to listen to in 2023. These podcasts offer a wealth of information ranging from budgeting and saving to investing and retirement planning.

1. The Dave Ramsey Show

If you’re looking for practical advice on debt management, budgeting, and personal finance, The Dave Ramsey Show is a must-listen. Dave Ramsey, a well-known financial expert, is famous for his “Baby Steps” approach to financial freedom. His no-nonsense advice is perfect for anyone looking to get out of debt and start saving money.

Key Highlights:

  • Focus on debt repayment and budgeting.
  • Offers actionable advice with a clear roadmap to financial freedom.
  • Features live calls from listeners seeking personal finance advice.

Dave’s straightforward and often motivational style has helped millions take control of their finances. Whether you’re in debt, struggling with financial discipline, or planning for the future, this podcast provides valuable insights that are easy to understand and implement.

2. The Tim Ferriss Show

Tim Ferriss is best known for his book The 4-Hour Workweek, but his podcast delves into much more than just productivity hacks. While it does touch on entrepreneurship and career growth, it also dives deep into topics like investing, wealth-building strategies, and the habits of successful people.

Key Highlights:

  • Features interviews with top entrepreneurs, authors, and financial experts.
  • Discusses investing strategies, money management, and lifestyle design.
  • Offers unique insights into how high achievers manage their money.

Tim’s podcast is ideal for anyone looking to optimize their finances while also improving their overall life strategy. He often brings in guests who share their personal financial experiences, offering listeners a diverse range of perspectives and tips.

3. The BiggerPockets Podcast

Real estate is one of the most effective ways to build wealth, and The BiggerPockets Podcast is the go-to resource for anyone looking to get started in real estate investing. Hosted by Brandon Turner and David Greene, the show covers everything from house hacking to commercial real estate investing.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on real estate investing strategies for beginners and experts.
  • Features interviews with real estate investors and industry experts.
  • Offers practical tips and advice for building wealth through property ownership.

Whether you’re a first-time homebuyer or a seasoned real estate investor, this podcast offers valuable insights into the ever-changing world of real estate and provides actionable advice for those looking to build long-term wealth.

4. Planet Money

For those who enjoy learning about economics and how it relates to personal finance, Planet Money is a fantastic choice. This podcast provides an engaging and often humorous take on complex financial topics. From explaining economic trends to discussing the impact of policy decisions on your wallet, Planet Money breaks down the world of finance into easily digestible episodes.

Key Highlights:

  • Offers an in-depth look at global economic trends and how they affect personal finance.
  • Features entertaining storytelling and expert commentary.
  • Discusses financial topics in a way that’s accessible for everyone.

What sets Planet Money apart is its ability to tackle difficult economic concepts and make them relatable. If you’re interested in understanding how the broader economy affects your financial decisions, this podcast is an excellent resource.

5. So Money with Farnoosh Torabi

Farnoosh Torabi is a personal finance expert who brings a unique perspective to the world of money management. On So Money, she interviews financial experts, entrepreneurs, and celebrities, diving into their money habits and the lessons they’ve learned along the way.

Key Highlights:

  • Features expert interviews on a wide range of personal finance topics.
  • Focuses on the emotional side of money and the mindset needed for financial success.
  • Covers investing, career development, and money habits.

The show covers topics ranging from budgeting to managing large sums of money, and Farnoosh’s approachable style makes complex financial issues easy to understand. If you’re looking to learn from the stories of others and apply their experiences to your own financial journey, So Money is a great choice.

6. ChooseFI

If you’re interested in the FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) movement, ChooseFI is a podcast you shouldn’t miss. Hosted by Brad and Jonathan, this show is dedicated to helping listeners achieve financial independence and retire early by teaching them how to optimize their finances, reduce expenses, and invest strategically.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on the principles of the FIRE movement.
  • Offers actionable tips for reducing expenses, saving more, and investing smarter.
  • Features expert interviews with people who have reached financial independence.

ChooseFI offers practical advice for anyone looking to retire early, but the strategies discussed can benefit anyone aiming to improve their financial situation. The hosts share their personal stories and experiences, offering a relatable and motivational approach to money management.

7. The Smart Passive Income Podcast

For anyone interested in building passive income streams, The Smart Passive Income Podcast is a great resource. Hosted by Pat Flynn, the show covers a variety of topics related to online business, entrepreneurship, and creating streams of passive income.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on entrepreneurship and building online businesses.
  • Discusses creating multiple streams of passive income through blogging, podcasting, and more.
  • Features interviews with successful entrepreneurs who share their strategies.

Pat Flynn has built a successful business around passive income, and through his podcast, he shares the knowledge and tools that helped him along the way. If you’re looking for ways to generate income outside of your regular job, this podcast is a great resource.

8. The Mad Fientist Financial Independence Podcast

For those serious about the FIRE movement, The Mad Fientist Financial Independence Podcast is an essential listen. Hosted by Brandon, aka “The Mad Fientist,” this podcast delves into the strategies and tools required to achieve financial independence. The show covers everything from tax optimization to investing, offering in-depth interviews with experts in the financial independence community.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on advanced strategies for achieving financial independence.
  • Offers deep dives into tax optimization and investing strategies.
  • Features interviews with financial experts who specialize in the FIRE movement.

If you’re already on your journey toward financial independence, this podcast offers high-level strategies and expert insights to help you accelerate your progress and achieve your goals faster.

9. The Financial Independence Podcast by ChooseFI

As an extension of the ChooseFI brand, The Financial Independence Podcast offers listeners a wealth of information on achieving financial freedom. This podcast is ideal for those who are looking to retire early, travel more, and live life on their terms.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on achieving financial independence and early retirement.
  • Covers topics like saving aggressively, investing efficiently, and building wealth.
  • Provides actionable steps to help you achieve financial freedom.

Whether you’re just beginning your journey toward financial independence or looking to refine your strategy, this podcast provides all the tools you need to take control of your financial future.

10. The Money Tree Investing Podcast

If you’re looking to deepen your understanding of investing, The Money Tree Investing Podcast is a must-listen. This podcast offers expert advice on investing in a variety of asset classes, including stocks, real estate, and even alternative investments.

Key Highlights:

  • Focuses on investing in stocks, bonds, real estate, and alternative assets.
  • Offers practical investment tips for beginners and seasoned investors alike.
  • Features expert interviews with investors who have experience in a variety of markets.

For anyone looking to build wealth through investing, The Money Tree Investing Podcast is a fantastic resource for learning about different investment strategies and finding new opportunities to grow your wealth.

Conclusion

In 2023, personal finance is more important than ever, and staying informed through podcasts is a fantastic way to enhance your financial knowledge. Whether you’re looking to eliminate debt, invest wisely, or achieve financial independence, there’s a podcast on this list to help you meet your financial goals.

From the practical, no-nonsense advice of Dave Ramsey to the in-depth investing insights from The Money Tree Investing Podcast, these shows offer something for everyone, regardless of where they are on their financial journey. By tuning into these podcasts, you can gain valuable knowledge, stay motivated, and ultimately take control of your financial future.

Remember, the key to financial success is continuous learning and adapting to the changing financial landscape. So, grab your headphones, start listening, and take the next step towards mastering your finances.
