The Best Spirits for Crafting Classic Highballs


The Highball is a timeless cocktail that has been a staple in the world of mixology for decades. Its simplicity and elegance make it a favorite among bartenders and enthusiasts alike. At its core, a Highball is a combination of a spirit and a carbonated water, served over ice with a twist of citrus. But not all spirits are created equal when it comes to crafting the perfect Highball. In this post, we'll explore the best spirits for this classic cocktail and provide you with some expert tips to elevate your Highball game. The Classics: Gin and Vodka When it comes to Highballs, gin and vodka are the two most traditional spirits used. Gin, with its botanicals and crisp flavor profile, is a natural fit for the Highball. A classic Gin Highball typically consists of gin, soda water, and a squeeze of lime. Some popular gin options for Highballs include:

Beefeater: A classic London Dry Gin with a bold, juniper-forward flavor profile. Bombay Sapphire: A more modern take on the classic London Dry Gin, with a crisp, refreshing taste. Gordon's: A versatile gin with a balanced flavor profile that works well in a variety of Highball recipes.

Vodka, on the other hand, is a more neutral spirit that pairs well with a wide range of flavor profiles. A classic Vodka Highball typically consists of vodka, soda water, and a squeeze of lemon. Some popular vodka options for Highballs include:

Grey Goose: A high-end vodka with a smooth, creamy flavor profile. Absolut: A versatile vodka with a crisp, clean taste. Ketel One: A premium vodka with a smooth, rich flavor profile.

The Whiskey Whirlwind Whiskey, particularly Scotch and Irish whiskey, has become increasingly popular in modern Highball recipes. A Whiskey Highball typically consists of whiskey, soda water, and a twist of citrus. Some popular whiskey options for Highballs include:

Johnnie Walker: A classic Scotch whiskey with a rich, smoky flavor profile. Jameson: An Irish whiskey with a smooth, approachable taste. Jameson Black Barrel: A premium Irish whiskey with a rich, complex flavor profile.

The Rum Revival Rum, particularly white and light rums, has become a popular choice for Highballs in recent years. A Rum Highball typically consists of rum, soda water, and a squeeze of lime. Some popular rum options for Highballs include:

Bacardi: A classic white rum with a crisp, clean flavor profile. Cruzan: A light rum with a smooth, approachable taste. Appleton Estate: A premium rum with a rich, complex flavor profile.

Expert Tips for Crafting the Perfect Highball While the spirits are the backbone of the Highball, there are a few expert tips to keep in mind when crafting the perfect cocktail:

Balance is key: The key to a great Highball is finding the perfect balance between the spirit and the soda water. Start with a small amount of spirit and add soda water to taste. Experiment with flavor profiles: Don't be afraid to try different spirits and flavor profiles to find the perfect combination for your taste buds. Use fresh citrus: Freshly squeezed citrus juice is essential for a great Highball. Use a manual or electric juicer to extract the perfect amount of juice. Don't forget the ice: Ice is essential for a great Highball. Use large ice cubes to prevent the drink from becoming watered down.

In conclusion, the Best spirits for crafting classic Highballs are a matter of personal preference. Whether you're a traditionalist who sticks to gin and vodka or an adventurous spirit who likes to experiment with whiskey and rum, there's a spirit out there for everyone. With a little practice and patience, you'll be crafting the perfect Highball in no time.
