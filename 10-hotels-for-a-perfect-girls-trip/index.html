10 Hotels for a Perfect Girls' Trip


Planning a girls’ trip is an exciting venture filled with laughter, adventure, and memories to last a lifetime. One of the most important decisions to ensure your getaway is absolutely perfect lies in choosing the right hotel. Whether you’re chasing luxury, seeking vibrant nightlife nearby, or craving tranquil spa moments, the perfect hotel can set the tone for the entire trip.

Here’s a comprehensive guide to 10 hotels around the world where your girls’ trip will be nothing short of unforgettable. These hotels excel in comfort, offer exceptional amenities, and create spaces designed for bonding, relaxation, and celebration.

1. The Hoxton, Amsterdam – Chic Vibes Meet City Energy

Amsterdam’s Hoxton is an exceptional choice for groups looking to combine trendy design with central convenience. This hotel’s interior boasts an eclectic yet cozy vibe with communal workspaces and lounges that encourage socializing. The rooms are stylish yet practical, perfect for recharging after a day of exploring.

Located in the vibrant Herengracht canal district, The Hoxton gives easy access to iconic shopping streets, cafes, and nightlife. Enjoy brunch at their lively restaurant before heading out to local boutiques or hopping on a canal cruise. The atmosphere here is young, upbeat, and effortlessly cool — ideal for a girls’ trip that balances relaxation with city buzz.

2. Hotel Saint Cecilia, Austin – Boho Luxe for the Creative Soul

Nestled in the heart of Austin, Hotel Saint Cecilia is a luxury boutique hotel designed for those who appreciate artistic flair and intimate social spaces. This hidden gem’s aesthetic blends vintage rock ‘n’ roll with modern designer touches, creating a unique, welcoming atmosphere.

The spacious suites feature private pools and patios, perfect for late-night chats or morning mimosas. After lounging poolside, dive into Austin’s renowned live music scene or explore its eclectic food trucks. The hotel’s pool bar and chic lounges are popular spots for women looking to unwind and connect in style.

3. The Line Hotel, Los Angeles – Urban Cool Meets Relaxed Comfort

Los Angeles is a playground of glamour and grit, and The Line Hotel in Koreatown perfectly captures this essence. This hotel is a visit to the city’s pulsating heart, with floor-to-ceiling windows offering breathtaking city views and modern, minimalist design interiors that prioritize comfort.

Group travelers will appreciate the variety of dining options available within the hotel, including a rooftop bar that’s the perfect sunset spot. The Line’s proximity to museums, galleries, and buzzing nightlife makes it a strategic basecamp for a girls’ trip focused on culture and fun.

4. The Ned, London – Iconic Opulence with Vibrant Social Spaces

The Ned in London is a grand destination committed to an all-encompassing luxury experience. Set in a historic bank building, it features thirteen restaurants, a spa, and multiple bars, offering everything your group might desire under one roof.

The grandeur of The Ned is matched by its inclusive and lively atmosphere, where guests can move seamlessly from poolside relaxation to a lively evening gathering. It’s a perfect fit for a girls’ trip that values style, sophistication, and the excitement of London’s dynamic social scene.

5. The Surfjack Hotel & Swim Club, Honolulu – Retro Charm Meets Island Luxury

If your squad is dreaming of Hawaiian breezes and beach vibes, the Surfjack Hotel & Swim Club in Honolulu combines vintage charm with contemporary style brilliantly. Its poolside cabanas and vibrant murals create an inviting backdrop for those all-important Instagram shots.

Each room is uniquely decorated with mid-century modern aesthetics, while the hotel’s restaurant serves farm-to-table Hawaiian cuisine that’s both fresh and authentic. This spot is perfect for groups wanting to relax in a laid-back environment while still enjoying luxury touches and proximity to Honolulu’s best beaches.

6. The Scarlet, Cornwall – Seaside Serenity for Mind and Body

A trip focused on wellness, rejuvenation, and coastal beauty calls for a stay at The Scarlet Hotel in Cornwall, England. This eco-conscious luxury hotel overlooks the rolling waves of the Atlantic Ocean, offering spectacular cliffside views and an emphasis on mindfulness and relaxation.

The Scarlet’s architecture blends with the natural landscape, featuring spacious suites with wood-burning stoves and balconies to witness glorious sunsets. World-class spa facilities, yoga classes, and a delectable organic menu invite your group to recharge and bond deeply amid tranquility.

7. 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge – Sustainable Luxury with Iconic Views

For groups who love sustainability but don’t want to sacrifice style, 1 Hotel Brooklyn Bridge is an inviting destination. This hotel emphasizes green design and wellness while giving guests unobstructed views of Manhattan’s skyline and the Brooklyn Bridge.

The eco-friendly materials and local art create a soothing, modern environment. With a rooftop pool, farm-to-table dining, and easy access to Brooklyn’s bohemian neighborhoods, this hotel offers a perfect blend of urban exploration and mindful luxury for a girls’ trip full of discovery and style.

8. The Jade Hotel, Miami Beach – Vibrant and Modern Beachfront Escape

Miami’s energy is contagious, and The Jade Hotel capitalizes on this with sleek design, oceanfront views, and a lively atmosphere. Located right on Collins Avenue, this hotel places guests right where the fun begins, near iconic clubs, galleries, and tons of shopping.

The Jade’s rooftop pool provides a fantastic spot for daytime lounging or evening cocktails. Spacious suites with modern amenities ensure comfort after dancing the night away. This spot suits groups who crave sun, sand, vibrant nightlife, and a beachside chic aesthetic.

9. Hotel Valencia Riverwalk, San Antonio – Historic Charm Meets Sophisticated Comfort

In the heart of San Antonio’s vibrant Riverwalk, Hotel Valencia offers a sophisticated, boutique experience rich in history and Texan hospitality. It’s the ideal setting for groups who want to mix cultural exploration with relaxing, luxurious accommodations.

The Mediterranean-inspired architecture, lush courtyards, and chic interiors create an inviting atmosphere for those late-night chats over handcrafted cocktails. Located steps from the famous Riverwalk, your girls’ trip can blend sightseeing, shopping, and dining effortlessly.

10. Hotel Teatro, Denver – Classic Elegance in the Mile High City

Dynamic Denver blends urban sophistication with the great outdoors, and Hotel Teatro exemplifies this mix beautifully. Situated in Denver’s bustling theater district, this hotel offers plush amenities within a historically rich building.

For active groups, the proximity to hiking trails and outdoor excursions pairs perfectly with the hotel’s elegant gathering spaces and seasonal menus. Whether it’s a girls’ trip focused on outdoor adventures or city nightlife, Hotel Teatro offers a polished and comfortable retreat.

Making the Most of Your Girls’ Trip Hotel Choice

Choosing the right hotel can elevate your girls’ trip from memorable to legendary. Look for places offering:

  • Comfortable and spacious rooms for everyone to feel at home.
  • Social spaces like rooftop bars, communal lounges, or pool areas to bond without leaving the hotel.
  • Great location near attractions, restaurants, and nightlife to maximize your time.
  • Unique experiences and amenities such as spas, private pools, or curated events that add an extra sparkle.

Consider your group’s vibe—is it more about nightlife and energy, or calm and relaxation? Do you prefer boutique charm or grand luxury? Whichever direction you take, these 10 hotels offer diverse options tailor-made to provide a superb backdrop for a girls’ getaway filled with joy, connection, and unforgettable moments.

Planning your trip around a hotel experience that suits your group will create the perfect balance of comfort and adventure, setting the stage for stories you’ll recount for years to come. Now gather your girlfriends, pack your bags, and prepare for a trip designed to strengthen friendships, ignite laughter, and celebrate life together. Cheers to the ultimate girls’ trip!
