Quick and Easy Weeknight Dinner Recipes


After a long day at work or school, the last thing you want to do is spend hours in the kitchen preparing dinner. Luckily, there are plenty of quick and easy weeknight dinner recipes that can be whipped up in no time, allowing you to enjoy a delicious meal without the stress. In this post, we’ll explore a variety of recipes that are not only simple to prepare but also packed with flavor. From one-pan wonders to quick stir-fries, these meals will have you out of the kitchen and enjoying your dinner in a flash.

1. One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken and Asparagus

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 1 bunch of asparagus, trimmed
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, zested and juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, lemon zest, salt, and pepper.
  3. Add the chicken breasts to the bowl and toss to coat.
  4. Spread the chicken and asparagus on a baking sheet, arranging the asparagus around the chicken.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and the asparagus is tender. Serve hot.

This one-pan meal not only cuts down on cleanup time but also delivers a burst of fresh flavor that will please everyone at the table.

2. Quick Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups mixed bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the beef slices and cook until browned, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Toss in the bell peppers and broccoli, stirring frequently for another 3-4 minutes.
  5. Pour in the soy sauce and stir to combine. Cook for an additional minute.
  6. Serve over cooked rice.

This beef stir-fry is a speedy dinner solution that can easily be customized with your favorite vegetables.

3. Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces pasta of choice
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced tomatoes and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the heavy cream and bring to a gentle simmer. Cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  4. Mix in the cooked pasta and basil, and season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve warm with additional basil if desired.

This creamy pasta dish is not only easy to make but also a comforting option for busy weeknights.

4. Sheet Pan Fajitas

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fajita seasoning
  • Tortillas for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. On a large baking sheet, toss the chicken, peppers, onion, olive oil, and fajita seasoning together until everything is evenly coated.
  3. Spread the mixture in a single layer and bake for 20-25 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  4. Serve with warm tortillas and your favorite toppings.

These sheet pan fajitas are a fun and interactive dinner that the whole family will enjoy.

5. Easy Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, heat sesame oil over medium heat.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for about 2 minutes.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs on the other side. Scramble the eggs until cooked.
  4. Add the cooked rice and soy sauce, mixing everything together until heated through.
  5. Top with sliced green onions and serve.

This quick vegetable fried rice is perfect for using up leftover rice and is a great way to sneak in some veggies.

6. Pesto Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1/2 cup pesto
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 4 tortillas
  • Olive oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the shredded chicken and pesto until well combined.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add a little olive oil.
  3. Place one tortilla in the skillet, and layer half of the tortilla with the chicken mixture and mozzarella cheese.
  4. Fold the tortilla in half and cook until golden brown on both sides, about 3-4 minutes.
  5. Repeat with the remaining tortillas and filling. Slice and serve.

These quesadillas are not only quick to make but also a delicious way to enjoy the flavors of pesto and chicken.

7. Quick Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • Corn tortillas
  • Slaw mix for topping
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, toss the shrimp with olive oil, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat, add the shrimp, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until opaque.
  3. Warm the corn tortillas in a separate skillet or microwave.
  4. Assemble the tacos by placing shrimp on tortillas and topping with slaw mix. Serve with lime wedges.

These shrimp tacos are a light and refreshing option that can be prepared in under 15 minutes.

8. 15-Minute Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups arugula or spinach
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Balsamic glaze for drizzling
  • Olive oil, salt, and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine arugula, cherry tomatoes, and mozzarella.
  2. Toss gently and top with fresh basil leaves.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and olive oil, then season with salt and pepper to taste.

This Caprese salad is a perfect side dish or light dinner option that takes no time to prepare.

9. Quick Chili

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 packet chili seasoning
  • 1 cup corn (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, brown the ground beef or turkey over medium heat.
  2. Drain excess fat and add kidney beans, diced tomatoes, chili seasoning, and corn if using.
  3. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Serve hot.

This quick chili recipe is hearty and satisfying, making it a perfect weeknight meal.

10. Simple Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork.
  5. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

This baked salmon dish is simple yet elegant, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner that feels special.

Conclusion

Incorporating quick and easy weeknight dinner recipes into your routine can make mealtime less stressful and more enjoyable. With these recipes, you can spend less time preparing and more time enjoying dinner with your loved ones. Whether you’re in the mood for something hearty or light, there is a recipe here to satisfy your cravings. So, gather your ingredients and get ready to whip up a delicious dinner in no time!
