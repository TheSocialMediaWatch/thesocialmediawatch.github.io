10 Must-Try DIY Projects for Fall Decor


As the crisp air and golden hues of autumn set in, it’s the perfect time to bring the cozy vibes of fall into your home with some creative DIY decor. Whether you’re looking to update your living room, spruce up your front porch, or add a seasonal touch to your dining area, fall offers endless opportunities for crafting unique pieces that will elevate your space. Here are 10 must-try DIY projects that will add warmth, charm, and a bit of autumn magic to your home.

1. DIY Pumpkin Centerpieces

Pumpkins are synonymous with fall, and they make the perfect centerpiece for your dining table or entryway. But instead of simply placing a store-bought pumpkin in the middle of the table, why not create a custom DIY pumpkin centerpiece?

Materials:

  • Small faux pumpkins (or real pumpkins if preferred)
  • Craft paint in fall colors (orange, white, gold, etc.)
  • Ribbon or twine
  • Hot glue gun
  • Decorative elements (acorns, cinnamon sticks, small fall leaves)

Instructions:

  1. Start by painting your pumpkins in shades of gold, white, or muted pastels for a modern look, or stick to classic orange for a traditional vibe.
  2. Once dry, wrap ribbons or twine around the stems, adding a small bow or knot.
  3. For an extra touch, glue small decorative elements like acorns, cinnamon sticks, or faux leaves around the base of the pumpkins.
  4. Place them in a shallow bowl or tray for a stunning centerpiece.

This DIY project brings the essence of fall right to your table and can easily be customized to match your home’s color scheme.

2. Autumn Leaf Garland

Transform your home with a beautiful, colorful garland made from fall leaves. This simple project allows you to incorporate the vibrant colors of the season while giving your home a cozy, festive atmosphere.

Materials:

  • Real or faux fall leaves
  • Twine or string
  • Scissors
  • Needle and thread (or glue)

Instructions:

  1. Collect a mix of colorful autumn leaves or purchase faux ones from a craft store.
  2. Lay the leaves flat and cut them to the desired size, if necessary.
  3. Using a needle and thread, carefully sew each leaf onto the twine, spacing them evenly.
  4. Alternatively, glue the leaves onto the twine with a hot glue gun if sewing isn’t your style.
  5. Hang your garland on your mantel, around windows, or along a stair railing.

This DIY leaf garland will bring natural beauty into your home and is perfect for both indoor and outdoor fall decor.

3. Rustic Wooden Signs

Nothing says fall like rustic charm. Create your own rustic wooden signs with autumn phrases or images to add a farmhouse feel to your home.

Materials:

  • Wooden boards (pallet wood works great)
  • Chalk paint or acrylic paints
  • Paintbrushes
  • Stencils (optional)
  • Sandpaper

Instructions:

  1. Sand the wooden boards to create a smooth surface.
  2. Paint the board with your desired background color, such as a soft cream, dark brown, or muted green.
  3. Once dry, use a stencil or freehand paint autumn phrases like “Hello Fall,” “Grateful,” or “Give Thanks.”
  4. For an added rustic touch, distress the edges of the sign with sandpaper to give it a weathered look.
  5. Hang your wooden sign in your living room, kitchen, or on your front porch.

These wooden signs offer a charming, welcoming touch for any space and evoke the spirit of the harvest season.

4. Mason Jar Fall Lanterns

Mason jars are incredibly versatile and can easily be transformed into beautiful fall lanterns. With the right decorations and lighting, these jars will cast a warm, inviting glow in your home.

Materials:

  • Mason jars
  • Faux fall leaves, small pinecones, or acorns
  • Battery-operated tea light candles or fairy lights
  • Hot glue gun
  • Twine or burlap for decoration

Instructions:

  1. Fill each mason jar with faux fall leaves, pinecones, or acorns, creating a cozy, autumn vibe.
  2. Place a battery-operated tea light candle or a string of fairy lights inside each jar.
  3. Tie a piece of twine or burlap around the neck of the jar for an extra rustic feel.
  4. Arrange the jars as a centerpiece or line them along your mantel for a warm and inviting fall ambiance.

These DIY lanterns add a magical glow to your fall decor, perfect for both day and night.

5. Faux Fur Throw Pillows

Nothing says cozy like a plush, faux fur throw pillow. Create your own fall-themed throw pillows to add texture and warmth to your living room or bedroom.

Materials:

  • Faux fur fabric
  • Pillow inserts
  • Sewing machine or fabric glue
  • Scissors

Instructions:

  1. Measure the pillow inserts and cut two pieces of faux fur fabric to the appropriate size, leaving a small seam allowance.
  2. Sew the pieces together, leaving one side open.
  3. Turn the pillowcase inside out, insert the pillow, and sew the final side closed.
  4. If you don’t want to sew, you can simply use fabric glue to attach the edges.

These faux fur pillows are a chic and comfortable way to add some seasonal luxury to your space.

6. Fall Wreaths

A fall wreath is a classic and versatile decoration that can adorn your front door or be displayed indoors. Creating your own allows you to choose the materials and colors that match your personal style.

Materials:

  • Grapevine wreath base
  • Fall foliage (real or faux)
  • Pinecones
  • Ribbon (optional)
  • Hot glue gun

Instructions:

  1. Begin by attaching small branches of fall foliage (such as orange and yellow leaves) to the grapevine wreath base using a hot glue gun.
  2. Add small pinecones or other natural elements for texture and interest.
  3. If desired, attach a ribbon in a color that complements the autumn hues of the wreath.
  4. Hang the wreath on your front door, above the fireplace, or on a wall for a welcoming fall accent.

Wreaths are a timeless fall decor piece that adds a personal touch to your entryway or living space.

7. Pumpkin Spice Candles

Fall wouldn’t be complete without the cozy scent of pumpkin spice filling the air. Create your own pumpkin spice candles to infuse your home with the delightful aroma of the season.

Materials:

  • Candle wax (soy or beeswax)
  • Candle wicks
  • Pumpkin spice fragrance oil
  • Small candle jars or containers
  • Dye (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Melt the candle wax according to the instructions on the package.
  2. Once melted, add a few drops of pumpkin spice fragrance oil to the wax.
  3. If desired, add a few drops of orange dye to give the candles a festive color.
  4. Attach the wick to the bottom of your container, then pour the wax mixture into the jar.
  5. Let the candle set for several hours, then trim the wick.

These homemade pumpkin spice candles will add a touch of fall warmth and fragrance to any room in your home.

8. Acorn and Twig Picture Frame

Bring the outdoors inside by creating a rustic picture frame adorned with acorns and twigs. This project is perfect for showcasing your favorite fall memories.

Materials:

  • A plain picture frame
  • Small twigs
  • Acorns (real or faux)
  • Hot glue gun
  • Scissors

Instructions:

  1. Begin by gluing the twigs around the perimeter of the picture frame, layering them for a rustic effect.
  2. Once the twigs are in place, glue acorns to the frame, spacing them evenly for added texture.
  3. Let the frame dry completely before inserting your favorite fall photo.

This DIY project adds a nature-inspired touch to your autumn decor and makes for a wonderful personalized gift.

9. Cinnamon Stick Bundles

The sweet, spicy scent of cinnamon is synonymous with fall, and you can incorporate it into your decor by making simple cinnamon stick bundles. These can be used as centerpieces, drawer sachets, or even as ornaments.

Materials:

  • Cinnamon sticks
  • Twine or ribbon
  • Dried orange slices (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Gather a bundle of cinnamon sticks and tie them together using twine or ribbon.
  2. For added fragrance and visual appeal, attach dried orange slices or small sprigs of eucalyptus to the bundle.
  3. Place the bundles in bowls around your home, or tie them to branches to create a unique fall garland.

These cinnamon stick bundles will infuse your home with the lovely scent of autumn while adding a rustic touch to your decor.

10. Harvest Basket Display

Gather your fall harvest and create a beautiful display with a simple wicker basket. This project is a fantastic way to showcase pumpkins, gourds, and other seasonal fruits.

Materials:

  • Wicker basket
  • Small pumpkins, gourds, or apples
  • Dried wheat or corn stalks (optional)
  • Decorative fall ribbon (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Fill the wicker basket with an assortment of pumpkins, gourds, and apples.
  2. For added texture, place some dried wheat or corn stalks around the edges of the basket.
  3. Tie a decorative fall ribbon around the basket’s handle for an extra touch of autumn charm.

This DIY harvest basket display brings the bounty of the season into your home and makes a charming addition to any room.

Fall is a season full of opportunities for creativity and warmth. With these 10 DIY projects, you can add a personal touch to your home while celebrating the beauty of the autumn season. Whether you prefer rustic, farmhouse-inspired decor or modern minimalism, there’s something here for everyone. So, grab your crafting supplies and start decorating for fall today!
