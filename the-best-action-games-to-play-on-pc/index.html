The Best Action Games to Play on PC


When it comes to PC gaming, action games remain one of the most thrilling and immersive genres. Whether you’re a fan of fast-paced shooters, adrenaline-pumping platformers, or intense beat ‘em ups, the world of PC action games offers something for everyone. Over the years, the evolution of technology and creative design has brought an incredible variety of titles that push boundaries and deliver unforgettable experiences.

In this article, we’ll dive deep into some of the best action games available on PC today. We’ll cover classics that have stood the test of time, modern masterpieces, and hidden gems that deserve more recognition. From heart-thumping combat sequences to intricate storylines paired with explosive gameplay, you’ll find plenty to add to your play queue.

What Makes a Great Action Game on PC?

Before we jump into the list, let’s quickly break down what distinguishes a fantastic action game on the PC platform:

  • Smooth Controls and Responsiveness: The best action titles feature fluid, precise controls that make combat and movement satisfying without frustration.
  • Engaging Combat Mechanics: Whether melee, ranged, or a mix of both, combat has to be dynamic, challenging, and rewarding.
  • Compelling Storytelling: Many top-tier action games weave narratives seamlessly with gameplay, adding depth beyond just button-mashing.
  • Visual and Audio Excellence: Stunning graphics and captivating sound design enhance immersion, especially on high-end PC setups.
  • Replayability and Content: Unlockables, side missions, multiplayer modes, or procedurally generated elements keep you coming back.
  • Optimization and Mod Support: PC gamers appreciate moddable games and titles optimized to leverage powerful hardware.

Keeping this in mind, let’s explore the cream of the crop.

1. DOOM Eternal — The Pinnacle of Fast-Paced Shooter Action

DOOM Eternal is a masterclass in relentless, brutal first-person shooting. Its lightning-fast combat emphasizes aggressive movement, precision aiming, and quick decision-making. On PC, the game shines with buttery smooth frame rates and crisp visuals that can be enhanced with modding.

Why it’s great:

  • Fast, satisfying combat with diverse weapons
  • Challenging enemy AI requiring tactical thinking
  • Beautifully designed demonic environments
  • Heavy metal soundtrack that pumps adrenaline
  • Extensive DLC expanding the storyline and gameplay

If you crave heart-pounding action and deep combat mechanics, DOOM Eternal is unparalleled.

2. Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice — Masterful Combat Meets Intriguing Lore

From the creators of Dark Souls comes Sekiro, a game that redefines what an action game can be. Unlike typical RPG-influenced hack-and-slash games, Sekiro emphasizes precision parrying, stealth, and high-stakes timing.

Key features:

  • Unique posture and deflection combat system
  • Beautiful Japanese Sengoku-era setting
  • Intense boss battles that test patience and skill
  • Stealth elements woven seamlessly with combat
  • Richly detailed world with hidden lore

The challenge is brutal but deeply rewarding. PC players benefit from crisp input recognition, which is vital in this timing-dependent gameplay.

3. Control — A Cinematic Action Experience with Supernatural Powers

Control takes the action genre into the realm of the surreal, blending gunplay with psychic abilities. Set in the mysterious Federal Bureau of Control, players engage in dynamic battles using both weapons and telekinetic powers.

Highlights include:

  • Stunningly realized environments with next-gen ray tracing tech
  • Telekinetic combat that feels fresh and layered
  • Intricate and eerie story that unravels as you play
  • Varied enemy types that demand tactical adaptation
  • Fluid exploration mechanics that reward curiosity

Control stands out on PC not only for its visuals but also for taking action gameplay beyond standard gunfights.

4. Hades — The Action Rogue-Lite with Endless Replayability

From Supergiant Games, the critically acclaimed Hades offers fast-paced action combined with rogue-lite mechanics and a compelling narrative rooted in Greek mythology. Combat feels tight and rewarding, with a variety of weapons and upgrades.

What makes Hades a gem:

  • Rapid combat with a variety of weapons and abilities
  • Procedurally generated levels that keep experiences fresh
  • Deep story delivered through engaging character interactions
  • Beautiful hand-drawn art and animations
  • Smooth PC performance even on modest systems

It’s perfect for players who love quick bursts of action wrapped in rich storytelling.

5. Batman: Arkham Knight — The Ultimate Superhero Action Adventure

No list of PC action games is complete without Batman: Arkham Knight. It takes the beloved Dark Knight and places him in a sprawling Gotham City filled with crime, chaos, and complex enemies.

Notable features:

  • Intense combat system with smooth combos and gadgets
  • Open-world Gotham to explore and protect
  • Immersive storytelling featuring iconic villains
  • Fluid vehicle gameplay with the Batmobile
  • Stunning graphics optimized for high-end PCs

The game’s balance between melee combat, stealth, and exploration makes it one of the best superhero action titles available.

6. Ghostrunner — Cyberpunk Parkour Meets Brutal Combat

Ghostrunner is an ultra-fast first-person action platformer that combines parkour and one-hit-kill combat. Its futuristic cyberpunk setting is a visually stunning playground for players with lightning reflexes.

Why it stands out:

  • Precise movement and fast-paced combat mechanics
  • Vertical level design encourages creative navigation
  • Synthwave soundtrack setting the perfect tone
  • Challenging enemies requiring perfect timing and strategy
  • Customizable difficulty options for casual or hardcore play

If you enjoy twitch-based gameplay and graceful, deadly combat, Ghostrunner is a must-try.

7. Devil May Cry 5 — Stylish Action at Its Finest

Devil May Cry 5 continues the series’ tradition of flamboyant combat and masterful style. Players control multiple characters, each with unique weapon sets and combos, across intricately designed levels.

Key points:

  • Deep combo system rewarding skill and creativity
  • Gorgeous graphics and animation fidelity
  • An engaging plot with memorable characters
  • High replay value with multiple difficulty modes
  • Variety of play styles catering to different preferences

The PC version boasts high resolution and frame rates, making every action-packed moment truly shine.

8. Titanfall 2 — Fluid Movement and Intense Multiplayer Action

While Titanfall 2 is well-known for its multiplayer, the single-player campaign offers an enthralling action experience with inventive storytelling and creative gameplay.

Standout features:

  • Parkour-inspired movement and wall-running mechanics
  • High-octane pilot vs. titan battles
  • Engaging single-player campaign with a heartfelt story
  • Tight weapon and ability design
  • Multiplatform support with optimized PC performance

Action fans interested in both single-player and multiplayer will find a lot to love here.

9. Bayonetta 3 (via Emulator) — A Stylish Action Title Worth Exploring on PC

Though Bayonetta 3 is a Nintendo Switch exclusive, many fans use emulators to experience its intense, over-the-top action on PC. The Bayonetta series is famous for its combo-heavy combat and bold presentation.

What you’ll experience:

  • Fast-paced, combo-driven horde battles
  • Creative monster designs and boss fights
  • Fluid character animations and stylish moves
  • Engaging narrative with quirky humor

While unofficial, playing Bayonetta 3 on PC can be a thrilling alternative for action enthusiasts.

10. Nier: Automata — Philosophical Storytelling Meets Brilliant Action

NieR: Automata blends profound narrative themes with exhilarating combat. The game masterfully shifts between hack-and-slash sequences, bullet-hell sections, and RPG elements.

Essentials to know:

  • Fluid and dynamic combat that evolves over time
  • Multiple endings with layered storytelling
  • Unique camera and perspective shifts during gameplay
  • Atmospheric music and stunning art design
  • Innovative approach to game structure challenging conventions

It’s extraordinary not just for its action but for the emotional depth it delivers.

Making the Most of Your PC Action Gaming Experience

To fully enjoy these action games, consider investing in some PC accessories like a responsive mouse, mechanical keyboard, or a gamepad suited to your style. Plus, tweaking graphical settings can ensure smooth frame rates, especially for visually demanding titles.

If you’re looking to share experiences or dive into multiplayer modes, joining online communities can enrich your gaming journey and introduce you to fellow fans.

Wrapping Up

The PC platform offers an unmatched diversity of action games catering to all tastes, from frenetic shooters to story-driven adventures. The titles highlighted here represent some of the finest examples available right now. Whether you’re new to the genre or a veteran, these games hold countless hours of excitement, challenge, and memorable moments.

Ready to embark on your next action-packed journey? Fire up your PC, pick a title, and prepare to dive headfirst into adrenaline-fueled battles and epic storytelling. Action awaits!
