The Best Fishing Vlogs and YouTube Channels to Follow


Fishing is not just a hobby; it’s a way of life for many enthusiasts. The thrill of casting a line, the excitement of a catch, and the serenity of being on the water create a unique experience. With the rise of social media, especially YouTube, fishing enthusiasts have found a new way to connect, share tips, and explore the vast world of angling through vlogs. In this post, we will delve into some of the best fishing vlogs and YouTube channels that every angler should follow. Whether you are a seasoned pro or a beginner, these channels offer a wealth of knowledge, entertainment, and inspiration.

1. Jon B.

Jon B. has quickly become one of the most popular fishing YouTubers, known for his engaging personality and a knack for storytelling. His channel features a mix of freshwater and saltwater fishing adventures, showcasing various techniques and locations. Jon’s videos often include challenges, collaborations with other YouTubers, and a behind-the-scenes glimpse into his life as a professional angler. His relatable approach and high-quality production make his content both enjoyable and informative.

Highlights:

  • Diverse Content: Jon covers everything from bass fishing to saltwater adventures, appealing to a broad audience.
  • Engaging Challenges: Regularly hosts fun fishing challenges that encourage viewer participation.
  • Community Focus: Actively interacts with fans through comments and social media, fostering a sense of community.

2. Flair

Flair is a channel that blends humor with fishing expertise. Known for his entertaining personality, Flair offers a unique take on fishing vlogs. His videos often feature fishing challenges, unusual fishing methods, and even attempts at catching exotic species. Flair’s energetic style and comedic flair keep viewers entertained while also providing valuable tips and insights into various fishing techniques.

Highlights:

  • Unique Challenges: Flair often tackles unconventional fishing challenges, making for exciting content.
  • Humor and Knowledge: Balances entertainment with educational content, making it fun to learn new fishing techniques.
  • Interactive Audience: Encourages his viewers to suggest challenges and share their experiences in the comments.

3. Fishin’ With Flair

Not to be confused with Flair’s main channel, Fishin’ With Flair focuses more on the fishing aspect while still incorporating his signature humor. This channel is all about the thrill of the catch, featuring tutorials, gear reviews, and fishing trips. Fishin’ With Flair appeals to those looking to improve their skills while enjoying a good laugh.

Highlights:

  • Tutorials and Gear Reviews: Provides practical advice on gear and techniques.
  • Catch-Centric Content: Focuses on the excitement of fishing and the joy of catching various species.
  • Consistent Uploads: Regular updates keep the content fresh and engaging.

4. The Fish Whisperer

If you’re looking for a channel that dives deep into the art of fishing, The Fish Whisperer is a must-follow. This channel is dedicated to providing detailed tutorials on fishing techniques, bait selection, and location scouting. The host’s calm demeanor and detailed explanations make it easy for beginners to grasp essential skills while also offering insights for experienced anglers.

Highlights:

  • Detailed Tutorials: In-depth guides on various fishing techniques and strategies.
  • Skill Development: Focuses on teaching viewers how to become better anglers.
  • Calm and Informative: The host’s soothing voice and thorough explanations create an inviting atmosphere.

5. TacticalBassin

For those interested in bass fishing, TacticalBassin is the go-to channel. The hosts, Matt and Tim, provide excellent insights into bass fishing techniques, seasonal patterns, and gear recommendations. Their expertise is backed by years of experience, and they often share tips that can significantly increase your catch rates. The channel features everything from instructional content to vlogs of their fishing trips.

Highlights:

  • Expert Insights: The hosts share their extensive knowledge of bass fishing.
  • Seasonal Tips: Offers guidance on how to adapt your fishing tactics throughout the year.
  • High Production Quality: Well-edited videos with clear visuals and sound.

6. LunkersTV

LunkersTV is a dynamic fishing channel that captures the essence of adventure fishing. The host, Rob, takes viewers on exciting fishing trips, showcasing various locations and species. The channel highlights both the thrill of the catch and the beauty of the outdoors. Rob’s charismatic personality and sense of humor make every video enjoyable, while his passion for fishing shines through.

Highlights:

  • Adventure-Focused: Emphasizes exciting fishing experiences and beautiful locations.
  • Personality-Driven Content: Rob’s engaging nature keeps viewers coming back for more.
  • Diverse Species: Covers a wide range of fishing types, from bass to saltwater species.

7. Ryan’s Fishing Adventures

For those who prefer a more laid-back approach to fishing, Ryan’s Fishing Adventures is a channel that captures the tranquility of the sport. Ryan shares his fishing experiences in various picturesque locations, often highlighting the peacefulness of angling. His videos focus on the beauty of nature and the joy of fishing, making it a perfect escape for viewers.

Highlights:

  • Nature-Centric: Focuses on the beauty of fishing and the surrounding environment.
  • Relaxed Vibe: Offers a calming escape with soothing visuals and sounds.
  • Personal Stories: Shares personal anecdotes that resonate with viewers.

8. Apex Fishing

Apex Fishing is a channel dedicated to exploring the technical side of fishing. This channel is perfect for those looking to delve deeper into fishing techniques, tackle reviews, and fishing gear. The hosts provide comprehensive insights into various fishing methods, making it a valuable resource for serious anglers.

Highlights:

  • Technical Expertise: Focuses on in-depth reviews and explanations of fishing gear and techniques.
  • Informative Content: Offers detailed guides that help viewers improve their skills.
  • Engaging Format: Combines entertainment with education to keep viewers interested.

9. Salt Strong

For saltwater fishing enthusiasts, Salt Strong is a treasure trove of information. This channel focuses on saltwater fishing techniques, gear recommendations, and fishing strategies for various species. The hosts are passionate about fishing and share their knowledge to help viewers become more successful anglers. Their friendly and approachable style makes learning about saltwater fishing enjoyable.

Highlights:

  • Saltwater Focus: Specifically targets saltwater fishing techniques and species.
  • Community Engagement: Encourages viewer interaction and feedback.
  • Comprehensive Guides: Provides valuable tips for both beginners and experienced anglers.

10. Fishing with Luiza

Fishing with Luiza offers a unique perspective on fishing through the eyes of a female angler. Luiza’s channel is not only about fishing techniques but also about empowering women in the fishing community. She shares her adventures, tips, and experiences in a relatable and encouraging manner.

Highlights:

  • Empowerment Focus: Inspires women to engage in fishing and outdoor activities.
  • Personal Touch: Luiza’s relatable storytelling creates a strong connection with viewers.
  • Inclusive Community: Fosters a welcoming environment for all anglers.

Conclusion

The world of fishing vlogs and YouTube channels is vast and varied, offering something for everyone. Whether you are looking for expert tips, entertaining challenges, or simply a peaceful escape into nature, the channels listed above are some of the best in the community. By following these creators, you can enhance your fishing skills, discover new techniques, and connect with fellow anglers. So grab your gear, head to the water, and don’t forget to tune into these fantastic fishing vlogs for inspiration and knowledge. Happy fishing!
