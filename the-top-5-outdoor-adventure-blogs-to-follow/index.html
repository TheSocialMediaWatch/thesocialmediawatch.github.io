The Top 5 Outdoor Adventure Blogs to Follow


If you’re an outdoor enthusiast looking for inspiration, tips, and stories that ignite your wanderlust, you’ve landed in the right place. With the digital landscape overflowing with content, it can be challenging to pinpoint which blogs are truly worth your time. Here, we present a curated list of the top five outdoor adventure blogs that not only capture the essence of nature but also inspire readers to explore the great outdoors. Each blog is unique in its offering, catering to different aspects of outdoor adventure, from hiking and camping to climbing and travel.

1. The Adventure Junkies

The Adventure Junkies is an essential destination for anyone who craves adventure in the wild. This blog is a treasure trove of resources that cater to all types of adventurers, whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner looking to dive into the world of outdoor sports.

Why Follow The Adventure Junkies?

The Adventure Junkies combines expert advice with personal anecdotes, making it both informative and relatable. The blog covers a wide range of topics, including:

  • Gear Reviews: In-depth reviews of the latest outdoor gear, ensuring you make informed purchasing decisions.
  • Skill Guides: Step-by-step tutorials on various outdoor activities like rock climbing, kayaking, and hiking.
  • Destination Guides: Detailed guides to some of the most breathtaking locations to explore.

Additionally, their engaging writing style and stunning photography draw readers in, making every post a delightful journey. They also have a strong social media presence, allowing followers to stay updated on the latest outdoor trends and adventures.

2. REI Co-op Journal

If you’re passionate about outdoor adventures, the REI Co-op Journal is a must-follow. As part of the renowned REI brand, this blog is dedicated to all things outdoor lifestyle. It not only promotes outdoor activities but also emphasizes sustainability and community.

What Makes REI Co-op Journal Stand Out?

  • Diverse Content: The blog features articles on a wide range of outdoor pursuits, including hiking, biking, camping, and climbing.
  • Expert Insights: With contributions from outdoor experts and enthusiasts, readers gain access to professional advice and tips.
  • Sustainability Focus: REI places a strong emphasis on environmental stewardship, providing insights on how to enjoy the outdoors while minimizing impact.

The REI Co-op Journal also fosters a sense of community among outdoor lovers, encouraging readers to share their experiences and adventures. This collaborative spirit makes it a fantastic resource for anyone looking to connect with like-minded individuals.

3. Bearfoot Theory

Bearfoot Theory is the brainchild of Kristen Bor, an outdoor enthusiast passionate about sharing her adventures and knowledge with the world. This blog is an incredible resource for those who seek to explore the great outdoors, especially in the national parks and off-the-beaten-path locations.

Why Bearfoot Theory is Worth a Read

  • Personal Stories: Kristen’s engaging storytelling draws readers in, making them feel as if they’re part of her adventures.
  • Practical Tips: The blog offers practical advice on camping, hiking, and outdoor gear, making it especially helpful for beginners.
  • Focus on Inclusivity: Bearfoot Theory promotes inclusivity in the outdoor community, encouraging people of all backgrounds to explore and enjoy nature.

Kristen’s vibrant personality shines through her writing, making Bearfoot Theory not just informative but also an enjoyable read. Whether you’re looking for travel inspiration or practical advice, this blog has something for everyone.

4. The Hiking Life

For those who find solace in the mountains, The Hiking Life is a blog that should be on your radar. Created by Andrew Skurka, an experienced long-distance hiker and backpacker, this blog is a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in hiking and backpacking.

What to Expect from The Hiking Life

  • In-Depth Guides: Andrew provides detailed guides on popular hiking trails, including maps, gear recommendations, and personal insights.
  • Training Advice: The blog includes training tips for aspiring hikers, helping them build the necessary skills and endurance for long treks.
  • Personal Experiences: Andrew shares his personal hiking experiences, offering a unique perspective on the joys and challenges of outdoor adventures.

The Hiking Life is perfect for those who are not just looking to explore but also want to enhance their hiking skills. Andrew’s expertise and passion for hiking make this blog a must-follow for outdoor enthusiasts.

5. Outdoor Voices

Outdoor Voices is more than just a blog; it’s a movement that encourages people to get outside and be active. Founded with the mission to encourage more people to participate in outdoor activities, this blog is filled with inspiring stories, tips, and community-driven content.

Reasons to Follow Outdoor Voices

  • Community Engagement: The blog fosters a sense of community, encouraging readers to share their outdoor experiences and connect with others.
  • Diverse Activities: Outdoor Voices covers a wide range of activities, from running and cycling to hiking and yoga, appealing to a broad audience.
  • Motivational Content: The blog features motivational pieces that inspire readers to push their boundaries and embrace the outdoors.

Outdoor Voices stands out for its positive and inclusive approach to outdoor activity. Their motto, “Doing Things,” encapsulates their philosophy of encouraging everyone to participate in outdoor adventures, regardless of their skill level.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for practical advice, inspiring stories, or a sense of community, these five outdoor adventure blogs cover it all. From The Adventure Junkies’ comprehensive gear reviews to Bearfoot Theory’s personal insights, each blog offers unique content that resonates with outdoor enthusiasts.

Following these blogs will not only keep you informed about the latest trends and tips in the outdoor world but also motivate you to embark on your next adventure. So grab your gear, lace up your hiking boots, and let these blogs inspire you to explore the great outdoors!
