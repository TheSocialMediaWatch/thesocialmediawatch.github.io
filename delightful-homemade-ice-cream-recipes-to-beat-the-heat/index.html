Delightful Homemade Ice Cream Recipes to Beat the Heat


The scorching summer heat is upon us, and what better way to beat it than with some delightful homemade ice cream recipes? Ice cream has always been a classic treat, loved by people of all ages. Whether you prefer creamy classics or refreshing fruit flavors, we have you covered with these easy and delicious recipes.

Creamy Vanilla Bean Ice Cream

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 vanilla bean, split lengthwise
  • 6 large egg yolks

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, milk, and half of the sugar. Scrape the seeds from the vanilla bean and add them to the mixture. Heat over medium-low heat until it starts to steam, but not boil.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and the remaining sugar until well combined.
  3. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg yolk mixture, whisking constantly to prevent curdling.
  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and strain the custard through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps or cooked egg bits.
  6. Let the custard cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  7. Once chilled, transfer the custard to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  8. Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for an additional 2-3 hours to firm up before serving.

Refreshing Strawberry Sorbet

Preparation time: 15 minutes

Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. In a blender or food processor, puree the strawberries until smooth.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar and water. Heat over medium heat until the sugar dissolves completely, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove the sugar syrup from heat and let it cool for a few minutes.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the strawberry puree, sugar syrup, and lemon juice. Stir well to combine.
  5. Cover the bowl and refrigerate the mixture for at least 2 hours to chill.
  6. Once chilled, transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  7. Transfer the sorbet to an airtight container and freeze for an additional 2-3 hours to firm up before serving.

Decadent Chocolate Peanut Butter Swirl Ice Cream

Preparation time: 30 minutes

Serves: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the heavy cream, milk, sugar, and cocoa powder. Heat over medium-low heat until it starts to steam, but not boil.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks until well combined.
  3. Slowly pour the hot cream mixture into the egg yolks, whisking constantly to prevent curdling.
  4. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over low heat, stirring constantly until it thickens and coats the back of a spoon. This should take about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the vanilla extract.
  6. Transfer half of the custard to a separate bowl and whisk in the peanut butter until smooth.
  7. Let both custards cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Once chilled, pour the chocolate custard into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
  9. In the last few minutes of churning, slowly drizzle the peanut butter custard into the ice cream to create swirls.
  10. Transfer the churned ice cream to an airtight container and freeze for an additional 2-3 hours to firm up before serving.

Conclusion

With these delightful homemade ice cream recipes, you can beat the heat and indulge in refreshing treats that are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. Whether you prefer the classic creaminess of vanilla, the fruity freshness of strawberry sorbet, or the decadent combination of chocolate and peanut butter, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your ingredients, get creative, and start churning your way to homemade ice cream bliss. Stay cool and enjoy!
