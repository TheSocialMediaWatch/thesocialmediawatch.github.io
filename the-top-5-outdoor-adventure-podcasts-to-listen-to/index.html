The Top 5 Outdoor Adventure Podcasts to Listen to


Are you an outdoor enthusiast looking for some inspiration, tips, and thrilling stories to fuel your next adventure? If so, you’re in luck! The world of podcasts is brimming with exciting content tailored specifically for outdoor enthusiasts like you. Whether you’re an avid hiker, a camping aficionado, a passionate rock climber, or simply someone who loves to immerse themselves in tales of outdoor escapades, there’s a podcast out there for you. To help you navigate through the sea of options, we’ve curated a list of the top 5 outdoor adventure podcasts that are guaranteed to ignite your wanderlust and keep you entertained for hours on end.

1. The Dirtbag Diaries

Hosted by Fitz Cahall, The Dirtbag Diaries is a renowned podcast that delves into the raw and captivating stories of outdoor adventurers from all walks of life. From heart-pounding tales of survival to introspective reflections on the transformative power of nature, each episode is a rollercoaster ride through the untamed wilderness. Whether you’re embarking on a solo trek or planning a group camping trip, The Dirtbag Diaries will fill your ears with riveting narratives that will leave you itching to venture into the great outdoors.

2. Outside Podcast

Produced by Outside Magazine, the Outside Podcast is a treasure trove of thought-provoking discussions, expert advice, and captivating interviews with prominent figures in the outdoor community. Whether you’re seeking tips on gear, insights into environmental conservation, or a deep dive into the science behind outdoor pursuits, the Outside Podcast has something for everyone. With its diverse range of topics, this podcast is a must-listen for anyone passionate about outdoor exploration and environmental stewardship.

3. The Adventure Sports Podcast

For those who thrive on adrenaline-pumping activities and extreme sports, The Adventure Sports Podcast is a goldmine of adrenaline-fueled content. Hosted by Mason Gravley, this podcast features in-depth conversations with athletes, adventurers, and industry experts who share their experiences and expertise in a wide array of adventure sports. Whether you’re into mountain biking, whitewater rafting, or backcountry skiing, this podcast offers valuable insights and exhilarating stories that will fuel your passion for adventure.

4. She Explores

She Explores is a captivating podcast that celebrates the diverse experiences of women in the outdoors. Through engaging interviews, personal narratives, and thought-provoking discussions, this podcast sheds light on the unique perspectives and challenges faced by women in the outdoor community. Whether you’re a female outdoor enthusiast seeking relatable stories and empowering insights, or simply someone who appreciates the multifaceted nature of outdoor exploration, She Explores offers a refreshing and inclusive take on the outdoor adventure podcast genre.

5. The First 40 Miles

If you’re new to the world of outdoor adventure or looking to brush up on your skills, The First 40 Miles is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by Heather Legler and Josh Legler, this podcast is geared towards beginner adventurers, offering practical advice, gear reviews, and inspiring stories to help you embark on your outdoor journey with confidence. Whether you’re planning your first backpacking trip or seeking essential tips for outdoor survival, The First 40 Miles provides a wealth of valuable information in an accessible and engaging format.

In conclusion, the world of outdoor adventure podcasts is a rich tapestry of captivating stories, expert advice, and diverse perspectives that are sure to appeal to outdoor enthusiasts of all backgrounds. Whether you’re seeking adrenaline-pumping tales of extreme sports, empowering narratives from female adventurers, or practical tips for your next outdoor excursion, these top 5 podcasts have got you covered. So, grab your headphones, lace up your hiking boots, and get ready to embark on an audio journey through the great outdoors!
