Top Marketing Podcasts to Follow in 2024


In the fast-paced world of marketing, staying updated with the latest trends, strategies, and industry insights is non-negotiable. Podcasts have emerged as a go-to resource for marketers looking to engage with diverse perspectives, expert advice, and case studies—all while on the move. Whether you’re a seasoned marketer, a business owner, or someone dipping their toes into digital marketing, the right podcast can fuel your knowledge, spark creativity, and elevate your marketing game.

As 2024 rolls in, new marketing challenges and opportunities demand fresh thinking, and podcasts have adapted to these shifts with content that’s sharper, data-driven, and more practical than ever before. In this comprehensive guide, we uncover the top marketing podcasts to follow in 2024 that promise not just to inform, but to inspire action.

Why Marketing Podcasts Are a Must in 2024

Before diving into the list, it’s worth reflecting on why marketing podcasts continue to gain traction. The flexibility of audio content allows busy professionals to learn during commutes, workouts, or between meetings. Podcasts often feature interviews with marketers from top companies, deep dives into analytics, or explorations into emerging technologies like artificial intelligence in marketing.

In 2024, marketing is not just about brand visibility but about creating meaningful customer experiences and leveraging data for personalization. Therefore, the best marketing podcasts blend theory with actionable steps, enabling listeners to implement new tactics immediately.

The Ultimate List: Marketing Podcasts to Watch in 2024

1. Marketing Over Coffee

One of the longstanding favorites, Marketing Over Coffee is perfect for marketers who appreciate a blend of old-school marketing principles and modern digital tactics. Hosts John Wall and Christopher Penn bring a conversational tone mixed with expert guests, covering topics including SEO, social media trends, email marketing, data analytics, and automation.

The appeal? Short, punchy episodes that respect your time without skimping on valuable insights. For those who want a steady stream of both strategic concepts and trending tools, this podcast remains a go-to.

2. The Growth Marketing Toolbox

Hosted by Nicholas Scalice, The Growth Marketing Toolbox zeroes in on the tactical side of marketing. If you’re curious about the best tools for boosting growth—be it analytics dashboards, SEO enhancers, or content marketing platforms—this podcast is invaluable.

Each episode dives deep into one tool or technique, often featuring expert demos and practical applications. The detail-oriented nature allows listeners to discover new software while gaining a clear understanding of how it fits into broader growth strategies.

3. Advertising Is Dead

Brought to you by Scott King and host Erik Deutsch, Advertising Is Dead challenges conventional marketing ideas, pushing boundaries by focusing on innovation and culture-led marketing. This podcast is a breath of fresh air for marketers craving creativity balanced with data-driven insights.

Episodes often discuss marketing’s intersection with societal shifts, brand storytelling, and the evolving role of the consumer. It’s perfect for anyone wanting to rethink traditional advertising and reimagine marketing’s role in 2024.

4. Everyone Hates Marketers

The title itself sets the tone for Everyone Hates Marketers, which is hosted by Louis Grenier. This podcast embraces a no-fluff approach to marketing, tackling the clichés and often misleading tactics prevalent in the industry.

Grenier’s interviews with marketing experts cut to the chase, providing listeners with actionable advice on building authentic brands and cutting through noise in crowded markets. It’s a must-listen for those fed up with jargon-heavy marketing and wanting real-world strategies that work.

5. The Science of Social Media

Tailored specifically for social media marketers, The Science of Social Media by Buffer digs into platform updates, algorithm changes, and the psychology behind social engagement. Each episode balances data intelligence with creativity, making it an essential resource for anyone managing social channels.

With content often backed by research, the podcast equips you to optimize social campaigns across platforms like TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and newer emerging channels, keeping your social strategy relevant in 2024’s ever-shifting landscape.

6. Call to Action

From the team at Unbounce, Call to Action delves into conversion rate optimization and landing page strategies, critical for marketers focused on turning traffic into revenue. The podcast offers case studies, testing tips, and interviews with CRO experts.

In an era where personalization and customer journey mapping are key, learning to optimize calls to action can significantly boost ROI. This podcast is an outstanding resource for digital marketers who want to maximize every click’s potential.

7. Content Inc.

Led by Joe Pulizzi, founder of the Content Marketing Institute, Content Inc. is the go-to podcast for building audiences through powerful content strategies. Pulizzi shares stories and tactics from businesses and influencers who have successfully built brands from content-first approaches.

Content marketing continues to dominate many budgets in 2024, and this podcast helps ensure your content stands out, engages the right audiences, and supports sustainable growth.

8. Marketing School

Hosts Neil Patel and Eric Siu offer bite-sized daily episodes packed with quick tips, strategies, and industry news in Marketing School. The approachable, fast-paced format makes it easy to absorb actionable ideas even during the busiest schedules.

From SEO hacks to email marketing tweaks, this podcast is a practical tool for marketers who want continuous learning without committing to long-form content.

9. The Digital Marketing Podcast

A global perspective is what The Digital Marketing Podcast provides, with hosts Ciaran Rogers and Daniel Rowles exploring trends affecting marketers worldwide. This podcast blends case studies, expert interviews, and marketing news in a comprehensive package.

As marketing becomes increasingly interconnected on an international scale, this podcast helps you understand cultural nuances and strategies that work beyond local markets.

10. Call Me CMO

Focusing on leadership in marketing, Call Me CMO features conversations with marketing executives about the challenges and opportunities of steering marketing teams and brands. The podcast covers topics like team building, budget management, marketing technologies, and brand evolution.

If you’re aspiring to or already in a chief marketing role, tuning in can boost your leadership skills and strategic vision.

Bonus Picks for Niche and Emerging Marketing Areas

  • Voice Marketing Podcast – Explores trends in voice search and smart assistants, a growing frontier in digital marketing.
  • AI Marketing Insights – Delivers updates on how artificial intelligence is transforming marketing automation, analytics, and customer experiences.
  • Local Marketing Made Simple – Focuses on strategies for small businesses and local markets, invaluable for community-based entrepreneurs.

How to Make the Most of Your Marketing Podcast Experience in 2024

With so many options available, you might wonder how to choose the right podcasts and integrate listening into your professional routine:

  1. Define Your Goals: Are you seeking strategic insight, tactical advice, or inspiration? Different podcasts serve different needs.
  2. Create a Playlist: Use platforms like Spotify or Apple Podcasts to organize episodes around specific topics or themes.
  3. Take Notes & Implement: Don’t just listen passively. Capture key takeaways and brainstorm how to apply them to your own campaigns.
  4. Engage with Hosts & Communities: Many podcasts have associated online groups or social media where you can continue discussions, ask questions, and network.
  5. Balance Quantity & Quality: Aim for consistent listening but avoid overload. Sometimes one insightful episode can be more valuable than several superficial ones.

Looking Ahead: Marketing Trends Amplified by Podcasts in 2024

As marketers engage with these podcasts, several overarching trends become clear:

  • Data and Analytics Dominate: Podcasts increasingly highlight the necessity of data-backed decisions and experimentation.
  • AI and Automation Integration: Discussion around AI tools for content creation, personalization, and customer service is accelerating.
  • Customer Experience as King: Authenticity, transparency, and emotional connection are redefining marketing narratives.
  • Cross-Channel and Omnichannel Marketing: Strategic synchronization across platforms is a recurring theme.
  • Sustainability and Ethics: While subtle, more marketing conversations incorporate brand values aligned with social responsibility.

Tuning into these top marketing podcasts offers a front-row seat to discussions that shape industry direction and the opportunity to stay nimble in a competitive marketplace.

Final Thoughts

Choosing the right marketing podcasts to follow in 2024 can be a game-changer for your professional development and your company’s marketing efforts. From in-depth tool reviews to innovative storytelling and leadership advice, the podcasts featured here provide a diverse toolkit for all kinds of marketers.

Plug in during your daily routine, engage actively with the content, and watch your marketing approach evolve with fresh insights and proven tactics. The marketing landscape of 2024 is dynamic and full of promise—these podcasts are your trusted allies on the journey towards smarter, more effective marketing.

If you’re ready to elevate your marketing knowledge, pick a few from this list, subscribe, and let the learning begin!
