10 Must-Visit Car Shows Around the World


Car enthusiasts, collectors, and casual fans alike eagerly anticipate annual car shows, which serve as a vibrant showcase of automotive culture and innovation. These events are not just about the vehicles; they encompass the lifestyle, passion, and community surrounding cars. From classic beauties to cutting-edge designs, here are ten must-visit car shows that promise to ignite your passion for automobiles and leave you with unforgettable memories.

1. Geneva International Motor Show - Switzerland

Held annually in March, the Geneva International Motor Show is a prestigious event that attracts major automotive manufacturers from around the globe. This show is renowned for unveiling stunning new models and innovative concepts. Attendees get the first glimpse of the latest automotive technology, including electric and hybrid vehicles, showcasing the industry’s shift towards sustainability.

One of the highlights of the 2023 show was the debut of the Porsche Mission R, a fully electric racing car that combines performance with environmental consciousness. The atmosphere is electric, with media coverage and industry executives mingling, making it a must-visit for anyone serious about cars.

2. Los Angeles Auto Show - USA

Taking place every November, the Los Angeles Auto Show is a blend of glitz and innovation, reflecting the unique Californian car culture. The event features a variety of exhibits ranging from luxury vehicles to eco-friendly models. With a strong focus on electric vehicles, the 2023 show saw the launch of the much-anticipated Lucid Air, which offers both luxury and impressive performance.

Beyond the models, the show often incorporates and discussions on the future of the automotive industry, giving visitors insights into trends shaping the market. The vibrant atmosphere and celebrity appearances make it a fun experience for all attendees.

3. Tokyo Motor Show - Japan

The Tokyo Motor Show, held biennially, is a celebration of Japanese automotive technology and innovation. The 2022 edition showcased stunning concept cars like the Toyota bZ4X, highlighting the country’s forward-thinking approach to sustainability and design.

When you walk through the exhibition halls, you’ll encounter a mix of fantasy and reality, with manufacturers presenting vehicles that seem straight out of sci-fi films. The show also emphasizes robotics and AI technologies, which are increasingly integrated into vehicles, making it a fascinating experience for tech enthusiasts as well.

4. Goodwood Festival of Speed - UK

For those who appreciate the thrill of motorsport, the Goodwood Festival of Speed in West Sussex is an unmissable event. Taking place every July, this festival is a celebration of both classic and modern motorsport. Visitors can watch legendary cars tackle the famous Goodwood Hillclimb, offering a unique blend of speed and history.

The 2023 festival saw the return of iconic cars like the McLaren F1 and celebrated racing legends like Sir Jackie Stewart. The atmosphere is filled with excitement, as fans gather to experience the roar of engines and witness thrilling demonstrations that bring automotive history to life.

5. Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance - USA

Every August, the Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance in California transforms the picturesque coastline into a car lover’s paradise. This event is renowned for its focus on classic and vintage automobiles, showcasing some of the rarest cars in the world.

Attendees can admire beautifully restored vehicles, such as the 1937 Talbot-Lago T150-C-SS, which took home the Best of Show award in 2022. Beyond the competition, the event serves as a charity fundraiser, with proceeds benefiting various local organizations. The elegance of the setting and the quality of the cars make it an unforgettable experience.

6. SEMA Show - USA

The SEMA Show, held every November in Las Vegas, is the premier automotive specialty products trade event in world. While it’s more industry-focused, the creativity and innovation on display are truly captivating. The show features everything from aftermarket parts to high-performance vehicles.

The 2022 SEMA Show unveiled groundbreaking builds like the Ford Bronco RTR, showcasing how customization can transform ordinary vehicles into extraordinary machines. Attendees can immerse themselves in workshops, networking events, and competitions, making it a vital experience for anyone in the automotive aftermarket industry.

7. Paris Motor Show - France

Known as one of the oldest automotive shows, the Paris Motor Show is a biennial event that blends automotive history with modern innovation. The 2022 edition featured a stunning array of electric vehicles, with brands like Renault and Peugeot showcasing their latest models.

The charm of the Paris Motor Show lies not only in the vehicles but also in the atmosphere of the city itself. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll through the exhibits while soaking in the French culture, culinary delights, and art that surrounds the event. The combination of automotive innovation and the beauty of Paris makes this show a unique experience.

8. Monterey Car Week - USA

Monterey Car Week, held in August, is a week-long celebration of automotive culture, culminating in the prestigious Pebble Beach Concours d’Elegance. This event attracts car collectors, enthusiasts, and industry insiders from around the world.

Highlights include auctions from renowned houses like RM Sotheby’s, where rare vehicles like the 1955 Ferrari 410 Sport Spider can fetch astronomical prices. The various events throughout the week, including rallies and classic car shows, create an unparalleled atmosphere of camaraderie and passion for automobiles.

9. Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition - China

The Shanghai International Automobile Industry Exhibition is one of the largest auto shows in the world, typically held in April. It showcases a broad spectrum of vehicles, from luxury cars to the latest electric models, reflecting China’s burgeoning automotive market.

The 2023 show spotlighted domestic brands like NIO and BYD, which are redefining the electric vehicle landscape. With a focus on innovation and technology, this exhibition attracts global attention and serves as a platform for international brands to penetrate the booming Chinese market.

10. Cincinnati Auto Expo - USA

For a more localized experience, the Cincinnati Auto Expo, held each February, offers a relaxed atmosphere perfect for families and casual car enthusiasts. The expo features a mix of new models, unique customizations, and educational exhibits.

The 2023 event included various seminars on vehicle maintenance and eco-friendly driving, making it an informative experience. Local dealerships showcase their newest offerings, giving attendees a chance to explore and even test-drive the latest vehicles in a friendly environment.

These car shows not only spotlight the latest automotive advancements but also create a unique atmosphere where enthusiasts can connect, learn, and celebrate their shared passion. Whether you’re a serious collector or simply enjoy the beauty of cars, each of these events offers something special that you won’t want to miss. Make your travel plans, grab your friends, and immerse yourself in the vibrant world of automotive excellence!
