Seasonal Planting Guide: What to Plant Each Month


在新年来临之际，我们总是充满希望和梦想。gardening也是一样，我们可以通过种植一些适合季节的植物来提高我们的精神和体质。January is a great time to start planting some cool-season crops, such as broccoli, cauliflower, and kale. These plants thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in as little as 50 days. You can also plant some root vegetables like carrots, beets, and radishes, which can be harvested in about 60 days.

二月

February is a great time to keep planting those cool-season crops, as well as some hardy perennials like daffodils and tulips. These bulbs will bloom in the spring and add some color to your garden. You can also start seedlings indoors for some of the warmer-season crops, like tomatoes and peppers. This will give them a head start on the growing season and ensure a strong and healthy plant.

三月

As the weather starts to warm up in March, it’s time to start planting some of the warmer-season crops. Tomatoes, peppers, and eggplants are all great options for this time of year. You can also start planting some herbs like basil and cilantro, which will do well in the warmer temperatures. Don’t forget to continue planting those cool-season crops, as they will still do well in the cooler temperatures.

四月

April is a great time to start planting some of the spring-flowering bulbs, like hyacinths and crocuses. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden. You can also start planting some of the warmer-season crops, like beans and squash. These plants will thrive in the warmer temperatures and can be harvested in about 60 days.

五月

May is a great time to start planting some of the warm-season crops, like corn and okra. These plants will thrive in the warmer temperatures and can be harvested in about 70 days. You can also continue planting those cool-season crops, as they will still do well in the cooler temperatures. Don’t forget to water your plants regularly, as the warmer temperatures can cause them to dry out quickly.

六月

June is a great time to start planting some of the summer-flowering annuals, like marigolds and zinnias. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden. You can also start planting some of the warm-season crops, like cucumbers and melons. These plants will thrive in the warmer temperatures and can be harvested in about 70 days.

七月

July is a great time to start planting some of the warm-season crops, like eggplants and okra. These plants will thrive in the warmer temperatures and can be harvested in about 70 days. You can also continue planting those cool-season crops, as they will still do well in the cooler temperatures. Don’t forget to water your plants regularly, as the warmer temperatures can cause them to dry out quickly.

八月

August is a great time to start planting some of the fall-flowering annuals, like asters and chrysanthemums. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden. You can also start planting some of the cool-season crops, like broccoli and kale. These plants will thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in about 50 days.

九月

September is a great time to start planting some of the fall-flowering bulbs, like daffodils and tulips. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden. You can also start planting some of the cool-season crops, like carrots and beets. These plants will thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in about 60 days.

十月

October is a great time to start planting some of the fall-flowering annuals, like marigolds and zinnias. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden. You can also start planting some of the cool-season crops, like kale and spinach. These plants will thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in about 50 days.

十一月

November is a great time to start planting some of the cool-season crops, like broccoli and cauliflower. These plants will thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in about 50 days. You can also start planting some of the fall-flowering bulbs, like hyacinths and crocuses. These will bloom in the next few weeks and add some color to your garden.

十二月

December is a great time to start planning your garden for the next year. You can also start planting some of the cool-season crops, like kale and spinach. These plants will thrive in the cooler temperatures and can be harvested in about 50 days. Don’t forget to mulch your garden bed to protect the soil and retain moisture.

By following this seasonal planting guide, you can ensure a bountiful harvest and enjoy the beauty of your garden throughout the year. Remember to always check the specific planting times for your area and to plant according to the weather conditions. Happy gardening!
