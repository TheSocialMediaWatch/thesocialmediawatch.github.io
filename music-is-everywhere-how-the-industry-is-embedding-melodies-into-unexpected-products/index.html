Music Is Everywhere: How the Industry is Embedding Melodies Into Unexpected Products


The humble melody has come a long way from the days of singing around the campfire. Today, music is ubiquitous – not just infiltrating our headphones and playlists, but embedding itself into every aspect of our lives. The music industry has truly embraced the potential to partner with diverse brands and products, ultimately making music an integral and unexpected part of our daily routines.

Recently, music has started sneaking its way into some very unconventional products - a testament to the fact that entrepreneurs, musicians, and innovators alike recognize that embedding tunes into our day can enhance seemingly mundane experiences. From soundtracks to facilitate physical activity, to the auditory accompaniment of a relaxing soak, here are 15 surprising ways music is finding itself into the unlikeliest of places.

  1. Office Equipment: In this fast-paced world, we’re all in need of a little productivity boost. Now, you can have your music, too, without having to blast it on your phone and disturb your colleagues. Companies like Reyco Music have seamlessly married great design with integrated speakers. Featuring a sleek and minimalist appearance, these contemporary office gadgets come equipped with a dedicated music system that allows for a harmonious balance of work and sound, all without disturbing those around you.

  2. Coffee Makers: How many days have you started your day without the perfect blend of coffee and some soul-stirring tunes? With an entire range of smart coffee makers now compatible with music streaming services, you can rejoice in the simplicity of curating your favorite songs to not just wake you up, but truly set the tone for the day ahead.

  3. Treadmills and Exercise Bikes: Staying active isn’t just about counting calories burned or tracking your time spent running. Music has long been proven to help boost mood and motivation, and that’s why today’s exercise machines have responded with built-in music systems. Sync your playlist directly to your fitness equipment, allowing your workout to be attended by your favorite tunes, as you pound the pavement or ride your way to healthy living.

  4. Smart Toys: As technology advances, so too does the connectivity of our children’s playthings. The latest in children’s toys, like smart doodle pads or interactive robots, have begun to incorporate music into their functionalities, creating a multisensory experience that both entertains and educates.

  5. Art Installations: A step outside the conventional music venue, artists and galleries have explored innovative ways of incorporating music into their works. Some have turned to soundscapes, where melodies, rhythms, and sounds become a puzzle, while others experiment by invoking auditory environments both familiar and unknown. In these artistic realms, music transcends its staple format and breathes life into the creative canvas.

  6. Luxury Cars: Driving has never been the same with the emergence of premium automotive brands integrating state-of-the-art audio systems into their vehicle designs. From Mercedes-Benz to BMW, music is not just an add-on, but a complete sensory experience as you cruise along. As per the latest automotive trends, some of these sound systems now include a cutting-edge equalizer that can even adjust to the cabin’s acoustics for the perfect musical backdrop.

  7. Toiletries and Beauty Products: The bathroom may not seem the most fitting place for a music tune-in, yet manufacturers have thoughtfully woven melodies into soaps, shampoos, and even toothpaste tubes. A gentle melody on the tongue, for instance, not only enhances the sensory experience while brushing but has been known to uplift a person’s mood while going about their morning routine.

  8. Gardening Tools: Gardening can be hard work, so why not make it a little more enjoyable? Imagine grafting your favorite tunes into your garden as you plant, prune, and tend to your garden - all while being accompanied by music you love. Here is where a carefully curated playlist becomes the sound backdrop to your outdoor sanctuary.

  9. Smart Clothes: Clever weavers have devised fabrics with conductive threads woven in, allowing clothes to charge electronic devices, carry Wi-Fi signals through the garment, and play music when exposed to the right frequencies. Be it through touch-triggered speakers in your jacket or embedded songbirds included in your pajamas, smart clothing brings wearable technology to new heights.

  10. Fitness and Yoga Mats: Fitness enthusiasts may now exercise in harmony with their favorite sounds, with fitness mats designed with music-playing capabilities built right in. These mats make workouts more enjoyable and enliven your practice as you flow through yoga poses, dance routines, or a Zumba class.

  11. E-readers and Books: With e-readers and audiobooks, you now have at your fingertips the ability to purchase books that contain a soundtrack to accompany the text. While some authors have created vivid soundscapes around their books, others have translated the words into a symphony, creating an additional dimension for avid readers eager to experience the narrative through sound.

  12. Pet Products: Your pets are not left behind in this ever-evolving music revolution. With a range of pet toys and accessories that emit music, your four-legged friends can now enjoy bopping their head along to a beat. Plus, interactive feeding bowls that chime along with your child’s step-by-step feeding routine prove that music truly knows no bounds.

  13. Water Purifiers: The bottled water industry is now tapping into the trend with music-infused hydrators and water dispensers. By offering ambient sounds or melodies to accompany your water intake, hydration becomes not just a necessary evil but a calming and enjoyable experience.

  14. Smart Hair Tools: With an abundance of smart hair product systems now appearing on the market, even your hairbrush can vibrate to the beat and get you moving as you beautify. Operating as a mini-mp3 player, you can enjoy music all while you style, creating a personal harmonic soundscape.

  15. Riding Gear: Cycling, skating, or skateboarding can feel enhanced with wearable devices that feature integrated music systems. Just strap on your tunes and shred the streets or trails with a soundtrack to keep you motivated and inspired.

What’s essential amidst this ever-growing list is the innovation and creativity evident in how music enhances every walk of life. As we continue to accept music as an integral part of our experiences, it’s fascinating to observe how the music industry continues to innovate and integrate melodies into even the most offbeat surroundings.

True enough, the impact of music beyond the borders of traditional entertainment has taken the world by storm, and it’s only going to grow. How will music continue to infiltrate our daily lives? Only time (and some ingenious music-loving entrepreneurs) will tell. But one thing is for sure, the harmony that music brings into our lives can no longer be ignored – it’s truly everywhere.
