Best Hotels with Swimming Pools for Your Summer Vacation


When it comes to planning a perfect summer vacation, there’s nothing quite like having access to a beautiful swimming pool. Whether you’re looking to unwind, splash around with family, or simply enjoy the serenity of crystal-clear waters, the best hotels with swimming pools offer a perfect combination of luxury, comfort, and relaxation. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the top hotels with outstanding swimming pools, providing a range of options for different types of vacationers, from families and honeymooners to solo travelers seeking some tranquility.

1. The Four Seasons Resort, Maui

Maui is one of the most stunning tropical destinations in Hawaii, and the Four Seasons Resort stands out as a premier option for vacationers looking for a mix of luxury and natural beauty. The resort features multiple swimming pools, including an adults-only pool for those seeking peace and quiet. The main pool is set amidst lush gardens, offering spectacular ocean views. Guests can relax on comfortable poolside lounges, enjoy delicious cocktails from the pool bar, or opt for a private cabana for an exclusive experience.

Key Features:

  • Infinity Pool: Overlooks the Pacific Ocean, offering stunning sunset views.
  • Family-Friendly: A dedicated children’s pool with water slides and shallow areas.
  • Swim-up Bar: Perfect for enjoying tropical cocktails while in the pool.

Whether you’re basking under the Hawaiian sun or lounging by the pool, the Four Seasons Resort in Maui promises an unforgettable poolside experience.

2. The Ritz-Carlton, Bali

Bali is known for its rich culture, serene landscapes, and breathtaking beaches. The Ritz-Carlton offers an elevated vacation experience with its world-class amenities, including a stunning collection of swimming pools. Set on a cliffside overlooking the Indian Ocean, the resort features several infinity pools that seem to blend seamlessly with the horizon. This design creates an illusion of swimming directly into the ocean, providing an unparalleled experience for travelers.

Key Features:

  • Oceanfront Infinity Pools: Enjoy a seamless view of the ocean, ideal for both relaxation and photo opportunities.
  • Private Pool Villas: Perfect for those seeking privacy and luxury, with private plunge pools in each villa.
  • Spa Pools: The Ritz-Carlton also boasts specialized pools for wellness, offering serene spaces to unwind after a spa treatment.

For those who want a truly luxurious experience combined with gorgeous ocean views, the Ritz-Carlton in Bali is one of the best options.

3. The St. Regis, Punta Mita

Punta Mita is a renowned beach destination in Mexico, and The St. Regis offers a luxurious and tranquil escape that incorporates both beauty and relaxation. The resort is home to multiple pools, including the iconic “Infinity Pool” overlooking the Pacific Ocean. The large pool area is perfect for swimming laps, while the poolside cabanas provide shade and comfort for those wanting to relax with a good book.

Key Features:

  • Ocean View Pool: A breathtaking infinity pool that blends seamlessly with the ocean horizon.
  • Adults-Only Pool: For those seeking a quiet and intimate experience without the noise of children.
  • Poolside Service: Enjoy gourmet snacks and refreshing cocktails delivered directly to your lounger.

The St. Regis in Punta Mita provides a serene, upscale retreat where the beauty of nature and the comfort of luxury come together effortlessly.

4. Atlantis The Palm, Dubai

For those seeking an extravagant vacation filled with entertainment and luxury, Atlantis The Palm in Dubai offers one of the most iconic pool experiences. With its sprawling water park, private beaches, and numerous swimming pools, this resort provides an all-encompassing vacation experience. The resort’s main pool area is ideal for families, while the adults-only pools offer a more tranquil setting.

Key Features:

  • Aquaventure Waterpark Pools: A massive waterpark that includes adrenaline-pumping rides and lazy rivers.
  • Royal Pool: A lavishly designed pool with luxurious cabanas for ultimate relaxation.
  • Underwater Suites: The resort also offers suites where guests can enjoy views of marine life directly from their beds, an experience unlike any other.

The Atlantis The Palm in Dubai is ideal for both thrill-seekers and those looking for a more peaceful poolside retreat.

5. The Langham, Sydney

Nestled in the heart of Sydney, The Langham offers an elegant and relaxing stay in one of Australia’s most vibrant cities. With a world-class spa, impeccable service, and a luxurious pool, it is the perfect base for exploring the city’s stunning landmarks while enjoying a peaceful swim. The pool area at The Langham is spacious, with large windows offering sweeping views of the Sydney skyline.

Key Features:

  • Indoor Pool: Set in a tranquil environment, providing year-round swimming regardless of the weather.
  • Spa & Wellness Area: The pool is part of the resort’s spa facilities, allowing guests to unwind with a range of wellness treatments.
  • Private Poolside Cabanas: Available for rent, ensuring a personalized and exclusive experience.

For visitors to Sydney, The Langham provides an opulent yet peaceful environment for those who love to unwind by the water.

6. The Mulia, Nusa Dua, Bali

Another gem in Bali, The Mulia is a luxury resort that features not only incredible beachfront views but also an array of impressive swimming pools. With pools designed for both relaxation and recreation, guests can find everything they need for a memorable vacation experience. The pool areas are strategically placed throughout the resort, offering guests a variety of atmospheres, from vibrant social areas to serene quiet spaces.

Key Features:

  • Mulia’s Lagoon Pool: One of the largest in the area, ideal for swimming laps or relaxing by the water.
  • Private Villas with Pools: The resort offers private villas with their own plunge pools, perfect for those seeking ultimate privacy.
  • Poolside Dining: Enjoy delicious cuisine while lounging by the pool, with menus designed to fit any taste.

The Mulia stands as one of Bali’s finest resorts, offering a luxurious setting for a summer getaway.

7. Belmond Hotel Caruso, Ravello

Located in the stunning Amalfi Coast of Italy, Belmond Hotel Caruso offers one of the most picturesque swimming pools in the world. Set atop a cliff, this pool overlooks the sparkling Mediterranean Sea, creating a serene, postcard-like backdrop. The combination of historic charm and modern luxury makes this hotel a standout for those seeking elegance and relaxation.

Key Features:

  • Infinity Pool: Offering panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the hills of Ravello.
  • Exclusive Poolside Service: Guests can enjoy premium drinks, snacks, and fine Italian cuisine while relaxing by the pool.
  • Secluded Location: The hotel’s location offers a sense of privacy and tranquility, perfect for a romantic getaway.

For those looking to combine the beauty of the Amalfi Coast with the luxury of a five-star hotel, Belmond Hotel Caruso is a top choice.

8. The Waldorf Astoria, Beverly Hills

For a sophisticated and luxurious experience in sunny Southern California, the Waldorf Astoria in Beverly Hills is a prime option. The hotel features a rooftop pool with panoramic views of the city and the surrounding hills. Whether you’re visiting for a family vacation or a romantic getaway, this hotel’s pool offers the perfect retreat after a busy day exploring Beverly Hills.

Key Features:

  • Rooftop Pool: Offers stunning views of the Los Angeles skyline and the Hollywood Hills.
  • Exclusive Poolside Cabanas: Available for rent, providing private and plush seating with excellent service.
  • Luxury Spa Treatments: Combine poolside relaxation with soothing spa treatments for the ultimate indulgence.

With its modern amenities and luxurious atmosphere, the Waldorf Astoria in Beverly Hills is the epitome of California luxury.

9. Mandarin Oriental, Bangkok

Bangkok is known for its vibrant culture, delicious food, and lively atmosphere. The Mandarin Oriental offers a luxurious retreat from the hustle and bustle of the city with a beautiful outdoor pool that overlooks the Chao Phraya River. The resort offers an oasis of calm with impeccable service and various dining options right by the water.

Key Features:

  • Riverfront Pool: Enjoy a unique swimming experience with views of the Chao Phraya River.
  • Traditional Thai Wellness: Experience traditional Thai massages and spa services that can be combined with a relaxing swim.
  • Poolside Dining: Delight in gourmet meals from world-class chefs while lounging by the pool.

For travelers looking to experience Bangkok in style while having access to a serene and luxurious pool, the Mandarin Oriental is an ideal choice.

10. Hotel Santa Caterina, Amalfi Coast

Another stunning destination on the Amalfi Coast, Hotel Santa Caterina offers a romantic and idyllic retreat. Set on a cliff with breathtaking views of the Tyrrhenian Sea, the hotel features a variety of pools, including a saltwater infinity pool that overlooks the coast. The elegant design and top-notch service make this one of the top hotel choices for a luxurious summer vacation.

Key Features:

  • Saltwater Infinity Pool: Overlooking the crystal-clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea, ideal for swimming and relaxation.
  • Private Beach Access: Guests can enjoy a private beach area in addition to the pool.
  • Scenic Dining Options: Enjoy delicious Italian cuisine at the hotel’s restaurant while taking in the stunning views.

Hotel Santa Caterina offers a perfect combination of relaxation, natural beauty, and luxury for those visiting the Amalfi Coast.

Conclusion

Summer vacations are all about relaxation, and there’s no better way to unwind than by lounging by a beautiful swimming pool. From the beaches of Hawaii to the Mediterranean coast of Italy, the best hotels with swimming pools offer an unforgettable experience that combines luxury, comfort, and scenic beauty. Whether you’re looking for an adults-only retreat or a family-friendly destination, the hotels listed above provide everything you need for a perfect summer getaway. So pack your sunscreen, grab your favorite swimsuit, and get ready to dive into your dream vacation!
