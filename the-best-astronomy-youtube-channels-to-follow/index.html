The Best Astronomy YouTube Channels to Follow


The universe has an uncanny ability to spark curiosity in anyone who takes the time to gaze up at the stars. From ancient civilizations that charted the heavens to modern telescopes peering billions of light-years into the cosmos, astronomy taps into our innate desire to explore and understand the grand workings of existence. Fortunately, with platforms like YouTube, the wonders of astronomy are more accessible than ever.

If you’re looking to feed your cosmic curiosity, diving into astronomy-focused YouTube channels is one of the best ways to stay inspired, informed, and entertained. Whether you’re a casual stargazer, an aspiring astrophysicist, or simply someone fascinated by the universe, these creators can help you expand your knowledge and appreciation for the vastness of space.

1. Fraser Cain and Universe Today

Fraser Cain is a name recognized by anyone who’s spent time exploring astronomy content online. As the publisher of Universe Today—a website dedicated to space and astronomy—Cain’s YouTube channel is rich with engaging videos about everything from black holes to space exploration missions. He explains complex scientific concepts in a way that’s approachable, ensuring that even beginners can dive into the world of astronomy without feeling overwhelmed.

What makes his channel stand out is the “Open Space” livestreams and podcast episodes featuring guest appearances from leading scientists, astronomers, and educators. These sessions often answer audience questions, giving viewers a unique opportunity to learn directly from experts in the field.

2. PBS Space Time

If theoretical physics and the mysteries of the cosmos intrigue you, PBS Space Time is a must-follow channel. Hosted by astrophysicist Matt O’Dowd, the content ventures into topics like quantum mechanics, the fabric of spacetime, the Big Bang, and multiverse theories. The videos are well-researched and backed by scientific rigor, blending fascinating visuals and engaging narration.

It’s not just about presenting facts—the channel takes you on a deep dive into the “why” and “how” behind cosmic phenomena. It challenges your understanding of the universe and often leaves you reeling (in a good way!) with new perspectives and questions.

3. Anton Petrov

Anton Petrov’s channel shares educational and approachable explanations of groundbreaking discoveries in space science and astronomy. His frequent uploads, often titled “What the Math?,” provide consistent updates on the latest findings, whether it’s about exoplanets, supernovae, or movements within our Milky Way.

The channel has developed a reputation for presenting space news in a digestible and engaging way, making it an excellent resource for those who want to stay updated on current astronomical events without diving too deeply into scientific jargon.

4. Dr. Becky Smethurst

Dr. Becky Smethurst, an astrophysicist from the University of Oxford, brings a blend of expertise, enthusiasm, and relatability to her YouTube channel simply known as Dr. Becky. Her videos cover a range of topics including black holes, galaxy formation, and telescope observations. The “Night Sky News” series is particularly beloved by her audience, as it provides monthly overviews of celestial events to look out for.

Dr. Becky’s approachable teaching style ensures that her videos feel less like scientific lectures and more like exciting discussions with a friend passionate about astronomy. She pairs complex topics with practical how-to guides for stargazing, making it a great channel for everyone from beginners to seasoned enthusiasts.

5. SciShow Space

SciShow is already a household name when it comes to science communication, and their space-specific channel, SciShow Space, is among the best resources for astronomy enthusiasts. The channel covers a broad array of topics, from the history of space exploration to mind-bending cosmological theories.

Its weekly schedule of uploads means you’ll always have something new to learn, and the concise format of most videos ensures you can absorb fascinating facts quickly. Topics are often creative and unexpected, such as “The Strangest Stars We’ve Discovered” or “Could We Survive Proxima B?” That unique approach makes SciShow Space a standout in the astronomy community.

6. Astrum

Astrum is a visually stunning channel that focuses on astronomy and astrophysics, with an emphasis on what we’re discovering across the solar system and beyond. The high-quality animations and graphics make complex topics accessible while maintaining a sense of wonder and awe. Whether it’s an explanation of Saturn’s moon Enceladus or an overview of the different types of stars in the galaxy, Astrum’s content is ideal for anyone looking for informative and visually enriching videos.

The channel strikes a balance between technical depth and user-friendly explanations, making it perfect for curious minds eager to learn about everything from space probes to nebulae.

7. Joe Scott – Answers With Joe

Joe Scott’s channel dives into all things science, but his episodes on astronomy and the universe deserve particular recognition. With a mix of dry humor and clear explanations, Joe breaks down challenging concepts like wormholes, the Fermi Paradox, and the Kardashev Scale.

The approachable, often entertaining tone of the channel also makes it a great entry point for those who want to learn about space without feeling like they’re buried in equations or textbooks. Joe Scott’s love for the unknown and his refreshing perspective on the cosmos make his channel worth subscribing to.

8. SEA: The Science & Engineering Channel

SEA (The Science & Engineering Channel) is an underappreciated gem that focuses on the technical side of space exploration and astronomy. This channel often explores topics like the design and engineering of satellites, rockets, and observatories—all of which are crucial tools that drive our understanding of the universe.

If you’re interested in the “how” behind space exploration—the mechanics of telescopes or the challenges of deep-space travel—this channel is perfect for you. It’s especially appealing for those who enjoy knowing more about space missions like Mars rovers or the James Webb Space Telescope.

9. Cosmic Perspective

Cosmic Perspective blends artistic visuals, inspiring stories, and scientific knowledge to showcase the awe of space exploration and the human connection to the cosmos. The channel often collaborates with astronauts, engineers, and space enthusiasts, shining a spotlight on both the technical achievements and emotional journeys of those who venture into the unknown.

Unlike other channels that might focus solely on data and research, Cosmic Perspective captures the deeper spiritual and philosophical connection many people feel when contemplating the stars. It’s a uniquely uplifting corner of YouTube for anyone fascinated by the intersection of humanity and the infinite.

A Final Note on Exploring These Channels

Astronomy-focused YouTube channels have revolutionized the way we learn about and connect with the cosmos. They offer a powerful combination of education, inspiration, and entertainment, ensuring there’s something for everyone, regardless of their level of knowledge.

From understanding the mechanics of black holes to exploring the engineering behind interstellar travel or simply marveling at the wonders of the universe, the creators on these channels are experts at making the vast unknown feel a little closer to home. Whether you’re looking to deepen your understanding of astrophysics or just bask in the beauty of space imagery, these channels can deliver.

So grab your laptop, settle in, and let YouTube take you on an unforgettable journey through the universe—one video at a time.
