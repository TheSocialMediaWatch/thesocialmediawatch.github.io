The Impact of Social Media on Photography Trends


Social media platforms have revolutionized the world of photography, giving birth to innovative trends that have changed the way we interact with images. This sea change has offered photographers greater freedom to explore their creativity, while also giving ordinary users a chance to learn advanced techniques.

Once confined to galleries, museums, and the occasional coffee table book, images are now easily shared, viewed, and even manipulated. Simply put, the democratization of photography has transformed its perception and value, especially in the realm of social media.
