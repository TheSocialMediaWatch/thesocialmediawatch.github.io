The Astrological Guide to Home Decorating: Designing a Space Fit for Your Sign


When it comes to creating a home that reflects your personality and brings harmony into your life, astrology can be a powerful tool. By taking your sun sign into account, you can decorate your space in a way that complements your unique traits and brings balance to your life. Discover the best ways to design a space that’s truly fit for your astrological sign.

Aries (March 21 - April 19)

As the fire sign of the zodiac, Aries are passionate, energetic, and love to be in control. To decorate your home as an Aries, choose bold colors and dynamic patterns that exude confidence and determination. Aim for a spacious and functional layout that allows for freedom of movement. As heads of the household, Aries should place their ruler, Mars, at the heart of their living space to promote a sense of power and leadership.

Taurus (April 20 - May 20)

Steadfast, practical, and loving, Taureans appreciate comfort, luxury, and the finer things in life. Your home should be a haven of tranquility and elegance, filled with rich fabrics, warm colors, and indulgent materials such as wood, metal, and leather. Earth is the ruling element of Taurus, so be sure to incorporate plenty of greenery and natural elements to create a sense of groundedness and connection to nature.

Gemini (May 21 - June 20)

Geminis are curious, intelligent, and sociable, with a restless spirit that craves variety. Reflect your adaptability by creating thematic spaces within your home that cater to your many interests, allowing you to shift between environments as your mood dictates. Use elements of air, such as glass, mirrors, and wispy curtains, to inject a feeling of lightness and movement into your surroundings.

Cancer (June 21 - July 22)

Home is where the heart is for the nurturing, emotional, and family-focused Cancer. Your decor should be a reflection of your unyielding love and commitment to those close to you, using soft colors, soothing textures, and plenty of heartwarming memorabilia to create a cozy, safe haven. Add a water element, such as a fish tank or fountain, to your home to remind you of the ever-present flow of empathy and compassion in your life.

Leo (July 23 - August 22)

With a generous heart and a stage-ready charisma, Leo is the king or queen of the zodiac. Your home should express your confidence and passion for life with rich, golden hues, luxurious materials, and an abundance of natural light. As the ruling planet of Leo is the sun, centralize your living space around a statement piece that becomes the focal point and shines as the star of the room.

Virgo (August 23 - September 22)

Practical, analytical, and service-oriented, Virgos need a tidy, organized, and functional home. Stick to a neutral color palette, incorporating earthy tones and green accents to represent your connection to earth’s rejuvenating power. Create a space for relaxation and rejuvenation with a corner for meditation, yoga, or simply unwinding after a hectic day. Place the planet Mercury, your ruling celestial body, near your primary workspace to boost learning, communication, and mental agility.

Libra (September 23 - October 22)

As the scales of justice and the seeker of balance, Libra adores beauty, symmetry, and all things aesthetically pleasing. A Libran home should be a visual feast for the eyes, brimming with balanced design, mirrored surfaces, and an abundance of art and objets d’art. In order to find harmony within your space, place the ruling planet Venus in a prominent position to attract love, pleasure, and serenity.

Scorpio (October 23 - November 21)

Scorpios are known for their intensity, depth, and transformative nature. To create a space that mirrors these qualities, consider incorporating deep, rich colors – from emerald green to sapphire blue – and invest in pieces that have an intriguing story or history. According to occult tradition, placing a scorpion figurine or symbol will invoke both protection and the powerful energy that Scorpios are known for.

Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21)

Sagittarians are powerful dreamers, their adventurous hearts constantly yearning for the horizon. Bring this restless spirit into your home with a color palette that includes bright blues and greens, accompanied by natural textures and items that inspire and provide a sense of freedom – like globe lights or maps adorning the walls. A nod to your ruling planet, Jupiter, inspires growth and expansion.

Capricorn (December 22 - January 19)

Capricorn, ruled by the stoic and earthbound Saturn, seeks stability, pragmatism, and structure in their home life. A Capricorn-built space should be timeless, dignified, and practical. Incorporate elements like stone, slate, or dark wood furniture to embody your earthy side, and aim for orderly, organized spaces that emphasize a sense of responsibility and conscientiousness.

Aquarius (January 20 - February 18)

Aquarians possess an innovative, progressive, and humanitarian spirit. Your living space should reflect your eccentric and inventive nature with bold color combinations, eye-catching shapes, and unusual textures. The presence of the metal sculpture or other unconventional materials can bring a futuristic, avant-garde edge, while your ruling planet, Uranus, brings creativity and a rebellious energy.

Pisces (February 19 - March 20)

The gentle, empathetic, and dreamy Pisces seeks a home that brings a sense of solitude and spiritual connection to the world. Achieve this by incorporating flowing lines, tranquil colors (like iridescent blues and silvery grays), and plenty of mirrors to create a dreamlike atmosphere. A small water feature or other piece representing your ruling planet, Neptune, can work wonders for your home’s sense of serenity and imagination.

By evaluating your astrological properties and customizing your design elements accordingly, you can create a truly unique and personalized home that resonates with your spirit and enhances your inherent strengths.
