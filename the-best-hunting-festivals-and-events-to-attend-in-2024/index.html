The Best Hunting Festivals and Events to Attend in 2024


Hunting is not just a sport; it’s a culture, a tradition, and a lifestyle for many. As the seasons change and the leaves begin to fall, hunters across the globe gear up for their favorite pastime. In 2024, several exciting hunting festivals and events are set to take place, offering enthusiasts a chance to celebrate their passion, learn new skills, and connect with fellow hunters. Here’s a comprehensive guide to the best hunting festivals and events you won’t want to miss this year.

1. The Great American Outdoor Show

Location: Harrisburg, Pennsylvania
Dates: February 3-11, 2024

The Great American Outdoor Show is one of the largest outdoor expos in the country, attracting hunters, anglers, and outdoor enthusiasts from all walks of life. Spanning over nine days, the event features hundreds of exhibitors showcasing the latest in hunting gear, firearms, and outdoor equipment. Attendees can participate in seminars led by industry experts, learn about conservation efforts, and even try their hand at archery in designated areas. With a variety of activities for the whole family, the Great American Outdoor Show is a must-attend event for anyone passionate about the outdoors.

2. National Wild Turkey Federation Convention

Location: Nashville, Tennessee
Dates: February 14-17, 2024

The National Wild Turkey Federation (NWTF) Convention is an annual gathering that celebrates the sport of turkey hunting and the conservation efforts that protect turkey habitats. Attendees can expect a packed schedule of informative seminars, workshops, and demonstrations focused on turkey hunting techniques, conservation policies, and wildlife management. With guest speakers from the hunting community and opportunities to meet fellow enthusiasts, this convention is perfect for those looking to enhance their knowledge and skills in turkey hunting.

3. Wisconsin Deer & Turkey Expo

Location: Wisconsin Dells, Wisconsin
Dates: April 12-14, 2024

The Wisconsin Deer & Turkey Expo is a premier event that draws thousands of attendees each year. This three-day festival celebrates the state’s rich hunting heritage and offers a wide range of activities, including seminars from expert hunters, a showcase of the latest hunting gear, and opportunities to meet manufacturers directly. A highlight of the expo is the Big Buck Contest, where hunters can showcase their trophies and compete for prizes. Families will enjoy the kid-friendly activities, making this an event that caters to all ages.

4. Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation Elk Camp

Location: Denver, Colorado
Dates: April 25-27, 2024

Elk Camp is an annual event hosted by the Rocky Mountain Elk Foundation, focusing on elk hunting and conservation efforts. This gathering features a variety of educational workshops, a trade show with top hunting gear, and informative discussions on wildlife management. Attendees can enjoy live auctions and raffles, with proceeds supporting elk habitat conservation projects across the country. Elk Camp is an excellent opportunity for hunters to learn more about sustainable practices while connecting with like-minded individuals who share a passion for elk hunting.

5. Hunting & Fishing Day

Location: Various Locations Nationwide
Dates: September 28, 2024

National Hunting and Fishing Day is celebrated across the United States, with various events organized at state parks, wildlife refuges, and community centers. These events aim to promote outdoor activities and educate the public about the importance of wildlife conservation. Local hunting organizations often host free workshops, demonstrations, and hands-on activities for both newcomers and experienced hunters. It’s a fantastic opportunity to introduce friends and family to the world of hunting and fishing while enjoying a day outdoors.

6. The World Predator and Hunting Expo

Location: Columbus, Ohio
Dates: October 4-6, 2024

The World Predator and Hunting Expo is a premier event for hunters specializing in predator hunting. This expo features an extensive range of exhibitors focused on predator hunting gear and techniques. Attendees can participate in seminars led by hunting professionals who share their insights on effective hunting strategies. The expo also includes a variety of contests, including calling contests and shooting competitions, allowing hunters to showcase their skills and learn from one another. Whether you’re a seasoned predator hunter or just getting started, this event is sure to inspire and educate.

7. Texas Trophy Hunters Extravaganza

Location: Various Locations in Texas
Dates: Multiple dates throughout August 2024

The Texas Trophy Hunters Extravaganza is an iconic event celebrating the state’s rich hunting culture. With multiple locations across Texas, the event features a massive trade show, educational seminars, and opportunities for hunters to showcase their recent trophies. Visitors can explore the latest in hunting gear and equipment while enjoying delicious food and live entertainment. The extravaganza is a fantastic way to meet fellow hunters, learn new skills, and experience the vibrant hunting community in Texas.

8. Montana’s Hunting & Fishing Day

Location: Helena, Montana
Dates: September 28, 2024

Celebrated in various locations across Montana, this event highlights the state’s rich hunting and fishing heritage. Activities include hands-on workshops, demonstrations, and opportunities to learn about wildlife conservation. Participants can engage in fishing, shooting, and archery activities, making it a family-friendly event that encourages outdoor exploration. Montana’s Hunting & Fishing Day is an excellent way to connect with nature while celebrating the importance of conservation.

9. The Pheasant Fest & Quail Classic

Location: Sioux Falls, South Dakota
Dates: February 16-18, 2024

Pheasant Fest & Quail Classic is a celebration of upland hunting, focusing on pheasants and quail. This event features a large trade show, educational seminars, and interactive workshops that cover everything from hunting techniques to dog training. The festival also includes a variety of family-friendly activities, making it a great option for hunters looking to introduce their families to the sport. With countless exhibitors showcasing the latest gear and equipment, attendees can find everything they need for their next hunting adventure.

10. Deerassic Park’s Outdoor Festival

Location: Cambridge, Ohio
Dates: June 7-9, 2024

The Deerassic Park’s Outdoor Festival is a unique event that combines outdoor sports with family-friendly activities. With a focus on deer hunting, this festival includes seminars, demonstrations, and a variety of competitions for all ages. Guests can enjoy food vendors, live music, and a chance to explore the beautiful surroundings. This event not only promotes hunting but also emphasizes the importance of conservation and wildlife management.

Conclusion

As the hunting season approaches, these festivals and events provide a perfect opportunity to immerse yourself in the culture of hunting. From educational workshops and trade shows to family-friendly activities, there’s something for everyone. Attending these events not only enhances your skills and knowledge but also allows you to connect with a community of like-minded individuals who share your passion for the great outdoors.

Mark your calendars and get ready to join fellow hunters in celebrating the thrill of the hunt in 2024! Whether you’re a seasoned veteran or a newcomer to the sport, these events promise unforgettable experiences and valuable insights into the world of hunting.
