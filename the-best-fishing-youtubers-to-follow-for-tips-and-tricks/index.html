The Best Fishing YouTubers to Follow for Tips and Tricks


Fishing is a relaxing and rewarding hobby that allows you to get in touch with nature, unwind, and enjoy some tranquility. For many, it’s not just about catching fish; it’s about the overall experience. Watching your line dip into the water, feeling the tug, and ultimately reeling in your prize is an exhilarating experience. While it’s a beloved pastime, fishing can also be a challenge, especially for beginners. This is where fishing YouTubers come into play. They share their expertise and experiences, offering insights on where to fish, which bait to use, and how to land that elusive catch.

In no particular order, here are some top fishing YouTubers to follow for tips and tricks:

  1. Capt. James “Jim” Bleakley Jim Bleakley is a third-generation fisherman from Texas, sharing his wealth of experience in the Galveston bay area. From his YouTube channel “Fishmanship,” you’ll learn the ins and outs of fishing in the Gulf, including the ever-popular redfish, speckled trout, and flounder. His laid-back, humoristic approach will keep you entertained as you grow your fishing knowledge.

  2. The Bitter(stream) Featuring the larger-than-life characters, Bruce “The Bitter” Smith and Luke, “Bear” Clendening, their channel offers no-holds-barred humor and fishing wisdom. Their content covers everything from outings in rural, picturesque ponds to the intensity of saltwater fishing. Their banter will have you giggling while you hone your fishing skills.

  3. Chad “coast Tackle” Belding Chad Belding’s channel is the perfect place for learning about tackle, techniques, and secrets of the fishing world. With a vast array of videos on various species, locations, and strategies, Chad shares his knowledge to help you increase your success rate on the water.

  4. Fishing Coach Andy Cates Outdoor adventure and fishing enthusiast Andy Cates teaches you how to fish with an emphasis on lure selection and presentation. His slow-motion tutorials break down techniques, making it easier for novice anglers to visualize and master new skills. From bass fishing in Florida to blackfish in New Jersey, Andy’s wealth of knowledge is available for everyone.

  5. Emson Outdoors Ever wanted to catch massive fish like the professionals? Follow Emson Outdoors, the brainchild of Vietnam veteran and entrepreneur Kevin VanDam. Kevin shares tips and tutorials on techniques, gear, and strategies to help you reel in bigger catches. His content covers a broad range of species from walleye to bass to catfish.

  6. Bass Assassins Fishing For all bass fishing enthusiasts, the Bass Assassins fishing channel is a treasure trove of knowledge. Hosts Jake Runnels and Blaine Christensen give you insights into the mindset and tactics needed for successful bass fishing. With videos on everything from different tackle and techniques to secret fishing spots, they’ll help you up your bass-catching game.

Following these talented and diverse fishing YouTubers will enhance your understanding of the sport, broaden your fishing techniques, and, most importantly, get you catching more fish. So, grab a fishing rod, and let the journey begin!
