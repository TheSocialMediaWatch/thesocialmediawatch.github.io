The Best Fitness Apps for Outdoor Workouts


In today’s fast-paced world, staying fit and healthy has become more important than ever. With the rise of technology, fitness apps have emerged as valuable tools that can help you stay motivated and track your progress. Outdoor workouts, in particular, offer a refreshing break from the confines of a gym, allowing you to connect with nature while breaking a sweat. If you’re looking to enhance your outdoor fitness routine, here’s a comprehensive guide to the best fitness apps for outdoor workouts.

Why Choose Outdoor Workouts?

Outdoor workouts come with a myriad of benefits. They allow you to enjoy fresh air, sunshine, and the ever-changing scenery of nature. Research suggests that exercising outdoors can boost your mood, increase energy levels, and even improve mental health. Here are a few reasons why outdoor workouts are a great choice:

  • Connection with Nature: Being outside can enhance your workout experience, making it more enjoyable and less monotonous.
  • Variety of Environments: Parks, beaches, or hiking trails offer diverse terrains that can challenge your body in different ways.
  • Social Interaction: Outdoor workouts can easily be turned into group activities, promoting social interaction and camaraderie.
  • Vitamin D Exposure: Sunshine is a natural source of Vitamin D, which is essential for overall health.

Given these benefits, let’s explore some of the best fitness apps that can help you maximize your outdoor workout experience.

1. Strava

Strava is a powerhouse among fitness apps, especially for outdoor enthusiasts. Originally designed for cyclists, Strava has expanded to include a wide range of outdoor activities, including running, hiking, and swimming.

Features:

  • Activity Tracking: Record your runs, rides, or hikes using GPS, allowing you to see distance, pace, and elevation.
  • Segments: Compete against yourself and others on specific routes or segments, adding a competitive edge to your workouts.
  • Social Features: Connect with friends and join clubs to share your achievements and motivate each other.
  • Challenges: Participate in monthly challenges to keep your workouts fresh and exciting.

Strava’s user-friendly interface and vibrant community make it an excellent choice for anyone looking to enhance their outdoor fitness routine.

2. MapMyRun

MapMyRun, developed by Under Armour, is a versatile app that caters to runners and outdoor fitness enthusiasts. With its robust tracking capabilities, it’s perfect for those who want to monitor their progress while enjoying the great outdoors.

Features:

  • Route Mapping: Create and save custom routes, allowing you to explore new areas without getting lost.
  • Audio Feedback: Receive real-time updates on your pace, distance, and time during your workout.
  • Nutrition Tracking: Log your meals and hydration to complement your fitness goals.
  • Community Support: Join challenges and connect with other users for motivation and inspiration.

The combination of tracking, community features, and nutrition logging makes MapMyRun a well-rounded app for outdoor fitness.

3. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a comprehensive fitness app that offers a variety of workouts, including many that can be done outdoors. While it provides gym-based routines, it also has a robust selection of bodyweight exercises ideal for parks or open spaces.

Features:

  • Workout Library: Access over 185 free workouts designed by professional trainers, focusing on strength, endurance, yoga, and mobility.
  • Customizable Plans: Tailor your fitness program to meet your goals, whether that’s building strength or improving flexibility.
  • Video Demonstrations: Follow along with video instructions to ensure proper form and technique.
  • Social Sharing: Share your achievements and workouts with friends to stay accountable.

Nike Training Club’s extensive workout library and flexibility make it an excellent companion for outdoor training.

4. Komoot

For those who love hiking and biking, Komoot is a must-have app. It specializes in outdoor navigation and route planning, making it easier to explore new trails and paths.

Features:

  • Detailed Maps: Access offline maps that include terrain details and points of interest.
  • Route Planning: Plan your route based on your preferred activity, whether it’s hiking, biking, or mountain biking.
  • Voice Navigation: Enjoy hands-free navigation with audio cues, allowing you to focus on the trail ahead.
  • Community Recommendations: Discover popular routes shared by other users to find the best outdoor experiences.

Komoot’s focus on navigation and community insights makes it the perfect app for adventurers looking to explore the great outdoors.

5. AllTrails

AllTrails is another fantastic app for hiking enthusiasts. With a vast database of trails, it’s great for finding the best outdoor locations for your workout.

Features:

  • Trail Database: Access thousands of user-generated trails, complete with reviews, photos, and difficulty ratings.
  • GPS Tracking: Track your hikes in real-time and access offline maps for remote areas.
  • Create Custom Trails: Plan your own routes and share them with the community.
  • Safety Features: Download maps for offline use and share your location with friends for safety during remote hikes.

With its extensive collection of trails and user-generated content, AllTrails is a go-to app for outdoor hiking.

6. Fitbit App

If you own a Fitbit device, the Fitbit app is a valuable tool for tracking your outdoor workouts and overall health. It seamlessly integrates with your wearable device to provide comprehensive fitness insights.

Features:

  • Activity Tracking: Monitor your outdoor activities, including steps, distance, and calories burned.
  • Heart Rate Monitoring: Keep track of your heart rate during workouts to optimize your training intensity.
  • Health Metrics: Access insights into your sleep, nutrition, and overall wellness.
  • Challenges and Community: Participate in challenges with friends and family to stay motivated.

The Fitbit app’s integration with its devices and its focus on overall health make it a fantastic companion for outdoor fitness.

7. Runkeeper

Runkeeper is a popular app for runners that offers a user-friendly interface and a plethora of features designed to enhance your outdoor running experience.

Features:

  • GPS Tracking: Track your runs in real-time and receive detailed stats on distance, pace, and time.
  • Goal Setting: Set personalized goals and receive training plans to help you achieve them.
  • Audio Cues: Get real-time audio updates on your progress during your run.
  • Community Engagement: Join challenges and connect with other runners for inspiration and motivation.

Runkeeper’s simplicity and effective tracking capabilities make it a favorite among outdoor runners.

8. MyFitnessPal

While primarily a nutrition tracking app, MyFitnessPal can be a valuable tool for outdoor workouts, especially if you’re looking to complement your fitness routine with proper nutrition.

Features:

  • Calorie Tracking: Log your meals and track your caloric intake to maintain a balanced diet.
  • Exercise Log: Record your outdoor workouts to keep track of calories burned and exercise routines.
  • Recipe Importer: Easily add recipes and meals to your log for more accurate tracking.
  • Community Support: Engage with other users for support and motivation.

MyFitnessPal’s focus on nutrition and exercise tracking makes it an excellent app for holistic fitness management.

9. Sworkit

Sworkit is a versatile fitness app that allows you to create custom workouts that can be done outdoors. It’s perfect for those who enjoy flexibility in their routines.

Features:

  • Custom Workouts: Choose from a variety of workout types, including strength, cardio, yoga, and stretching.
  • Time Flexibility: Select workout durations that fit your schedule, from quick 5-minute sessions to longer workouts.
  • Video Instructions: Follow along with video demonstrations to ensure proper technique.
  • Goal-Oriented Plans: Set personal fitness goals and receive tailored workouts to help you achieve them.

Sworkit’s adaptability makes it a great choice for outdoor fitness enthusiasts who want to vary their routine.

10. Outdooractive

Outdooractive is an app designed specifically for outdoor enthusiasts, focusing on hiking, biking, and other adventure sports. It’s perfect for those who want to explore new outdoor activities.

Features:

  • Comprehensive Route Planning: Access detailed maps and tailor your routes based on your preferred activity.
  • Offline Access: Download maps for offline use, ensuring you can navigate even in remote areas.
  • Weather Information: Stay informed about weather conditions for better planning of your outdoor activities.
  • Community Insights: Discover popular routes and trails recommended by other users.

Outdooractive’s specialized features make it an excellent app for outdoor adventures.

Conclusion

With the right fitness app, outdoor workouts can become more enjoyable, structured, and effective. Whether you’re tracking your runs, planning a hike, or creating custom workouts, the apps mentioned above can help you stay motivated and engaged in your fitness journey. Embrace the great outdoors and let these apps guide you to a healthier, happier lifestyle. As you explore nature and push your limits, remember to enjoy the journey and celebrate your progress along the way. Happy exercising!
