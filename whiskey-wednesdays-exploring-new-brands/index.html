Whiskey Wednesdays: Exploring New Brands


Discovering new whiskeys is an adventure, much like a Wednesday evening-turned-exploration. Gone are the days when we would limit ourselves to just a handful of familiar brands; it’s time to venture into uncharted territories and broaden our horizons with new flavors and surprises. Let’s dive right into some extraordinary whiskeys that have caught our attention lately.

Alt FieldCraft Distillery – Quarto

Alt FieldCraft Distillery, nestled in the heart of California, has been tantalizing the tastes of those in the know with their Quarto expression. Quarto is their flagship whiskey, crafted through a marriage of four grains - rye, barley, oat, and wheat. The combination of the grains offers a unique dance on our palate, each note layered with hints of caramel, oak, and subtle fruitiness. This distinct whiskey is perfect for those seeking to escape the monotony of ordinary options.

Into The Jungle – Ubiquitous Jungle Gin

When we think about gin, we often zero in on juniper-infused spirits, silent or not. However, there’s an emerging player that’s making waves - Ubiquitous Jungle Gin. Produced in the heart of the Scottish Highlands, this unexpected concoction incorporates botanicals found deep within the wild and dense Scottish landscape. Legend has it that the creator, Michael Crummack, walked for miles armed with a pocketknife and bag, collecting these mysterious and indigenous ingredients. Drinking Into The Jungle feels like a journey into uncharted taste territory – an extraordinary whiskey to experience.

Balcones Distilling – The Texas Single Malt

Though Texas is synonymous with oil, cowboys, and hats, the Lone Star State has earned a spot on the world map for its distillery, Balcones Distilling’s Texas Single Malt. The whiskey, made with 100% Texas blue corn, offers a robust and dark character. Its smoky and peaty notes pay homage to the Islay malts while also showcasing the unique flavor profile of Texas terroir - an intriguing blend of familiar and novel.

Hoagland & Whole Heartedly - Redemption Bourbon

Hoagland & Co., a distillery based in Los Angeles, offers a unique taste through their Redemption Bourbon. Their mission is to treat every bottle with the utmost attention, refusing to rush or compromise. The end product: a bourbon full of heart and soul. Their Bourbon is aged meticulously, with a focus on the slower, more contemplative process resulting in a richer, powerful taste. It’s a perfectly balanced ritual for any Wednesday whiskey aficionado.

As these Whiskey Wednesday explorations reveal, the world of whiskey is vast, dynamic, and ever-evolving. The captivating allure of discovering new brands offers a continuous reminder of the world’s vast array of flavors, proving once again that the beauty lies in the adventure. So this Wednesday, perhaps consider branching out and smudging the lines of familiarity. Dare to embark on a taste journey that lies beyond the ordinary, and who knows where the adventure might take you.

The always evolving and diverse world of spirits continues to surprise us, leaving us eager for the next Whiskey Wednesday. Keep your senses open, and always remember to savor each new taste with curiosity and a sense of wonder.
