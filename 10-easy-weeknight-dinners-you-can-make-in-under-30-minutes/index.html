10 Easy Weeknight Dinners You Can Make in Under 30 Minutes


After a busy day at work or school, the last thing anyone wants to do is spend hours in the kitchen. However, quick and delicious meals can still be satisfying and nutritious without compromising on flavor. Whether you’re cooking for yourself or your family, these 10 easy weeknight dinners can be whipped up in under 30 minutes. Perfect for those hectic evenings when time is of the essence but you still want a homemade meal.

1. Garlic Butter Shrimp Pasta

If you’re a fan of seafood and pasta, this garlic butter shrimp pasta is a simple and flavorful dish that can be made in just 20 minutes. The combination of tender shrimp, rich garlic butter, and perfectly cooked pasta is comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz spaghetti or your favorite pasta
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While pasta cooks, heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp to the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink and cooked through.
  4. Toss in the cooked pasta, season with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with chopped parsley before serving.

This dish is light yet full of flavor, making it a perfect option for a quick weeknight meal.

2. Chicken Stir-Fry with Vegetables

Chicken stir-fry is a classic and versatile dish that you can easily customize with your favorite vegetables. It’s also a great way to use up leftover vegetables from your fridge. With a few key ingredients, you can have a tasty, balanced meal ready in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, sliced into strips
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken and cook for 5-6 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add sesame oil, garlic, and ginger. Stir-fry for about 1 minute.
  4. Add the bell pepper, onion, and broccoli, and cook for another 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and add soy sauce. Toss everything together and cook for 1-2 minutes to combine the flavors.

Serve with rice or noodles for a complete meal.

3. Beef Tacos

Tacos are a crowd-pleaser and can be made in a flash. You can load them up with your favorite toppings or keep them simple with seasoned ground beef. This dish is a great way to satisfy your cravings without spending much time in the kitchen.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 small soft tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Salsa and sour cream for topping

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks, for about 5-7 minutes.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and a little water (as directed on the seasoning packet), and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in the microwave or on a dry skillet.
  4. Assemble tacos by adding the seasoned beef to each tortilla and topping with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, salsa, and sour cream.

Tacos are a fantastic option for a quick and customizable meal that everyone will love.

4. One-Pan Baked Lemon Herb Chicken

When you need a meal that requires minimal effort and cleanup, one-pan baked chicken is the answer. The lemon and herb seasoning make the chicken flavorful, while the veggies cook alongside the chicken, absorbing all those delicious flavors.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups baby potatoes, halved
  • 1 cup baby carrots

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place chicken breasts, potatoes, and carrots on a baking sheet.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over everything and season with thyme, rosemary, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Arrange lemon slices on top of the chicken.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.

This meal is not only quick but also requires minimal prep, making it perfect for a busy weeknight.

5. Caprese Salad with Grilled Chicken

A fresh Caprese salad paired with grilled chicken makes for a light yet satisfying dinner. The combination of juicy tomatoes, fresh mozzarella, and basil makes this dish incredibly refreshing, and the addition of chicken gives it that extra protein boost.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic glaze
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook for 5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil.
  3. Slice the chicken and add it to the salad. Drizzle with balsamic glaze and serve immediately.

This meal is light, healthy, and full of vibrant flavors.

6. Vegetable Fried Rice

Vegetable fried rice is a fantastic option for a quick and easy dinner. Packed with colorful veggies and flavored with soy sauce, this dish is a great way to use leftover rice and clean out your fridge.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2 eggs, scrambled
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook for 2 minutes until softened.
  2. Add the frozen peas and carrots and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Push the vegetables to one side of the pan and scramble the eggs on the other side.
  4. Once the eggs are cooked, add the rice to the pan. Stir in soy sauce and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve.

This dish is both filling and customizable – you can add any vegetables or protein you have on hand.

7. Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Spaghetti Aglio e Olio is an Italian classic that requires only a few ingredients but delivers incredible flavor. Perfect for those nights when you need a quick meal with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for 2-3 minutes until golden brown.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cook for an additional 30 seconds.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some of the pasta water. Add the pasta to the pan with garlic and toss to coat, adding a little pasta water if necessary to help coat the pasta.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, then sprinkle with fresh parsley and grated Parmesan if desired.

This simple pasta dish is quick, flavorful, and perfect for a weeknight dinner.

8. Chicken Caesar Wraps

If you’re craving a Caesar salad but want something a bit heartier, try chicken Caesar wraps. They’re easy to make and can be packed with all your favorite salad ingredients, but wrapped up in a soft tortilla for a convenient and portable meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup Caesar dressing
  • 1 cup romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 large flour tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and cook in a skillet over medium heat for 6-7 minutes per side until fully cooked. Slice the chicken thinly.
  2. Lay out the tortillas and add a generous amount of chopped romaine lettuce in the center of each.
  3. Top with sliced chicken, Caesar dressing, and grated Parmesan.
  4. Roll up the tortillas tightly and slice them into wraps.

These wraps are quick, satisfying, and perfect for those busy evenings.

9. Eggplant Parmesan

Eggplant Parmesan is a vegetarian favorite that’s easy to make in under 30 minutes. With crispy breaded eggplant slices, marinara sauce, and melty cheese, it’s a comforting meal that’s both hearty and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium eggplants, sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Dip the eggplant slices into the beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
  3. Place the coated eggplant slices on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes until crispy.
  4. Top the baked eggplant with marinara sauce and mozzarella, then bake for another 5 minutes until the cheese is melted.

This dish is easy to prepare, packed with flavor, and a great vegetarian option for dinner.

10. Turkey and Avocado Lettuce Wraps

If you’re looking for a low-carb option that’s quick and easy, turkey and avocado lettuce wraps are perfect. Packed with protein and healthy fats, these wraps are light yet filling.

Ingredients:

  • 8 large lettuce leaves (such as Romaine or Butter lettuce)
  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salsa or Greek yogurt for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ground turkey in olive oil over medium heat, breaking it up as it cooks. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Once the turkey is cooked through, remove from heat and let it cool slightly.
  3. To assemble, place a scoop of turkey onto each lettuce leaf, then top with avocado slices.
  4. Add salsa or Greek yogurt for extra flavor, then wrap the lettuce around the filling.

These wraps are quick, healthy, and an excellent choice for a low-carb dinner.

These 10 easy weeknight dinners are sure to save you time in the kitchen without compromising on flavor or nutrition. Whether you’re in the mood for pasta, a fresh salad, or a warm comfort meal, these dishes offer a variety of options to suit all tastes. The best part? You can have them on the table in 30 minutes or less, making your busy evenings a little bit easier.
