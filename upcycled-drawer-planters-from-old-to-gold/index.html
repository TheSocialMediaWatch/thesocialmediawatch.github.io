Upcycled Drawer Planters: From Old to Gold


In today’s world, where sustainability is becoming increasingly important, upcycling has gained popularity as a creative and eco-friendly way to repurpose old items. One such upcycling project that has caught the attention of many is the transformation of old drawers into beautiful planters. In this blog post, we will explore the art of upcycled drawer planters and how you can turn your old, forgotten drawers into stunning additions to your garden or indoor space.

Why Upcycled Drawer Planters?

Upcycled drawer planters offer a unique and stylish way to showcase your plants. Instead of throwing away old drawers, you can give them a new lease on life by repurposing them as planters. Not only does this help reduce waste, but it also adds a touch of creativity and personality to your living spaces.

Choosing the Right Drawer

When selecting a drawer for your upcycling project, consider the size and condition of the drawer. Look for drawers that are structurally sound and free from any major damage. Additionally, think about the style and aesthetic you want to achieve. Vintage or rustic drawers can add a charming touch, while modern or colorful drawers can create a more contemporary look.

Preparing the Drawer

Before you start planting, you’ll need to prepare the drawer. Begin by cleaning it thoroughly, removing any dust, dirt, or debris. If the drawer has a glossy finish, you may need to sand it lightly to create a better surface for paint or sealant.

Adding Drainage Holes

To ensure proper drainage for your plants, it’s essential to add drainage holes to the bottom of the drawer. Using a drill or a hammer and a nail, create several small holes evenly spaced along the bottom. This will prevent water from pooling and causing root rot.

Painting and Sealing

Now comes the fun part! You can unleash your creativity by painting the drawer in your desired color or design. Consider using eco-friendly paint or repurposing leftover paint from previous projects. Once the paint has dried, apply a sealant to protect the drawer from moisture and weathering.

Planting Your Upcycled Drawer Planter

With the preparation complete, it’s time to bring your upcycled drawer planter to life! Start by adding a layer of gravel or pebbles to the bottom of the drawer. This will further aid in drainage. Next, fill the drawer with potting soil, leaving enough space for your plants to grow.

Choose a variety of plants that suit your gardening style and the amount of sunlight your chosen location receives. Consider mixing different heights, colors, and textures to create an eye-catching display.

Caring for Your Upcycled Drawer Planter

To ensure the longevity of your upcycled drawer planter, it’s important to provide proper care. Regularly water your plants, taking care not to overwater or let the soil dry out completely. Keep an eye out for any pests or diseases and take appropriate measures to control them.

Conclusion

Upcycled drawer planters offer a creative and sustainable way to repurpose old drawers. By transforming them into unique planters, you not only reduce waste but also add a personal touch to your garden or indoor space. So, the next time you come across an old drawer, don’t discard it. Instead, let your creativity flourish and turn it from old to gold with an upcycled drawer planter!
