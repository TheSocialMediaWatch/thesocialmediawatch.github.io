The Best Hunting YouTube Channels to Follow in 2024


As the hunting community grows and evolves, so does the wealth of information available at our fingertips. YouTube has become a treasure trove for hunters, offering a plethora of channels dedicated to everything from gear reviews to hunting tips, techniques, and thrilling hunts. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or a novice looking to learn the ropes, here’s a rundown of the best hunting YouTube channels to follow in 2024.

1. The Hunting Public

The Hunting Public has quickly become a favorite among hunting enthusiasts. This channel stands out for its relatable content and focus on public land hunting. The team behind The Hunting Public shares their experiences, techniques, and strategies, making it accessible for hunters of all skill levels. Their approach to hunting is refreshingly honest, as they often showcase both successes and failures. With a strong emphasis on scouting and understanding animal behavior, this channel is a must-follow for anyone looking to improve their hunting game.

Notable Content:

  • Public Land Strategies: Insights on hunting in areas that are accessible to everyone.
  • Scouting Tips: Techniques for finding deer and other game before the season starts.
  • Engaging Vlogs: Personal stories that make hunting relatable and enjoyable.

2. MeatEater

Hosted by Steven Rinella, MeatEater is more than just a hunting channel; it’s a lifestyle. The content is rich with information on hunting, cooking, and conservation. Rinella’s passion for the outdoors is palpable, and he often dives into the ethical aspects of hunting and wildlife management. The production quality is top-notch, and the storytelling is engaging, making it easy to binge-watch episodes.

Notable Content:

  • Cooking Segments: Learn how to prepare wild game with delicious recipes.
  • Conservation Focus: Discussions on the importance of sustainable hunting practices.
  • Adventure Series: Join Rinella on epic adventures across various landscapes.

3. Hushin

Hushin is a channel that thrives on adventure and camaraderie. The creators, a group of friends, share their hunting experiences across various states, focusing primarily on big game hunting. Their enthusiasm for the outdoors is infectious, and they provide valuable insights into the challenges and rewards of hunting. The camaraderie among the group makes for entertaining content as they celebrate successes and learn from failures together.

Notable Content:

  • Big Game Hunts: Experience the thrill of chasing elk, deer, and more.
  • Gear Reviews: Honest assessments of the latest hunting gear and equipment.
  • Vlogs and Journeys: Follow the ups and downs of their hunting adventures.

4. The Bowhunter’s Life

For those who prefer archery, The Bowhunter’s Life is a fantastic channel to explore. This channel caters specifically to bowhunting enthusiasts, offering tips, techniques, and insights that are invaluable for both beginners and experienced hunters. The hosts share their personal stories, making the content personal and relatable.

Notable Content:

  • Archery Techniques: Tips for improving accuracy and consistency.
  • Field Reports: Real-time updates during hunting seasons.
  • Product Reviews: In-depth looks at bows, arrows, and accessories.

5. The Outdoor Channel

The Outdoor Channel is a staple in the hunting community, and their YouTube presence is equally impressive. They feature a variety of hunting shows, offering something for everyone. From whitetail deer hunting to duck hunting, the Outdoor Channel showcases a broad range of activities that cater to different interests.

Notable Content:

  • Diverse Hunting Shows: A variety of programs covering multiple hunting styles.
  • Expert Interviews: Insights from seasoned hunters and conservationists.
  • Event Coverage: Highlights from hunting expos and competitions.

6. The Hunting Beast

The Hunting Beast is perfect for those who are serious about improving their hunting skills. The channel is run by Dan Infalt, a seasoned hunter known for his deep understanding of whitetail deer behavior. He shares advanced techniques that can help hunters take their skills to the next level. The content is educational and often dives deep into the strategies that can make a significant difference during hunting season.

Notable Content:

  • Advanced Strategies: Techniques for hunting mature bucks.
  • Q&A Sessions: Dan answers viewer questions, providing personalized insights.
  • Scouting Tips: Effective methods for scouting and tracking deer.

7. The Primals

The Primals is a channel that takes a holistic approach to hunting. They focus on the connection between nature, hunting, and sustainability. This channel is not only about the hunt; it’s about understanding the ecosystem and the role hunters play within it. The content is often philosophical, encouraging viewers to reflect on their relationship with nature.

Notable Content:

  • Nature Connection: Discussions on the spiritual and mental aspects of hunting.
  • Sustainable Practices: Emphasis on ethical hunting and wildlife conservation.
  • Seasonal Hunting: Tips tailored to specific times of the year and animal behavior.

8. Whitetail Adrenaline

Whitetail Adrenaline focuses on the excitement of hunting whitetail deer. The channel is known for its high-energy content and thrilling hunts. The hosts are passionate about their craft and share their adrenaline-filled experiences with viewers, making it an entertaining watch for any hunting fanatic.

Notable Content:

  • Action-Packed Hunts: Fast-paced videos that capture the thrill of the chase.
  • Hunting Techniques: Insights into stalking and ambush strategies.
  • Field Judging: Tips on how to assess deer before taking a shot.

9. The Wild Outdoors

The Wild Outdoors is a family-friendly channel that showcases hunting adventures with a focus on education. The hosts provide insights into various hunting methods and the importance of respecting wildlife. This channel is great for families looking to introduce their children to the world of hunting in a safe and responsible manner.

Notable Content:

  • Family Hunting Trips: Encouraging family involvement in outdoor activities.
  • Educational Segments: Teaching kids about wildlife and conservation.
  • Respecting Nature: Promoting ethical hunting practices.

10. The Truth About Hunting

As the name suggests, The Truth About Hunting aims to provide honest and straightforward content in the hunting world. The channel often addresses common misconceptions about hunting while providing practical tips and techniques. This no-nonsense approach resonates with viewers who appreciate transparency in the hunting community.

Notable Content:

  • Myth Busting: Addressing common myths and misconceptions about hunting.
  • Practical Tips: Real-world advice for improving hunting skills.
  • Community Engagement: Actively involving viewers in discussions around hunting.

Conclusion

YouTube is an incredible resource for hunters of all levels, offering a variety of channels that cater to different interests and preferences. Whether you’re looking to improve your skills, learn about new gear, or simply enjoy the thrill of the hunt, the channels listed above are some of the best to follow in 2024. Each channel brings its unique flavor to the hunting community, ensuring that there’s something for everyone. So grab your gear, hit subscribe, and get ready for an exciting hunting season ahead!
