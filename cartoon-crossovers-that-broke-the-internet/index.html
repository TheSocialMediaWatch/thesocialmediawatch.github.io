Cartoon Crossovers That Broke the Internet


The idea of combining beloved characters from different animated universes has always intrigued fans and creators alike. Over the years, some groundbreaking cartoon crossovers have managed to create a sensation on the internet, breaking new grounds in the realms of fan fiction, memes and even commissioned animations. In this article, we will be exploring five of these sensational crossover events and how they managed to captivate audiences worldwide.

1. Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles x Batman: The Brave and the Bold

In a crossover event that could only be dreamt up by a die-hard fan, the crews behind Nickelodeon’s Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Batman: The Brave and the Bold crossed paths in a never-before-seen collaboration. The animated shorts featured the TMNT joining forces with the Dark Knight himself, Batman, in their fight against the forces of evil. Shining a light on the shared themes of camaraderie and crime-fighting, this animated collaboration received critical acclaim and left fans eagerly awaiting more.

2. My Little Pony x Undertale

When two seemingly disparate franchises come together, the results can be nothing short of magical. This was certainly the case when the colorful world of My Little Pony met the touching narrative of Undertale. The fusion of Hasbro’s fan-favorite show with Toby “Radiant” Tyler’s game created an inventive crossover that resonated with fans of both series. The animation maintained the heartwarming vibe of MLP while also capturing the emotional depth of Undertale’s storylines, resulting in an internet sensation.

3. Adventure Time x Regular Show

If there’s one rule that applies to crossovers, it’s that the unusual pairing can often lead to the most delightful results. In the case of Adventure Time and Regular Show, two cartoon powerhouses from Cartoon Network, combining their distinct styles and storylines resulted in a glorious mash-up watched by millions. The unveiling of the mysterious “The Vault” series led to a collaboration that brought together the likes of Finn and Jake with Mordecai and Rigby, captivating audiences and leading to fan-theories and discussions for months to come.

4. Godzilla x RANMA ½

While Godzilla might be better known for his monstrous ventures on the big screen, he too has delved into the world of animation with some unexpected results. This crossover brought together Japanese legends Toho and Studio DEEN for a daunting and awe-inspiring encounter between the King of the Monsters and the unpredictable RANMA ½. The collaboration seemingly unified the world of anime, providing fans with not just an epic showdown but a unique exploration of the characters and their worlds.

5. Spider-Man x Squirrel Girl

As the Marvel world continues to expand, it only makes sense that its famous characters would find themselves in unexpected team-ups. In the case of Spider-Man and Squirrel Girl, the energetic fusion of comic book royalty with the quirky and relatable Squirrel Girl managed to break both the internet and some long-held perceptions about superhero pairings. This crossover harnessed the charm of both characters while also challenging traditional superhero tropes, leaving fans enthralled and excited.

To state the obvious, cartoon crossovers have a magical way of captivating their audience; be it by weaving familiar characters into new adventures or by combining storytelling styles for mind-bending results. As the internet continues to bring fans closer together than ever before, exciting crossovers will continue to dominate the conversation, leaving an indelible mark on the animation world.
