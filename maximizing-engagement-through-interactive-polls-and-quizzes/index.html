Maximizing Engagement Through Interactive Polls and Quizzes


As anyone familiar with social media knows, engagement is key. In the vast landscape of the internet, attention spans are as fleeting as a hummingbird’s wings, and keeping users hooked is no easy task. One effective method for achieving this is through interactive polls and quizzes. They are straightforward to create, inexpensive, and can pique users’ curiosity, sparking conversation and fostering a sense of community.

Interactive Polls: Generating Conversations

Polls can be used to garner opinions, initiate discussions, or simply spark interest. They are a fantastic tool for engaging users as they appeal to our innate desire to opine and voice our thoughts. Furthermore, including an “other” option encourages users to contribute their own ideas, further promoting engagement.

One successful example of a poll is the “What’s your favorite dessert?” poll that Reddit, the famous online forum, conducted on their subreddit, r/dataisbeautiful. This poll drew people in by tapping into everyone’s love for sweets, and the results page was filled with amusing comments discussing various desserts and dissertations on their unique attributes.

Quizzes: Creating a Gamified Experience

Quizzes are an engaging way to educate users, whether it’s testing their knowledge on a specific topic or challenging them to come up with their answers. Tom LeBron, author of “Engaging Stories:ded First-Word Copy that Boosts Website, Email, and Social Media Marketing,” asserts that quizzes:

“are one of the most popular content choices across multiple platforms, result[ing] in high levels of likes, shares, comments, and click-throughs.”

Quizzes are especially effective in a social media setting, where people expect bite-sized information that can be easily consumed in their mobile environment. For instance, Buzzfeed’s “Which Disney Princess Are You?” quiz catapulted them to internet stardom, appealing to our love of character identification.

To further engage users, make sure to create shareable graphics and content to distribute once the quiz is complete. Encourage users to share their results with friends and engage in discussion about the quiz experience.

Create Your Own Interactive Content

Creating interactive polls and quizzes requires little more than an idea and an online tool. Poll builders such as Poll Maker or Poll Nation offer robust features for creating polls, ensuring that your content reflects the polish and sophistication of your brand. Additionally, sites like Triventy, QuizMaker, and Canva allow users to create customizable quizzes with minimal effort.

Setting up a poll or quiz requires little more than an internet connection.
