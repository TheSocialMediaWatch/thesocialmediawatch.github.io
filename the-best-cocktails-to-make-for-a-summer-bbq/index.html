The Best Cocktails to Make for a Summer BBQ


Summer is the perfect time for backyard barbecues, where friends and family gather to enjoy delicious food, laughter, and good company. While the grill is sizzling away, it’s essential to have a refreshing drink in hand to complement the festivities. Cocktails can elevate your BBQ experience, adding a splash of flavor and fun to the day. In this guide, we will explore some of the best cocktails to make for your summer BBQ, ensuring that your gathering is not only delicious but also memorable.

Classic Mojito

Ingredients:

  • Fresh mint leaves
  • 1 lime (cut into wedges)
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 cup white rum
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Mint sprigs for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a sturdy glass, muddle the mint leaves and lime wedges with sugar until the mint is fragrant and the lime juice is released.
  2. Fill the glass with ice and pour in the rum.
  3. Top off with club soda and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with a mint sprig and a lime wedge.

The Mojito is a classic summer cocktail that is both refreshing and easy to make. The combination of mint, lime, and rum creates a crisp flavor profile that pairs perfectly with grilled meats and fresh salads.

Watermelon Basil Cooler

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups watermelon, cubed
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 lime (juiced)
  • 1 cup vodka
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Watermelon slices and basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, puree the watermelon until smooth. Strain the juice through a fine sieve into a pitcher.
  2. Add the lime juice and vodka to the watermelon juice.
  3. In a glass, muddle the basil leaves, then add ice and pour the watermelon mixture over it.
  4. Top with club soda and stir gently.
  5. Garnish with a watermelon slice and basil leaves.

This delightful cocktail brings the taste of summer to your BBQ. The sweetness of the watermelon, combined with the fragrant basil, makes for a refreshing drink that will keep your guests cool in the heat.

Spicy Pineapple Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh pineapple, diced
  • 1 jalapeño (sliced, seeds removed for less heat)
  • 1/2 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup tequila
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur (like Cointreau)
  • Agave syrup (to taste)
  • Ice
  • Pineapple wedges and jalapeño slices for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the pineapple and jalapeño, and blend until smooth. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a shaker.
  2. Add lime juice, tequila, orange liqueur, and agave syrup to the shaker. Fill it with ice and shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and jalapeño slice.

For those who enjoy a kick in their cocktails, the Spicy Pineapple Margarita is a perfect choice. The heat from the jalapeño balances beautifully with the sweetness of the pineapple, making it a standout drink at any summer BBQ.

Strawberry Basil Smash

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup gin
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Strawberries and basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a glass, muddle the strawberries, basil leaves, and sugar until combined and the strawberries are mashed.
  2. Fill the glass with ice and add gin and lemon juice.
  3. Top with club soda and stir gently.
  4. Garnish with additional strawberries and a basil leaf.

The Strawberry Basil Smash is a fruity, herbaceous drink that encapsulates the essence of summer. The vibrant colors and flavors make it a visually appealing choice that your guests will love.

Classic Pimm’s Cup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Pimm’s No. 1
  • 3 cups lemonade
  • Sliced cucumber
  • Sliced strawberries
  • Orange slices
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Fill a pitcher with ice and add Pimm’s No. 1 and lemonade.
  2. Add the sliced cucumber, strawberries, orange slices, and mint leaves.
  3. Stir gently to combine and let it sit for a few minutes for the flavors to meld.
  4. Serve in glasses filled with ice and garnish with additional mint leaves.

The Pimm’s Cup is a quintessential British summer drink that is light, refreshing, and perfect for outdoor gatherings. With its colorful presentation and fruity flavors, it’s sure to impress your guests.

Tropical Rum Punch

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup light rum
  • 1 cup dark rum
  • 2 cups pineapple juice
  • 1 cup orange juice
  • 1/2 cup grenadine
  • Fresh fruit slices (pineapple, orange, cherries)
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a large punch bowl, combine light rum, dark rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, and grenadine.
  2. Stir well to combine and chill in the refrigerator for at least an hour.
  3. Serve over ice, garnished with fresh fruit slices.

Tropical Rum Punch is a party favorite that’s perfect for summer BBQs. The combination of light and dark rum with fruity juices creates a deliciously sweet and tangy drink that will keep everyone refreshed and in high spirits.

Cucumber Mint Fizz

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 cup gin
  • Club soda
  • Ice
  • Cucumber slices and mint for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, muddle cucumber slices and mint leaves with lime juice.
  2. Add gin and ice, then shake well.
  3. Strain into a glass filled with ice and top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with cucumber slices and mint leaves.

The Cucumber Mint Fizz is a light and refreshing cocktail that’s perfect for hot summer days. The cooling effect of cucumber, combined with the invigorating mint, makes this drink an ideal choice for your BBQ.

Berry Sangria

Ingredients:

  • 1 bottle red wine (like Merlot or Tempranillo)
  • 1/2 cup brandy
  • 1/4 cup orange liqueur
  • 2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 orange (sliced)
  • 1 lemon (sliced)
  • 2 cups soda water or lemon-lime soda
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. In a large pitcher, combine red wine, brandy, and orange liqueur.
  2. Add the mixed berries, orange slices, and lemon slices. Stir gently.
  3. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to allow the flavors to meld.
  4. Just before serving, add soda water and ice to the pitcher.

Berry Sangria is a festive and fruity drink that is perfect for summer gatherings. Its vibrant colors and refreshing flavors will add a cheerful touch to your BBQ.

Tips for Hosting a Cocktail-Friendly BBQ

Now that you have a fantastic lineup of cocktails for your summer BBQ, here are some tips to make your cocktail experience even better:

  1. Prepare in Advance: Many of these cocktails can be prepared in larger batches ahead of time. This allows you to enjoy the party without being stuck behind the bar.

  2. Set Up a DIY Bar: Create a cocktail station with all the ingredients and tools needed for your chosen drinks. This allows guests to mix their cocktails and adds an interactive element to the party.

  3. Provide Non-Alcoholic Options: Not everyone drinks alcohol, so be sure to offer some refreshing non-alcoholic beverages as well. Sparkling water with fruit, iced tea, or mocktails can be great alternatives.

  4. Consider Food Pairings: Think about how your cocktails will pair with the food you’re serving. Light, refreshing drinks go well with grilled veggies and seafood, while richer cocktails can complement hearty meats.

  5. Stay Hydrated: Encourage guests to drink water between cocktails. It’s essential to stay hydrated, especially during hot summer days.

  6. Garnishes Make a Difference: Fresh herbs, fruit slices, and colorful garnishes not only enhance the flavor of your cocktails but also make them visually appealing. Don’t skip this step!

Conclusion

Summer BBQs are all about enjoying good food, great company, and refreshing drinks. With these delightful cocktail recipes, you’ll be sure to impress your guests and create a festive atmosphere. From classic favorites like the Mojito to unique twists like the Spicy Pineapple Margarita, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. So grab your ingredients, fire up the grill, and get ready for an unforgettable summer barbecue filled with delicious cocktails!
