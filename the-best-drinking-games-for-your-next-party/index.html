The Best Drinking Games for Your Next Party


Are you planning a party and looking for ways to create an unforgettable experience for your guests? Drinking games are an excellent way to add a fun and exciting twist to your gathering. Whether you're a seasoned party planner or a casual host, we've got you covered with our list of the best drinking games to try at your next party. Classics Never Go Out of Style Before we dive into the more modern and creative drinking games, let's revisit some timeless classics that are sure to be a hit with your guests.

Never Have I Ever: This game is a staple for a reason. Players take turns saying "never have I ever" statements, and those who have done the action take a drink. It's a great way to get to know your guests and spark some interesting conversations. Rock, Paper, Scissors, Shot: A variation of the classic game, this drinking game adds a shot to the winner of each round. It's a fun way to keep the energy high and the drinks flowing. Quarters: This game is a party favorite that's easy to learn and play. Players take turns bouncing a quarter off a table and into a cup. If the quarter goes in, the player continues. If it doesn't, they take a drink. Simple, yet entertaining.

Modern Twists on Classic Games If you're looking for something a little more modern and unique, we've got some great options for you.

Would You Rather: This game is a great way to get your guests thinking creatively and spark some interesting conversations. Players are presented with two difficult choices and must decide which one they would rather do. Those who choose the more challenging option take a drink. Scattergories: This game is a fun twist on the classic category game. Players must come up with words that fit certain categories, all starting with the same letter. If a player can't think of a word, they take a drink. Charades with a Twist: This game is a fun way to get your guests laughing and engaged. Players take turns acting out words or phrases, but with a twist - they can only use a specific number of words or phrases to describe it. If they can't get their teammate to guess it, they take a drink.

Outdoor Games for a Fun and Relaxing Atmosphere If you're planning a party in a backyard or outdoor setting, we've got some great options for you.

Flip Cup: This game is a fun way to get your guests moving and laughing. Players take turns trying to chug a beer and then flip a cup over. If they spill the beer or can't flip the cup, they take a drink. Balloon Pop Game: This game is a fun and interactive way to get your guests involved. Players must pop balloons to reveal a challenge or task. If they complete the task, they earn points. If they can't, they take a drink. Bullshit: This game is a fun way to get your guests laughing and engaged. Players take turns dealing cards and trying to get rid of their hand. If a player can't get rid of all their cards, they take a drink.

Drinking Games for Large Groups If you're planning a party with a large group of people, we've got some great options for you.

Giant Jenga: This game is a fun way to get your guests working together and having fun. Players take turns trying to remove blocks from a giant Jenga tower. If a player causes the tower to fall, they take a drink. Kings Cup: This game is a fun and interactive way to get your guests involved. Players take turns drawing cards and following the instructions on the card. If a player can't complete the task, they take a drink. Mafia: This game is a fun way to get your guests thinking strategically and working together. Players take turns trying to figure out who among them is a mafia member. If a player is accused of being a mafia member and it's true, they take a drink.

Tips and Tricks for a Successful Party When it comes to hosting a successful party, there are a few key things to keep in mind.

Set clear rules and expectations: Make sure your guests know what to expect and what the rules are. This will help prevent any confusion or disagreements. Have plenty of drinks and snacks: Make sure you have enough drinks and snacks on hand to keep your guests happy and energized. Encourage teamwork and collaboration: Drinking games are a great way to get your guests working together and having fun. Encourage them to collaborate and work together to achieve their goals. Keep the games light-hearted and fun: Remember, the goal of drinking games is to have fun and enjoy each other's company. Keep the games light-hearted and fun, and avoid anything that might be too intense or competitive.

By following these tips and incorporating some of the drinking games we've listed, you'll be well on your way to hosting a successful and unforgettable party. So go ahead, grab some drinks, and get ready to have a blast with your friends!
