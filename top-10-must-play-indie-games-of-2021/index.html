Top 10 Must-Play Indie Games of 2021


2021 has been a remarkable year for indie game developers, with a plethora of unique and captivating titles hitting the gaming scene. From gripping narratives to innovative gameplay mechanics, indie games have continued to push boundaries and offer players unforgettable experiences. If you’re looking to discover some hidden gems and support independent creators, here are the top 10 must-play indie games of 2021 that deserve a spot on your gaming playlist:

1. “Hollow Knight: Silksong”

Genre: Action-Adventure

Description: The highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed “Hollow Knight,” “Hollow Knight: Silksong” follows the journey of Hornet, a princess-protector, in a vast and beautifully crafted world filled with challenging enemies and mysteries to uncover.

2. “Death’s Door”

Genre: Action RPG

Description: In “Death’s Door,” you play as a crow on a mission to reap souls in a mysterious and atmospheric world. With tight combat mechanics and a gripping story, this game offers a blend of exploration and intense battles that will keep you hooked.

3. “Kena: Bridge of Spirits”

Genre: Action-Adventure

Description: “Kena: Bridge of Spirits” combines stunning visuals with engaging gameplay as you embark on a journey to uncover the secrets of a forgotten village. With adorable companions called the Rot, this game delivers a charming and emotional experience.

4. “Artful Escape”

Genre: Musical Platformer

Description: Step into the shoes of a young musician on a psychedelic journey of self-discovery in “Artful Escape.” With its surreal visuals, soul-stirring music, and inventive gameplay, this game offers a truly unique adventure.

5. “Loop Hero”

Genre: Deck-Building Roguelike

Description: “Loop Hero” challenges you to strategically place obstacles on a looping path to help your hero survive and uncover the mysteries of a world trapped in an eternal time loop. With its addictive gameplay loop and retro-inspired graphics, this game is a must-play for strategy enthusiasts.

6. “Unpacking”

Genre: Puzzle

Description: Dive into the meditative experience of “Unpacking,” where you unpack boxes in different living spaces and unravel the stories of the person they belong to. With its simple yet profound gameplay, this game offers a touching narrative-driven experience.

7. “Chicory: A Colorful Tale”

Genre: Adventure

Description: In “Chicory: A Colorful Tale,” you wield a magical paintbrush to restore color to a black-and-white world and solve puzzles along the way. With its charming art style and heartfelt storytelling, this game is a delightful journey of creativity and self-expression.

8. “The Wild at Heart”

Genre: Action-Adventure

Description: “The Wild at Heart” invites you to explore a magical forest filled with mysterious creatures and secrets waiting to be uncovered. With its enchanting art style and captivating narrative, this game offers a captivating adventure for players of all ages.

9. “Inscryption”

Genre: Card-Based Roguelike

Description: “Inscryption” combines deck-building with psychological horror in a unique and immersive experience. Dive into a dark and twisted world where every decision matters, and uncover the secrets hidden within the cards.

10. “Eastward”

Genre: RPG

Description: Set in a post-apocalyptic world, “Eastward” follows the journey of a miner and a mysterious girl as they unravel the mysteries of their world. With its beautiful pixel art and engaging storytelling, this game offers a captivating blend of exploration, combat, and heartfelt moments.

These top 10 indie games of 2021 showcase the diversity and creativity of independent game developers, offering players a rich tapestry of experiences to enjoy. Whether you’re a fan of action-packed adventures, thought-provoking puzzles, or heartfelt narratives, there’s something for everyone in the world of indie gaming. So, grab your controller, immerse yourself in these captivating worlds, and let the magic of indie games transport you to new and unforgettable realms. Happy gaming!

Remember, the indie gaming scene is always evolving, so keep an eye out for upcoming releases and support independent developers who continue to push the boundaries of creativity in the gaming industry.
