Comedy in Podcasting: The Funniest Shows to Listen to


Podcasting has become a popular form of entertainment, allowing people to listen to their favorite shows on the go. And when it comes to comedy, podcasting has opened up a whole new world of laughter. In this blog post, we will explore some of the funniest shows in the podcasting world that are guaranteed to make you burst out laughing.

1. “The Joe Rogan Experience”

Hosted by stand-up comedian Joe Rogan, “The Joe Rogan Experience” is a podcast that covers a wide range of topics, from comedy and current events to science and philosophy. With a laid-back and conversational style, Joe brings on some of the funniest comedians and celebrities for in-depth interviews. The show’s humor is spontaneous, raw, and often unfiltered, making it a must-listen for comedy enthusiasts.

2. “Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend”

Late-night talk show host Conan O’Brien brings his wit and charm to the podcasting world with “Conan O’Brien Needs a Friend.” Each episode features Conan engaging in hilarious conversations with celebrities, comedians, and friends. The show’s format allows for candid and unscripted conversations, resulting in laugh-out-loud moments and unexpected anecdotes.

3. “My Dad Wrote A Porno”

If you’re looking for something truly unique and side-splittingly funny, “My Dad Wrote A Porno” is the perfect podcast for you. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this show takes a hilarious twist as Jamie reads chapters from his dad’s erotic novel while his friends provide hilarious commentary. The combination of awkwardness, humor, and disbelief makes for a truly unique and entertaining experience.

4. “The Dollop”

“The Dollop” is a history podcast with a comedic twist. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, each episode delves into strange and often forgotten stories from history. Dave provides the historical context, while Gareth reacts with hilarious commentary and jokes. This podcast is not only educational but also incredibly funny, making it a favorite among history buffs and comedy lovers alike.

5. “Comedy Bang! Bang!”

“Comedy Bang! Bang!” is a comedy podcast hosted by Scott Aukerman. Known for its improvisational style, the show features a mix of celebrity guests, comedians, and characters. With its fast-paced humor and witty banter, “Comedy Bang! Bang!” never fails to deliver laughs. The show’s unpredictable nature keeps listeners engaged, wondering what hilarious twist will come next.

6. “How Did This Get Made?”

If you have a love for bad movies and enjoy laughing at their absurdity, “How Did This Get Made?” is the podcast for you. Hosted by Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this show dives into the world of terrible films. The hosts provide hilarious commentary and analysis, dissecting the plot holes, questionable acting, and bizarre choices that make these movies so entertainingly bad.

7. “The Misfits Podcast”

“The Misfits Podcast” is a hilarious podcast that features a group of YouTubers and friends sharing their outrageous and often embarrassing stories. Hosted by Fitz, SwaggerSouls, Zuckles, Toby on the Tele, McCreamy, and iNotorious, this show is known for its irreverent humor, unfiltered discussions, and outrageous anecdotes. Each episode feels like you’re hanging out with a group of funny friends, making it a perfect choice for those looking for a good laugh.

Conclusion

These are just a few examples of the funniest shows in the podcasting world. Whether you’re a comedy enthusiast or simply in need of a good laugh, these podcasts will surely brighten your day. So grab your headphones, find a comfortable spot, and get ready to laugh until your sides hurt. Happy listening!
