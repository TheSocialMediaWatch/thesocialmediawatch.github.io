Summoning the Spirits: How to Conduct Paranormal Seances


In the realm of the unknown and the unexplained, paranormal seances have long captured the imagination of spirits seekers and curious minds alike. Breaking through the veil between the living and the dead, these mystic gatherings allow us to connect with those who have transcended the physical plane. If you have ever been intrigued by the idea of communicating with the great beyond, this beginner’s guide aims to provide you with all the necessary knowledge to successfully conduct your very own paranormal seance.

Preparation is Key

Before diving into the exhilarating journey of spirit communication, ensure you approach it with the utmost respect and reverence. The world beyond the familiar plays by rules which we don’t fully understand, thus, take the time to understand and consider these guidelines:

  1. Clean, Clean, Clean: A clean and organized space promotes clear and effective communication with the spirits. Make sure your chosen location is free of distractions, noise, and clutter.

  2. Set Your Intentions: Knowing what you hope to achieve during the seance will provide focus and direction to your intentions. Whether it be seeking guidance, closure, or a message from your loved ones, clearly defining your purpose allows for a more profound connection.

  3. Gather the Tools: Several essential instruments can help facilitate communication with the other side, including:

    • A compass: Ensuring the space is free from any barrier or unwanted influences.

    • Incense or candles: These are symbolic aids in summoning spirits, acting as fragrant paths for departed souls to follow.

    • A glass: Some spirits appear to pick up and move objects on their own.

    • A planchette: This small wooden device is moved over a Ouija board as spirits allegedly move it along.

Conducting the Seance

Relax and allow yourself to open up to the experience. Here’s how to set the stage for your paranormal encounter:

  1. Create a Sacred Space: Arrange your chosen tools attractively on a small table in the center of your space. You may also wish to include crystals or sacred symbols.

  2. Working in a Circle: Draw a circle around you and your fellow seance attendees with salt or chalk. The circle creates a boundary that separates the spiritual energies of those present from any intrusive external energies.

  3. Establish a Guiding Ritual: Choose a person to lead the seance who will guide the ritual, meditate, and evoke the spirits. It will bring structure and peace to the gathering.

  4. Set a Meditation: Everyone at the seance should sit or sit comfortably in a circle. Close your eyes and take a few slow, deep breaths as you begin to meditate.

  5. Invoking the Spirits: Begin by inviting the right spirits into your circle using the words that resonate with you the most. You could say something along these lines:

    “Spirits of light and love, guiding forces of the universe, we welcome you into our sacred circle tonight. We ask for your presence and wisdom to guide us and those who have crossed over to join us. Please help them find peace and enlightenment.”

  6. Opening the Lines of Communication: Encourage the spirit to make their presence known through the tools at your disposal, such as moving the planchette on the Ouija board or making rapping noises with the glass.

  7. Closing the Seance: Once your message or answers have been received, thank the spirits and bid them farewell. Visualize them leaving your space and the energy returning to the way it was before your gathering.

Post-Seance Care

After concluding the seance, take the time to process the experience with your fellow participants. Reflect on the messages received and the spiritual energies experienced during the session. This will not only validate the experiences but allow you to integrate the event into your understanding of the passage between worlds.

Embrace the Mystery

Summoning the spirits is not for the faint-hearted. It’s a journey of self-discovery, enlightenment, and understanding that transcends time and space. Above all, keep an open mind and allow the experience to unfold. Remember, each seance not only offers the opportunity to connect with spirits but serves as a reflection of your thoughts, beliefs, and connection to the greater universe.

Embark on this fantastic voyage, and harness the power of paranormal seances as you discover the unexplored realms beyond the horizon of our everyday existence.
