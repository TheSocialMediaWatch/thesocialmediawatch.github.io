Astrology and the Deep State: Decoding Cosmic Influences on World Events


Astrology, the ancient belief system that posits the cosmos holds sway over our personalities, and even our fates, has long been subjected to skepticism and ridicule. Critics claim the stars’ supposed influence on our lives is nothing more than pseudoscience, aiming to explain complex phenomena in simple terms. Yet, amidst skepticism, some prominent figures including politicians, business leaders, and influential celebrities continue to embrace astrology’s allegedly mystical power. What are the driving forces behind their beliefs, and could there be a connection between astrology’s growing popularity and a lesser-known but highly influential group known as the “Deep State”? Let us explore the world of astrology, the Deep State, and the intricate connections that may exist between them.

What is the Deep State?

Before we dive into the world of astrology, let us first understand the concept of the Deep State. The term “Deep State” refers to a secretive network of politicians, military officials, intelligence officers, and government bureaucrats who effectively wield power outside of and above the formal checks and balances of government. Operative in the shadows, the Deep State is said to manipulate and control international events to preserve the existing hegemony of the wealthy and powerful elite.

Astrology and Its Rise in Popularity

Astrology has come a long way since its humble beginnings in ancient civilizations. In recent years, interest in astrology has surged, particularly among young adults. The increasing popularity of astrology can be attributed to various factors, including social media’s ability to disseminate knowledge and ideas rapidly, the desire to seek meaning and understanding in a chaotic world, and the human need to connect with something greater than ourselves.

Although critics continue to view astrology as a relic of ignorance, many of its proponents consider it a tool for personal growth, self-reflection, and intestinal guidance. Some believe that astrology offers insights into the cosmic influence on human behavior, suggesting that the alignment of planets can impact world events and individual lives.

The Arcane Connection Between Astrology and the Deep State

The notion that a secretive, powerful group manipulates global events may resonate with the esoteric beliefs held by some who advocate for astrology’s influence on human lives. Could the allure of astrology stem from a subconscious yearning to decode the mysteries of the universe and potentially expose those who control it? This desire for understanding may have led some individuals to seek out unconventional sources of knowledge – one of which is astrology.

Astrology and Predicting World Events

One notable crossover between astrology and the Deep State is their shared pursuit of understanding and prediction. Many high-level politicians and influential figures seek insight into world events from unconventional sources, including astrology. According to some theories, these individuals may use astrology to make strategic decisions, chart their courses of action, and influence public opinion.

Some allege that certain world leaders based their decisions on astrological guidance, both in war and in peace. These assertions remain unproven and highly controversial, but the belief in astrology’s predictive power gains traction in unexpected circles.

Astrology As Resistance to the Deep State?

The connection between astrology and the Deep State may seem tenuous. However, both belief systems offer a sense of control and understanding in worlds shrouded in mystery and unpredictability. In this context, astrology may serve as an alternative source of knowledge to challenge the oppressive, elitist control of the Deep State.

The rise of astrology aligns with the growing distrust of government and other institutions. People from all walks of life are seeking alternative ways to understand the underlying forces that control global events. Perhaps the profound allure of astrology and other esoteric beliefs stems from their potential to reveal the layers of deception masking the truth.

In conclusion, although the connection between astrology and the Deep State appears unconventional, both belief systems share a common thread – the desire to uncover and understand the hidden forces shaping our world. Increasing skepticism towards mainstream institutions has led to an interest in alternative knowledge sources, paving the way for astrology’s resurgence. The widespread adoption of astrology, and its acceptance among those in power, may signal a gradual shift in societal paradigms — toward a more curious and open-minded approach to understanding the world around us.

As we continue to explore these uncharted territories, let us never lose sight of the importance of embracing diverse perspectives and theories, even those that may initially appear bizarre or unconventional. After all, the most transformative discoveries often lie at the fringes of our collective understanding.
