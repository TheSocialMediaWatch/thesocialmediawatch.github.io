Comedy in Podcasting: Must-Listen Shows for a Good Laugh


Are you looking for a good laugh? Look no further than the world of podcasting. With the rise of on-the-go entertainment, podcasts have become a popular medium for comedy, offering a diverse range of humor and wit. Whether you’re into stand-up, improv, storytelling, or just casual banter, there’s a comedy podcast out there that will tickle your funny bone. In this post, we’ve curated a list of must-listen comedy shows that are guaranteed to bring joy and laughter into your life.

1. “The Joe Rogan Experience”

“The Joe Rogan Experience” is a long-form podcast hosted by comedian Joe Rogan. Known for its candid and unfiltered conversations, this show features a wide array of guests, including comedians, actors, musicians, and experts from various fields. With its blend of humor, thought-provoking discussions, and occasional absurdity, this podcast is a favorite among comedy enthusiasts.

2. “My Dad Wrote A Porno”

If you’re in the mood for something delightfully absurd, “My Dad Wrote A Porno” is the perfect choice. Hosted by Jamie Morton, James Cooper, and Alice Levine, this podcast takes an unconventional approach to comedy by reading and dissecting a hilariously bad erotic novel written by Morton’s own father. The result is a side-splitting journey through the world of amateur literature that will leave you in stitches.

3. “Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend”

Join late-night host Conan O’Brien as he embarks on a quest to find genuine friendship in the celebrity world. “Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend” features candid and humorous conversations with a variety of guests, where Conan and his co-hosts explore the intricacies of friendship, all while delivering plenty of laughs along the way.

4. “2 Dope Queens”

Hosted by comedians Phoebe Robinson and Jessica Williams, “2 Dope Queens” is a stand-up comedy podcast that celebrates diversity and inclusivity. Each episode features a lineup of talented comedians, providing a platform for underrepresented voices in the comedy scene. The result is a hilarious and empowering listening experience that will leave you feeling uplifted and entertained.

5. “How Did This Get Made?”

Ever watched a terrible movie and wondered how it even got made? Well, wonder no more with “How Did This Get Made?” Hosted by Paul Scheer, June Diane Raphael, and Jason Mantzoukas, this podcast delves into the world of awesomely bad movies, dissecting their absurdity and laughing at the sheer ridiculousness of it all. If you’re a fan of movie roasts and comedic analysis, this show is a definite must-listen.

6. “The Dollop”

For history buffs with a penchant for humor, “The Dollop” offers a unique blend of comedy and storytelling. Hosted by comedians Dave Anthony and Gareth Reynolds, this podcast explores bizarre and often unbelievable stories from American history, delivering them with a healthy dose of comedic commentary and witty banter. Prepare to be entertained and enlightened in equal measure.

7. “Armchair Expert with Dax Shepard”

Join actor and comedian Dax Shepard as he engages in candid and humorous conversations with a wide array of guests on “Armchair Expert.” With a mix of humor, vulnerability, and insightful discussions, this podcast offers a refreshing take on the comedy genre, providing listeners with laughter, empathy, and thought-provoking moments.

8. “The Bugle”

If you’re a fan of satirical news and witty commentary, “The Bugle” is the podcast for you. Hosted by comedians Andy Zaltzman and a rotating panel of guests, this show delivers a comedic take on current events and global happenings, offering a blend of sharp humor and intelligent satire that will keep you both informed and entertained.

Conclusion

In the fast-paced world we live in, laughter is a precious and necessary commodity. With the power of podcasting, humor is just a click away, waiting to brighten your day and lift your spirits. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up, storytelling, or satirical commentary, the world of comedy podcasts has something for everyone. So, the next time you need a good laugh, tune in to one of these must-listen shows and let the comedic magic unfold.

We hope you enjoy our recommendations and that they bring a smile to your face. Happy listening!
