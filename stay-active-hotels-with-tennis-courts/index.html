Stay Active: Hotels with Tennis Courts


When it comes to staying active while traveling, finding a hotel with excellent sports facilities can make all the difference. For tennis enthusiasts, having access to a tennis court right at your hotel can be a game-changer. Not only does it allow you to maintain your fitness routine, but it also adds an element of fun and excitement to your stay. In this blog post, we will explore some of the top hotels around the world that offer tennis courts for their guests.

1. The Grand Slam Hotel - Paris, France

Located in the heart of Paris, The Grand Slam Hotel is a paradise for tennis lovers. With its state-of-the-art tennis facilities, including multiple outdoor and indoor courts, this hotel provides an exceptional experience for both beginners and professional players alike. The hotel also offers private lessons with experienced instructors, making it an ideal destination for those looking to improve their game. After an intense match, guests can relax in the hotel’s luxurious spa or indulge in a gourmet meal at one of its renowned restaurants.

2. The Court Yard Resort - California, USA

Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of California, The Court Yard Resort offers a unique blend of luxury and natural beauty. The resort boasts several tennis courts, each surrounded by lush greenery and stunning mountain views. Whether you are a solo traveler or a family looking for some friendly competition, the Court Yard Resort has something for everyone. The hotel even organizes regular tennis tournaments, allowing guests to showcase their skills and connect with other tennis enthusiasts.

3. The Ace Retreat - Melbourne, Australia

If you’re a fan of tennis and the vibrant city life, The Ace Retreat in Melbourne is the perfect destination for you. This sleek and modern hotel not only offers top-notch accommodation but also features a world-class tennis center. The center is equipped with both clay and hard courts, allowing players to choose their preferred surface. The hotel’s location is also advantageous, as it is situated near the renowned Melbourne Park, where the Australian Open is held. Guests can easily catch a match or explore the city’s lively atmosphere when they’re not hitting the courts.

4. The Advantage Resort - Mallorca, Spain

Mallorca, known for its stunning beaches and serene landscapes, is also home to The Advantage Resort. This hotel provides a tennis experience like no other, with its exclusive tennis academy and professional coaching services. Whether you’re a beginner or an advanced player, the resort’s experienced instructors are dedicated to helping you improve your skills. With beautifully maintained courts and a tranquil setting, The Advantage Resort offers the perfect balance between relaxation and active pursuits.

5. The Racquet Club - Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is renowned for its luxurious hotels, and The Racquet Club is no exception. This hotel combines opulent accommodations with world-class tennis facilities. The Racquet Club boasts a range of courts, including grass, clay, and hard surfaces, catering to every player’s preference. Guests can also take advantage of the club’s tennis concierge service, which arranges private lessons and organizes friendly matches. After a satisfying game, guests can unwind in the hotel’s stunning pool or enjoy a refreshing drink at the rooftop bar, overlooking the city’s magnificent skyline.

Conclusion

When it comes to staying active during your travels, choosing a hotel with tennis courts is a fantastic way to ensure you can continue pursuing your passion for the sport. These hotels mentioned above offer not only exceptional tennis facilities but also a luxurious experience that will leave you feeling rejuvenated. So, the next time you plan your trip, consider staying at one of these hotels and indulge in a tennis-filled getaway like no other.
