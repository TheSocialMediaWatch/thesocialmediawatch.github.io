Fashionable and Fit: How to Dress for Your Body Shape


We’ve all been there - standing in front of our closets, staring at our wardrobes, wondering how to create an outfit that is both fashionable and comfortable. Staying up-to-date with the latest trends is one thing, but knowing how to dress for your unique body shape is a different challenge entirely. In this blog post, we’ll dive into understanding your body shape, outfit tips for each, and provide a roadmap for dressing fashionably and fittingly.

First, let’s discuss the four main body shapes: hourglass, pear, rectangular, and athletic.

  1. Hourglass Shape

If you have an hourglass body shape, you’re blessed with a narrow waist and wider hips, likely balanced by full breasts. To accentuate and flatter this shape, focus on draping and cinching. Empower your hourglass figure by resorting to a nipped-and-tucked approach, with a waist-cinching belt or a well-placed plunging neckline. Hourglass figures usually suit A-line dresses, high-waisted pants, and anything with an accentuating waist.

  1. Pear Shape

If your ribcage tapers to a fuller bottom, you possess a pear-shaped body. To balance proportions, consider dark-wash jeans or low-rise pants to lengthen your legs visually, while flowy tops and looser-fitting dresses maintain the focus on your upper body.Bright prints on top can help build the illusion of a wider shoulder, while fitted or flared bottoms will do the same for your hips. Remember, the key is to balance and elongate.

  1. Rectangular (or Apple) Shape

For those with a balanced or even width between hips and bust, a rectangular body shape is the least common of the four. Decoding this shape requires developing a keen eye for detail. However, with the right clothing, you can look and feel fabulous. Create an Hourglass illusion by combining empire-waist silhouettes with high-waisted pants for an attractive, feminine look. Curved or flared edges can give you more shape and dimension.

  1. Athletic Shape

If you have a muscular, toned body, you’re part of the athletic body shape category. Emphasize your strong frame by choosing garments highlighting your defined features. Show off your shoulders in broad, scoop-neck, or off-the-shoulder styles, and accentuate your legs in fitted trousers or an A-line skirt. As with other shapes, balance is crucial. Pair your dearly defined upper body with a flared-leg pant or a straight, streamlined skirt.

Besides understanding your body shape, some essential outfit tips apply to all:

  • Mix and match prints - playing with patterns adds flair and layers.
  • Nobody is perfect - don’t adhere rigidly to your shape’s specific rules. Fashion is here to break them!
  • High heels elongate legs and emphasize curves, making them your ally in all body shapes.

Life can be beautiful, made in stunning silhouettes and striking styles. It all starts with understanding your body shape. When you feel fashionable and fit, the world is your runway. So, step out boldly, knowing you look and feel your best. Your individuality shines, as does our shared acceptance and celebration of all body shapes. Dress accordingly, and you’ll do just that.
