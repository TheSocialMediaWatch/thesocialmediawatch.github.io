The Best Hotels for Pet Owners


Traveling with pets has become more common than ever before. Whether it’s a weekend getaway or a longer vacation, more and more pet owners are seeking out hotels that are pet-friendly, offering accommodations and amenities that allow for a seamless experience for both the pet and its owner. Choosing the right hotel can make all the difference in how you and your furry companion enjoy your time away from home.

This guide will explore the best hotels for pet owners, considering comfort, amenities, location, and unique features that make them stand out. Traveling with pets should be a stress-free and enjoyable experience, and these hotels have made it easier for both pets and their humans.

1. Four Seasons Hotels and Resorts

The Four Seasons brand is synonymous with luxury, and their pet-friendly offerings are no exception. At select locations, Four Seasons caters to pet owners with specialized services, including pet-sitting, dog-walking, and even personalized pet amenities such as food and water bowls, pet beds, and special treats.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Pet-friendly rooms are spacious, with plenty of room for your pet to move around comfortably.
  • Most Four Seasons locations offer no additional fee for pets, but some may charge a reasonable pet fee depending on the location.
  • Many Four Seasons properties are located in urban centers, near parks, and close to dog-friendly restaurants and cafes, making it easier for you and your pet to explore the local area together.

Notable Locations:

  • Four Seasons Hotel New York Downtown
  • Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea
  • Four Seasons Hotel Chicago

2. Kimpton Hotels

Kimpton Hotels has long been a leader in offering pet-friendly accommodations, allowing pets of all sizes with no additional fees. From small pups to large dogs, Kimpton understands that pets are a part of the family and should be treated as such.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Kimpton’s “Pet Program” offers pet amenities such as beds, bowls, and toys, ensuring your pet has everything they need during their stay.
  • Pets are welcome to join their owners for dining at some Kimpton locations that offer dog-friendly patios.
  • There is no additional charge for pets, and there are no weight restrictions, which is a huge benefit for owners of larger pets.
  • Kimpton offers a “Paw Program” in some locations that includes dog walking, pet massage, and other pampering services.

Notable Locations:

  • Kimpton Hotel Palomar Los Angeles Beverly Hills
  • Kimpton Hotel Monaco Washington DC
  • Kimpton Marlowe Hotel in Cambridge, MA

3. The Ritz-Carlton

The Ritz-Carlton is another luxury brand that offers excellent service for pet owners, though their pet policies may vary by location. Many Ritz-Carlton hotels and resorts welcome pets, offering upscale amenities and personalized service for pets traveling with their owners.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • The Ritz-Carlton offers pet-friendly accommodations with plenty of room for pets to settle in.
  • Some Ritz-Carlton properties even offer “pet butler” services, ensuring your pet is well taken care of during your stay.
  • Special pet packages may include things like custom pet beds, bowls, and even pet portraits.
  • Pet concierge services are available at select properties to help arrange pet-friendly activities and excursions.

Notable Locations:

  • The Ritz-Carlton, Lake Tahoe
  • The Ritz-Carlton, San Francisco
  • The Ritz-Carlton, Sarasota

4. Loews Hotels

Loews Hotels are well-known for their commitment to pets, with a number of amenities tailored to furry guests. Loews’ “Loews Loves Pets” program is widely regarded as one of the best pet-friendly hotel programs in the world.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Loews Hotels welcome pets of all sizes and provide pet amenities like beds, bowls, and a pet-friendly welcome kit that includes treats and toys.
  • Some locations offer special services, including dog-walking and pet-sitting, so you don’t have to worry about leaving your pet behind during activities.
  • Loews Hotels also host “Yappy Hours,” special events where pet owners can mingle with other travelers and their pets.

Notable Locations:

  • Loews Miami Beach Hotel
  • Loews Hollywood Hotel
  • Loews Ventana Canyon Resort in Tucson, AZ

5. Marriott Hotels

Marriott Hotels offers a wide range of pet-friendly properties across the globe, ensuring that travelers with pets have a variety of options to choose from. While not all Marriott locations are pet-friendly, the brand has made it easy for pet owners to find suitable accommodations with amenities for their furry friends.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Marriott properties often provide special pet-friendly rooms with access to outdoor spaces where pets can play.
  • Many Marriott hotels charge a nominal pet fee, and in some locations, they offer amenities like pet beds, bowls, and food and water.
  • Marriott also offers suggestions on nearby dog parks and pet-friendly restaurants to make it easy for pet owners to find local spots to explore.

Notable Locations:

  • Marriott Marquis San Francisco
  • JW Marriott Los Angeles L.A. Live
  • The Ritz-Carlton, Charlotte

6. The Westin Hotels & Resorts

The Westin Hotels & Resorts is committed to providing guests and their pets with the best experience possible. Their pet-friendly policies and services make them a standout option for pet owners.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • The Westin offers “Heavenly Dog Beds,” a premium bed for pets that ensures comfort during your stay.
  • Many Westin properties offer walking maps for nearby pet-friendly locations, including parks and pet-friendly restaurants.
  • Westin also provides special pet packages that include treats, toys, and grooming services.

Notable Locations:

  • The Westin San Francisco Airport
  • The Westin Resort & Spa, Cancun
  • The Westin Boston Waterfront

7. Best Western Hotels & Resorts

Best Western is another excellent option for pet owners, offering budget-friendly accommodations that cater to pets. Best Western has hundreds of pet-friendly locations across the U.S., making it an easy choice for travelers seeking an affordable and comfortable stay with their pets.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Best Western offers a variety of pet-friendly accommodations, including rooms with easy access to outdoor spaces for pets to stretch their legs.
  • Some locations provide additional amenities, such as dog parks and designated pet areas, so your pet has plenty of space to play and relax.
  • The brand often allows multiple pets per room, which is ideal for families traveling with several pets.

Notable Locations:

  • Best Western Plus Monterey Inn
  • Best Western Plus The Inn at the Peachtrees in Atlanta
  • Best Western Plus Victor Inn & Suites, Victor, NY

8. Dog-Friendly Bed & Breakfasts

While not traditional hotel chains, bed and breakfasts often offer a more personalized experience for both pet owners and their pets. Many bed and breakfasts (B&Bs) are located in scenic areas, offering a peaceful retreat for travelers and their furry friends.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • B&Bs typically offer a more homey atmosphere, making them a great option for pet owners who prefer a quiet, less crowded environment for their pets.
  • Many bed and breakfasts are located in areas with access to hiking trails, dog-friendly beaches, and other outdoor activities that pets can enjoy.
  • B&B owners often have a more flexible approach to pet accommodations, offering custom services such as pet sitting or pet massage.

Notable Locations:

  • The Bed & Biscuit Inn, Portland, Maine
  • Pet-friendly B&Bs in the Adirondacks, New York
  • The Dog-Friendly B&B at Cedar House in Utah

9. The Fairmont Hotels

Fairmont Hotels is a global brand that is known for offering luxury accommodations with a pet-friendly twist. Their properties often welcome pets and offer exceptional amenities designed to ensure that pets have an enjoyable stay.

Why it’s great for pets:

  • Fairmont provides pet-friendly rooms with luxury amenities, such as pet beds and bowls, and some locations even offer custom pet menus.
  • Many Fairmont properties offer a pet-friendly concierge who can arrange activities for your pet and ensure they have everything they need.
  • Some Fairmont locations also have pet-sitting services and dog-walking options.

Notable Locations:

  • Fairmont Banff Springs, Alberta
  • Fairmont Le Château Frontenac, Quebec City
  • Fairmont Scottsdale Princess, Arizona

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel for you and your pet can make all the difference in how enjoyable your vacation is. From luxury resorts to budget-friendly options, the hotels mentioned above are some of the best in the industry for providing pet-friendly accommodations. With special amenities, services, and activities tailored to pets, these hotels ensure that both you and your furry friend can relax, unwind, and make unforgettable memories together.

When traveling with pets, always make sure to double-check pet policies, fees, and amenities at the specific property you’re considering. With the right planning and a little research, traveling with your pet can be just as relaxing and enjoyable as any other vacation.
