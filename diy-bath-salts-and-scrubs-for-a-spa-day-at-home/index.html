DIY Bath Salts and Scrubs for a Spa Day at Home


When life gets overwhelming, indulging in a relaxing, spa-like experience at home can work wonders. One way to create this atmosphere is by crafting your own bath salts and scrubs. Not only is it an enjoyable and creative activity, but you also have control over the ingredients and can customize them to your liking.

Bath salts and scrubs serve different purposes—bath salts typically provide a dissipating and calming sensation while submerged in a warm bath, whereas bath scrubs hydrate the skin and stimulate blood circulation. Today, we’ll explore how to make two unique recipes for both bath salts and scrubs.

Before we delve into these delightful concoctions, let’s gather the essential ingredients:

BATH SALTS:

  • Epsom salt
  • Dead Sea salt
  • Dried rose petals
  • Lavender buds
  • Lavender essential oil
  • Citrus essential oil
  • Food coloring (optional)

BATH SCRUB:

  • Brown sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Coconut oil
  • Essential oil of choice (e.g., lavender, peppermint, or citrus oils)
  • A few drops of orange food coloring (optional)

Let’s get started on these DIY recipes!

Bath Salts Recipe:

Directions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine 2 cups of Epsom salt, 1 cup of Dead Sea salt, and 20 drops of lavender essential oil.
  2. Add a few drops of citrus essential oil, ensuring a relaxing and uplifting atmosphere.
  3. Mix in a handful of lavender buds and a teaspoon of dried rose petals for some natural texture and visual appeal.
  4. Consider adding food coloring to enhance the color of your salts. Add a few drops, and mix well to achieve your desired hue.
  5. Stir all ingredients thoroughly until everything is evenly combined.
  6. Pour your bath salts into an airttight container and let it sit for a day to settle.
  7. Your homemade rose and lavender bath salt is ready for use!

Bath Scrub Recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • ½ cup coconut oil
  • 20 drops of your choice of essential oil (e.g., lavender, peppermint, or citrus)
  • 2-3 drops of orange food coloring (optional)

Directions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine 1 cup of brown sugar and ½ cup each of olive oil and coconut oil. Mixing these ingredients will result in a deliciously tactile texture.
  2. Add 20 drops of your favored essential oil to provide an aromatic experience while using the scrub.
  3. You may opt to include food coloring to enhance the scrub’s appearance. Mix in a couple of drops of orange food coloring, adjusting the amount to achieve a vibrant orange hue.
  4. Blend all components until fully incorporated and reach a smooth consistency.
  5. Scoop your homemade bath scrub into an airtight container to store.
  6. Use the scrub as desired in your bath or shower routine.

By using these recipes, create a luxurious sanctuary within the comforts of your home. DIY bath salts and scrubs are not only thoughtful and environmentally friendly, but also an excellent way to manifest relaxation and self-care. Remember, the joy lies in the process, making time for self-pampering an enriching experience. Happy bath-making!
