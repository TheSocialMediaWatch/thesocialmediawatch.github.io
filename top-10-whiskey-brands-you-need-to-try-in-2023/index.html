Top 10 Whiskey Brands You Need to Try in 2023


Whiskey enthusiasts and casual sippers alike are always on the lookout for new and exciting brands to explore. The world of whiskey continues to evolve, bringing forth traditional favorites and innovative newcomers that push the boundaries of flavor, craftsmanship, and authenticity. Whether you lean toward the deep complexity of Scotch, the smooth warmth of Bourbon, or the rich heritage of Irish and Japanese whiskeys, 2023 brings plenty of remarkable options to your glass.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll journey through ten standout whiskey brands that are making waves this year. Each brand represents distinctive styles and stories, ensuring there’s something for every palate and occasion. Prepare to expand your whiskey horizons with these must-try selections.

1. GlenDronach

Hailing from the Scottish Highlands, GlenDronach has built an esteemed reputation for its rich, sherried single malt Scotch whiskies. Known for aging many of their expressions in oloroso sherry casks, GlenDronach offers a unique, full-bodied flavor profile characterized by dried fruits, dark chocolate, and spices.

In 2023, the GlenDronach 18-Year-Old Allardice remains a stellar choice, boasting deep complexity and an indulgent mouthfeel. For those exploring sherried Scotch for the first time, GlenDronach is a quintessential brand that marries tradition with decadent flavor.

2. Redbreast

When it comes to Irish whiskey, Redbreast stands out as a prime example of classic pot still whiskey craftsmanship. This brand has garnered critical acclaim for its full-flavored, balanced whiskeys that lean on rich malt, spicy warmth, and a smooth finish epitomizing Irish distilling traditions.

The Redbreast 12-Year-Old continues to be a benchmark expression in 2023. It’s revered for its harmonious notes of tropical fruits, nuts, and subtle oak. This brand is a gateway into the soulful charm of Irish whiskey and a compelling addition to any collection.

3. Yamazaki

Japan’s Yamazaki distillery has long been celebrated for elevating single malt whiskey to an art form that combines careful craftsmanship with Japanese precision. Yamazaki blends innovation and traditional Scottish methods but infuses them with Japan’s unique terroir and aesthetic sensibility.

The Yamazaki 18-Year-Old is one of the most coveted bottles in the whiskey world, exemplifying soft, fruity nuances with layers of smoke and spice. In 2023, it remains a pinnacle of Japanese whiskey mastery, worthy of both new drinkers and seasoned connoisseurs.

4. Buffalo Trace

Buffalo Trace is synonymous with classic American bourbon and is a phenomenal introduction into the flavors of Kentucky’s signature spirit. The distillery produces a wide array of bourbons that blend smoothness with bold character, often featuring notes of caramel, vanilla, and oak.

Buffalo Trace Bourbon itself is a powerhouse under $40, offering accessible richness and versatility. For collectors and cocktail lovers alike, this brand ensures a dependable and flavorful experience that exemplifies the best of bourbon innovation and tradition in 2023.

5. Ardbeg

For lovers of peaty and smoky Scotch, Ardbeg delivers an unmatched punch of intense flavor from the Isle of Islay. Known for its heavily peated expressions, Ardbeg weaves together notes of seaweed, iodine, and smoky campfire that keeps enthusiasts raving year after year.

The Ardbeg Ten Years Old remains the flagship, presenting a bold chase of smoke balanced by citrus and vanilla undertones. In 2023, this is a go-to brand for those who want the quintessential taste of Islay’s rugged spirit.

6. Blanton’s

Blanton’s is considered the pioneer of single barrel bourbon, offering whiskey aged and bottled from individual barrels to capture unique expressions and complexity. This attention to detail means no two bottles are exactly alike, making it an exciting brand to follow.

Known for rich notes of caramel, honey, and spice, Blanton’s flagship is perfect for sipping neat or savoring in classic cocktails. Its rising popularity in 2023 reflects a broader appreciation of single barrel craftsmanship in the bourbon market.

7. Tullamore D.E.W.

Tullamore D.E.W. represents a balanced, triple-distilled Irish whiskey experience that is approachable yet nuanced. Its blending process combines malt, grain, and pot still whiskey, producing a smooth character enhanced with gentle vanilla, fruit, and toasted wood.

The Tullamore D.E.W. Original remains a staple in 2023, a versatile option for both whiskey novices and seasoned drinkers. This brand’s accessibility and consistent quality make it a must-try addition for those exploring the diverse styles of Irish whiskey.

8. Springbank

Springbank, located in Campbeltown, Scotland, offers some of the most distinct single malts on the market, thanks to its traditional production methods and lightly peated profile. The distillery’s commitment to handcrafting its whiskey has earned it a loyal following among aficionados.

In 2023, Springbank 10-Year-Old is worth experiencing for its rich complexity, notes of toffee, brine, and a touch of smoke. This brand is ideal for whiskey drinkers seeking something that blends tradition with characterful flair.

9. Four Roses

Four Roses is celebrated for its approachable yet layered bourbon, using a unique approach of ten different recipes to create intricate flavor blends. Its bourbons range from mellow to robust, featuring bright fruitiness balanced with rich caramel and vanilla notes.

In 2023, Four Roses Single Barrel remains a crowd favorite for its distinctively smooth yet vibrant profile. This brand is a favorite among bourbon enthusiasts looking for thoughtful craftsmanship with wide appeal.

10. Glenfiddich

Last but not least, no whiskey exploration is complete without Glenfiddich. This Speyside giant is one of the most recognized single malt Scotch brands worldwide, known for its consistency, innovation, and accessibility.

Glenfiddich’s 15-Year-Old Solera Reserve showcases their unique solera maturation process, bringing together bourbon, sherry, and new oak casks for a layered, rich flavor. In 2023, Glenfiddich continues to offer a wide range that spans from beginner-friendly to sophisticated expressions, making it an essential brand for whiskey lovers.

Wrapping Up Your Whiskey Journey in 2023

Exploring whiskey means more than just tasting; it’s about connecting to history, geography, and craft. The brands listed here provide a diverse palette of experiences, from the rich and sherried malts of GlenDronach to the smoky intensity of Ardbeg’s Islay expressions, and the masterful bourbons from Buffalo Trace and Four Roses.

Whichever direction your taste buds lead you, this decade promises exciting discoveries filled with character, tradition, and innovation. Pour your glass, savor the stories in each bottle, and toast to another great year of whiskey exploration.

Cheers to your 2023 whiskey adventures!
