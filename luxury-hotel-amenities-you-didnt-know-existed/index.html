Luxury Hotel Amenities You Didn't Know Existed


The extravagant world of luxury hotels is an enchanting place where one can experience unparalleled hospitality and services beyond the average guest’s imagination. While dreams of a creatively-crafted bed-arrangement, a custom-made drink before check-in or a private helicopter tour often come true in these hotels, there are still certain amenities that remain hidden treasures for many.

Pre-arrival Personalization

Before you set foot in the property, luxury hotels are offering a new level of personalization. With the expansion of technology, hotels can understand guests’ preferences by analyzing data from their profiles, previous stays or even social media. This information helps them prep the room to the guest’s taste, ranging from aromatherapy, color of lighting, or even tails of upbeat music that one may enjoy.

Digitally Equipped Rooms

Luxury hotels aim to meet your every digital need with their state-of-the-art equipment for an unrivaled experience. For art enthusiasts, rooms can be set up with digital art walls, or if you’re an avid reader, digital bookshelves with thousands of e-books at your disposal. For the tech-savvy guests, hotel rooms equip iPads as the room’s control center.

Personal Gourmet Dining Experiences

Then there are the luxurious gastronomic experiences that these hotels deliver. From in-room private chefs who can whip up a personal meal just for you, to a romantic suppers underneath the stars, these hotels ensure that no meal is ever ordinary. Some hotels may even go as far as sourcing exotic or rare ingredients just for that one-time dining experience.

Unusual Spa Treatments

Just when you thought spa treatments can’t get any better, luxury hotels once again prove you wrong. Take a dip into the bath in a room with a virtual view of the cosmos, enjoy a chocolate massage, workout with personalized protein-infused robots - these hotels aim to make each treatment extraordinary.

Unique Wellness Offerings

Wellness is the new luxury, and luxury hotels keep that in mind. Many hotels offer Michelin-starred chefs who craft nutritional menus, taking into account your dietary preferences and needs. Holistic wellness retreats, rejuvenating ancestral practices, or private meditation classes are among some of the unique wellness offerings provided by such hotels.

In-room Personal Shopping

Luxury hotels understand their guests’ convenience needs, significantly reducing the hassle by extended services like in-room personal shopping. The hotels can arrange to have a curated designer wardrobe waiting in your room or enlist a personal stylist for an in-room fashion consultation.

Personalized Travel Planning

Lastly, personalized travel planning is becoming a sought-after service amongst luxury hotels. From organizing private art tours to planning stay in offbeat cities, these hotels go out of the way to craft your vacation experience around you.

In conclusion, even the minutest details in a luxury hotel make an indelible mark on the guests’ experience. With ever-evolving and innovative measures, such hotels aim to provide an unmatched and completely personalized hospitality service. So perhaps, on your next vacation, try diving into these luxury hidden treasures for a once-in-a-lifetime experience.
