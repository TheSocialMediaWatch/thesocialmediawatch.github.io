Camping Dessert Ideas: Sweet Treats for Your Outdoor Getaway


Are you planning a camping trip and wondering what sweet treats you can enjoy while embracing the great outdoors? Look no further! In this post, we’re going to share some mouthwatering camping dessert ideas that will make your outdoor getaway even sweeter. From classic favorites to creative twists, these desserts are sure to satisfy your sweet tooth. So, let’s get started!

1. S’mores Nachos

Who says nachos can only be savory? S’mores nachos are a fun and delicious twist on the classic campfire treat. Instead of using graham crackers, break them into pieces and place them in a cast iron skillet. Then, top them with mini marshmallows and chocolate chips. Place the skillet over the campfire or grill until the marshmallows are gooey and the chocolate is melted. Serve with a side of whipped cream or ice cream for an extra indulgent treat.

2. Campfire Cones

Campfire cones are a versatile and customizable dessert that everyone will love. Start by filling sugar cones with your favorite sweet fillings such as mini marshmallows, chocolate chips, peanut butter, or even fruit. Wrap the filled cones in foil and place them over the campfire for a few minutes until the ingredients are melted and gooey. Unwrap and enjoy your personalized campfire cones!

3. Grilled Fruit Skewers with Honey Yogurt Dip

If you’re looking for a healthier option, grilled fruit skewers with a honey yogurt dip are the perfect choice. Cut your favorite fruits like pineapple, peaches, and strawberries into bite-sized pieces. Thread them onto skewers and place them on a preheated grill. Grill the fruit for a few minutes on each side until it becomes slightly caramelized. Serve with a creamy dip made from Greek yogurt and a drizzle of honey for a refreshing and guilt-free dessert.

4. Dutch Oven Apple Crisp

A warm and comforting dessert like Dutch oven apple crisp is a must-have for any camping trip. Start by peeling and slicing apples, then toss them with cinnamon, sugar, and a squeeze of lemon juice. In a separate bowl, mix oats, flour, brown sugar, and butter to create the crisp topping. Place the apple mixture in a Dutch oven, sprinkle the crisp topping on top, and cover with the lid. Set the Dutch oven over the campfire and cook for about 30-40 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for the ultimate camping dessert experience.

5. Campfire Cinnamon Rolls

Why wait until breakfast to enjoy cinnamon rolls when you can have them for dessert too? Prepare a can of refrigerated cinnamon rolls and wrap each individual roll around a greased campfire skewer. Hold the skewer over the campfire, rotating it slowly, until the cinnamon roll is golden brown and cooked through. Remove from the skewer and drizzle with the included icing. These warm and gooey campfire cinnamon rolls are a delightful way to end a day of outdoor adventures.

Conclusion

Camping desserts are not just an afterthought – they can be the highlight of your outdoor getaway. Whether you’re craving a classic s’mores experience or feeling adventurous with grilled fruit skewers, these sweet treats will elevate your camping trip to a whole new level. So, pack your ingredients, gather around the campfire, and indulge in these delicious camping dessert ideas. Your taste buds will thank you!
