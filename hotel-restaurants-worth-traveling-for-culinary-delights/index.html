Hotel Restaurants Worth Traveling For: Culinary Delights


Hotels have always been more than just a place to rest your head at night. They’re a hub for unforgettable dining experiences, conviviality, and impeccable service. In this post, we’ll explore some hotel restaurants that stand out from the crowd, offering unique culinary experiences worth traveling for.

L’Atelier de Joël Robuchon - Paris, France

Considered one of the best hotel restaurants in the world, L’Atelier de Joël Robuchon is Exhibit A. Situated within a chic traffic-free tunnel in the heart of Paris, this Michelin-star-studded dining room is helmed by three-starred chef Joël Robuchon. Robuchon’s dishes showcase French gastronomy in spectacular, surprising ways, complete with contemporary and theatrical presentations. Whether enjoying the ten-course tasting menu or taking a seat at the kitchen counter, diners are in for a meal experience worthy of a European sojourn.

Ar_pkg - New York, United States

Housed within the One57 luxury tower, Ar_pkg takes New York’s dining scene to new heights. With a no-reservation policy and menu that changes monthly, the 12-seat restaurant—it’s impossible to call it a mere “restaurant”—doesn’t make it easy to secure a spot. But if you do find yourself in the privileged position of enjoying a meal prepared by the talented Ms. Asks, don’t hesitate to savor her modern Japanese creations, where scenes of Sicilian sunsets and miso-acid umami unite in experimental wonder.

St. Regis Maldives - Maldives

It’s not always a good idea to hide a restaurant in the remote reaches of the world, but in the case of the St. Regis Maldives, it’s pure genius. Gastronomical wonders await in this über-luxurious resort where head chef Gilles Cazes marries French and Maldivian cuisines—guessakulu mahfuz (slow-roasted spiced lamb) comes to mind—in breathtaking sunsets over the Indian Ocean. Book your stay for the right to experience ichiban dining under the stars.

The Elephanta Island Restaurant - Mumbai, India

Mumbai’s meal scene has its share of glitz and glamour, but it’s The Elephanta Island Restaurant that steals the show. A hefty 45-minute ferry ride from Mumbai, the restaurant provides a culinarily-tinged escape for those willing to venture a bit off the beaten path. Feast on dolphin fish cooked in banana leaf and other Maharashtrian specialties while marvelling at the ancient rock-cut temple carvings adorning the island. Let the exotic locations evoke dining memories that will last a lifetime.

Das Stue - Berlin, Germany

Berlin’s Kurfürstendamm may represent the heart of the city, but it’s Das Stue that pumps lifeblood into the district. With its timeless elegance as your backdrop, the restaurant offers an international, cutting-edge menu ranging from Brittany lobster to Iberico pork. With the Berlin Zoo setting just beyond your window, a meal on the terrace makes the experience all the sweeter.

Concluding the culinary tour, it’s time to symbolically close the book on our adventure. These hotel restaurants serve as welcome respites, reflecting the place that food deserves in our lives. Go forth and explore the world through your taste buds - who needs a reason to travel when we’ve got exquisite cuisine at our fingertips?

Remember, even if you can’t quite snag a reservation at one of these destinations, taking small steps and seeking out hidden gems within your backyard can also lead to extraordinary culinary discoveries. Bon appétit.
