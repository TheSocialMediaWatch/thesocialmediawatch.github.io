Top Manual Transmission Cars Still Available in 2023


Manual transmission cars have always held a special place in the automotive world. They’re the machines that require skill, precision, and a keen sense of timing, making driving feel less like a mundane task and more like an art form. For enthusiasts, a manual gearbox represents a connection to the vehicle—a way to feel every gear change, every surge of power, and every twist of the road more intimately.

While the manual transmission has seen a decline in availability due to advances in automatic transmissions and changing consumer preferences, manufacturers have not entirely abandoned them. In 2023, there are still a handful of cars that proudly bear the manual gearbox as an option for drivers who cherish the old-school experience. Let’s explore some of the top manual transmission cars still available this year.

1. Mazda MX-5 Miata

The Mazda MX-5 Miata continues to be an icon in the world of manual transmission enthusiasts. This lightweight roadster has consistently delivered an exhilarating driving experience, and its six-speed manual gearbox is a testament to Mazda’s commitment to traditional driving pleasure.

The Miata’s short-throw shifter and precise engagement make gear changes feel magical. Paired with its responsive 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine, every drive becomes a masterpiece of balance and control. The Miata isn’t just a car; it’s a celebration of driving simplicity, reminding us why manuals still matter.

2. Ford Mustang

For decades, the Ford Mustang has been synonymous with muscle and performance. Fortunately, 2023 keeps alive the option for a manual transmission in this American icon. The six-speed manual available on the Mustang GT ensures a thrilling experience for those who crave control over every surge of power from its robust 5.0-liter V8 engine.

Rev-matching technology in newer models provides smoother transitions between gears, elevating the driving experience without taking away from the raw feel that manual enthusiasts love. Few vehicles can match the Mustang’s ability to combine nostalgia with modernity, making it a perfect choice for gearheads.

3. Honda Civic Si

The Honda Civic Si has long been a symbol of budget-friendly performance, and 2023 is no exception. This sporty sedan or coupe comes exclusively with a six-speed manual transmission—providing drivers with an engaging experience that automatic transmissions simply can’t replicate.

Boasting responsive handling and a turbocharged 1.5-liter engine, the Civic Si proves that fun doesn’t have to break the bank. The tactile feel of revving up through each gear in the Civic Si reinforces Honda’s commitment to catering to driving enthusiasts who seek thrills behind the wheel.

4. Subaru WRX

Few cars meld practicality and excitement as seamlessly as the Subaru WRX. With its rally-inspired heritage, standard all-wheel drive, and a manual transmission option, the WRX remains one of the best choices for performance enthusiasts seeking versatility.

Equipped with a six-speed manual gearbox, the WRX delivers an unparalleled connection to its turbocharged 2.4-liter flat-four engine. The manual shifts are smooth yet mechanical, echoing the gritty charm of rally driving on every road. Whether darting through sharp corners or driving in challenging conditions, the WRX proudly delivers a thrilling experience.

5. Porsche 911

One of the most revered sports cars in history, the Porsche 911 continues to embrace the manual gearbox for purists in 2023. Select models of the 911, like the Carrera T and GT variants, offer a seven-speed manual option, allowing drivers to extract every bit of performance at their fingertips.

Powered by Porsche’s precision engineering and responsive flat-six engines, the 911’s manual transmission transforms driving into a mechanical symphony. The tactile interaction not only improves driver engagement but also reminds us why Porsche remains one of the few luxury automakers committed to manual driving precision.

6. Toyota GR86

Toyota’s GR86 (produced in collaboration with Subaru’s BRZ) keeps the spirit of affordable, driver-focused sports cars alive. Featuring a six-speed manual transmission, the GR86 lets drivers control its naturally aspirated 2.4-liter Boxer engine with precision.

Every aspect of this rear-wheel-drive coupe is designed for pure, unadulterated driving enjoyment. The manual transmission makes every corner feel sharper and every acceleration more rewarding. Lightweight, nimble, and responsive, the GR86 proves that you don’t need extravagant power or price tags to have fun behind the wheel.

7. Volkswagen Golf GTI

The Volkswagen Golf GTI stands as one of the most beloved hot hatches ever created, and its availability with a six-speed manual in 2023 continues to solidify its status among driving enthusiasts. This compact car combines practicality with excitement, offering peppy performance and razor-sharp handling.

The GTI’s turbocharged 2.0-liter engine pairs wonderfully with the manual gearbox, delivering an engaging driving experience that’s hard to match in its segment. Whether navigating city streets or tearing through twisty back roads, the GTI proves itself as a worthy contender for those who appreciate a manual transmission.

8. BMW M3

The BMW M3 has always been a pillar of performance excellence, and its manual transmission offering for 2023 is a testament to BMW’s understanding of enthusiast needs. The six-speed manual option is available on the M3’s “Pure” trim, catering to drivers who demand not just luxury but also authentic driving connection.

With its high-revving inline-six engine producing explosive power, shifting through the gears in an M3 feels nothing short of exhilarating. The manual option elevates this sports sedan to an engaging and visceral driving experience, proving that even in the age of paddle shifters and high-tech automatics, manual transmissions still hold a vital place in BMW’s lineup.

9. Dodge Challenger

The Dodge Challenger, a muscle car classic that exudes retro charm, continues to offer a manual transmission option in 2023. Pairing the manual six-speed gearbox with the available 5.7-liter and 6.4-liter HEMI V8 engines creates an irresistible combination of raw power and authentic engagement.

From its roaring exhaust notes to the joyous feeling of commanding a V8 through manual shifts, the Challenger delivers on its promise of a visceral driving experience. If you’re seeking old-school muscle in a highly customizable package, the Challenger is ready to justify its place as a manual transmission gem.

10. Mini Cooper S

The Mini Cooper S offers a delightful driving experience with its available six-speed manual transmission in 2023. Known for its modern design and exhilarating dynamics, this compact car is perfect for drivers who want a fun and practical ride without compromising on the joy of shifting gears.

The turbocharged 2.0-liter engine is energetic, and the manual gearbox allows you to extract every ounce of its playful personality. Whether darting through city streets or taking on curvy country roads, the Mini Cooper S ensures that driving remains an adventure.

Final Thoughts

In an era where automatic transmissions dominate and electric vehicles are gaining ground, these manual transmission cars serve as reminders of what driving can truly be. They hold onto the thrill, the engagement, and the tactile connection to the road that many drivers crave. Manufacturers like Mazda, Ford, Honda, Porsche, Toyota, and BMW have stayed true to the philosophy that the driving experience is not just about getting from point A to B—it’s about how you get there.

If you’re someone who appreciates the artistry behind driving and wants to feel every gear shift on a visceral level, these cars are worth exploring. While the future may bring significant shifts in automotive technology, 2023 gives the manual gearbox one more year to shine and connect passionate drivers with the road.
