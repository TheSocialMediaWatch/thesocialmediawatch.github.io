The Best Crypto Podcasts to Follow in 2024


As the cryptocurrency landscape continues to evolve at an unprecedented pace, staying informed is crucial for investors, enthusiasts, and anyone curious about blockchain technology. One of the most engaging ways to keep up with the latest trends, insights, and expert opinions in the crypto space is through podcasts. In 2024, a diverse array of podcasts are available, each offering unique perspectives and valuable information. Here’s a roundup of the best crypto podcasts to follow this year.

1. The Pomp Podcast

Hosted by Anthony Pompliano, The Pomp Podcast has consistently been a go-to source for anyone interested in cryptocurrency and finance. With a keen focus on interviews with industry leaders, Pompliano’s podcast covers a wide range of topics, from Bitcoin investment strategies to discussions on decentralized finance (DeFi). Listeners appreciate the in-depth analysis and the variety of guests, which include entrepreneurs, investors, and thought leaders.

Why Listen?

  • Expert Insights: The guests bring a wealth of experience and knowledge, offering listeners firsthand insights into the crypto market.
  • Wide Range of Topics: The podcast doesn’t just focus on Bitcoin; it explores altcoins, NFTs, and the broader implications of blockchain technology.
  • Engaging Format: Pompliano’s conversational style makes complex topics accessible and entertaining.

2. The Bad Crypto Podcast

For those who enjoy a humorous approach to cryptocurrency discussions, The Bad Crypto Podcast is a perfect fit. Hosted by Joel Comm and Travis Wright, this podcast aims to demystify the world of crypto while providing entertaining commentary. The duo covers the latest news, trends, and developments in the crypto space, making it an enjoyable listen for both newbies and seasoned investors.

Why Listen?

  • Lighthearted Tone: The hosts’ humor makes the often-complex world of crypto more relatable.
  • Educational Content: Despite the comedic approach, the podcast offers valuable insights and educational content.
  • Community Engagement: The Bad Crypto Podcast fosters a strong community through social media interactions and listener engagement.

3. Unchained by Laura Shin

Laura Shin, a former Forbes journalist, hosts Unchained, a podcast that dives deep into the world of blockchain and cryptocurrencies. Known for its journalistic integrity, the podcast features interviews with experts and innovators in the field, exploring critical issues and the future of finance. Shin’s ability to ask tough questions and her commitment to uncovering the truth makes this podcast a must-listen.

Why Listen?

  • Investigative Approach: The podcast often tackles controversial topics, providing listeners with a well-rounded understanding of the issues.
  • Diverse Guest List: Shin interviews a broad range of guests, from developers to policy makers, offering multiple perspectives.
  • Educational Value: Each episode is packed with insights that can enhance your understanding of the crypto ecosystem.

4. CoinDesk Podcast Network

The CoinDesk Podcast Network offers a variety of shows that cater to different interests within the cryptocurrency space. From daily news updates to in-depth analysis, CoinDesk provides comprehensive coverage of the latest happenings in the crypto market. Their flagship show, “The Breakdown,” features expert commentary on the most pressing issues facing the industry today.

Why Listen?

  • Variety of Shows: With multiple podcasts under the CoinDesk umbrella, there’s something for everyone, whether you’re looking for news or deep dives.
  • Timely Updates: Stay informed about the latest market trends, regulatory changes, and technological advancements.
  • Credibility: As one of the leading news sources in the crypto space, CoinDesk’s podcasts are an authoritative source of information.

5. The Nifty Show

For those interested in NFTs and the growing world of digital collectibles, The Nifty Show is a fantastic resource. Hosted by Joel Comm and Travis Wright, this podcast explores the latest trends in the NFT space, featuring interviews with creators, collectors, and industry insiders. The Nifty Show provides listeners with insights into the evolving landscape of digital art and ownership.

Why Listen?

  • Focused Content: The podcast zeroes in on NFTs, making it an ideal choice for those specifically interested in this aspect of crypto.
  • Engaging Interviews: The hosts interview artists and creators, providing a behind-the-scenes look at the NFT ecosystem.
  • Community-Centric: The Nifty Show fosters a vibrant community around NFTs, encouraging listener participation and engagement.

6. The Investor’s Podcast Network: We Study Billionaires

While not exclusively focused on cryptocurrency, The Investor’s Podcast Network offers valuable insights into the investment strategies of some of the world’s wealthiest individuals. Episodes often feature discussions on digital assets, providing listeners with a broader understanding of how cryptocurrencies fit into the larger investment landscape.

Why Listen?

  • Investment Strategies: Learn how billionaires approach investments, including their views on cryptocurrencies.
  • Diverse Topics: The podcast covers a range of investment topics beyond crypto, making it a well-rounded resource.
  • Expert Guests: The show features interviews with leading investors and financial experts, providing valuable insights.

7. Crypto 101

Crypto 101 prides itself on being the ultimate beginner’s guide to cryptocurrency. Hosted by Matthew Aaron, the podcast covers everything from basic concepts to advanced trading strategies. Whether you’re just starting or looking to deepen your knowledge, Crypto 101 provides a wealth of information in a digestible format.

Why Listen?

  • Beginner-Friendly: The podcast breaks down complex topics into easy-to-understand segments, perfect for newcomers.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Topics range from Bitcoin fundamentals to emerging altcoins and market trends.
  • Engaging Format: The conversational style keeps listeners engaged while learning.

8. What Bitcoin Did

Hosted by Peter McCormack, What Bitcoin Did focuses primarily on Bitcoin, exploring its impact on the financial system and society as a whole. McCormack interviews a variety of guests, including developers, economists, and industry leaders, delving into the philosophical and technological aspects of Bitcoin.

Why Listen?

  • In-Depth Discussions: The podcast goes beyond surface-level topics, exploring the implications of Bitcoin on a global scale.
  • Diverse Perspectives: McCormack’s guests come from various backgrounds, offering unique insights into the Bitcoin phenomenon.
  • Philosophical Insights: The show often touches on the broader implications of Bitcoin, making it thought-provoking and engaging.

9. The Chain Reaction Podcast

The Chain Reaction Podcast, produced by the team at The Block, focuses on the latest trends and developments in the blockchain and cryptocurrency space. The hosts, Lucas Matney and Anita Ramaswamy, provide insightful commentary on market movements, regulatory changes, and technological advancements.

Why Listen?

  • Current Trends: Stay updated on the latest happenings in the crypto world with timely analysis.
  • Expert Commentary: The hosts often bring in industry experts to discuss pressing issues, providing valuable insights.
  • Informative Format: The podcast’s structured format allows for clear and concise discussions, making it easy to follow.

10. The Decrypt Daily

The Decrypt Daily is a news-centric podcast that covers the most important stories in the cryptocurrency space. Hosted by Matthew Aaron, the show provides daily updates on market trends, regulatory news, and technological developments. With a focus on current events, The Decrypt Daily ensures listeners stay informed about what’s happening in the crypto world.

Why Listen?

  • Daily Updates: Get your daily dose of crypto news in a quick and digestible format.
  • Timeliness: The podcast covers breaking news and emerging trends, keeping you in the loop.
  • Diverse Topics: Each episode explores various aspects of the crypto space, from market analysis to interviews with experts.

Conclusion

As the cryptocurrency landscape continues to evolve, podcasts have emerged as a vital resource for staying informed and engaged. Whether you’re a seasoned investor or a curious newcomer, the podcasts listed above offer valuable insights, expert opinions, and entertaining discussions. In 2024, make it a goal to tune into these shows and enhance your understanding of the dynamic world of cryptocurrency. By immersing yourself in the latest trends and developments, you’ll be better equipped to navigate this exciting and ever-changing space. Happy listening!
