How to Rock Neon Colors This Summer


Summer is the perfect time to experiment with bold and vibrant colors, and one trend that is making a comeback this year is neon. Neon colors are eye-catching, fun, and can instantly elevate any outfit. Whether you’re heading to a beach party or a music festival, incorporating neon colors into your summer wardrobe is a surefire way to stand out from the crowd. In this blog post, we will share some tips and tricks on how to rock neon colors this summer and make a fashion statement.

1. Start with Accessories

If you’re not ready to dive headfirst into neon, start by incorporating neon accessories into your outfits. A neon belt, handbag, or a pair of statement earrings can instantly add a pop of color to any ensemble. Pair a neon belt with a simple white dress or use a neon handbag to add a playful touch to your jeans and t-shirt combo. Accessories are a great way to ease into the neon trend without going overboard.

2. Mix and Match

Neon colors are all about being bold and unapologetic, so don’t be afraid to mix and match different neon shades. Experiment with pairing neon green with neon pink or neon yellow with neon orange. The key is to find a balance and create a cohesive look. You can do this by wearing one neon piece as the focal point and keeping the rest of your outfit neutral. For example, pair a neon top with white jeans or a neon skirt with a black top. This way, the neon color will be the star of the show.

3. Play with Prints

If you’re feeling adventurous, try incorporating neon colors into your prints. Opt for a neon floral dress or a bold neon striped top. Prints can add an extra dimension to your outfit and make your neon colors more interesting. Just remember to keep the rest of your accessories and makeup minimal to let the print shine.

4. Go Monochrome

For a sleek and modern look, try going monochrome with neon colors. Dressing head to toe in neon might sound intimidating, but when done right, it can create a striking and cohesive look. Choose a neon color that complements your skin tone and experiment with different shades and textures within that color. For example, pair a neon yellow blouse with a neon yellow skirt and finish the look with neon yellow heels. This monochromatic look will make a bold statement and turn heads wherever you go.

5. Incorporate Neon into Your Makeup

Don’t limit your neon colors to just your clothes; experiment with neon makeup as well. Add a pop of neon eyeshadow to your usual neutral makeup look or try a neon lip color for a bold and vibrant pout. Remember, the key is to keep the rest of your makeup minimal to let the neon color be the focal point.

6. Confidence is Key

Last but not least, the most important tip for rocking neon colors this summer is to have confidence. Neon colors are bold and attention-grabbing, so you need to own your look and embrace the vibrancy. Walk with confidence, and you’ll be sure to turn heads wherever you go.

In conclusion, neon colors are a fun and exciting trend to try this summer. Whether you start with accessories, mix and match different neon shades, play with prints, go monochrome, or incorporate neon into your makeup, there are endless ways to rock neon colors and make a fashion statement. Remember, confidence is key, so embrace the boldness and have fun experimenting with neon this summer!
