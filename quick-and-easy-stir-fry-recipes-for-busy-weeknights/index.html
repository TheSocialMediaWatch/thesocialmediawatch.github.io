Quick and Easy Stir-Fry Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Do you find yourself reaching for takeout menus more often than not? Well, fret no more! In this blog post, we will share with you some quick and easy stir-fry recipes that are perfect for busy weeknights. These recipes are not only delicious but also packed with flavor and nutrients. So, let’s dive right in!

Teriyaki Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, mix together soy sauce, teriyaki sauce, honey, and sesame oil. Set aside.
  2. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, and garlic. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are crisp-tender.
  4. Return the chicken to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  5. In a separate small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water until smooth. Pour the mixture into the skillet and stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Shrimp and Vegetable Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup snow peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • Green onions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet or wok, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and cook until pink and opaque. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet, add the onion, bell peppers, zucchini, snow peas, and garlic. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce and oyster sauce. Pour the mixture into the skillet and stir well.
  4. In a separate small bowl, mix together cornstarch and water until smooth. Add the mixture to the skillet and stir until the sauce thickens.
  5. Return the shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat everything evenly.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with green onions. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Beef and Broccoli Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 head broccoli, cut into florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix together soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the beef and cook until browned. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic, broccoli, bell pepper, and mushrooms. Stir-fry for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  4. Return the beef to the skillet and pour in the sauce mixture. Stir well to coat everything evenly.
  5. Drizzle sesame oil over the stir-fry and toss to combine.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds. Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

Conclusion

These quick and easy stir-fry recipes are perfect for those busy weeknights when you want a delicious and healthy meal without spending hours in the kitchen. With just a few simple ingredients and minimal prep time, you can whip up a flavorful stir-fry that will satisfy your taste buds and keep you fueled throughout the evening. So, why not give these recipes a try? Your taste buds and your schedule will thank you!
