10 Easy Cocktails You Can Make with Just Three Ingredients


Are you tired of rummaging through your bar for an assortment of mixers and garnishes to create the perfect cocktail? Look no further! With just three ingredients, you can craft a variety of delicious and refreshing drinks that are sure to impress your friends and family. From classic combinations to modern twists, we've got you covered with these 10 easy cocktails that require only three ingredients.

  1. Classic Gin and Tonic

1 1/2 oz gin 4 oz tonic water Lime wedge

This timeless cocktail is a staple for a reason. The crisp, clean taste of gin pairs perfectly with the bitter sweetness of tonic water, all tied together with a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Simply pour the gin over ice, top with tonic, and garnish with a lime wedge. 2. Vodka Cranberry Spritzer

1 1/2 oz vodka 4 oz cranberry juice Splash of sparkling water

This refreshing drink is perfect for warm weather. The tartness of the cranberry juice is balanced by the crispness of the vodka, all topped off with a splash of sparkling water. Simply combine the vodka and cranberry juice in a glass filled with ice, and top with sparkling water. 3. Rum and Coke

1 1/2 oz rum 4 oz Coca-Cola Lime wedge

This classic combination is a staple for a reason. The rich, smooth taste of rum is perfectly balanced by the sweetness of Coca-Cola, all tied together with a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Simply pour the rum over ice, top with Coke, and garnish with a lime wedge. 4. Whiskey Sour

1 1/2 oz whiskey 3/4 oz lemon juice 1/2 oz simple syrup

This tangy cocktail is a twist on the classic whiskey sour. The smooth, rich taste of whiskey is balanced by the tartness of lemon juice and the sweetness of simple syrup. Simply combine the whiskey, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice, and shake until chilled. 5. Mojito

1 1/2 oz rum 1 oz lime juice Handful of mint leaves

This refreshing cocktail is perfect for warm weather. The crisp, clean taste of rum is balanced by the tartness of lime juice and the freshness of mint leaves. Simply muddle the mint leaves in a glass, add the rum and lime juice, and top with ice. 6. Dark 'N' Stormy

1 1/2 oz dark rum 4 oz ginger beer Lime wedge

This spicy cocktail is perfect for those who like a little kick. The rich, smooth taste of dark rum is balanced by the spiciness of ginger beer, all tied together with a squeeze of fresh lime juice. Simply pour the rum over ice, top with ginger beer, and garnish with a lime wedge. 7. Tequila Sunrise

1 1/2 oz tequila 4 oz orange juice Splash of grenadine

This classic cocktail is a staple for a reason. The smooth, rich taste of tequila is balanced by the sweetness of orange juice and the tartness of grenadine. Simply combine the tequila and orange juice in a glass filled with ice, and add a splash of grenadine. 8. Pimm's Cup

1 1/2 oz Pimm's No. 1 4 oz lemon-lime soda Cucumber slice

This refreshing cocktail is perfect for warm weather. The crisp, clean taste of Pimm's No. 1 is balanced by the tartness of lemon-lime soda and the freshness of cucumber. Simply combine the Pimm's No. 1 and lemon-lime soda in a glass filled with ice, and garnish with a cucumber slice. 9. Gin Fizz

1 1/2 oz gin 1 oz lemon juice 1/2 oz simple syrup

This classic cocktail is a twist on the classic gin and tonic. The smooth, rich taste of gin is balanced by the tartness of lemon juice and the sweetness of simple syrup. Simply combine the gin, lemon juice, and simple syrup in a shaker filled with ice, and shake until chilled. 10. Vodka Tonic with a Twist

1 1/2 oz vodka 4 oz tonic water Splash of grapefruit juice

This refreshing cocktail is a twist on the classic vodka tonic. The crisp, clean taste of vodka is balanced by the tartness of grapefruit juice and the bitterness of tonic water. Simply combine the vodka and grapefruit juice in a glass filled with ice, and top with tonic water. These 10 easy cocktails are perfect for anyone looking to mix up a drink without breaking the bank or rummaging through their bar for an assortment of mixers and garnishes. With just three ingredients, you can create a variety of delicious and refreshing drinks that are sure to impress your friends and family. So next time you're in the mood for a cocktail, try one of these simple recipes and see what you're missing out on!
