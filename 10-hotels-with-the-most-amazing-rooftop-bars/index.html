10 Hotels with the Most Amazing Rooftop Bars


In the world of travel and hospitality, rooftop bars have transcended from being mere luxury add-ons to iconic destinations in their own right. Whether it is the glittering cityscape views, the energetic vibe, or the expertly crafted cocktails served with a side of sunset, these elevated spaces offer unforgettable experiences. Here, we dive into ten hotels that boast some of the most amazing rooftop bars you can visit, perfect for anyone looking to sip, socialize, and soak up impressive panoramas.

1. The Standard, Downtown LA – Skybar

At the heart of Los Angeles, The Standard, Downtown LA offers a rooftop bar experience that’s both trendy and glamorous. Skybar perches 16 floors above the bustling streets, providing 360-degree views of downtown Los Angeles. The design merges sleek modern aesthetics with casual comfort—ideal for relaxing afternoons or lively nights. Craft cocktails are served with precision, featuring local ingredients and an adventurous mixology approach. On weekends, expect DJ sets and a vibrant crowd that mirrors LA’s eclectic culture.

2. The Hoxton, Amsterdam – Floor 17

Amsterdam’s skyline pairs perfectly with the cool, contemporary vibe of The Hoxton’s rooftop bar, Floor 17. Located right in the bustling city center, this bar offers panoramic views of historic canal houses and modern architecture alike. Its ambiance is laid-back, filled with comfortable seating and greenery, giving a garden-on-the-roof feel. The cocktail menu often includes Dutch twists on classics, paired with light bites that showcase local flavors. This rooftop is an excellent spot for both sunset watching and late-night drinks.

3. Hotel Skykitchen, Tokyo – Rooftop Lounge

In the heart of Tokyo’s dynamic Shibuya district, Hotel Skykitchen’s Rooftop Lounge is a tranquil oasis above the urban hustle. The bar specializes in minimalist Japanese design, using natural wood and soft lighting to create an intimate atmosphere. Its drink selection features premium sake, innovative sake cocktails, and an assortment of rare whiskies. Guests often admire Cherry Blossom views in spring or neon city lights in the nocturnal hours, making it a renowned hotspot for both locals and travelers seeking an authentic experience.

4. The Peninsula, Bangkok – Vertigo

Bangkok’s tropical climate is best enjoyed on a rooftop, and The Peninsula’s Vertigo bar delivers a breathtaking open-air experience. This bar sits 61 stories above the Chao Phraya River, offering stunning views of the city’s sprawling skyline and river traffic below. Vertigo features an extensive wine list and signature cocktails, expertly crafted to complement its gourmet menu. The ambiance is upscale yet relaxed, with subtle lighting and sleek furnishings that enhance romantic evenings or celebratory occasions.

5. Hotel 50 Bowery, New York – Northside

Hotel 50 Bowery presents one of New York City’s most vibrant rooftop bars: Northside. Situated in Chinatown, it offers a unique perspective of the city’s lower Manhattan skyline, including the Brooklyn Bridge and One World Trade Center. The design blends industrial chic with cozy lounges, and the bar serves inventive cocktails that often incorporate Asian-inspired flavors. Live music, seasonal events, and a lively crowd fuel an energetic atmosphere that captures New York’s spirit.

6. W Barcelona – Eclipse Bar

The iconic sail-shaped architecture of W Barcelona is matched only by the height and grandeur of its Eclipse Bar. Located on the 26th floor, this rooftop bar overlooks the Mediterranean and offers sweeping views of the Barcelona skyline. It’s a hotspot for both tourists and locals, known for its modern décor, live DJs, and a menu that features refreshing cocktails and tapas. The combination of sea breeze, sunsets, and pulsating beats makes it a must-visit for nightlife aficionados.

7. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore – Cé La Vi

Perched atop one of Singapore’s most famous landmarks, the three-towered Marina Bay Sands, Cé La Vi is a rooftop bar that defines luxury. The infinity pool views alongside the city’s futuristic skyline create an almost surreal backdrop. Guests enjoy a blend of Asian-inspired dishes and imaginative cocktails crafted with tropical fruits and premium spirits. The atmosphere is chic and sophisticated, attracting an international crowd looking for glamorous nights out in one of Asia’s busiest cities.

8. The Ned, London – The Roof Terrace

The Ned in London offers multiple rooftop bars, but its Roof Terrace stands out for its blend of historic charm and urban sophistication. Situated in the City of London, it boasts a sun-drenched space during the day and an illuminated haven at night. Vines and cozy seating create a garden vibe amid the skyscrapers. The cocktail menu is broad, showcasing classic British favorites alongside global inspirations, making it a superb place to unwind after a day’s work or sightseeing.

9. Hotel Emiliano, São Paulo – Emiliano Rooftop Lounge

Brazilian flair reaches new heights at Hotel Emiliano’s Rooftop Lounge. This bar embraces São Paulo’s vibrant cultural scene with a combination of contemporary luxury and tropical aesthetics. The rooftop offers sweeping views of the city’s iconic skyline, enhanced by lush plantings and modern minimalist furniture. Drinks highlight Brazilian ingredients, featuring cachaça in many inventive cocktails. This location is perfect for those wanting to experience São Paulo’s energy with a touch of elegance.

10. The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong – Ozone

Extremely high up on the 118th floor, Ozone at The Ritz-Carlton, Hong Kong is one of the world’s highest rooftop bars. The futuristic interior design utilizes LED lighting and sleek surfaces, matching the breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour and Hong Kong’s towering skyscrapers. The bar offers a vast selection of creative cocktails, premium champagne, and gourmet bar snacks. The atmosphere combines cosmopolitan glamour with an unmatched sense of verticality, making it an exceptional place for a night out.

Why Rooftop Bars Are More Than Just a Trend

Rooftop bars transform any hotel stay into a sensory experience that blends taste, sight, and social connection. They provide an elevated perspective not only physically but also culturally, as many offer region-specific drinks and themes, reflecting the spirit of their cities.Beyond stunning visuals, these bars create social hubs where locals and travelers mingle, often accompanied by live music, DJ sets, or curated events.

Whether you are looking for a quiet evening with spectacular sunsets or a vibrant party scene above the city lights, these rooftop bars offer a variety of moods and styles. Their unique locations atop architectural gems enhance the experience, making every visit memorable.

Tips for Enjoying Rooftop Bars at Hotels

  • Timing is key: Arrive before sunset to secure the best views and watch daylight transition into night.
  • Dress code: Many rooftop bars, especially in upscale hotels, enforce dress codes. Smart casual is usually a safe bet.
  • Reservations: Popular rooftops can fill up quickly, especially on weekends or holidays. Booking ahead is recommended.
  • Try local flavors: Look for signature cocktails or beverages that highlight local ingredients or mixology traditions.
  • Mind the weather: Outdoor rooftop bars can be affected by weather; check forecasts in advance and dress accordingly.

Exploring the world’s finest rooftop bars at top hotels isn’t just an activity; it’s a journey through architecture, flavor, and atmosphere woven deeply into the fabric of each city. These ten destinations are some of the finest examples where hospitality meets skyline grandeur, giving anyone fortunate enough to visit a truly elevated experience. Next time you check into a hotel, make sure to ask if there’s a rooftop bar—you might just discover the highlight of your trip.
