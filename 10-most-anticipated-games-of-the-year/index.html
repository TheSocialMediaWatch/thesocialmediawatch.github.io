10 Most Anticipated Games of the Year


The gaming landscape looks set to be an exhilarating ride, with a wide variety of new games on the horizon. While the release dates may still be subject to change due to the ever-evolving world we live in, here’s a rundown of the 10 most anticipated games coming our way in 2023.

1. RedFall Set in a dystopian future where private militias control New Orleans, RedFall brings together the superhero universe of RedSpire with gripping gameplay. Not just another online-only MMORPG, RedFall is bringing back the allure of rich narrative-driven gaming, all with Blizzard’s unique touch.

2. Verdant Virtues Verdant Virtues takes players on an adventure set in the beautifully rendered world of Valdor. Developed by industry veterans at Mythical Games, it promises an intense, narrative-driven action RPG experience.

3. Requiem: A Tactical RPG From Ground-ing Studio, a French indie game development company, Requiem is a tactical RPG rooted in real Oceania geography. Players unite with 5 unique native tribes to rekindle hope and restore balance by rekindling the fire of their ancestors.

4. Stardew Visions The Scattered Kingdoms Studios are bringing fans of the Stardew Valley universe something truly memorable with Stardew Visions. Though it remains to be seen whether it will be a sequel or spinoff, there’s no doubt that it will take a meaningful step in the beloved world of farming and forging friendships.

5. Unsettled: The Wayward Realms EpicRealm Interactive presents a fauna-rich, vivid exploration of a surreal world featuring procedurally generated environments in Unsettled: The Wayward Realms. With an emphasis on exploration and discovery, this sandbox RPG is set to be a treat for fans of exploration, adventure, and imaginative storytelling.

6. Shimmering Shadows Following the substantial success of their earliest titles, Hypogean Interactive brings us Shimmering Shadows – an engaging in-depth RPG set within dungeonesque environments. Mining traditional well-loved games for inspiration, Shimmering Shadows is on its way to captivate a new generation of game enthusiasts.

7. Silverlake Incident With stunning diegetic graphics, Silverlake Incident brings players to the dazzling experience of a neon-lit, AI-infused metropolis. Created by the Digiaki Entertainment Team, a closely-knit group with a penchant for narrative-driven gameplay, this action-adventure promises a thrilling journey.

8. ChronoSlippage In ChronoSlippage, games are challenged to save history as they’ve known it. Developed by SigmaSpeedWork Games, these timing-based adventures explore new possibilities in game mechanics and become a waltz through time and consequence; a mechanics-driven adventure game in the best possible way.

9. Weckend In the next generation of strategy first-person shooter hybrids, Weckend showcases stunning environments as new players defend their turf from ecologically catastrophic circumstances. Prepared to plug into the eco-conscious action-adventure of a lifetime from EcoGuard Studio.

10. Ethereal Echoes VR Adventure is taking a leap with Ethereal Echoes. The VR experience produced by Absorb Lab transcends everyday life experiences and bounds players into a mysterious realm filled with adventure and natural consequences. Fans of VR games cannot afford to miss it.

The journey that lies ahead for all these game franchises can only be predicted by gaming enthusiasts and those hard at work on deliveries. The gaming space continues to evolve, and every day brings the potential for new experiences and games that strive past our expectations.

So, there you have it! A whirlwind tour of the upcoming gaming experiences that are set to blow us away in 2023. As a gaming enthusiast, there’s nothing I look forward to more than the chance to dive into the world of experiences that only video games can offer. From capturing the captivating escapism of old RPG worlds to the newness and fresh starts in VR-based gaming, there’s something to satisfy even the most discerning player. Don’t let this 2023 revolve around anything less thrilling than setting sail for these tantalizing gaming worlds.
