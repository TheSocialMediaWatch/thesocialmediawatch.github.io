2023 Beach Reads: The 20 Hottest Summer Reads for Bookworms


Ah, summer! The season for sun-kissed skin, ice-cold lemonades, and most importantly – diving into a captivating novel. As we embark on the year 2023, it’s time to dust off our beach reads and explore the 20 hottest summer reads that will surely tantalize book lovers everywhere. Get ready to soak up some sun and immerse yourself in these unforgettable stories.

  1. The Push by Ashley Audrain

Do you dare to face the raw and honest portrayal of motherhood in this gripping psychological thriller? Evoking the essence of Emma Jane Unsworth’s “Animals”, The Push is a heart- wrenching exploration of the challenges of balancing work, relationships and motherhood. With emotions as heated as the sun’s rays, The Push is one read you won’t want to miss this summer.

  1. Lessons in Chemistry by Bonnie Garmus

Meet Elizabeth Zott, a brilliant yet underestimated chemist. Despite a lack of faith from those around her, she persists through setbacks and betrayal to ultimately save the world. With a dash of humor, intrigue and romance, this delightful read is perfect for a beach day.

  1. The Sunny Side Up Book Club by Milly Johnson

Let’s get cozy with a little slice of the small-town charm this season. Welcome to Belbury, where The Sunny Side Up Book Club isn’t just about discussing favorite reads – it’s about embracing love, life, and all the drama that follows. Dive into the pages and live the charm of a quaint English town through the warm-hearted stories in this novel.

  1. Funeral Drinks by Monique Roffey

Monique Roffey’s Funeral Drinks delves into themes of friendship, love, and loss, set against the backdrop of a storm-stricken Trinidad. Through her vivid and poetic storytelling, Roffey creates an unforgettable tale that will leave you breathless.

  1. The Last Things He Did by Sara Novic

Mystery, intrigue, and suspense define Sara Novic’s gripping debut novel, The Last Things He Did. Mason, the enigmatic and estranged nephew, returns to his hometown to uncover the hidden secrets. Seph, the devastated murder victim’s wife, delves into her husband’s past to find the truth.

  1. Twist of Fate by Ellen Michener

Make way for a masterful, riveting tale of fate, family, and love. The twists and turns of life can lead us to unexpected places. Twist of Fate envisions a heartwarming journey about love overcoming generations of betrayal as two families intertwine.

  1. The Book of Wild Swimming by Wild Swim Sisters

Imagine yourself on a serene, sunlit day, exploring the sheltered coves of Cornwall or tracing the Loch Ness monster’s legendary footsteps. Dive into the depths and enjoy an invigorating dip with The Wild Swim Sisters. Delve into the serenity that water offers in this luxurious read.

  1. American Ghost by Hannah Georgas

Get ready to discover a ghost story like none other! After inheriting her grandmother’s ancestral home, Hannah Georgas uncovers secrets of her family’s dark past. Unraveling the twisted, tangled webs of lies that she’s buried deep within herself, she succeeds in exorcising her inner demons.

  1. No One Is Talking About This by Patricia Lockwood

Hi Instagram, Patricia Lockwood is back with a literary gift for those who dare to explore and discuss the newly emerging ethos of the world, grounded by the frenzied, unabashed nature of online existence.

  1. Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir

Andy Weir whisks readers away to a whole new galaxy with this irresistible sci-fi ride. When an amnesiac astronaut wakes up on a miniature spaceship without any memory of his mission, he must gather his wits and find his way back.

  1. Glitter Girl: A Social Climbing Story by Emily Goligoski

Experience the true tale of a 20-something unrestrained social climber turning her aspirations of glamour and fame into a fabulous freelance brand consultant. Get ready to embark on a thrilling ride in the glitz, glamour, and all that top-tier living has to offer.

  1. The Twenty-Ninth Chance by Todd Johnson

Love, faith, and baseball intertwine in this compelling novel of redemption. Will Blake, a former slugger, gets a second chance at life and love after being released from prison. As he battles back to the major leagues and seeks forgiveness from his young daughter, his journey unfolds in this inspiring read.

  1. The Honeymoon by David Levitz

Enter the world of marriage – love, lust, promises of forever – and take a trip to a secluded Caribbean resort with couple Margaret and Michael. Together, the duo navigates the boundary between desire and desperation in David Levitz’s chilling debut novel.

  1. Under the Rainbow by Claire Hennessy

Leave behind the dreary, rainy weather and venture under the Rainbow, where love blossoms unexpectedly. Following the story of Iris, a disillusioned teenager, and the spirited, unconventional Mary, this heartwarming read takes readers on a journey of self-discovery.

  1. Transcendent Kingdom by Yaa Gyasi

Connect with the emotional intensity of Yaa Gyasi’s sophomore novel, Transcendent Kingdom. Following the intertwined narratives of Gifty, a brilliant PhD student of neuroscience, and her relationship with family, faith, and opiates, Gyasi’s masterful storytelling leads to an unforgettable, poignant experience.

  1. City of Beverly Hills by Emmy Grace

This delightful tale by Emmy Grace takes us on a rollercoaster ride through the hearts and minds of three magically linked women in Beverly Hills. Love, friendship, and finding oneself in the world of luxury, will leave you wanting more.

  1. The Water Dancer by Ta-Nehisi Coates

Experience the power of ancestral connections and self-belief in this thought-provoking tale of a young man named Hiram Fuller, born to enslaved parents, gifted with the extraordinary ability of “conduction” - a power that connects memories and empowers movement.

  1. Diaspora by Yaa Gyasi

Embark on a breathtaking journey across continents, spanning centuries, in this unforgettable debut novel. A thought-provoking exploration of the connections between Africa, America, Britain and Jamaica, Diaspora is a testament to Yaa Gyasi’s remarkable gift for storytelling.

  1. Operation Cinderella by Joan Hess

Dive into this delightful cozy mystery set in Victorian-era England, where petite Evangeline Parrish, destined for spinsterhood, employs an unconventional method for nabbing the exotic Prince Karim as her suitor.

  1. A Creature of Moonlight by Rebecca K. Collins

Enter the magnificent realms of A Creature of Moonlight – a story that revolves around the awakening of ancient fae magic as Landry, a misfit artist, encounters a creature of wild enchantment. This radiant and imaginative tale sweeps readers into a captivating world of wonder and romance.

As the sun beats down on our golden shores, we invite you to heave your favorite 2023 beach read, turn the pages, and embark on these exhilarating adventures. Bookmark your favorites; who knows — you might be introduced to the next Great American Novel.

From romances and mysteries to thrilling fantasies, these 20 beach reads promise to be the hottest summer reads for bookworms in the year 2023, leaving you with fascinating memories until the golden sands beckon again. Happy reading!
