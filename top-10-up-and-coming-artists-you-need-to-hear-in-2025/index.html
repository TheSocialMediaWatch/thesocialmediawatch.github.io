Top 10 Up-and-Coming Artists You Need to Hear in 2025


As the music industry constantly evolves, new talent emerges every year, bringing fresh sounds and perspectives to the global stage. While some established names continue to dominate the charts, there’s a whole wave of rising stars in 2025 who are ready to shake things up and make their mark. From indie rock to experimental pop, these up-and-coming artists are showcasing incredible creativity and potential. Here are the top 10 artists you need to hear in 2025.

1. Juno Bliss

Juno Bliss is one of the most exciting new talents to emerge from the underground music scene. With a sound that blends ethereal dream-pop, ambient textures, and soft R&B, Juno’s music transports listeners to a different world. Her debut EP Falling Without Wings has garnered attention for its intimate lyrics and smooth production, which beautifully complement her mesmerizing voice.

Juno Bliss’ music often explores themes of love, loss, and personal growth. She’s been compared to artists like Clairo and FKA twigs, but her unique voice and ability to create captivating atmospheres set her apart. As she gains more traction in 2025, Juno is expected to dominate indie music charts and festivals.

2. Kai Odyssey

If you haven’t heard of Kai Odyssey yet, it’s time to pay attention. This genre-blending artist fuses alternative rock with electronic elements and rap, creating a sound that’s both raw and polished. His debut album Neon Shadow was released in late 2024 and has already received rave reviews from music critics. With its infectious hooks and introspective lyrics, Kai’s music has the power to draw in listeners from all corners of the musical spectrum.

Known for his charismatic stage presence and deep, thought-provoking lyrics, Kai Odyssey is a force to be reckoned with. In 2025, expect to see him on major festival lineups and climbing the charts.

3. Livia Hale

Livia Hale is a rising pop artist with a unique voice that blends contemporary pop with soulful undertones. Her debut single Lost in Time became an instant favorite, thanks to its catchy chorus and relatable lyrics. Livia’s music is a blend of vulnerability and strength, often exploring themes of self-empowerment, love, and inner struggle.

Her ability to tell compelling stories through her songs has caught the attention of both fans and music industry professionals. Livia Hale’s debut album is slated for release in 2025, and with her ever-growing fanbase, she’s poised to become one of pop music’s brightest stars.

4. Theo Vale

Theo Vale is an alternative artist whose melancholic style is winning over fans worldwide. His music effortlessly blends elements of folk, indie, and ambient soundscapes. His voice has been described as hauntingly beautiful, and his songwriting is deeply poetic. Theo Vale’s debut album Echoes in the Dark explores themes of isolation, nostalgia, and the passage of time, making it a compelling listen for those who enjoy introspective music.

In 2025, Theo Vale is expected to continue building his following, with his unique sound making him a standout in the indie music scene. His live performances, which are known for their emotional intensity, are also a must-see.

5. Zara Knight

Zara Knight’s journey to becoming a music sensation is a testament to the power of perseverance and dedication. Growing up in a small town, Zara taught herself how to play multiple instruments and began recording her own music in her bedroom. Her sound blends indie rock, pop, and electronic influences, creating a style that is both innovative and accessible.

Her debut album Wasted Youth is a testament to her raw talent, featuring catchy, upbeat tracks alongside more introspective, slower ballads. Zara’s music is known for its infectious energy and unapologetic honesty, making her a voice for a generation of listeners seeking authenticity.

6. Eli Storm

Eli Storm is an experimental pop artist who’s redefining what it means to create catchy, thought-provoking music. With a penchant for blending different genres—pop, electronic, jazz, and even classical—Eli’s music is a breath of fresh air. His single Rising Tide captivated listeners with its complex production, shifting time signatures, and unconventional song structure.

In 2025, Eli Storm is expected to push the boundaries of modern pop music even further, exploring new ways to experiment with sound and narrative. If you’re into innovative artists who aren’t afraid to break the rules, Eli Storm is one to watch.

7. Rhea Voss

Rhea Voss has quickly become one of the most talked-about names in the indie folk scene. Her powerful voice and evocative songwriting have earned her comparisons to artists like Phoebe Bridgers and Julien Baker. Rhea’s debut album Fires We Built was a breakthrough, showcasing her ability to craft hauntingly beautiful melodies that resonate deeply with listeners.

Her sound is both delicate and strong, with lyrics that capture the nuances of heartbreak, growth, and the beauty of the natural world. Rhea’s authenticity and raw emotion in her music have made her a favorite among critics and fans alike. With a busy 2025 tour schedule, Rhea Voss is undoubtedly on her way to becoming a household name.

8. Savannah Blue

Savannah Blue is a rising star in the world of alternative R&B. With her smooth, sultry voice and jazzy production, Savannah’s music is a mix of old-school vibes and modern sensibilities. Her debut album Midnight Reverie introduced her as a fresh new voice in R&B, with tracks that feature hypnotic rhythms and dreamy melodies.

Her unique approach to R&B, which incorporates elements of soul, jazz, and electronic music, has caught the attention of both mainstream and underground music fans. As she continues to develop her sound, Savannah Blue is on track to become a major influence in the genre in 2025 and beyond.

9. Jasper Quinlan

Jasper Quinlan is an artist whose music blends electronic and indie pop with a touch of folk. His soothing vocals and intricate arrangements have earned him a growing fanbase. His debut album Floating Through the Storm received widespread praise for its thoughtful lyrics and beautifully layered soundscapes. Jasper’s music often explores themes of self-discovery, change, and the pursuit of happiness.

What sets Jasper apart from other up-and-coming artists is his ability to merge different genres seamlessly, creating music that feels both familiar and innovative. In 2025, Jasper Quinlan is expected to continue to build on his success, releasing more music that pushes the boundaries of indie pop.

10. Ryder Knight

Ryder Knight is an indie rock artist who’s been steadily gaining attention for his electrifying performances and powerful songwriting. His debut album Waves of Grey was a raw, unapologetic exploration of life’s struggles, with themes of depression, love, and personal growth. Ryder’s music is known for its energetic, guitar-driven sound and emotionally charged lyrics.

With a growing fanbase and critical acclaim, Ryder Knight is poised to make big waves in 2025. His electrifying live shows and catchy, anthemic tracks are sure to make him a household name in the years to come.

Conclusion

2025 is shaping up to be a groundbreaking year for music, with these up-and-coming artists leading the charge. From the ethereal sounds of Juno Bliss to the genre-defying music of Eli Storm, each of these artists brings something unique to the table. Whether you’re a fan of pop, rock, R&B, or experimental music, there’s something in this list for everyone.

As these artists continue to evolve and grow in their careers, it’s safe to say that they will be at the forefront of the music scene for years to come. Keep an eye on them, because their best work is yet to come.
