Best Hotels for a Long-Term Stay or Extended Vacation


When it comes to planning an extended vacation or a long-term stay, choosing the right accommodation is crucial. The comfort, amenities, and convenience of your stay can make or break the experience. Whether you’re traveling for work, leisure, or simply taking some time off to explore new destinations, it’s important to find a hotel that meets your needs for both short and long durations.

Unlike typical short-term stays, long-term accommodations require extra considerations, such as more space, home-like amenities, and easy access to local services. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best hotels for long-term stays, highlighting features, services, and locations that will help make your extended stay unforgettable.

Key Features to Look for in Long-Term Stay Hotels

Before diving into our list of recommended hotels, let’s first explore the key features that you should prioritize when choosing a hotel for an extended stay.

1. Spacious Rooms and Suites

Unlike standard hotel rooms, long-term stays call for more space. Look for hotels offering larger suites or studio apartments with separate living, dining, and sleeping areas. A spacious room allows you to spread out and live more comfortably during your extended stay.

2. Full Kitchen or Kitchenette

Having access to a kitchen or kitchenette is a game-changer. Whether you’re preparing a quick meal, cooking a home-cooked feast, or simply storing snacks, the ability to make your own meals can save you time and money. Hotels with full kitchens often provide a refrigerator, microwave, stovetop, and basic cookware.

3. Laundry Facilities

During a long-term stay, access to laundry facilities is a necessity. Many extended-stay hotels offer on-site laundry rooms or in-room washers and dryers, which makes it easy to take care of laundry without having to leave the property. This is especially important if you’re staying for a month or more.

4. Business Services and High-Speed Internet

For those traveling for work or mixing business with leisure, reliable internet and business amenities are essential. Hotels that offer high-speed internet, business centers, and meeting rooms can help you stay productive throughout your stay.

5. Pet-Friendly Options

If you’re bringing a pet along, you’ll need to find a hotel that accommodates animals. Many extended-stay hotels have pet-friendly policies, allowing you to travel with your furry friend without any hassle.

6. On-Site Amenities and Services

Extended stays can sometimes feel isolating, especially if you’re staying in a new city for an extended period. Hotels with on-site amenities such as a fitness center, pool, restaurant, or bar can help you unwind and stay active during your downtime. Some hotels also offer additional services like daily housekeeping, concierge, and shuttle services, which can make your stay more convenient and enjoyable.

Top Hotels for Long-Term Stays

Here are some of the best hotels that offer great services and accommodations for those planning a long-term stay or extended vacation.

1. Residence Inn by Marriott

With a reputation for providing a comfortable and home-like atmosphere, Residence Inn by Marriott is a popular choice for long-term stays. The hotel chain offers spacious suites equipped with kitchens, separate living areas, and plenty of storage space. Most locations also offer free breakfast and evening socials, making it easy to meet other guests or unwind after a long day.

Residence Inn also has a variety of locations across major cities, making it ideal for travelers who may need to move around or explore different destinations. Many properties feature pet-friendly policies and extended stay discounts, making it a great option for both business and leisure travelers.

2. Homewood Suites by Hilton

Homewood Suites by Hilton is another excellent choice for extended stays. The hotel offers all the necessary amenities for long-term stays, including fully equipped kitchens, spacious rooms, and complimentary breakfast. For those looking to relax and recharge, Homewood Suites offers evening social hours where guests can enjoy snacks and drinks in a casual environment.

In addition to the standard amenities, Homewood Suites offers a 24-hour fitness center and access to on-site laundry facilities. Its strategic locations near major business districts and tourist attractions make it a great choice for both corporate travelers and vacationers.

3. Staybridge Suites

Staybridge Suites provides a warm, welcoming environment for those planning an extended stay. The hotel chain’s spacious suites are equipped with fully functional kitchens, including a full-sized refrigerator, microwave, and stovetop. For those who prefer not to cook, Staybridge Suites offers a free breakfast buffet and an evening social where guests can enjoy light dinner and drinks.

Staybridge Suites also offers a variety of services, such as weekly housekeeping, free Wi-Fi, and an on-site fitness center. Many Staybridge Suites properties are located near key business centers, making it an ideal choice for those in town for work.

4. The Langham Hotels and Resorts

If you’re looking for luxury during your extended stay, The Langham Hotels and Resorts offer unparalleled service and amenities. Known for its high-end accommodations, the Langham provides both short-term and long-term guests with lavish suites that come with fully equipped kitchens, spacious living areas, and elegant interiors.

Long-term guests at The Langham enjoy an array of services, including daily housekeeping, 24-hour concierge, and a range of on-site dining options. The hotels also offer wellness amenities such as spas and fitness centers to help guests stay relaxed and rejuvenated throughout their stay. Locations in cities like London, New York, and Los Angeles offer convenient access to cultural landmarks, shopping, and fine dining.

5. Extended Suites by Wyndham

For those who need affordable, no-frills accommodation for an extended stay, Extended Suites by Wyndham is a great choice. The hotel offers clean, comfortable rooms with essential amenities such as a microwave, refrigerator, and coffee maker. Many locations also provide kitchenettes for longer stays, which is perfect for guests looking to prepare their own meals.

While the hotel may not offer the same level of luxury as some other options, it provides excellent value for money. Extended Suites is an ideal option for travelers on a budget or those staying for a longer duration who don’t require extra amenities but still need a comfortable space to call home during their stay.

6. The Westin Hotels & Resorts

The Westin Hotels & Resorts caters to guests seeking both luxury and long-term comfort. Known for their wellness-focused services, The Westin offers amenities that make it easy to relax and stay healthy while on the road. Spacious rooms, well-designed kitchens, and stylish living areas make it ideal for guests who need a comfortable, functional space for their extended stay.

In addition to its accommodations, The Westin provides services such as a 24-hour fitness center, rejuvenating spa treatments, and gourmet dining options. Long-term guests can also benefit from The Westin’s loyalty program, which rewards frequent travelers with special perks, discounts, and upgrades.

7. Citadines Apart’hotels

For travelers who prefer a more independent experience, Citadines Apart’hotels are the perfect blend of hotel services and apartment-style living. With locations across Europe, Asia, and the Middle East, Citadines offers fully furnished studios and apartments with kitchens, laundry facilities, and workspaces.

The flexibility of Citadines is one of its most attractive features. Guests can stay for a few nights, a few weeks, or even a few months, depending on their needs. Citadines also offers services such as housekeeping, concierge, and 24-hour reception, ensuring that guests have all the support they need during their stay.

8. Aloft Hotels

Aloft Hotels is an ideal choice for those seeking a modern, trendy, and convenient long-term stay. Aloft provides spacious rooms with open layouts, large windows, and contemporary design elements that make guests feel at home. Each room is equipped with a mini-fridge, microwave, and coffee maker, ensuring you have all the essentials.

Aloft is also known for its vibrant social scene. The hotel’s common areas feature live music, lively social events, and a great atmosphere, making it easy to connect with other guests. If you’re staying for an extended period, Aloft offers various amenities, such as a fitness center, pool, and on-site dining, making it a fun and comfortable place to stay.

9. Marriott Executive Apartments

For guests seeking top-tier luxury and flexibility, Marriott Executive Apartments offers upscale accommodations ideal for long-term stays. These fully furnished apartments include everything you need for a comfortable, extended stay: a fully equipped kitchen, spacious living areas, and housekeeping services. Guests also enjoy access to Marriott’s premium hotel amenities, including on-site dining, fitness centers, and business services.

Marriott Executive Apartments provide the perfect balance of comfort, style, and convenience, making them a great choice for corporate executives, traveling families, and individuals looking for a home away from home.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel for a long-term stay or extended vacation can significantly impact your experience. By considering factors like space, amenities, location, and services, you can find a hotel that suits your needs and helps you enjoy a comfortable and enjoyable stay. Whether you prioritize luxury, budget, or convenience, the hotels listed above are some of the best options available for long-term accommodations, offering everything from full kitchens to pet-friendly policies and spacious rooms.

The key is to identify what features matter most to you and choose a hotel that aligns with your lifestyle, whether you’re traveling for business, leisure, or a combination of both. With the right hotel, your extended stay can be as comfortable, convenient, and memorable as you desire.
