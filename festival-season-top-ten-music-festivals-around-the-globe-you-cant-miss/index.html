Festival Season: Top Ten Music Festivals Around the Globe You Can't Miss


Festival season is upon us, and it’s time to experience some unforgettable musical moments! Are you ready to board the plane, ship or RV and explore new destinations, bond with fellow music enthusiasts and watch exceptional live performances? Look no further, we’ve got the top ten music festivals from around the globe that you are going to regret missing out on.

  1. Glastonbury Festival - Pilton, England

Taking place primarily on Worthy Farm in Pilton, England, Glastonbury is one of the most famous festivals in the world. It’s not just about the music, think random shenanigans, theater performances, comedy, dance acts and art installations too! Expect to be walking in the shoes of legends including David Bowie,Prince, Oasis, and Stormzy, all of whom have graced the Pyramid Stage in their time.

  1. Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival - Indio, California

This festival in the California desert attracts some of the biggest names in music and also showcases cutting-edge styles in fashion and art. The global influence of Coachella adds an extra spark, mixing incredible electronic beats with rock, indie and even hip-hop. A noteworthy Coachella experience involves gallivanting around a kaleidoscope of colors and costumes, in a post-apocalyptic desert wonderland!

  1. Tomorrowland - Boom, Belgium

Tomorrowland is renowned for its dreamlike environment where the music never stops, and the vibe is always inspiring. Epic sets by globally acclaimed DJs, magnificent decorations and fancy dress-ups from visitors make this festival a must-visit. Do note that ravers will have to wait for a few years for the projected 2023 comeback, so make your plans accordingly.

  1. Bonnaroo Music & Arts Festival - Manchester, Tennessee

Located in the Great Stage Park in Manchester, Tennessee, Bonnaroo offers a fantastic and diverse line-up covering many genres. From indie music to world-class comedy, this four-day retreat in the countryside is bound to satisfy your cultural cravings. Managed eco-friendly initiatives, such as taking all garbage to the local landfill, having a solar power project and promoting water conservation, Bonnaroo tries to prove that good things can come from going green.

  1. Lollapalooza - Chicago, Illinois

With a fantastic blend of indie, alternative, hip-hop and electronic favorites over several stages, Lollapalooza, held in the heart of Chicago’s Grant Park, is an unforgettable experience. This is where global musicians let their guard down, have a little fun, and deliver stellar performances. Also, the millions of upbeat Chicagoans joining in on the infectious energy simply cannot be overlooked.

  1. Sziget Festival - Budapest, Hungary

Nestled on an island in the heart of Budapest, Sziget Festival is where you’ll find over 1,000 concerts across multiple stages featuring music of every genre—rock, jazz, hip-hop, and electronica, to name a few. Alongside performances, this festival is specially famous for its vibrant atmosphere complete with theater shows, art installations, and panel discussions with global thought leaders.

  1. Mawazine - Rabat, Morocco

This festival, also known as the “World Music Festival,” draws millions of attendees to swap stories under the Moroccan sun while savoring eclectic sounds. Mawazine is known for its showcase of diverse musical talents across all styles like jazz, rock, pop, and reggae, just with a twist of Moroccan charm alongside historic majesties and vibrant street life.

  1. Austin City Limits (ACL) - Austin, Texas

With sweeping landscapes and an eclectic mix of music, Austin City Limits festival stands out by booking artists representing multiple genres from around the world. The host, Zilker Park in Austin, Texas, adds a whole other level of charm with lush green surroundings to complement the upbeat ambiance that thousands of patrons effortlessly radiate.

  1. Primavera Sound - Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona’s gem - Primavera Sound - is not just about ripping to electronic beats or grooving to indie rock. It is where international artists and bands display incredible performances. The festival location, Parc del Fòrum, is a picturesque spot by the ocean, providing a fantastic view for music lovers to share memories.

  1. Beyond the Walls - Athens, Greece

Transforming Athens into one giant open-air museum, The Beyond the Walls festival highlights the rich cultural history of Greece alongside its modern vibrancy. This festival presents an incredibly wide range of music, theater, and art performances and events form various cultural workshops to archaeological visits, offering one of a kind cultural experience blending the new with the old.

There you have it! Experience the best of the global music scene in no time. As much as these festivals offer a change of scenery, they also provide a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to bond and create memories with like-minded souls. Let’s embrace our inner wanderlust and jump into the spirit of travel and music - the perfect recipe for an unforgettable experience!
