The Quietest Cars You Can Buy in 2023


In today’s fast-paced world, the demand for a serene and peaceful driving experience has never been higher. Whether you’re commuting through a busy city, embarking on a long road trip, or simply enjoying a leisurely drive through scenic landscapes, the desire for a quiet cabin is becoming increasingly important to drivers. A quieter ride not only enhances comfort but also reduces driver fatigue and creates a more enjoyable experience. For 2023, manufacturers have continued to refine their engineering, focusing on noise isolation, soundproofing, and overall cabin tranquility. If you’re in the market for a car that offers the best in quietness, here are some of the most notable models that promise a whisper-quiet driving experience.

1. Mercedes-Benz S-Class

The 2023 Mercedes-Benz S-Class continues to set the benchmark for luxury and comfort, and that extends to its cabin acoustics. With cutting-edge noise-reducing technology and superior soundproofing materials, this flagship sedan ensures that road noise, wind noise, and even engine sounds are kept to a minimum. The S-Class utilizes advanced active noise cancellation, which uses microphones inside the cabin to detect unwanted noise and then generates opposing sound waves to neutralize it.

Additionally, the S-Class features double-glazed windows and extra-thick seals around doors, which further contribute to its reputation as one of the quietest vehicles on the market. Whether you’re in the front seat or relaxing in the back, the S-Class provides an ultra-smooth, serene driving environment. It’s a perfect choice for those who want both performance and an almost silent cabin.

2. Lexus LS

Lexus has long been synonymous with luxury, and the 2023 LS continues that tradition with an emphasis on creating a quiet, tranquil cabin. The LS features an extensive suite of sound-deadening measures, such as laminated windows, triple-sealed doors, and sound-absorbing materials throughout the cabin. These features work together to keep unwanted noise from the road, tires, and engine out of the interior.

Lexus also offers its renowned “Active Sound Control” system, which uses strategically placed microphones and speakers to cancel out any remaining noise. The result is a cabin that is nearly as quiet as a luxury lounge, offering a comfortable retreat for both driver and passengers. If you value tranquility, the Lexus LS is a standout option.

3. BMW 7 Series

The BMW 7 Series, particularly the 2023 model, combines performance and luxury in a way few other cars can. The 7 Series is designed with aerodynamics in mind, and one of the results of this design focus is an exceptionally quiet cabin. The 2023 model features multiple soundproofing layers, including specialized acoustic glass and improved insulation in the doors and floorboards.

BMW’s iDrive system also plays a role in reducing cabin noise, as it can adjust settings to minimize engine noise during cruising. Whether you choose the gasoline-powered version or the hybrid, the 7 Series delivers a quiet and composed ride, ensuring that road and wind noises do not disrupt your journey. It’s the perfect car for those who appreciate both the serenity of a quiet ride and the thrill of dynamic performance.

4. Audi A8

Audi’s A8 has long been a leader in luxury sedans, and its 2023 version takes things even further when it comes to interior quietness. The A8 comes equipped with laminated acoustic glass, which reduces the amount of noise that enters the cabin from the outside. Audi also utilizes a special soundproofing material inside the doors and roof to create a near-silent atmosphere.

The A8’s refined air suspension system contributes to an exceptionally smooth ride, further reducing road noise. Inside, the cabin is filled with premium materials that enhance not only comfort but also quietness. When driving the Audi A8, you’ll notice that the engine hums along in the background, leaving only the sound of your music or the gentle conversation of your passengers.

5. Tesla Model S

As electric vehicles continue to rise in popularity, many drivers are discovering the peace and quiet that electric power can offer. The Tesla Model S is a prime example of an electric car that excels in noise reduction. Since electric vehicles have no internal combustion engine, they naturally produce less noise than traditional gas-powered cars.

The 2023 Model S takes things a step further with soundproofing materials throughout the cabin, including laminated windows that block out noise from the outside world. Tesla has also worked on reducing wind noise, ensuring that the Model S offers a serene, almost silent driving experience. The low center of gravity and smooth acceleration contribute to a quiet ride, while the minimalist interior design enhances the sense of peace.

6. Genesis G90

Genesis, Hyundai’s luxury arm, has made a name for itself with cars that offer premium features at a more affordable price point. The 2023 Genesis G90 is no exception and is often praised for its serene cabin. The G90 uses triple-layered soundproof glass, which effectively reduces outside noise. Additionally, the car’s cabin is constructed using materials that absorb sound, including soft-touch fabrics and acoustic foam in the floorboards.

The G90 also features active noise cancellation, ensuring that any remaining noise is eliminated for a smooth, quiet ride. Whether you’re driving around the city or on the highway, the G90’s cabin remains exceptionally quiet, offering a luxurious experience without the steep price tag typically associated with top-tier luxury vehicles.

7. Volvo S90

Volvo has long been known for its commitment to safety and comfort, and the 2023 S90 continues that tradition by offering one of the quietest cabins in its class. The S90 is equipped with advanced noise-canceling technologies, including acoustic glass that minimizes both wind and road noise. Additionally, the car’s suspension system is tuned for comfort, ensuring that the ride is smooth and quiet even on less-than-perfect roads.

Inside the cabin, Volvo has used sound-absorbing materials to further reduce unwanted noise, and the result is a calm, serene atmosphere that enhances every drive. Whether you’re navigating through busy city streets or cruising on the highway, the S90 ensures that noise won’t intrude on your experience.

8. Porsche Taycan

For those who prefer an electric car with a performance edge, the 2023 Porsche Taycan is a great option. While many performance-oriented vehicles are known for their noise, the Taycan is surprisingly quiet, thanks to its electric powertrain. Without the rumble of a traditional engine, the Taycan offers a smooth and nearly silent ride.

Porsche has also incorporated soundproofing materials into the Taycan’s design, helping to minimize road noise and wind turbulence. The Taycan’s advanced air suspension system contributes to a smooth ride, ensuring that every journey is as quiet as it is thrilling. For drivers seeking both performance and peace, the Taycan is an ideal choice.

9. Toyota Avalon

While Toyota may not be synonymous with high-end luxury in the same way as some of the other brands on this list, the 2023 Toyota Avalon offers an impressively quiet ride for those who want a more affordable option. The Avalon comes equipped with an extensive array of soundproofing materials, including laminated windows, extra-thick seals, and noise-dampening foam in the cabin.

The Avalon’s comfortable, refined suspension system further helps to reduce road noise, making it a great option for long-distance travelers. The cabin is peaceful, allowing for an enjoyable driving experience without the need to splurge on a luxury sedan. It’s a fantastic choice for those seeking quietness without the premium price tag.

10. Honda Accord

For those looking for a budget-friendly sedan that still delivers a quiet ride, the 2023 Honda Accord stands out. While not as luxurious as some of the other vehicles on this list, the Accord does an excellent job of minimizing road and engine noise. Honda has equipped the Accord with sound-deadening materials throughout the cabin, including thick carpets and enhanced door seals.

The Accord’s smooth suspension system ensures that bumps in the road don’t lead to excessive cabin noise. As a result, the Accord offers a surprisingly tranquil ride for a midsize sedan, making it a top choice for those who want a quiet vehicle at a reasonable price.

Conclusion

In 2023, there are a wide variety of vehicles that offer a quiet and peaceful driving experience, catering to all types of drivers and budgets. From the luxurious Mercedes-Benz S-Class to the more affordable Honda Accord, automakers have made significant strides in soundproofing and noise-canceling technologies. Whether you’re looking for a high-performance electric car like the Porsche Taycan, a refined luxury sedan like the BMW 7 Series, or an entry-level sedan like the Honda Accord, there’s a quiet car that fits your needs.

When shopping for a new vehicle, consider not just the engine power or design but also the level of noise isolation and overall cabin tranquility. A quiet car not only enhances your driving experience but also improves your overall comfort and enjoyment. In the end, driving should be a relaxing and peaceful experience, and the cars listed above are some of the best options for achieving that goal in 2023.
