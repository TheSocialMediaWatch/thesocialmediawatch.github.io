Incredible Falafel Recipes: Traditional and Creative


Falafel, a delectable Middle Eastern dish, delights taste buds around the world with its scrumptious mixture of spices, herbs, and legumes. Imagine a warm pita stuffed with moist and fluffy falafel balls generously drizzled with tahini sauce, and you know what the excitement is all about. In this blog post, we’ll explore the traditional falafel recipe and some innovative twists to spark your culinary creativity!

Traditional Falafel Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (also known as garbanzo beans)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 small green chilies, chopped (remove seeds for less heat)
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons chickpea flour or all-purpose flour (optional)
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Soak chickpeas in enough water for 24 hours. Drain and rinse the soaked chickpeas.
  2. In a food processor, add the soaked chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cilantro, green chilies, cumin, coriander, and salt. Pulse until the mixture is well combined and finely chopped.
  3. If your mixture appears wet, mix in the chickpea flour or all-purpose flour gradually until it forms a pliable dough that holds together.
  4. Shape the mixture into small balls, approximately 1-inch in diameter, by rolling them in your hands.
  5. Heat the vegetable oil in a deep frying pan or saucepan to 350°F (180°C). You can use a thermometer to check the temperature.
  6. Fry the falafel balls in small batches until golden brown, for approximately 3-4 minutes per batch.
  7. Use a slotted spoon to remove the falafel from the oil and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  8. Serve the falafel warm or at room temperature, tucked inside pita bread, or atop a salad.

Creative Falafel Recipes

1. Falafel Frittata

Capture the flavors of falafel in a heavenly egg dish!

Ingredients:

  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup crumbled falafel (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Tahini sauce and cucumber-tomato salad for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, salt, and pepper to combine.
  3. In a 10-inch oven-safe skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the diced onion and cook until translucent (approximately 3-4 minutes).
  4. Stir in the falafel and parsley, cooking for an additional 1-2 minutes before adding the whisked eggs to the skillet.
  5. Cook the eggs on the stovetop for 4-5 minutes until set around the edges.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the oven and bake the frittata for approximately 12-15 minutes, or until the eggs are fully cooked.
  7. Remove from oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve with tahini sauce and cucumber-tomato salad for a delightful fusion of flavors!

2. Falafel Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Transform a simple sweet potato into an extraordinary meal filled with flavors and nutrients.

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup crumbled falafel (homemade or store-bought)
  • 1/2 cup shredded red cabbage
  • 1/4 cup hummus
  • 2 tablespoons tahini
  • Tahini sauce and chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prick the sweet potatoes with a fork, then place them directly on the oven rack and bake for 45-50 minutes until tender.
  3. While the sweet potatoes are baking, prepare the falafel filling by heating the falafel in a skillet over medium heat with a small amount of water for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Once the sweet potatoes are ready, slice them down the middle, and fluff the insides with a fork.
  5. Top with the falafel, red cabbage, hummus, and tahini.
  6. Drizzle tahini sauce and garnish with chopped parsley. Serve immediately and savor this scrumptious, well-rounded meal!

Expanding your horizons with these creative falafel recipes can create a lively and unique dish, keeping your taste buds and loyal dining companions raving for more. Happy cooking and enjoy the sumptuous journey with these incredible falafel creations!
