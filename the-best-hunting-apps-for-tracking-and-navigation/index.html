The Best Hunting Apps for Tracking and Navigation


Hunting has evolved over the years, with modern technology offering hunters innovative ways to improve their experience. One of the most significant advancements is the development of hunting apps that aid in tracking, navigation, and planning. These apps are essential tools for both beginners and seasoned hunters who want to optimize their time in the field, enhance their safety, and track game effectively.

In this guide, we’ll explore the best hunting apps designed to provide superior navigation and tracking features. Whether you’re looking for a tool to help you mark your hunting spots, track animal movement, or navigate through unfamiliar terrain, there’s an app for every need.

Why You Need a Hunting App

In today’s fast-paced world, relying solely on traditional methods for hunting can limit your potential for success. Gone are the days of using physical maps and compasses to guide your way. Modern hunting apps provide real-time data, such as weather forecasts, topographic maps, GPS tracking, and more. These apps can transform your hunting experience by making your trip more efficient, safer, and enjoyable.

Moreover, apps that offer tracking capabilities allow you to follow the movement of animals, identify patterns, and even share your experiences with other hunters. Whether you’re hunting in dense forests or vast open fields, an app tailored to your needs can significantly enhance your chances of a successful hunt.

Let’s take a closer look at the top hunting apps available for tracking and navigation, and discover what makes them stand out.

1. OnX Hunt

OnX Hunt is often regarded as one of the best all-around hunting apps. This app is an absolute game-changer for hunters who need detailed mapping and accurate GPS navigation. OnX Hunt offers access to topographic maps, aerial imagery, private and public land boundaries, and more.

Key Features:

  • Land Ownership Maps: OnX provides precise information about land ownership, so you’ll know whether you’re hunting on public or private land. It’s incredibly useful for navigating unfamiliar areas and avoiding trespassing.
  • Offline Maps: The ability to download maps for offline use is a lifesaver in areas with no cell service.
  • Tracking: OnX allows hunters to mark waypoints and tracks, making it easier to track your movements and hunting spots over time.
  • Advanced Mapping Tools: You can use the app’s measuring tool to estimate distances between locations and plan routes with accuracy.

OnX Hunt has earned its place as a top-tier app for hunters who demand reliability and precision. Its mapping system is incredibly detailed, and the integration of tracking and land ownership data makes it invaluable for planning hunting trips.

2. HuntStand

HuntStand is another highly popular app for hunters, offering an array of tools for tracking, navigation, and planning. It combines mapping technology with social features to create a robust platform for hunters who want to stay connected and informed.

Key Features:

  • Interactive Maps: HuntStand provides satellite imagery, topographic maps, and even weather overlays, allowing hunters to plan their hunts down to the smallest detail.
  • Waypoints and Tracks: Similar to OnX, HuntStand allows users to mark waypoints and create tracks of their movements, providing useful data for future hunts.
  • Weather and Moon Phases: This app offers a built-in weather tracker, as well as moon phase data, which can be crucial for predicting animal behavior.
  • Hunting Log: The app includes a hunting log where you can record your hunting activities, making it easy to track past hunts and plan future trips.
  • Public and Private Land Boundaries: It helps hunters understand where they can legally hunt, minimizing the risk of accidental trespassing.

HuntStand’s blend of mapping capabilities, weather tracking, and social sharing features make it an excellent choice for hunters who want to stay on top of their game while connecting with others in the hunting community.

3. Gaia GPS

Gaia GPS is known for its high-quality mapping and navigation features, making it a fantastic choice for hunters who need detailed and reliable maps. While it’s not specifically designed for hunting, its robust GPS features make it a powerful tool for outdoor adventures, including hunting.

Key Features:

  • Topographic Maps: Gaia GPS excels in offering detailed topographic maps, which are especially useful for hunters navigating rugged terrains.
  • Offline Capabilities: With Gaia GPS, you can download maps for offline use, ensuring you’re never lost even when there’s no service.
  • Custom Waypoints: You can mark waypoints, record your routes, and track your hunting trips with ease.
  • Advanced Mapping Tools: The app allows you to measure distances, plot routes, and explore various types of maps (satellite, terrain, etc.) depending on your specific needs.

While Gaia GPS isn’t exclusively a hunting app, its superior mapping capabilities and offline functionality make it an excellent option for hunters who want detailed navigation and tracking tools.

4. iHunt Calls

iHunt Calls is a specialized app designed for hunters who want to improve their success rate by using calls to attract animals. This app offers a wide range of animal calls, including those for predators, waterfowl, and game animals. The app’s simplicity and effectiveness make it an essential tool for hunters looking to enhance their tracking and hunting techniques.

Key Features:

  • Animal Calls: iHunt Calls offers a library of animal sounds, including calls for coyotes, elk, ducks, and more.
  • Recording Functionality: You can record your own calls or create custom sounds for specific situations.
  • GPS Functionality: While not as advanced as other apps in terms of navigation, iHunt Calls still offers basic GPS features to help you track your location and mark hunting spots.

For hunters who rely on calls to attract animals, iHunt Calls is a must-have app. Its straightforward design and focus on animal communication set it apart from other apps in the market.

5. ScoutLook Hunting

ScoutLook Hunting is another great app for hunters looking for detailed weather and mapping tools. This app is designed to provide weather data that can help hunters predict animal behavior, making it an excellent tool for tracking game.

Key Features:

  • Weather Forecasting: ScoutLook offers weather data specifically tailored for hunters. It includes temperature, wind speed, barometric pressure, and more.
  • Wind Mapping: One of ScoutLook’s most popular features is its wind mapping tool. It shows you how the wind will behave at different points in your hunting area, allowing you to plan your approach accordingly.
  • ScentCone: This unique feature shows how your scent will disperse based on wind direction, helping you avoid detection by game animals.
  • Waypoints: Similar to other apps, ScoutLook lets you mark waypoints and track your hunting locations over time.

ScoutLook is a great option for hunters who want to focus on the environmental factors that influence animal movement. Its advanced weather forecasting and wind mapping tools make it especially useful for deer and predator hunters.

6. SpyPoint Hunt

SpyPoint Hunt is an app designed to integrate with SpyPoint’s trail cameras, making it a perfect choice for hunters who want to track game remotely. The app syncs with your trail cameras to provide real-time photos and data, allowing you to monitor game activity in your hunting area.

Key Features:

  • Trail Camera Integration: Syncs with SpyPoint trail cameras to provide you with real-time photos and videos of wildlife activity.
  • Remote Monitoring: With SpyPoint Hunt, you can remotely monitor your hunting grounds, making it easier to track game movement without having to be in the field.
  • Weather and Moon Data: The app includes weather forecasting and moon phase data, both of which can help predict when animals will be most active.
  • Photo Sharing: You can share your photos with other hunters or on social media, making it easier to communicate about your hunting trips.

SpyPoint Hunt is an excellent tool for hunters who use trail cameras and want to maximize the effectiveness of these devices. Its integration with SpyPoint cameras adds significant value to the hunting experience.

7. Trimble GPS Hunt

Trimble GPS Hunt is a professional-grade GPS navigation app designed for hunters who need precise location tracking in remote areas. It’s perfect for those who hunt in challenging terrains or need the highest level of accuracy.

Key Features:

  • High-Accuracy GPS: Trimble GPS Hunt uses advanced GPS technology to provide precise location tracking, making it an ideal tool for hunters in unfamiliar or rugged environments.
  • Offline Maps: You can download maps for offline use, ensuring you have access to detailed navigation even when cell service is unavailable.
  • Waypoints and Tracks: The app allows you to mark key locations, track your movements, and navigate easily in the field.
  • Customizable Maps: You can customize the maps to show specific features like trails, water sources, and hunting zones.

Trimble GPS Hunt is ideal for hunters who require the highest level of navigation and location tracking. Its precision and offline capabilities make it a powerful tool for serious hunters.

Conclusion

The hunting apps available today are revolutionizing the way hunters approach their trips. Whether you’re tracking animals, navigating through the woods, or planning your next hunt, the right app can make all the difference. From detailed maps and waypoints to weather data and trail camera integration, these apps offer a range of features to enhance your hunting experience.

Each app has its strengths, so the best choice depends on your specific needs. Whether you’re looking for detailed mapping, offline capabilities, or animal call libraries, there’s an app that fits your hunting style. The key is to explore the options available and find the one that complements your hunting preferences. Happy hunting!
