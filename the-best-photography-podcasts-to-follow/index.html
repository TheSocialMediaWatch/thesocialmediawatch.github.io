The Best Photography Podcasts to Follow


Photography is an ever-evolving art form that combines technical skill, creativity, and a sharp eye for detail. Whether you’re a professional photographer, an enthusiastic hobbyist, or simply someone fascinated by visual storytelling, immersing yourself in the world of photography through podcasts can be incredibly enriching. Podcasts offer a unique way to learn from industry experts, discover new techniques, stay up-to-date on gear reviews, and hear inspiring stories from behind the lens.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore some of the best photography podcasts that have gained acclaim for their engaging content, expert insights, and practical advice. These shows cover a wide range of topics—from landscape and portrait photography to post-processing, lighting, and the business side of photography. Ready to plug in your earphones and dive deep into the fascinating world of photography? Let’s get started!

Why Listen to Photography Podcasts?

Before listing the top podcasts, it’s worth considering why they make such a valuable resource:

  • Learn On the Go: Whether you’re commuting, working out, or simply relaxing, podcasts let you soak up photography knowledge anytime, anywhere.
  • Stay Updated: Photography trends, technology, and gear evolve quickly. Podcasts keep you in the loop with the latest developments.
  • Inspiration & Motivation: Hearing stories from seasoned photographers can kickstart your creativity and encourage you toward new challenges.
  • Practical Tips & Tutorials: Many podcasts provide actionable advice that you can implement immediately during your shoots.
  • Community Connection: Engaging with podcast hosts and listeners can expand your network and deepen your passion.

Top Photography Podcasts to Follow

1. The Candid Frame

Hosted by Ibarionex Perello, The Candid Frame is a long-standing podcast that delves into the personal stories and philosophies of exceptional photographers around the globe. This show excels in exploring the human side of photography, helping listeners understand what drives artists to create. Episodes often feature renowned photographers, discussing their journey, process, and insights on the industry.

Why should you listen? If you are seeking inspiration coupled with deep, meaningful conversations on creativity and artistry, this podcast will resonate deeply.

2. PetaPixel Photography Podcast

Brought to you by the popular photography website PetaPixel, this podcast covers a broad spectrum of topics relevant to photographers at all levels. The hosts bring a fresh and informal style to discussions on new camera technology, editing software, photography trends, and interviews with top photographers and industry insiders.

Why it stands out: It blends technical reviews with storytelling, making it perfect for those wanting to stay updated and learn simultaneously.

3. The Art of Photography

Hosted by Ted Forbes, The Art of Photography merges the practical with the theoretical. It covers photographic techniques, inspirations, art history, and the nuances of visual expression. This show is ideal for those who want to elevate their photography beyond mere snapshots into a true form of artistic communication.

What makes it unique? Ted’s background as an educator shines through in his detailed, easy-to-understand explanations mixed with insightful critiques of famous photographers.

4. B&H Photography Podcast

Produced by the renowned B&H Photo Video store, this podcast features interviews with prolific photographers, educators, and industry professionals. The conversations often touch on workflow, creative development, and business advice for photographers who want to take their craft to the next professional level.

Ideal listeners: Photographers who want to learn about the business side of photography and hear success stories from industry leaders.

5. This Week in Photo (TWiP)

This Week in Photo is a lively weekly podcast that blends news, gear reviews, and community discussions. Hosts share the latest photography headlines, discuss new product launches, and even bring in listener feedback and questions.

Why tune in? It’s an excellent resource for staying current on what’s new in the photography world, packed with energy and humor that keeps you entertained while you learn.

6. Focus

With beautifully produced episodes, Focus offers compelling visual stories and behind-the-scenes perspectives from photographers worldwide. This podcast is less about technical tips and more about the creative narrative and emotional aspects of photography.

Who benefits most? Visual storytellers and anyone interested in how photography can capture complex human experiences and profound moments.

7. The Landscape Photography Podcast

Dedicated entirely to landscape photography, this podcast dives into the technical aspects of capturing stunning natural scenes. Topics range from composition and lighting to equipment recommendations and field techniques for outdoor photography enthusiasts.

Why it’s invaluable: For landscape photographers craving targeted advice and inspiration, this podcast delivers practical knowledge alongside breathtaking nature stories.

8. The Beginner Photography Podcast

Every expert was once a beginner, and this show caters specifically to those at the start of their photographic journey. Practical tutorials, jargon explanations, and beginner FAQs are staples of this accessible podcast.

Perfect for: Novices eager to master their camera’s basics and build a strong foundation.

Deep Dive: Topics Explored in Photography Podcasts

To give a clearer idea of the richness these podcasts offer, here are some of the recurring themes and topics explored across these shows:

Gear and Technology

  • In-depth reviews of cameras, lenses, lighting equipment.
  • Updates on software like Photoshop, Lightroom, and new editing tools.
  • Discussions on drones, mirrorless vs DSLR, sensor technology.

Techniques and Tutorials

  • Composition tips: rule of thirds, leading lines, framing.
  • Lighting: natural, studio, flash, reflectors.
  • Post-processing: color grading, retouching, workflow optimization.

Artistic and Philosophical Insights

  • What makes a photo “art.”
  • Developing a unique style or voice.
  • The emotional power of imagery.

Business and Marketing

  • Building a photography brand.
  • Pricing models and client relations.
  • Social media strategies for photographers.

Storytelling and Visual Narratives

  • Photojournalism insights.
  • Documentary photography ethics.
  • Long-form storytelling through images.

How to Choose the Right Photography Podcast for You

With so many great options, how do you pick the perfect podcast? Consider:

  • Your Skill Level: Beginners may prefer podcasts with step-by-step tutorials, while advanced photographers might look for deep discussions on creative philosophy or business.
  • Photography Focus: Are you into landscapes, portraits, street photography, or commercial work? Pick podcasts specializing in your interests.
  • Format and Style: Some podcasts feature solo hosts, others have panel discussions or interviews. Figure out which presentation style you find most engaging.
  • Frequency: Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly episodes — select a schedule that fits your listening habits.
  • Community Interaction: Look for podcasts that encourage listener engagement if you want to connect with like-minded peers.

Maximizing Your Learning from Photography Podcasts

Listening to a podcast is one thing, but maximizing what you learn takes some deliberate effort:

  • Take Notes: Jot down techniques, gear recommendations, or concepts to explore later.
  • Apply Ideas: Challenge yourself to try new camera settings or compositions inspired from episodes.
  • Join Online Communities: Many podcasts have forums or social media groups where listeners share photos and advice.
  • Revisit Favorite Episodes: Great content is worth multiple listens to fully absorb subtleties.
  • Stay Curious: Use podcasts as a springboard to further research topics or photographers mentioned.

Conclusion

Photography podcasts open doors to a world of knowledge, creativity, and inspiration that can elevate your photographic skills, whether you’re just starting or aiming to become a professional. The diversity of content—from gear talk and tutorials to storytelling and business advice—ensures there’s something for every kind of photography enthusiast.

With the array of outstanding podcasts available, your next creative breakthrough or technical mastery might just be one episode away. So, find your favorite show(s), hit subscribe, and let the journey behind the lens deepen your passion and expertise.

Happy listening, and even happier shooting!
