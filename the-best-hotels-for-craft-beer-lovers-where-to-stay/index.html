The Best Hotels for Craft Beer Lovers: Where to Stay


Craft beer enthusiasts are often on the lookout for the perfect getaway that combines their love for unique brews with comfortable accommodations. Fortunately, many hotels around the world cater specifically to this niche market, offering a range of amenities that not only celebrate craft beer but also provide a memorable experience. In this post, we will explore some of the best hotels for craft beer lovers, where you can sip on local brews, participate in brewery tours, and enjoy a cozy atmosphere perfect for unwinding after a day of exploration.

1. The DogHouse Hotel, Columbus, Ohio

Nestled in the heart of Columbus, The DogHouse Hotel is a dream destination for beer aficionados. This innovative hotel is attached to the BrewDog Brewery, one of the largest craft breweries in the UK. Each room features a self-dispensing tap with BrewDog beers, allowing guests to pour their own pints right from the comfort of their beds.

In addition to the unique in-room experience, guests can enjoy brewery tours, tastings, and a delicious menu at the on-site restaurant that pairs perfectly with their craft beers. The hotel also boasts a beer garden and a rooftop bar, providing ample opportunities to enjoy a cold one while mingling with fellow craft beer lovers.

2. The Foundry Hotel, Asheville, North Carolina

Asheville is renowned for its vibrant craft beer scene, and The Foundry Hotel is the perfect base for exploring it. This historic hotel, which was once a foundry, now offers comfortable accommodations with a modern twist. Located just a short walk from downtown Asheville, guests can easily access numerous breweries, including the famous Sierra Nevada and Wicked Weed.

The Foundry Hotel features an on-site bar, the Riveter, which showcases a rotating selection of local craft beers. Additionally, the hotel often hosts events such as beer tastings and food pairings, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the local brewing culture. With its rich history and prime location, The Foundry Hotel is an excellent choice for craft beer enthusiasts.

3. The Craft House, Fort Collins, Colorado

Fort Collins is known as a craft beer mecca, home to several award-winning breweries. The Craft House offers a unique experience by combining a boutique hotel with a craft beer bar. Each room is designed to reflect the local brewing culture, with decor inspired by the artistry of craft beer.

The on-site bar features an extensive selection of local brews, and guests can participate in beer tastings and brewery tours arranged by the hotel. The Craft House also has a beautiful outdoor patio, perfect for enjoying a cold drink while soaking up the Colorado sun. With its focus on local craft beer and a welcoming atmosphere, The Craft House is a must-visit for beer lovers.

4. The Beer Hotel, Belgium

Belgium is synonymous with craft beer, and The Beer Hotel in Bruges offers a unique blend of comfort and beer culture. This hotel features a bar stocked with over 400 different Belgian beers, allowing guests to sample a variety of flavors and styles. Each room is elegantly decorated, providing a cozy retreat after a long day of exploring the city’s breweries.

Guests can take advantage of the hotel’s beer-tasting sessions, where they can learn about the history and brewing process of Belgium’s famous beers. The Beer Hotel also offers guided tours of local breweries, ensuring that guests experience the best that Bruges has to offer in terms of craft beer.

5. The Old Brewery Hotel, Kingston upon Thames, England

For those looking to combine historical charm with craft beer, The Old Brewery Hotel is an excellent choice. Located in a former brewery, this hotel features stunning views of the River Thames and easy access to some of London’s best craft breweries. The Old Brewery has a cozy bar that serves a selection of local ales and craft beers.

Guests can explore nearby breweries and participate in tasting events organized by the hotel. The Old Brewery Hotel’s unique atmosphere and proximity to London make it an ideal spot for craft beer lovers seeking a memorable experience.

6. The Thompson Nashville, Tennessee

Nashville has become a hotspot for craft beer lovers, and The Thompson Nashville is perfectly positioned to take advantage of this vibrant scene. This chic hotel features a rooftop bar with stunning views of the city skyline, serving a selection of local craft beers alongside a curated cocktail menu.

The Thompson Nashville is within walking distance of several acclaimed breweries, including Yazoo Brewing Company and Jackalope Brewing Company. Guests can take advantage of the hotel’s beer-themed packages, which often include brewery tours and tastings, making it a fantastic choice for those looking to explore Nashville’s craft beer offerings.

7. The Alchemist Hotel, Stowe, Vermont

Vermont is known for its craft beer culture, and The Alchemist Hotel in Stowe is the perfect place to experience it. This hotel is affiliated with The Alchemist Brewery, famous for its Heady Topper beer. Guests can enjoy exclusive tastings and brewery tours, getting an insider’s look at the brewing process.

The Alchemist Hotel features comfortable accommodations and a vibrant atmosphere, making it an ideal base for exploring Vermont’s lush landscapes and numerous breweries. With its commitment to craft beer and the local community, The Alchemist Hotel is a top pick for beer lovers.

8. The Brew House, San Diego, California

San Diego is home to one of the largest craft beer scenes in the United States, and The Brew House is a unique hotel that celebrates this culture. Located in the heart of the city, The Brew House offers guests easy access to over 150 breweries within a short drive.

The hotel features a taproom that serves a rotating selection of local craft beers, and guests can participate in guided brewery tours organized by the hotel. With its laid-back atmosphere and focus on local brews, The Brew House is a fantastic option for craft beer enthusiasts looking to explore San Diego’s vibrant beer scene.

9. The Hotel at the University of Maryland, College Park, Maryland

For craft beer lovers visiting the Washington D.C. area, The Hotel at the University of Maryland is a convenient and comfortable option. This modern hotel is located near several renowned breweries, including Town Hall and Calvert Brewing Company.

The hotel features an on-site bar that showcases local craft beers and often hosts brewery-themed events for guests. The Hotel at the University of Maryland is not only a great base for exploring the local craft beer scene but also offers easy access to the attractions of D.C., providing the perfect blend of culture and beer enjoyment.

10. The Four Seasons Hotel, Denver, Colorado

For those looking to indulge in luxury while enjoying craft beer, The Four Seasons Hotel in Denver is a top choice. This upscale hotel is situated in the heart of the city and offers easy access to a variety of breweries, including Great Divide Brewing Company and Denver Beer Co.

The hotel features a stylish bar that serves a selection of local craft beers, along with a curated menu of cocktails and wines. Guests can also take advantage of the hotel’s beer concierge service, which provides personalized recommendations and arrangements for brewery tours. With its luxurious accommodations and commitment to showcasing local brews, The Four Seasons Hotel is an excellent choice for discerning craft beer lovers.

Conclusion

Craft beer lovers have an abundance of options when it comes to choosing a hotel that caters to their passion for unique brews. From innovative accommodations with self-dispensing taps to historic hotels with rich brewing histories, these destinations provide the perfect backdrop for enjoying craft beer while exploring new cities.

Whether you’re looking to relax in a cozy bar, participate in brewery tours, or simply enjoy a cold pint after a day of adventure, these hotels offer something for every craft beer enthusiast. So pack your bags, grab your favorite beer glass, and get ready for a memorable journey through the world of craft beer at these exceptional hotels.
