Hotels with the Best Views of the Northern Lights


Are you an avid traveler seeking a magical experience under the starry skies? Imagine witnessing the mesmerizing dance of the Northern Lights, also known as Aurora Borealis, from the comfort of a cozy hotel. This natural light display, often seen in high-latitude regions, is a bucket-list phenomenon for many. To make your dream a reality, we have curated a list of hotels around the world that offer some of the best views of the Northern Lights.

Iceland: Where Nature Paints the Sky

Hotel Ion Adventure
Located in Iceland’s Thingvellir National Park, Hotel Ion Adventure provides a front-row seat to the dazzling Northern Lights. Nestled amidst stunning lava fields, this eco-conscious hotel offers panoramic views of the colorful night sky. Guests can enjoy luxurious amenities while gazing at the celestial spectacle from the comfort of their rooms.

Frost and Fire Hotel
Situated near Iceland’s geothermal town of Hveragerdi, Frost and Fire Hotel boasts geothermally heated rooms ideal for witnessing the Aurora Borealis. The hotel’s riverside location and hot springs create a serene setting for guests to marvel at the dancing lights above. Indulge in warm baths while the sky above comes to life with vibrant hues.

Finland: A Winter Wonderland Retreat

Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort
For a unique Northern Lights experience, head to Kakslauttanen Arctic Resort in Finland. Stay in glass igloos that offer unobstructed views of the starlit sky. Imagine lying in bed and watching the Aurora dance across the horizon. This Arctic retreat provides a cozy and unforgettable way to witness nature’s light show.

Arctic TreeHouse Hotel
Nestled in the snowy forests of Rovaniemi, the Arctic TreeHouse Hotel allows guests to admire the Northern Lights from the comfort of their modern treehouse suites. These elevated accommodations provide an exclusive vantage point for observing the celestial display. Enjoy the blend of luxury and nature while chasing the Aurora.

Norway: Where the Lights Meet the Fjords

Svinoya Rorbuer
In Norway’s Lofoten Islands, Svinoya Rorbuer offers traditional fisherman’s cabins that overlook the Norwegian Sea. This charming retreat provides a cozy ambiance for guests to witness the Northern Lights shimmering above the dramatic fjords. Immerse yourself in the local culture while embracing the celestial wonders.

Eggum Arctic Lodge
Perched on a cliff overlooking the Arctic Ocean, Eggum Arctic Lodge in Norway offers a secluded escape for Aurora enthusiasts. Guests can unwind in modern cabins and witness the Northern Lights painting the sky above the rugged coastline. Experience tranquility and natural beauty at this remote Northern Lights haven.

Canada: A Northern Lights Extravaganza

Blachford Lake Lodge
Escape to the wilderness of Canada’s Northwest Territories at Blachford Lake Lodge, where the Northern Lights illuminate the night sky in all their glory. This off-the-grid lodge provides a rustic yet comfortable setting for observing the Aurora Borealis. Immerse yourself in the serenity of the Canadian wilderness.

Aurora Village
Located near Yellowknife in Canada’s Northwest Territories, Aurora Village offers a premier Northern Lights viewing experience. Stay in cozy glass chalets that allow guests to marvel at the dancing lights from the warmth of their accommodations. Embrace the enchanting beauty of the Arctic landscape.

Conclusion

Embark on a journey to witness the enchanting Northern Lights from these exceptional hotels around the world. Whether you choose to chase the Aurora in Iceland, Finland, Norway, or Canada, each destination promises a unique and unforgettable experience under the cosmic spectacle of the night sky. Prepare to be awe-struck as nature paints the heavens with vibrant colors, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

Experience the magic of the Northern Lights at these remarkable hotels and let the celestial dance of the Aurora Borealis captivate your senses. Plan your getaway to one of these dreamy destinations and prepare for a truly extraordinary adventure in the land of the midnight sun. Enjoy every moment under the shimmering glow of the Northern Lights!
