The Best Fishing Podcasts for Anglers


Are you an avid angler looking for ways to enhance your fishing knowledge and skills? Look no further than fishing podcasts! With the rise of podcasting, there are now numerous shows dedicated to the art and science of fishing. Whether you’re a beginner or a seasoned pro, these podcasts offer valuable insights, tips, and tricks to help you become a better angler. In this blog post, we’ll explore some of the best fishing podcasts out there, covering various aspects of the sport.

1. The Itinerant Angler Podcast

Hosted by Zach Matthews, The Itinerant Angler Podcast is a must-listen for all fly fishing enthusiasts. With over 200 episodes, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including fly tying, gear reviews, conservation, and interviews with notable anglers. The host’s passion for the sport shines through in every episode, making it both informative and entertaining.

2. Bass Edge Radio

For all the bass fishing aficionados out there, Bass Edge Radio is a show you don’t want to miss. Hosted by Aaron Martin and Kurt Dove, this podcast delves deep into the world of bass fishing, providing expert advice on techniques, tackle, and strategies. The interviews with professional anglers and industry experts offer valuable insights that can take your bass fishing game to the next level.

3. Kayak Bass Fishing Radio

If you’re a kayak angler or interested in kayak fishing, Kayak Bass Fishing Radio is a podcast that deserves a spot on your playlist. Hosted by Chad Hoover, this show focuses specifically on the unique challenges and techniques associated with kayak fishing. From gear recommendations to tournament strategies, this podcast covers it all, making it a valuable resource for kayak anglers of all skill levels.

4. The Fisherman’s Post Saltwater Podcast

Saltwater anglers rejoice! The Fisherman’s Post Saltwater Podcast is here to provide you with all the information you need for successful saltwater fishing. Hosted by Dr. Bogus and Billy Thorpe, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, including inshore and offshore fishing, tackle selection, and hotspots along the East Coast. Whether you’re targeting redfish, snook, or marlin, this podcast has got you covered.

5. The Fishing Podcast

Hosted by Joe Sills, The Fishing Podcast is a well-rounded show that covers various aspects of fishing. From freshwater to saltwater, fly fishing to ice fishing, this podcast offers a diverse range of topics to cater to anglers of all interests. With insightful interviews, fishing stories, and tips from experienced anglers, this podcast is sure to keep you entertained and informed.

Conclusion

Podcasts have revolutionized the way we consume information, and fishing podcasts have become a valuable resource for anglers looking to improve their skills and knowledge. Whether you prefer fly fishing, bass fishing, kayak fishing, or saltwater fishing, there’s a podcast out there for you. The shows mentioned in this blog post are just a few examples of the many fantastic fishing podcasts available. So grab your headphones, sit back, and let the experts guide you on your fishing journey. Happy listening and tight lines!
