The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune Into


Are you a fashion enthusiast looking for some stylish inspiration on the go? Look no further than the world of fashion podcasts. These audio gems are a great way to stay updated on the latest trends, get insider tips from industry experts, and dive into the fascinating world of fashion from anywhere, anytime. Whether you’re a designer, a fashion student, or simply someone who loves all things fashion, here are some of the best fashion podcasts to tune into for your regular dose of style and glamour.

1. The Business of Fashion Podcast

Hosted by Imran Amed, founder, and editor-in-chief of The Business of Fashion, this podcast delves into the business side of the fashion industry. From interviews with top designers and executives to discussions on the latest industry trends and challenges, this podcast provides valuable insights into the dynamic world of fashion business.

2. Fashion No Filter

This podcast, hosted by fashion journalists Camille Charriere and Monica Ainley, takes a deep dive into the fashion industry, covering everything from sustainability and social media’s impact on fashion to behind-the-scenes stories from the fashion weeks around the world. With a mix of interviews and candid conversations, this podcast offers an intimate look at the fashion world.

3. American Fashion Podcast

For those interested in the American fashion scene, this podcast is a treasure trove of information. With a focus on the business, design, and culture of American fashion, the hosts delve into topics such as emerging designers, retail, and the impact of technology on the industry.

4. Dressed: The History of Fashion

If you’re a fashion history buff, this podcast is a must-listen. Each episode explores the cultural and social impact of fashion throughout history, from ancient civilizations to modern-day trends. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this podcast brings the fascinating stories behind the clothes we wear to life.

5. The Cutting Room

Hosted by fashion designer Recho Omondi, The Cutting Room is a refreshingly candid podcast that provides a behind-the-scenes look at the fashion industry. Omondi’s interviews with industry insiders and her take on the latest fashion news make this podcast an insightful and entertaining listen.

6. Fashion Revolution Podcast

For those passionate about sustainable and ethical fashion, the Fashion Revolution Podcast is a great resource. Hosted by Sarah Ditty and Orsola de Castro, co-founders of the Fashion Revolution movement, this podcast explores the positive changes being made in the industry and the people driving them.

7. The Thread With OZY

Hosted by fashion journalist and editor, Tamara El-Waylly, The Thread with OZY is a podcast that weaves together the biggest stories in fashion, style icons, and the cultural impact of what we wear. With a focus on the intersection of fashion and society, this podcast provides a thought-provoking take on the fashion world.

8. Unravel: A Fashion Podcast

This podcast, hosted by Jasmine, Dana, and Joy, takes a critical and analytical approach to fashion. From dissecting the cultural significance of clothing to exploring the influence of fashion on identity, this podcast offers a fresh perspective on the world of style and design.

9. The Glossy Podcast

Hosted by Lauren Sherman, The Glossy Podcast covers the intersection of fashion, business, and technology. With interviews featuring industry leaders, this podcast provides in-depth insights into the ever-evolving landscape of fashion and retail.

10. Pop Fashion

This lively podcast hosted by Kaarin Vembar and Lisa Rowan is a delightful mix of fashion news, industry insights, and witty commentary. With a focus on the latest fashion headlines and trends, this podcast is both informative and entertaining.

Whether you’re looking to stay informed about the latest fashion trends, gain insights into the business of fashion, or explore the cultural and historical aspects of style, these podcasts offer something for every fashion aficionado. So, grab your headphones and tune into these fabulous fashion podcasts for a captivating auditory journey through the world of style and creativity.
