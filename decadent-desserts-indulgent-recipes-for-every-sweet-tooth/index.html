Chocolate Lava Cake

Servings: 4 | Preparation Time: 20 mins | Cooking Time: 12 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C) and grease 4 ramekins.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter in a bowl over simmering water.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Gently fold the melted chocolate into the egg mixture.
  5. Add flour and salt, then divide the batter into the ramekins.
  6. Bake for 12 minutes until the edges are set but the center remains gooey.
  7. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Berry Cheesecake Parfait

Servings: 6 | Preparation Time: 15 mins | Chilling Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 cup cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mixed berries

Instructions:

  1. Mix graham cracker crumbs with melted butter and press into the base of serving glasses.
  2. In a bowl, beat cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Layer the cream cheese mixture and berries in the glasses.
  4. Chill for at least 1 hour before serving.

Salted Caramel Brownies

Servings: 12 | Preparation Time: 15 mins | Baking Time: 30 mins

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup caramel sauce
  • Sea salt for topping

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C) and grease a baking pan.
  2. Melt butter, sugar, and cocoa powder in a bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs one at a time, then fold in flour and salt.
  4. Pour half of the batter into the pan, drizzle with caramel sauce, then add the remaining batter.
  5. Swirl the batter with a knife and sprinkle sea salt on top.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes until set. Cool before cutting into squares.
