10 Best Workouts for Increasing Speed in Soccer


Speed is an essential attribute in soccer that can make a significant difference on the field. Players who possess great speed have a competitive edge over their opponents, enabling them to outpace defenders, create scoring opportunities, and cover more ground during a match. If you’re looking to enhance your speed on the soccer field, incorporating specific workouts into your training routine can help you achieve your goal. Here are 10 of the best workouts to increase your speed in soccer:

1. Sprint Intervals

Sprint intervals are an excellent way to improve your acceleration and top speed. Incorporate short sprints of 20-30 meters followed by brief rest periods. Repeat this pattern to simulate the quick bursts of speed required in soccer.

2. Plyometric Exercises

Plyometric exercises like box jumps, squat jumps, and bounding drills can help increase your explosive power and leg strength, translating to faster sprinting speed and agility on the field.

3. Hill Sprints

Running uphill forces your muscles to work harder, building strength and power in your legs. Hill sprints are a challenging yet effective workout for improving speed, acceleration, and overall cardiovascular fitness.

4. Agility Ladder Drills

Agility ladder drills are fantastic for improving footwork, coordination, and quick changes in direction. These drills enhance your agility and reaction time, crucial for evading opponents and maneuvering swiftly on the pitch.

5. Resistance Training

Incorporate resistance training exercises like sled pulls, resistance band sprints, or parachute sprints to add resistance to your sprinting movements. These workouts can help develop greater leg strength and speed.

6. Cone Dribbling Drills

Set up a series of cones and practice dribbling through them at high speed. This drill not only improves your ball control but also enhances your ability to maneuver quickly with the ball at your feet, a vital skill for speedy attacks.

7. Lateral Bounding

Lateral bounding exercises focus on side-to-side movements, strengthening the muscles responsible for quick changes in direction. By incorporating lateral bounds into your training, you can enhance your agility and explosiveness on the field.

8. Fartlek Training

Fartlek training involves alternating between periods of sprinting and jogging at varying intensities. This workout helps improve both your aerobic and anaerobic fitness, contributing to increased speed endurance during matches.

9. Single-Leg Hops

Single-leg hops are great for developing balance, stability, and explosive power in each leg individually. Practicing single-leg hops can improve your overall speed and agility while reducing the risk of injury.

10. Circuit Training

Circuit training combines multiple exercises targeting different muscle groups in quick succession. Design a circuit that includes speed-focused exercises like burpees, high knees, and mountain climbers to boost your overall athleticism and speed on the field.

Incorporating a combination of these top 10 workouts into your soccer training regimen can help you elevate your speed and performance on the field. Remember to focus on proper form, consistency, and gradual progression to maximize the benefits of these exercises. Speed is a valuable asset in soccer, and with dedication and hard work, you can enhance your speed to become a more formidable player on the pitch. Gear up, lace up your cleats, and sprint towards your speed goals with these effective workouts!

Stay swift, stay agile, and dominate the game!
