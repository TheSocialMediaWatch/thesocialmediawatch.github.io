Cooking with Canned Goods: Easy and Delicious Recipes


Are you looking for quick and convenient meal solutions that don’t compromise on taste? Look no further than your pantry! Canned goods are often overlooked heroes in the kitchen, offering versatility, affordability, and a longer shelf life. In this blog post, we will explore a variety of easy and delicious recipes that you can whip up using canned goods.

Breakfast

1. Chickpea Breakfast Hash

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of chickpeas
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 1 bell pepper, chopped
    • 1 can of diced tomatoes
    • Spices of your choice (such as paprika, cumin, and garlic powder)
  • Instructions:
    1. In a skillet, sauté the onion and bell pepper until softened.
    2. Add the chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and spices.
    3. Cook until heated through and serve hot.

2. Corn and Black Bean Breakfast Tacos

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of corn
    • 1 can of black beans
    • 1 teaspoon of cumin
    • Tortillas
  • Instructions:
    1. Drain and rinse the corn and black beans.
    2. Heat them in a skillet with cumin.
    3. Fill tortillas with the mixture and enjoy a flavorful breakfast.

Lunch

1. Tuna Stuffed Avocados

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of tuna
    • 2 ripe avocados
    • 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Instructions:
    1. Mix the drained tuna with mayonnaise, salt, and pepper.
    2. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
    3. Fill the avocado halves with the tuna mixture for a healthy and satisfying lunch.

2. Three Bean Salad

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of kidney beans
    • 1 can of garbanzo beans
    • 1 can of green beans
    • Red onion, finely chopped
    • Italian dressing
  • Instructions:
    1. Rinse and drain the canned beans.
    2. Mix them with chopped red onion and dressing.
    3. Chill before serving for a refreshing and protein-packed salad.

Dinner

1. Chicken and White Bean Chili

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of white beans
    • 1 can of diced green chilies
    • Cooked shredded chicken
    • Chicken broth
  • Instructions:
    1. In a pot, combine white beans, green chilies, chicken, and broth.
    2. Simmer until flavors meld together, and serve with your favorite toppings.

2. Tomato Basil Pasta

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
    • Garlic, minced
    • Fresh basil
    • Pasta of your choice
  • Instructions:
    1. Sauté garlic in a pan, then add crushed tomatoes and simmer.
    2. Toss in cooked pasta and fresh basil for a simple yet flavorful meal.

Dessert

1. Pineapple Upside-Down Cake

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of pineapple rings
    • Maraschino cherries
    • Yellow cake mix
  • Instructions:
    1. Arrange pineapple rings and cherries in a cake pan.
    2. Pour the cake mix over the fruits and bake according to package instructions for a classic dessert.

2. Coconut Rice Pudding

  • Ingredients:
    • 1 can of coconut milk
    • Cooked rice
    • Sugar
    • Vanilla extract
  • Instructions:
    1. Cook coconut milk with sugar and vanilla until slightly thickened.
    2. Stir in cooked rice and chill before serving a creamy and indulgent treat.

Whether you’re a busy professional, a college student, or a parent on the go, incorporating canned goods into your cooking routine can save you time and effort without sacrificing flavor. Experiment with these recipes and let the convenience and deliciousness of canned goods elevate your meals. Happy cooking!

Remember, the key to successful cooking with canned goods is to always drain and rinse them before use to reduce sodium content and enhance the flavors of your dishes. Enjoy the simplicity and taste that canned goods bring to your table!
