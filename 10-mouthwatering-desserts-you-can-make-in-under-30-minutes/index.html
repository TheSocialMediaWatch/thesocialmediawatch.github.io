This healthy yet indulgent treat is perfect for those who love frozen desserts. Frozen yogurt bark is simple to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings, from fresh fruit to nuts and chocolate.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
  • A handful of crushed nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the Greek yogurt and honey together until smooth.
  3. Spread the yogurt mixture onto the prepared baking sheet in an even layer.
  4. Sprinkle with mixed berries, chocolate chips, and nuts.
  5. Freeze for at least 2 hours, then break into pieces before serving.
