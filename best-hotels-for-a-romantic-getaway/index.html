Best Hotels for a Romantic Getaway


A romantic getaway offers the perfect opportunity to escape the everyday hustle and reconnect with a loved one. Whether you’re celebrating an anniversary, honeymoon, or simply want to spend quality time together, choosing the right hotel can transform your trip into an unforgettable experience. From luxurious retreats nestled in nature to boutique hotels in the heart of vibrant cities, the world is filled with exceptional destinations perfect for lovebirds.

In this post, we’ll guide you through some of the best hotels for a romantic getaway, each offering unique amenities, stunning views, and intimate settings to help you and your partner create lasting memories. No matter where your journey takes you, these hotels provide the perfect backdrop for romance.

1. The St. Regis Bora Bora Resort, French Polynesia

For couples seeking an idyllic tropical escape, the St. Regis Bora Bora Resort in French Polynesia is the epitome of luxury and romance. Located on a private island in the turquoise waters of the South Pacific, this resort is surrounded by breathtaking views of Mount Otemanu and crystal-clear lagoons.

The overwater bungalows here are designed with romance in mind, offering the perfect blend of privacy and stunning scenery. You can enjoy private dinners on the beach, indulge in spa treatments with views of the ocean, or simply relax by your own infinity pool.

The St. Regis also offers personalized butler service to ensure every need is met, making it a haven for couples who want to be pampered. Whether you want to explore the island or stay in the comfort of your luxurious suite, Bora Bora’s most renowned resort delivers the ultimate romantic experience.

Highlights:

  • Overwater bungalows and villas with private pools
  • Intimate private beach dinners
  • Stunning views of the lagoon and Mount Otemanu
  • World-class spa and wellness services

2. The Aman Venice, Italy

Venice is one of the most romantic cities in the world, and The Aman Venice offers the perfect setting for couples who want to experience the city’s charm in privacy and luxury. Set in a magnificent 16th-century palace along the Grand Canal, this five-star hotel exudes opulence and offers a romantic atmosphere steeped in history.

Guests can enjoy a private gondola ride through the canals, indulge in exquisite Italian cuisine at the hotel’s restaurant, or unwind with a relaxing spa treatment. The hotel’s beautifully restored interiors include ornate ceilings, classic Venetian furniture, and stunning views of the canals.

Aman Venice’s elegant surroundings make it the perfect romantic retreat for couples looking to immerse themselves in the beauty of Venice while experiencing world-class service and luxury.

Highlights:

  • Historic 16th-century palace on the Grand Canal
  • Private gondola rides for a truly Venetian experience
  • Exclusive dining options with views of the canals
  • Spacious rooms with rich Venetian decor

3. The Mulia, Bali, Indonesia

Bali is known for its lush landscapes, serene beaches, and vibrant culture, making it an ideal destination for a romantic getaway. The Mulia in Nusa Dua offers a luxurious retreat with its private beachfront setting, spacious villas, and unparalleled service.

The resort is home to some of the most stunning pools in Bali, including an infinity pool that overlooks the Indian Ocean. Couples can enjoy intimate dinners on the beach, indulge in relaxing spa treatments, or take part in yoga and wellness activities that promote relaxation and connection.

Whether you prefer lounging by the pool, enjoying a sunset cocktail on your private terrace, or exploring Bali’s rich culture, The Mulia provides the perfect setting for a romantic escape.

Highlights:

  • Private beachfront location
  • Stunning ocean views and infinity pools
  • Wellness-focused activities and spa treatments
  • Personalized service and exclusive dining options

4. Ashford Castle, Ireland

Nestled on 350 acres of lush gardens and woodlands, Ashford Castle in County Mayo offers an enchanting escape for couples seeking a romantic, fairytale-like getaway. This medieval castle, originally built in the 13th century, has been meticulously restored to provide a blend of historic charm and modern luxury.

Guests can stay in opulent rooms or luxurious suites with views of the sprawling estate, while enjoying fine dining at the castle’s Michelin-starred restaurant. Couples can take part in a range of activities, including horse riding, fishing, and boat rides on Lough Corrib, or simply relax in the castle’s lavish spa.

Ashford Castle is a truly magical place where couples can experience a royal-style retreat and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Highlights:

  • Historic castle set in a 350-acre estate
  • Horse riding, archery, and other outdoor activities
  • Michelin-starred restaurant and luxury spa
  • Stunning lakeside views and serene surroundings

5. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto, Japan’s former imperial capital, is known for its tranquil beauty and rich cultural heritage. The Ritz-Carlton Kyoto offers a peaceful and luxurious retreat in this ancient city, blending traditional Japanese aesthetics with modern elegance.

The hotel is located along the Kamogawa River, surrounded by gardens and traditional temples, providing a serene atmosphere perfect for couples. Rooms feature elegant Japanese-inspired interiors, and the hotel’s spa offers a range of treatments to help guests unwind and rejuvenate.

Couples can explore Kyoto’s historic sites, take a stroll through the nearby gardens, or enjoy a private tea ceremony in the hotel’s tranquil surroundings.

Highlights:

  • Serene riverside location in Kyoto’s historic district
  • Traditional Japanese-inspired rooms and interiors
  • Luxurious spa and wellness offerings
  • Proximity to iconic cultural sites like Kiyomizu-dera Temple

6. Le Meurice, Paris, France

Paris, the city of love, offers countless romantic hotels, but none compare to the elegance and grandeur of Le Meurice. Located just steps away from the Louvre and the Tuileries Gardens, this five-star hotel embodies the romance and sophistication that Paris is known for.

Le Meurice boasts stunning rooms and suites with views of the Eiffel Tower or the Tuileries Gardens. Guests can enjoy Michelin-starred dining, relax at the elegant spa, or sip cocktails at the bar while taking in the beautiful city views.

Whether you want to explore the art and culture of Paris or simply enjoy a quiet romantic moment with your partner, Le Meurice provides the perfect luxury setting.

Highlights:

  • Central location near the Louvre and Tuileries Gardens
  • Michelin-starred dining and elegant lounges
  • Views of the Eiffel Tower and other iconic Paris landmarks
  • Sophisticated interiors and luxurious amenities

7. The Chedi Andermatt, Switzerland

Nestled in the Swiss Alps, The Chedi Andermatt is a modern alpine retreat perfect for couples looking for a cozy, yet luxurious, getaway in the mountains. The hotel’s architecture seamlessly blends traditional Swiss chalet style with sleek, contemporary design.

Guests can unwind by the fire in the hotel’s inviting lounges, indulge in exceptional Swiss cuisine, or relax in the spa while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding mountains. In winter, the resort transforms into a ski-in, ski-out paradise, while summer offers a chance to explore hiking trails, pristine lakes, and charming villages.

For couples who enjoy both relaxation and adventure, The Chedi Andermatt provides the perfect balance of romance and activity in one of the most stunning mountain settings in the world.

Highlights:

  • Ski-in, ski-out access in winter and hiking trails in summer
  • Modern alpine design with traditional Swiss elements
  • Extensive spa and wellness services
  • Spectacular mountain views

8. Nihiwatu Resort, Sumba Island, Indonesia

If you’re looking for ultimate seclusion and natural beauty, Nihiwatu Resort on Sumba Island in Indonesia is a hidden gem. This luxurious resort is located on a pristine stretch of beach, offering the perfect private escape for couples who want to enjoy intimacy and tranquility in a breathtaking environment.

The resort’s villas are designed with natural materials, offering the perfect balance of comfort and luxury. Many of the villas come with their own private pools and outdoor living spaces, allowing guests to enjoy their surroundings in privacy. Nihiwatu is also home to a world-class spa and offers an array of activities, including surfing, horseback riding, and cultural experiences with the local Sumbanese people.

For couples who want a romantic and adventurous escape off the beaten path, Nihiwatu is the perfect destination.

Highlights:

  • Private beachfront villas with outdoor pools
  • Extensive spa and wellness services
  • Surfing, horseback riding, and cultural activities
  • Secluded, pristine location on Sumba Island

9. Badrutt’s Palace Hotel, St. Moritz, Switzerland

For couples seeking romance in the snow-capped mountains of the Swiss Alps, Badrutt’s Palace Hotel in St. Moritz is an iconic luxury resort that has been hosting celebrities and royals for over a century. The hotel’s regal architecture and elegant interiors create a truly luxurious atmosphere, while its location overlooking Lake St. Moritz offers some of the most stunning views in Europe.

The hotel offers a range of winter activities, from skiing and ice skating to snowshoeing and tobogganing. After a day of outdoor adventures, couples can unwind at the hotel’s spa, dine at world-class restaurants, or enjoy a cocktail in the glamorous bar.

Whether you’re visiting in the winter for snow sports or in the summer for hiking and outdoor activities, Badrutt’s Palace is the perfect place to experience romance in the heart of the Swiss Alps.

Highlights:

  • Historic luxury hotel with views of Lake St. Moritz
  • Ski-in, ski-out access and outdoor winter activities
  • Elegant dining and luxury spa
  • Iconic Swiss alpine location

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel for your romantic getaway can make all the difference in creating an unforgettable experience. Whether you’re seeking tropical tranquility, alpine adventure, or the charm of a historic city, the above hotels offer unparalleled luxury and romance that will elevate your escape.

From private beachfront villas to historic castles and opulent city retreats, there is a perfect romantic destination for every couple. So pack your bags, escape the everyday, and immerse yourself in the romance of these incredible hotels – your dream getaway awaits.
