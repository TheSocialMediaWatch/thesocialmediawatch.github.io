The Most Instagrammable Hotels for the Perfect Travel Photos


Are you a social media enthusiast looking for the perfect backdrop for your travel photos? Look no further! In this blog post, we will take you on a journey to explore some of the most Instagrammable hotels around the world. From stunning architecture to breathtaking views, these hotels are sure to make your followers green with envy.

1. The Amangiri - Utah, USA

Nestled in the heart of the Utah desert, The Amangiri offers a truly unique experience for the adventurous traveler. The hotel’s sleek and minimalist design seamlessly blends into the surrounding landscape, providing the perfect canvas for your Instagram feed. Don’t miss the opportunity to capture the iconic pool, which appears to merge seamlessly with the horizon.

2. The Marina Bay Sands - Singapore

If you’re looking for a hotel that will take your breath away, look no further than The Marina Bay Sands in Singapore. This architectural marvel boasts three towers connected by a sky park, complete with an infinity pool that offers stunning views of the city skyline. Whether you’re lounging by the pool or capturing the vibrant city lights at night, this hotel is a photographer’s dream come true.

3. The Giraffe Manor - Nairobi, Kenya

For a truly unique and unforgettable experience, head to The Giraffe Manor in Nairobi, Kenya. This boutique hotel is famous for its resident herd of Rothschild giraffes, who often poke their heads through the windows during breakfast. Imagine the incredible photo opportunities of feeding these majestic creatures up close and personal. Your Instagram feed will never be the same again!

4. Fogo Island Inn - Newfoundland, Canada

Located on a remote island off the coast of Newfoundland, Fogo Island Inn offers a one-of-a-kind escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. The hotel’s contemporary design, perched on the rugged coastline, provides a stunning backdrop for your travel photos. Whether you’re capturing the dramatic seascapes or the colorful fishing villages, this hotel offers endless Instagrammable moments.

5. The Royal Mansour - Marrakech, Morocco

Step into a fairytale at The Royal Mansour in Marrakech, Morocco. This luxurious hotel is a masterpiece of Moroccan craftsmanship, with intricate tilework, ornate archways, and lush gardens. From the opulent suites to the private riads, every corner of this hotel is a photographer’s paradise. Don’t forget to explore the vibrant souks of Marrakech and capture the vibrant colors and bustling atmosphere.

6. The Icehotel - Jukkasjärvi, Sweden

For a truly unique winter experience, head to The Icehotel in Jukkasjärvi, Sweden. This hotel is entirely made of ice and snow, offering an otherworldly setting for your travel photos. Imagine sleeping in a sculpted ice room or sipping cocktails at the ice bar. From the intricately carved ice sculptures to the mesmerizing Northern Lights, this hotel will transport you to a magical winter wonderland.

7. The Taj Lake Palace - Udaipur, India

Floating gracefully on Lake Pichola, The Taj Lake Palace in Udaipur, India, is a sight to behold. This white marble palace exudes elegance and grandeur, making it a favorite among Instagrammers. Capture the reflection of the palace in the tranquil lake or wander through the ornate courtyards adorned with colorful flowers. This hotel offers a true feast for the eyes.

Conclusion

Whether you’re chasing architectural wonders, natural landscapes, or unique experiences, these Instagrammable hotels will provide the perfect backdrop for your travel photos. From the desert to the mountains, from the city to the wilderness, each hotel offers its own unique charm and beauty. So pack your bags, charge your camera, and get ready to capture the perfect travel moments that will leave your followers in awe. Happy travels and happy Instagramming!
