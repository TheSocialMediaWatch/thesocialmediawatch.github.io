The Top 10 Most Pet-Friendly Hotels in the World


Are you a pet lover who can’t bear the thought of leaving your furry friend behind when you travel? Well, you’re in luck! We’ve scoured the globe to bring you the top 10 most pet-friendly hotels in the world. These hotels go above and beyond to ensure that both you and your beloved pet have a comfortable and memorable stay. So, pack your bags, grab your pet’s favorite toy, and get ready to embark on a vacation of a lifetime!

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch - Avon, Colorado, USA

Nestled in the heart of the picturesque Colorado mountains, The Ritz-Carlton, Bachelor Gulch welcomes pets with open arms. From gourmet pet menus to luxurious bedding and toys, this hotel spares no expense in pampering your four-legged companion. The surrounding trails and outdoor activities make it a perfect retreat for adventurous pets and their owners.

2. Hotel Nikko - San Francisco, California, USA

Situated in the vibrant city of San Francisco, Hotel Nikko is renowned for its pet-friendly amenities. Upon arrival, your pet will receive a special welcome package, complete with treats, toys, and a plush bed. The hotel’s central location provides easy access to pet-friendly parks and attractions, allowing you and your pet to explore the city together.

3. The Milestone Hotel & Residences - London, United Kingdom

For a taste of British hospitality, The Milestone Hotel & Residences in London is a pet-friendly oasis in the heart of the city. Your pet will be treated to gourmet meals, walking services, and even pet spa treatments. The hotel’s proximity to Hyde Park offers a scenic setting for leisurely strolls with your furry friend.

4. Kimpton Hotel Monaco - Portland, Oregon, USA

As part of the renowned Kimpton hotel group, Hotel Monaco in Portland is a haven for pet lovers. The hotel’s pet amenities include pet-sitting services, loaner beds, and water bowls. Additionally, the hotel hosts a nightly wine reception where pets are more than welcome to join their owners.

5. The Langham - Sydney, Australia

In the bustling city of Sydney, The Langham stands out as a pet-friendly paradise. Upon arrival, pets are greeted with their own personalized welcome amenities, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay. The hotel’s proximity to the iconic Sydney Harbour allows for scenic walks and pet-friendly adventures.

6. Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise - Alberta, Canada

Nestled in the breathtaking Canadian Rockies, Fairmont Chateau Lake Louise offers a pet-friendly escape amidst stunning natural beauty. The hotel provides pet beds, water bowls, and an extensive list of outdoor activities for you and your pet to enjoy, including hiking, canoeing, and even dog sledding in the winter months.

7. The Ritz-Carlton, Vienna - Vienna, Austria

For a taste of luxury in the heart of Vienna, The Ritz-Carlton welcomes pets with open arms. The hotel offers a range of pet services, including gourmet dining options, walking services, and even a pet psychic for those curious about their furry friend’s inner thoughts. The hotel’s central location allows for easy exploration of this historic city alongside your pet.

8. The Peninsula Beverly Hills - Beverly Hills, California, USA

In the heart of the glamorous city of Beverly Hills, The Peninsula accommodates pets with the same level of luxury as their human guests. Pets are provided with designer pet beds, gourmet treats, and personalized pet menus. The hotel’s lush garden and pet-friendly surroundings offer a tranquil retreat for you and your pet.

9. The Westin Excelsior - Rome, Italy

For a pet-friendly escape in the Eternal City, look no further than The Westin Excelsior in Rome. The hotel provides a range of pet amenities, including walking services, gourmet meals, and special bedding. The hotel’s central location offers easy access to pet-friendly parks and historic sites for you and your pet to discover together.

10. The Oberoi Amarvilas - Agra, India

In the enchanting city of Agra, The Oberoi Amarvilas offers a pet-friendly retreat with a touch of Indian splendor. Pets are pampered with personalized amenities, including grooming services and gourmet meals. The hotel’s proximity to the iconic Taj Mahal provides a stunning backdrop for leisurely walks and exploration with your pet.

These pet-friendly hotels around the world go the extra mile to ensure that your furry friend feels just as welcome as you do. So, the next time you plan a vacation, consider one of these top 10 pet-friendly hotels for a truly unforgettable experience with your beloved pet. After all, the joy of travel is even sweeter when shared with our faithful companions.
