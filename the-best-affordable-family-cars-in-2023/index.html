The Best Affordable Family Cars in 2023


When it comes to selecting the right car for your family, there’s a balance that needs to be struck between affordability, safety, comfort, and practicality. Families often need a vehicle that provides ample space for passengers and cargo, delivers strong fuel efficiency, offers advanced safety features, and comes at a reasonable price. With so many options available in 2023, it can be overwhelming to narrow down the best choices. Fortunately, there are several family-friendly vehicles that meet these criteria without breaking the bank. Let’s dive into the best affordable family cars available this year.

1. Toyota Corolla Cross

Price Range: $23,000 - $28,000
Fuel Economy: 31 MPG city / 33 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Toyota Corolla Cross is a compact SUV that combines the iconic Corolla reliability with the added benefits of an SUV’s versatility. With a spacious interior, advanced technology, and excellent fuel economy, the Corolla Cross offers exceptional value for families. Whether you’re commuting to work or heading out on a weekend adventure, this affordable vehicle can handle it all with ease.

It features a smooth ride with an available all-wheel drive option, making it an excellent choice for families who live in areas with unpredictable weather. Inside, you’ll find user-friendly tech features such as a touchscreen infotainment system with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto. With ample cargo space and a comfortable cabin, the Toyota Corolla Cross is an ideal choice for families looking for an affordable, well-rounded vehicle.

2. Honda CR-V

Price Range: $27,000 - $35,000
Fuel Economy: 28 MPG city / 34 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Honda CR-V remains one of the best family cars year after year, and 2023 is no exception. This mid-size SUV has long been a go-to option for families seeking a blend of comfort, space, and practicality. The CR-V has an expansive cabin that can comfortably seat five adults, with enough legroom and headroom to keep everyone comfortable on long drives.

Honda’s reputation for reliability shines through with the CR-V, offering a well-engineered ride that balances fuel efficiency with a powerful engine. The 2023 model offers a turbocharged engine for those who want more performance without sacrificing fuel efficiency. Families will appreciate the suite of safety features like Honda Sensing, which includes adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and collision mitigation braking.

3. Kia Sorento

Price Range: $30,000 - $45,000
Fuel Economy: 24 MPG city / 29 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 6 or 7
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

For families that need more space, the Kia Sorento offers a fantastic combination of affordability and features. This mid-size SUV comes with three rows of seating, making it an ideal choice for larger families. The Sorento is equipped with a variety of modern tech features, including a 12.3-inch digital display, wireless charging, and available all-wheel drive.

In terms of safety, the Kia Sorento doesn’t disappoint, with a suite of driver-assistance technologies such as forward-collision warning, blind-spot monitoring, and rear cross-traffic alert. The Sorento offers excellent fuel economy for its size, and the hybrid model is particularly appealing for those looking for a greener option without sacrificing performance.

4. Hyundai Tucson

Price Range: $25,000 - $35,000
Fuel Economy: 26 MPG city / 33 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Hyundai Tucson is a compact SUV that provides plenty of space, advanced technology, and an upscale interior at a very affordable price. The 2023 model of the Tucson is packed with features, including an intuitive touchscreen infotainment system, wireless Apple CarPlay, and Android Auto. The interior is spacious and offers high-quality materials, making it feel more premium than its price tag suggests.

Fuel efficiency is another strong point, making the Tucson a great choice for families who want to save on fuel costs over time. The vehicle is equipped with Hyundai’s SmartSense suite of safety features, which includes forward-collision warning, lane-keeping assist, and pedestrian detection.

5. Mazda CX-5

Price Range: $26,000 - $38,000
Fuel Economy: 24 MPG city / 30 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

Mazda is known for producing cars that are fun to drive, and the Mazda CX-5 is no exception. This compact SUV combines sporty handling with practicality and an upscale design. The CX-5’s interior is a highlight, offering a well-crafted cabin with plenty of space for passengers and cargo. The upscale feel extends to the exterior as well, with a sleek design that stands out in its class.

For families looking for a vehicle that provides a bit of excitement without compromising comfort, the Mazda CX-5 is a great choice. It offers solid fuel economy and is equipped with a comprehensive list of standard safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-departure warning, and blind-spot monitoring.

6. Nissan Rogue

Price Range: $26,000 - $36,000
Fuel Economy: 26 MPG city / 34 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Nissan Rogue is a compact crossover that offers families a solid mix of affordability, practicality, and comfort. With a spacious interior, user-friendly tech features, and a smooth ride, it’s easy to see why the Rogue is a popular choice among families. The cabin is packed with high-tech features, including a touchscreen with Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and a Wi-Fi hotspot.

In addition to its comfortable and quiet cabin, the Rogue is equipped with a suite of advanced safety features, such as automatic emergency braking, blind-spot monitoring, and rear cross-traffic alert. With solid fuel economy and a smooth ride, the Nissan Rogue is a well-rounded family vehicle that offers good value for the price.

7. Subaru Outback

Price Range: $28,000 - $40,000
Fuel Economy: 26 MPG city / 33 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Subaru Outback is one of the most versatile family vehicles available. With its rugged all-wheel-drive system and impressive ground clearance, the Outback is a great choice for families who enjoy outdoor activities or live in areas that experience harsh weather. It has a spacious interior with plenty of room for passengers and cargo, making it perfect for long trips or weekend getaways.

For safety-conscious families, the Outback comes with a full suite of safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and pre-collision braking. The Outback is also known for its high resale value, making it a smart investment for families who plan to keep their vehicle for many years.

8. Ford Edge

Price Range: $37,000 - $45,000
Fuel Economy: 21 MPG city / 29 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 5
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

The Ford Edge is a midsize SUV that is perfect for families who need a bit more space but don’t want to pay for the larger, more expensive options. The Edge has a comfortable cabin with plenty of room for passengers, and the ride quality is excellent. The 2023 model features a choice of powerful engines, including a turbocharged four-cylinder and a more powerful V6 engine.

Ford’s suite of advanced safety technologies includes automatic emergency braking, lane-keeping assist, and blind-spot monitoring. The Edge is also equipped with a large touchscreen and advanced infotainment features, making it a great option for families who want both comfort and connectivity.

9. Chevrolet Traverse

Price Range: $35,000 - $55,000
Fuel Economy: 18 MPG city / 27 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 7 or 8
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

For families that need more seating, the Chevrolet Traverse is an affordable option in the three-row SUV segment. With room for up to eight passengers, the Traverse offers one of the most spacious interiors in its class. The Traverse is perfect for families who need to transport multiple kids or frequently have family members riding along. It also features ample cargo space, so you won’t have to compromise on storage for extra passengers.

The Traverse comes with a strong V6 engine, providing enough power for highway driving and towing. The 2023 model offers a host of standard safety features, including forward-collision warning, lane-departure warning, and a rearview camera.

10. Chrysler Pacifica

Price Range: $37,000 - $50,000
Fuel Economy: 19 MPG city / 28 MPG highway
Seating Capacity: 7 or 8
Safety Rating: 5-Star NHTSA

Minivans may not be the most glamorous vehicles, but they are some of the most practical and family-friendly options available. The Chrysler Pacifica is an excellent choice for families who prioritize space, comfort, and convenience. With room for up to eight passengers, the Pacifica provides plenty of room for growing families. It also offers one of the most flexible interiors, with second-row seats that can slide and third-row seats that can fold flat for maximum cargo space.

The Pacifica is also equipped with an impressive list of tech features, including an optional Uconnect Theater system that provides rear-seat passengers with entertainment on long trips. For families who want a hybrid option, the Pacifica Hybrid offers all the benefits of the gas-powered model but with better fuel economy.

Conclusion

Choosing the right family car in 2023 is about finding a balance between space, safety, comfort, and affordability. While there are countless options on the market, these vehicles offer the best combination of value, features, and family-friendly design. Whether you’re looking for a compact SUV, a spacious three-row vehicle, or a versatile minivan, these models stand out as some of the best affordable family cars of the year. As you consider your options, remember that the best family vehicle is the one that suits your lifestyle and meets your family’s unique needs.
