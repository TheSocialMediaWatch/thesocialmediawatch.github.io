The Best Fantasy Games to Escape Reality


Fantasy games have long been a gateway to worlds filled with magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests. Whether you’re looking to unwind after a long day or immerse yourself in a universe of unparalleled creativity, fantasy games offer an escape that is as immersive as it is exhilarating. From vast open worlds to intricate storylines, fantasy games have the power to transport players into realms where the impossible becomes possible. In this post, we’ll explore some of the best fantasy games to escape reality and lose yourself in magical adventures.

1. The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

Skyrim is perhaps one of the most iconic fantasy games of all time. Released in 2011 by Bethesda, this open-world RPG offers an expansive environment where players can choose their destiny, create their character, and explore a vast, richly detailed world. Set in the land of Tamriel, Skyrim is home to the nigh-legendary Dragonborn, who is destined to save the world from an ancient evil.

The game allows for unparalleled freedom, with players free to pursue the main quest or indulge in hundreds of side missions. Whether you’re crafting weapons, fighting dragons, joining guilds, or exploring ancient ruins, Skyrim’s world is alive with possibilities. The main story is engaging, and the amount of lore and detail within the game ensures that every exploration feels new and rewarding.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
Skyrim offers an unparalleled sense of freedom and immersion. Its vast world, filled with hidden secrets and rich history, ensures that no two experiences are alike. Players can lose themselves for hours, even days, in this expansive fantasy realm, making it a perfect escape from the pressures of reality.

2. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

Released by CD Projekt Red in 2015, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is often hailed as one of the best role-playing games of all time. Set in a gritty, dark medieval-inspired fantasy world, it follows the journey of Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter with a complicated past. Geralt is tasked with finding his adopted daughter, Ciri, who is being hunted by a mysterious force known as the Wild Hunt.

The game is renowned for its deep narrative, memorable characters, and richly developed world. Every decision made throughout the game impacts the story, leading to multiple endings, and giving players the feeling that their actions truly matter. The open world is breathtaking, filled with beautiful landscapes, dangerous monsters, and countless side quests that enrich the player’s experience.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
The Witcher 3’s atmospheric world is a blend of haunting beauty and tragedy. Players are thrust into a world of moral ambiguity, where every choice can have lasting consequences. The storytelling and character development are second to none, and the breathtaking environments ensure that every moment feels like a cinematic experience. For those looking for a more immersive, narrative-driven fantasy game, The Witcher 3 is a must-play.

3. Dark Souls Series

The Dark Souls series, developed by FromSoftware, is infamous for its difficulty and its deep, dark, and mysterious world. These games present players with a challenging and rewarding experience, as they take on the role of an undead character seeking to end the curse of immortality. The world of Dark Souls is bleak, with atmospheric environments that seem to be weighed down by centuries of decay and corruption.

What makes the Dark Souls series unique is its combat system, which rewards patience, strategy, and timing. Players must learn the enemy’s moves and be ready to strike at the right moment, making each fight feel like a test of skill and endurance. The interconnected world design and cryptic lore keep players coming back for more, as every corner of the world hides secrets and stories waiting to be discovered.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
Dark Souls doesn’t just provide an escape—it challenges the player. Its immersive world, filled with atmospheric environments and rich lore, invites players to dive deep into its mysteries. The sense of achievement from overcoming its challenges makes it all the more satisfying, offering an escape into a dark and unforgiving world where perseverance and resilience are key.

4. Final Fantasy XV

The Final Fantasy franchise has been a staple in the fantasy gaming world for decades, and Final Fantasy XV continues this legacy with its blend of traditional fantasy elements and cutting-edge visuals. The game follows Noctis, the crown prince of a kingdom on the brink of war, as he embarks on a journey to reclaim his throne and protect his kingdom.

Final Fantasy XV stands out for its open-world exploration, real-time combat system, and deep character development. The bond between Noctis and his friends is a central theme, and the game’s story weaves emotional moments with high-stakes action. Whether you’re driving across vast landscapes or battling colossal monsters, the game provides an expansive and emotionally charged adventure.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
The game’s combination of open-world exploration and emotional narrative offers an escape into a beautiful and dynamic world. The stunning visuals, engaging story, and memorable characters ensure that players will find themselves fully immersed in the world of Eos. The game strikes a perfect balance between fantasy and realism, offering a unique experience for players looking to escape into a magical world filled with adventure and heart.

5. World of Warcraft

For those who prefer an MMORPG experience, World of Warcraft (WoW) remains one of the most popular and influential fantasy games of all time. Since its release in 2004, WoW has created a vast, persistent world that allows players to create and customize their characters, embark on quests, and engage in epic battles with players from around the world.

WoW’s expansive world is home to numerous races, classes, and factions, all of which have rich histories and unique abilities. The game’s social aspect is one of its strongest features, as players can join guilds, participate in large-scale raids, and engage in PvP battles. WoW constantly evolves with regular expansions and updates, ensuring that players always have new content to explore.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
World of Warcraft offers one of the most immersive multiplayer experiences in gaming. The vast world, rich lore, and collaborative gameplay make it easy to get lost in the fantasy realm for hours on end. The social aspects, like forming alliances and battling alongside others, further enhance the sense of immersion. For players looking for a community-driven fantasy experience, WoW is an excellent choice.

6. Dragon Age: Inquisition

Dragon Age: Inquisition, developed by BioWare, is a fantasy RPG set in the war-torn world of Thedas. Players take on the role of the Inquisitor, a leader tasked with closing rifts in the fabric of reality and protecting the world from a mysterious, otherworldly threat. The game features a complex narrative, branching storylines, and rich character interactions that allow players to shape the world and their relationships with companions.

Dragon Age: Inquisition’s world is massive, filled with a variety of environments ranging from lush forests to war-torn cities. The game’s combat system is both tactical and action-oriented, allowing players to pause and strategize or dive straight into battle. The game’s character interactions and personal stories add depth to the experience, and the choices made throughout the game can lead to significant consequences.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
The world of Thedas is one filled with political intrigue, complex relationships, and epic battles. The game’s deep lore, branching storylines, and rich character development ensure that every playthrough offers something new. The world feels alive, and players can lose themselves in the struggle to save a realm teetering on the edge of destruction. If you love story-driven RPGs, Dragon Age: Inquisition is the perfect escape into a world of fantasy and magic.

7. Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Released by Nintendo in 2017, Breath of the Wild is an open-world fantasy adventure game that reinvents the Zelda series. Players take on the role of Link, who awakens from a long slumber to find the kingdom of Hyrule in ruins, under the control of the evil Calamity Ganon. The game’s open-world design allows for unparalleled freedom, encouraging exploration, puzzle-solving, and combat.

What makes Breath of the Wild stand out is its focus on player freedom. Unlike many other games, there are no strict paths or linear objectives to follow. Players can explore the world at their own pace, discovering secrets, battling enemies, and completing quests as they see fit. The game’s dynamic weather system, physics-based puzzles, and beautiful landscapes all contribute to an incredibly immersive experience.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
Breath of the Wild offers an escape into a beautiful and open-ended world. Its sense of freedom, combined with its atmospheric setting and stunning visuals, makes it one of the most relaxing yet engaging fantasy games available. The game encourages exploration and problem-solving, giving players the freedom to experience the world on their terms.

8. Kingdom Come: Deliverance

Kingdom Come: Deliverance is a unique fantasy RPG that focuses on historical realism and narrative-driven gameplay. Set in the medieval kingdom of Bohemia, players take on the role of Henry, a young blacksmith who embarks on a quest for revenge after his family is slaughtered in a raid. The game’s attention to detail in both its environments and combat systems is impressive, with a deep focus on realism.

The game’s combat system is based on medieval fighting techniques, with an emphasis on strategy and timing. The story is deeply immersive, with well-written characters and a world that feels alive with politics, religion, and social dynamics. Kingdom Come: Deliverance provides a more grounded fantasy experience, focusing on personal survival in a gritty, medieval world.

Why It’s a Great Escape:
For players who enjoy a more realistic take on the medieval fantasy genre, Kingdom Come: Deliverance offers a rich and immersive experience. The game’s attention to historical accuracy, combined with its deep narrative, provides an escape into a gritty, realistic version of the past. If you’re looking for a fantasy game that feels grounded and authentic, this one is worth exploring.

These are just a few of the best fantasy games that offer the perfect escape from reality. Whether you’re looking to explore vast open worlds, embark on epic quests, or immerse yourself in intricate narratives, there’s a fantasy game for every type of player. The immersive worlds, memorable characters, and rich lore of these games ensure that players can lose themselves in magical realms that provide an unforgettable experience. So, pick up your controller, dive into these worlds, and let the adventure begin!
