The Best Upscale Bars in Major Cities to Visit


When it comes to enjoying a night out, the choice of venue can significantly enhance your experience. Upscale bars offer not only exquisite drinks but also an ambiance that sets the perfect backdrop for socializing and unwinding. Whether you’re in the mood for craft cocktails, fine wines, or simply a refined atmosphere, the best upscale bars in major cities promise to deliver. Here’s a curated list of some of the most sought-after upscale bars across various metropolitan areas.

New York City: The Dead Rabbit

Nestled in the Financial District, The Dead Rabbit is a must-visit for cocktail aficionados. This award-winning bar combines Irish pub charm with a cocktail lounge that has garnered international acclaim. The extensive drink menu features classic cocktails with a twist, such as the ‘Irish Coffee’ and the ‘Dead Rabbit Grog,’ crafted with precision by expert mixologists. The bar’s dark wood interiors, vintage decor, and the warm glow of candlelight create a welcoming atmosphere, making it ideal for both gatherings and intimate conversations.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Dead Rabbit Grog’ — a refreshing concoction of rum, tropical flavors, and spices.
  • Ambiance: A blend of rustic charm and sophisticated design.
  • Experience: Live music and events enhance the vibrant nightlife.

Chicago: The Aviary

For a truly immersive experience, The Aviary in Chicago redefines the cocktail scene. Boasting a two-Michelin-star rating, this bar takes mixology to new heights, quite literally. The Aviary is famous for its innovative approach to cocktails, with drinks presented in theatrical fashion. Enjoy a cocktail served in a glass dome, filled with aromatic smoke that adds an element of surprise to every sip.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Pisco Sour’ — made with fresh ingredients and presented with flair.
  • Ambiance: Modern, sleek design with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the city.
  • Experience: Seasonal menus that showcase local ingredients and flavors.

San Francisco: Top of the Mark

With panoramic views of the Golden Gate Bridge and the San Francisco skyline, Top of the Mark is an iconic rooftop bar located atop the InterContinental Mark Hopkins Hotel. This bar offers an extensive cocktail and wine list, making it a prime spot for both tourists and locals. The sophisticated atmosphere, coupled with live music performances, creates a perfect setting for memorable evenings.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Mark Hopkins Martini’ — a classic martini with a modern twist.
  • Ambiance: Elegant décor with stunning views that change with the sunset.
  • Experience: Live jazz music enhances the sophisticated vibe.

Los Angeles: The NoMad Rooftop

The NoMad Rooftop offers a chic escape in the heart of downtown Los Angeles. This upscale bar features a vibrant atmosphere complemented by a stunning rooftop pool. Guests can enjoy a selection of inventive cocktails and gourmet bites while soaking in the city’s stunning skyline. The lush greenery and stylish décor create an inviting atmosphere perfect for socializing or relaxing after a long day.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Mexican Firing Squad’ — a fiery blend of tequila and house-made grenadine.
  • Ambiance: Lush surroundings with a relaxed yet upscale vibe.
  • Experience: Weekend DJ sessions and a lively crowd make for an energetic atmosphere.

Miami: The Broken Shaker

Located within the Freehand Hotel, The Broken Shaker is a vibrant bar known for its creative cocktails made from fresh ingredients. The eclectic outdoor space is adorned with colorful furniture and a laid-back beach vibe, making it a favorite among locals and visitors alike. The bar features an impressive selection of craft cocktails that change with the seasons, offering something new to discover with each visit.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Hibiscus Daiquiri’ — a refreshing twist on the traditional daiquiri.
  • Ambiance: Relaxed and bohemian, perfect for casual yet upscale gatherings.
  • Experience: Regular events and live music add to the lively atmosphere.

Seattle: Canon

Canon is not just a bar; it’s a sanctuary for whiskey lovers. This upscale bar is home to one of the largest whiskey collections in the world, featuring over 4,000 bottles. The dimly lit interior, with its vintage decor and intimate seating, creates a cozy yet sophisticated environment. The knowledgeable staff is eager to guide you through the extensive whiskey menu, ensuring you find the perfect sip to suit your palate.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Old Fashioned’ — made with a selection of premium whiskeys.
  • Ambiance: Intimate and warm, ideal for quiet conversations.
  • Experience: Whiskey tastings and education sessions enhance the experience.

London: The American Bar at The Savoy

The American Bar at The Savoy is an institution in London, renowned for its classic cocktails and luxurious setting. As one of the oldest cocktail bars in the UK, it exudes a sense of history and elegance. The expert mixologists craft drinks that pay homage to classic recipes while incorporating modern twists. The stunning Art Deco design and polished service make it a perfect destination for a sophisticated night out.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Savoy Cocktail’ — a timeless classic with a rich history.
  • Ambiance: Elegant interiors with a touch of vintage charm.
  • Experience: Live jazz performances elevate the sophisticated atmosphere.

Tokyo: High Five

High Five, located in the upscale Ginza district, is a hidden gem that embodies the art of Japanese hospitality. This intimate bar specializes in bespoke cocktails tailored to your preferences. The skilled bartenders take the time to understand your taste, crafting cocktails that are both innovative and deeply satisfying. The minimalist decor reflects the essence of Japanese craftsmanship, providing a serene backdrop for an unforgettable drinking experience.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘High Five Martini’ — perfectly shaken or stirred, based on your preference.
  • Ambiance: Intimate and minimalist, emphasizing focus on the drink.
  • Experience: Personalized cocktail experiences that cater to individual tastes.

Paris: Le Bar Hemingway

Located in the luxurious Ritz Paris, Le Bar Hemingway is a tribute to the legendary author who frequented the establishment. The bar is renowned for its classic cocktails and its refined yet cozy ambiance. The expert mixologists pride themselves on using the finest ingredients, ensuring each drink is a work of art. With its rich history and elegant setting, Le Bar Hemingway is a top destination for those seeking an upscale experience in the City of Lights.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Death in the Afternoon’ — a luxurious blend of champagne and absinthe.
  • Ambiance: Historic and elegant, adorned with Hemingway memorabilia.
  • Experience: A rich history combined with exceptional cocktail craftsmanship.

Toronto: Bar Raval

Bar Raval is a vibrant, Spanish-inspired bar located in Toronto’s Little Italy. Known for its stunning woodwork and uniquely designed space, this bar offers an extensive list of cocktails and a selection of Spanish tapas. The atmosphere is lively and inviting, making it a great place to enjoy drinks with friends or a romantic evening out.

Highlights:

  • Signature Drink: The ‘Gin Tonic’ — featuring unique botanicals and garnishes.
  • Ambiance: Artistic and vibrant, with a focus on community.
  • Experience: Tapas pair perfectly with a curated cocktail menu.

Conclusion

Whether you’re traveling or exploring your local city, these upscale bars offer more than just drinks; they provide an experience that engages all the senses. Each venue brings its unique charm, expertly crafted cocktails, and an unforgettable ambiance. From the intimate corners of Tokyo to the vibrant rooftops of Los Angeles, the best upscale bars promise a memorable night out. So, gather your friends or enjoy a solo evening, and discover the art of cocktail culture in these remarkable locations.
