10 Creative Cocktails to Make with Rum


Rum is one of the most versatile spirits in the world of mixology. Its rich history, diverse styles, and unique flavors make it the perfect foundation for a wide range of cocktails. Whether you prefer the deep, molasses notes of a dark rum or the bright, clean character of a white rum, there’s a rum cocktail to suit every palate. In this post, we’ll explore ten creative rum cocktails that not only highlight the spirit’s complexity but also offer refreshing twists and inventive combinations. Grab your shaker and stirrer—you’re about to embark on a flavorful journey through the world of rum mixology.

1. Tropical Sunset Rum Punch

A vibrant, fruity punch is an instant crowd-pleaser, blending tropical flavors with the warmth of rum.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz dark rum
  • 1 oz pineapple juice
  • 1 oz orange juice
  • 0.5 oz lime juice
  • 0.5 oz grenadine
  • Splash of club soda
  • Pineapple wedge and cherry for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker filled with ice, combine dark rum, pineapple juice, orange juice, and lime juice.
  2. Shake vigorously until chilled.
  3. Strain into a tall glass over fresh ice.
  4. Slowly pour grenadine down the side of the glass—watch it create a beautiful gradient.
  5. Top off with a splash of club soda for light fizz.
  6. Garnish with a pineapple wedge and a cherry.

This cocktail balances the sweetness of fruit juices with the bold character of dark rum, offering a visual and flavorful treat that’s perfect for summer evenings.

2. Spiced Vanilla Rum Old Fashioned

An intriguing twist on the classic Old Fashioned, this version incorporates spiced rum and vanilla to deepen the flavor profile.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz spiced rum
  • 0.25 oz vanilla syrup
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing glass, combine the spiced rum, vanilla syrup, and bitters.
  2. Add ice and stir gently to chill and dilute slightly.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass with one large ice cube.
  4. Express the oils from an orange peel over the glass and rim, then drop it in.

The warm spices meld with vanilla’s sweetness, creating a cocktail that’s both comforting and sophisticated.

3. Rum Basil Smash

This herby, fresh cocktail is a great way to highlight white rum with a garden-fresh twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup
  • 6-8 fresh basil leaves
  • Basil sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, muddle the basil leaves gently to release their aroma.
  2. Add white rum, lemon juice, simple syrup, and ice.
  3. Shake hard and double strain into a chilled coupe glass.
  4. Garnish with a fresh basil sprig.

The herbal notes of basil combined with citrus and rum produce a refreshing, zesty cocktail perfect for brunch or warm afternoons.

4. Coconut Rum Mojito

An island-inspired take on the classic mojito, adding coconut for a creamy twist.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz coconut rum
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup
  • 6-8 fresh mint leaves
  • Soda water
  • Lime wheel and mint sprig for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Muddle mint leaves with lime juice and simple syrup in a tall glass.
  2. Fill the glass with crushed ice.
  3. Add coconut rum and stir well.
  4. Top with soda water and give it a gentle stir.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel and mint sprig.

Coconut rum brings mild sweetness and tropical flair, elevating the mojito into a vacation-worthy sipper.

5. Smoky Pineapple Rum Sour

A tropical twist incorporating smoky notes for depth and intrigue.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz aged rum
  • 1 oz fresh pineapple juice
  • 0.75 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz honey syrup (equal parts honey and water)
  • 0.25 oz mezcal (optional for smokiness)
  • Pineapple leaf for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine rum, pineapple juice, lemon juice, honey syrup, and mezcal in a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously.
  3. Strain into a rocks glass over ice.
  4. Garnish with a pineapple leaf.

The mezcal adds an unexpected, smoky complexity that contrasts beautifully with the juicy pineapples and smooth aged rum.

6. Rum Espresso Martini

Elevate your after-dinner drink with a touch of caffeine and the richness of rum.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz dark rum
  • 1 oz fresh espresso (cooled)
  • 0.5 oz coffee liqueur
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup

Instructions:

  1. Add all ingredients to a shaker filled with ice.
  2. Shake vigorously until well chilled and frothy.
  3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.

This cocktail marries the sweetness and depth of rum with espresso’s bold flavor—great for night owls or coffee lovers.

7. Ginger Lime Rum Fizz

A zesty, bubbly concoction with a spicy kick from fresh ginger.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz lime juice
  • 0.75 oz ginger syrup (homemade or store-bought)
  • Soda water
  • Lime wedge and candied ginger for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add rum, lime juice, and ginger syrup.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Top with soda water.
  5. Garnish with a lime wedge and candied ginger.

The fiery warmth of ginger complements the crispness of rum and lime, creating a lively, refreshing drink.

8. Rum Chocolate Martini

Perfect for dessert or when you’re craving something indulgent.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz dark rum
  • 1 oz chocolate liqueur
  • 0.5 oz cream or coconut cream
  • Cocoa powder for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Combine rum, chocolate liqueur, and cream in a shaker with ice.
  2. Shake well until smooth and chilled.
  3. Strain into a martini glass.
  4. Dust with cocoa powder on top.

The luscious blend of chocolate and rum makes this cocktail feel like a sophisticated after-dinner treat.

9. Passion Fruit Rum Collins

A lively and exotic take on the classic Tom Collins, perfect for warm weather.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz white rum
  • 1 oz passion fruit puree
  • 0.75 oz lemon juice
  • 0.5 oz simple syrup
  • Club soda
  • Lemon wheel and passion fruit seeds for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine rum, passion fruit puree, lemon juice, and simple syrup with ice.
  2. Shake and strain into a tall glass filled with ice.
  3. Top with club soda.
  4. Garnish with lemon wheel and passion fruit seeds.

The tropical sweetness of passion fruit combines perfectly with lemon’s tartness and rum’s character for a vibrantly refreshing cocktail.

10. Rum and Spiced Apple Cider

A cozy cocktail that’s ideal for fall or winter.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz dark or spiced rum
  • 4 oz spiced apple cider
  • 0.25 oz maple syrup (optional)
  • Cinnamon stick and apple slice for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Heat apple cider gently until warm but not boiling.
  2. Stir in rum and maple syrup, if using.
  3. Pour into a heat-resistant glass or mug.
  4. Garnish with a cinnamon stick and apple slice.

This drink combines the comforting flavors of autumn with rum’s rich sweetness—the perfect companion for chilly nights.

Closing Thoughts

Rum’s versatile nature makes it a fantastic ingredient for both simple and sophisticated cocktails. From fruity punches to smoky sours and warm, spiced drinks, there’s a rum cocktail to fit every occasion and mood. Experimenting with different rum varieties—white, dark, spiced, or aged—can unlock even more creative possibilities.

Next time you’re in the mood to mix a drink, don’t limit yourself to classics; try one of these creative rum cocktails and surprise your taste buds with new flavor profiles. Cheers to the joyful exploration of rum!
