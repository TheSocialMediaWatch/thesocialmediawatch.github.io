The Top 10 Pet-Friendly Wineries in the US


When it comes to visiting wineries, many of us think that our furry friends are left behind. However, some wineries are pet-friendly, allowing you to bring your beloved companions along for the wine-tasting experience. In this blog post, we’ll explore the top 10 pet-friendly wineries in the US, where you can indulge in some of the world’s finest wines while your pets enjoy the beautiful scenery.

1. Jordan Vineyard & Winery, California

Located in Sonoma County, California, Jordan Vineyard & Winery is a pioneer in pet-friendly wineries. They welcome dogs of all sizes and even offer a “Pup of the Month” feature on their social media channels. With a stunning view of the vineyards, you can enjoy a wine tasting and tour with your furry friend by your side.

2. Bonny Doon Vineyard, California

Bonny Doon Vineyard, also in California, is a pet-friendly haven for wine enthusiasts.
