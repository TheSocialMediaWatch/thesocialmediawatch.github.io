Best Fantasy Novels for Fans of Adventure and Romance


Fantasy novels have long captured the imaginations of readers, offering an escape into worlds brimming with magic, mythical creatures, and epic quests. For those who crave a perfect mix of adventure and romance, the fantasy genre provides endless options that combine heart-pounding action with tender, heartwarming love stories. Whether you’re a fan of high-stakes battles, enchanted landscapes, or passionate love affairs, there’s a fantasy novel that can transport you into the worlds of both peril and passion.

In this guide, we explore some of the best fantasy novels that not only deliver epic adventures but also indulge your desire for romance. From historical settings with magical twists to contemporary worlds where love is tested by magic and fate, these books will captivate both adventure enthusiasts and romance lovers alike.

1. A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J. Maas

Sarah J. Maas’s A Court of Thorns and Roses (ACOTAR) series has become a hallmark of fantasy romance. The first book in the series introduces Feyre, a young huntress who unknowingly kills a faerie disguised as a wolf. In repayment for her crime, she is taken to the lands of the fae, where she becomes entangled in a dangerous and passionate romance with Tamlin, a powerful faerie lord.

ACOTAR seamlessly combines elements of high fantasy with emotional depth. The fae world Maas has created is rich with politics, danger, and magic, while the blossoming romance between Feyre and Tamlin, along with the unexpected twists in their relationship, will keep you hooked. As the series progresses, Maas masterfully deepens the characters and the stakes, blending adventure and romance in a way that feels both thrilling and heart-wrenching.

2. The Priory of the Orange Tree by Samantha Shannon

Samantha Shannon’s The Priory of the Orange Tree is a sweeping epic that offers a unique mix of dragon-riding adventure and fierce, blossoming romance. Set in a world where dragons and humans coexist, the novel explores multiple points of view, weaving together the stories of women leaders, dragon riders, and powerful sorceresses.

At the heart of the story is the battle to protect the world from an ancient dragon who threatens to bring destruction. Amid the high stakes of saving the realm, Shannon also presents a tender romance between two strong female characters, a beautifully layered dynamic that stands as a testament to the novel’s emotional depth. With its lush world-building and memorable characters, The Priory of the Orange Tree is an unforgettable read for fantasy fans who want a complex, diverse, and romance-infused adventure.

3. Graceling by Kristin Cashore

Kristin Cashore’s Graceling introduces readers to Katsa, a fierce warrior with a special “Grace”—a supernatural talent that makes her a deadly fighter. Set in a world where certain individuals possess extraordinary abilities, Graceling is a story of action, betrayal, and, at its heart, a love story that grows between Katsa and Po, a prince with a powerful Grace of his own.

The romance in Graceling is slow-burn and filled with emotional tension. Katsa, who has been raised to distrust love and affection, is forced to confront her feelings for Po as their journey unfolds. Their partnership is central to the novel’s plot, combining intense action with a romance that feels earned and poignant. Graceling is the perfect read for those who crave a strong, independent protagonist whose romance is as thrilling as her battles.

4. The Wrath and the Dawn by Renée Ahdieh

The Wrath and the Dawn is a retelling of the classic Arabian Nights tale, set in a world of intrigue, magic, and forbidden love. Shahrzad, the novel’s heroine, volunteers to marry Khalid, the Caliph of Khorasan, who has been executing his brides at dawn. Determined to exact revenge for her best friend’s death, Shahrzad embarks on a dangerous plan to survive the night.

What follows is a beautiful and intricate tale of love, trust, and betrayal. As Shahrzad gets closer to Khalid, she uncovers secrets that challenge her perceptions of the man she was meant to hate. The chemistry between Shahrzad and Khalid is electric, and their evolving relationship provides an emotional backbone to the novel. This is a captivating adventure steeped in romance, perfect for fans of both genres.

5. Serpent & Dove by Shelby Mahurin

Shelby Mahurin’s Serpent & Dove is an enchanting blend of romance and adventure, where magic and fierce enemies collide. The story centers around Lou, a witch on the run, and Reid, a witch hunter sworn to rid the world of magic. Their paths cross in the most unexpected way when Lou, disguised as a common thief, is forced into a reluctant marriage with Reid.

The romantic tension between Lou and Reid is palpable throughout the book, with both characters struggling to reconcile their growing attraction with their seemingly opposing beliefs. The novel mixes high-stakes adventure with heart-thumping romance, as Lou and Reid’s complicated relationship serves as a counterpoint to the larger battle between witches and humans. Serpent & Dove is an unforgettable ride that balances thrilling action with a love story that’s both passionate and full of twists.

6. Shadow and Bone by Leigh Bardugo

Leigh Bardugo’s Shadow and Bone is the first book in the Grishaverse series, a dark, immersive world where magic is a key force in both politics and warfare. The story follows Alina Starkov, a young soldier who discovers that she possesses a rare and powerful magic that could change the fate of her war-torn kingdom.

Alina’s journey is filled with adventure, magic, and intrigue, but at its core is her evolving relationship with the enigmatic Darkling, a leader of the Grisha, and the heartfelt connection she shares with Mal, her childhood friend. Bardugo deftly intertwines romance with the narrative, exploring the tension between Alina’s duty and her heart. The world-building in Shadow and Bone is rich, and the romance adds emotional depth to the high-flying adventure.

7. The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern

Erin Morgenstern’s The Night Circus isn’t your typical fantasy novel—it’s a lush, atmospheric tale of magic, rivalry, and romance set against the backdrop of a mysterious circus that only opens at night. The novel revolves around two young illusionists, Celia and Marco, who are bound by a magical competition that will determine who is the superior magician.

While The Night Circus is filled with breathtaking imagery and a unique plot, it is also a captivating love story. As Celia and Marco’s fates become intertwined, their growing love offers both tenderness and tragedy. The slow-burn romance is carefully crafted, and it feels as magical and elusive as the circus itself. For readers who crave a story that mixes adventure with a surreal, otherworldly romance, The Night Circus delivers in spades.

8. The Song of the Shattered Sands by Bradley P. Beaulieu

For those who crave epic adventure with a touch of romance, The Song of the Shattered Sands series by Bradley P. Beaulieu is a must-read. Set in a desert world of political intrigue, magic, and warring factions, the series follows the journey of Çeda, a young woman seeking revenge for the death of her family at the hands of the powerful and corrupt Khalifate.

As Çeda becomes embroiled in the larger conflicts of the land, she also finds herself drawn to multiple characters, including the enigmatic and powerful man who holds the key to unlocking her family’s legacy. The Song of the Shattered Sands weaves together high-stakes action with moments of romance that feel earned and real, making it perfect for fans of both genres.

9. The Bridge Kingdom by Danielle L. Jensen

Danielle L. Jensen’s The Bridge Kingdom is a captivating blend of high fantasy and romantic adventure. The story follows Lara, a skilled assassin who is sent to marry the king of a rival kingdom in a plot to destroy his nation from within. But as she spends more time with her new husband, she begins to question everything she thought she knew about loyalty, love, and duty.

The chemistry between Lara and her husband, Aren, is intense, and their relationship slowly unfolds as both characters discover each other’s true strengths and vulnerabilities. Full of political intrigue, deception, and unexpected romance, The Bridge Kingdom offers an engaging blend of action and love that will keep readers turning pages.

10. The Blood duology by Tessa Gratton

For readers looking for a high-octane, emotionally charged fantasy that combines adventure and romance, Tessa Gratton’s The Blood duology is a perfect choice. The first book, The Blood Keeper, introduces readers to a world of forbidden magic, betrayal, and romance. As the characters uncover dark secrets about their pasts, their growing love serves as a poignant anchor amid the whirlwind of adventure.

Gratton’s writing skillfully blends heart-pounding action with tender moments between the two protagonists, creating a narrative that feels both epic and deeply personal. The romance is both passionate and complicated, making it a compelling read for fantasy fans who crave both adventure and romance in their stories.

Conclusion

These ten fantasy novels are perfect for readers who enjoy a thrilling mix of adventure and romance. Whether you’re looking to escape into a magical realm, experience a slow-burn love story, or embark on a heart-pounding quest, these books offer something for everyone. From dragon-riding adventures to forbidden love and high-stakes battles, each of these stories blends romance and adventure in ways that are sure to captivate your imagination. So, grab your favorite beverage, curl up in your cozy reading nook, and get ready to lose yourself in these unforgettable worlds.
