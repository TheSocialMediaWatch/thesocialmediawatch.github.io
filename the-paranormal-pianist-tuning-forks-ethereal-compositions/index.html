The Role of Tuning Forks in Paranormal Pianisms

The tuning fork is a simple yet complex object, engineered to produce an individual musical pitch. Unlike traditional pianos, their mechanism relies upon a steel rod stretched taut against the strain created by pressure. The force you apply on it causes the rod to vibrate, oscillating at a specific frequency that emits an incredibly pure and resonant tone.
