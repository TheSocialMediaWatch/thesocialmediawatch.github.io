Satisfying and Delicious Pasta Salad Recipes


Pasta salads are a versatile and delicious dish that can be enjoyed year-round. Whether you’re looking for a refreshing summer side dish, a light and satisfying lunch, or a crowd-pleasing potluck contribution, pasta salads are the way to go. Here are some mouthwatering pasta salad recipes that are sure to delight your taste buds and impress your friends and family.

Classic Italian Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound rotini pasta
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup cubed mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the rotini pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, cherry tomatoes, black olives, red onion, bell pepper, cucumber, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to coat evenly.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Tangy Greek Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound penne pasta
  • 1/2 cup Greek vinaigrette dressing
  • 1/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup halved Kalamata olives
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  1. Cook the penne pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the Greek vinaigrette dressing, lemon juice, dill, oregano, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and parsley.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to coat evenly.

Spicy Southwestern Pasta Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound fusilli pasta
  • 1/2 cup chipotle ranch dressing
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1 cup diced green bell pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 cup shredded pepper jack cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fusilli pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse under cold water.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the chipotle ranch dressing, lime juice, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper to make the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked pasta, black beans, corn, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, green onions, cilantro, and pepper jack cheese.
  4. Pour the dressing over the pasta salad and toss to coat evenly.

Pasta salads are incredibly versatile, allowing you to customize them with your favorite ingredients and flavors. These recipes are just a starting point - feel free to get creative and add your own twist to make them truly your own. Whether you’re a fan of classic Italian flavors, crave the tanginess of Greek cuisine, or enjoy the boldness of Southwestern spices, there’s a pasta salad recipe for everyone. So go ahead, whip up one of these satisfying and delicious pasta salads, and watch as it becomes a staple in your recipe collection.
