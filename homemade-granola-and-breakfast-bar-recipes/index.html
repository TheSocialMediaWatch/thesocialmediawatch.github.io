Homemade Granola and Breakfast Bar Recipes


Are you tired of store-bought granola and breakfast bars that are packed with preservatives and added sugars? Why not try making your own delicious and nutritious versions at home? Not only are homemade granola and breakfast bars healthier, but they also allow you to customize the ingredients to suit your taste preferences. In this blog post, we’ll explore some mouthwatering recipes for homemade granola and breakfast bars that you can easily whip up in your own kitchen.

Crunchy Granola Delight

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup nuts (almonds, walnuts, or pecans)
  • 1/2 cup seeds (pumpkin or sunflower)
  • 1/2 cup honey or maple syrup
  • 1/3 cup coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, or apricots)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 325°F (165°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, mix oats, nuts, seeds, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a small saucepan, heat honey (or maple syrup) and coconut oil until melted. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the liquid mixture over the dry ingredients and mix well.
  5. Spread the mixture evenly on the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through.
  7. Once golden brown, remove from the oven and let it cool.
  8. Add dried fruits and mix well.
  9. Store in an airtight container.

Chewy Breakfast Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups oats
  • 1/2 cup almond butter
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup dried fruits (dates or figs)
  • 1/4 cup nuts (cashews or pistachios)
  • 1/4 cup seeds (chia or flax)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. In a food processor, pulse oats, dried fruits, nuts, seeds, cinnamon, and salt until combined.
  3. In a saucepan, heat almond butter and honey until smooth.
  4. Mix the dry and wet ingredients until well incorporated.
  5. Press the mixture firmly into the baking dish.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  7. Let it cool completely before cutting into bars.
  8. Store in the refrigerator for freshness.

By making your own granola and breakfast bars, you not only control what goes into your food but also get to enjoy the process of creating something wholesome. Experiment with different nuts, seeds, fruits, and spices to tailor these recipes to your liking. Whether you prefer crunchy granola or chewy breakfast bars, these recipes are sure to kickstart your day with a burst of flavor and energy.

So, why not give these recipes a try and elevate your breakfast game with homemade goodness? Your taste buds and body will thank you for the nutritious and delicious treats that you’ve prepared with love. Enjoy the satisfaction of knowing exactly what you’re eating and savor every bite of these homemade granola and breakfast bars. Happy cooking!

Remember, breakfast is the most important meal of the day, so make it count with these delectable homemade creations!
