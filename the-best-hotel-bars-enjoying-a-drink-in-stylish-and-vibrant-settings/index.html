The Best Hotel Bars: Enjoying a Drink in Stylish and Vibrant Settings


When it comes to enjoying a drink in style and immersing yourself in a vibrant atmosphere, hotel bars offer a unique experience that is hard to beat. These carefully curated spaces combine luxurious settings, impeccable service, and expertly crafted cocktails to create an unforgettable evening for both guests and locals alike. In this blog post, we will explore some of the best hotel bars around the world that promise to elevate your drinking experience to new heights.

1. The Rooftop Bar at The Peninsula, Hong Kong

Situated on the 30th floor of The Peninsula Hong Kong, The Rooftop Bar offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city’s stunning skyline. As you sip on your expertly crafted cocktail, you’ll be treated to a visual feast of glittering skyscrapers and Victoria Harbour. The bar’s stylish and modern decor, coupled with the warm ambiance, creates a sophisticated yet relaxed setting that is perfect for unwinding after a long day of exploring.

2. The Bar Hemingway at The Ritz, Paris

Step into The Bar Hemingway at The Ritz Paris and you’ll be instantly transported back to the glamorous era of the 1920s. This legendary bar, named after the iconic writer Ernest Hemingway, exudes an old-world charm that is hard to find elsewhere. The bartenders here are true masters of their craft, creating classic cocktails with a modern twist. With its plush leather armchairs and dimly lit ambiance, The Bar Hemingway offers a cozy and intimate setting where you can savor your drink in style.

3. The American Bar at The Savoy, London

For over a century, The American Bar at The Savoy London has been a favorite haunt for cocktail enthusiasts from around the world. This historic bar has played host to countless iconic figures, including Winston Churchill and Marilyn Monroe. The American Bar’s timeless elegance, combined with its extensive cocktail menu, makes it a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a taste of history. Whether you prefer a classic martini or a unique creation, the talented bartenders here will ensure that every sip is a memorable one.

4. The Long Bar at Raffles Hotel, Singapore

No list of the best hotel bars would be complete without mentioning The Long Bar at Raffles Hotel Singapore. This iconic establishment is the birthplace of the world-famous Singapore Sling cocktail, and stepping into The Long Bar feels like stepping back in time. The bar’s colonial-era decor, with its dark wood furnishings and rattan ceiling fans, adds to its charm. Sit back, relax, and enjoy the lively and convivial atmosphere as you sip on a perfectly mixed cocktail.

5. The Broken Shaker at Freehand, Miami

If you’re looking for a hotel bar with a laid-back and bohemian vibe, look no further than The Broken Shaker at Freehand Miami. This hip and eclectic bar has earned a reputation for its innovative cocktails and relaxed atmosphere. With its lush tropical garden and quirky decor, The Broken Shaker provides a refreshing escape from the hustle and bustle of Miami. Whether you’re a fan of classics or experimental concoctions, the talented mixologists here will ensure that your taste buds are tantalized.

In conclusion, hotel bars offer a unique and unforgettable experience for those seeking a memorable evening of drinks in stylish and vibrant settings. From the breathtaking views of The Peninsula Hong Kong’s Rooftop Bar to the timeless elegance of The American Bar at The Savoy London, each of these establishments has its own distinct charm and character. So, the next time you find yourself in one of these cities, make sure to visit these best hotel bars and let them transport you to a world of luxury and indulgence. Cheers!
