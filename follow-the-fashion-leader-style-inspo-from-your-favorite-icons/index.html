Follow the (Fashion) Leader: Style Inspo from your Favorite Icons


Fashion has always been an art form that’s heavily influenced by icons and trendsetters. These individuals have a unique ability to turn heads and stir-up a new wave of trends that others can follow. They’ve mastered the art of taking a vision and bringing it to life through fashion. As fashion enthusiasts, we can learn a thing or two from these style icons. In this post, we’ll dive into some of the most influential fashion icons of all time and the inspiration they’ve provided for us.

Audrey Hepburn

The timeless elegance of Audrey Hepburn has made her an enduring icon in the fashion industry. Her classic, sophisticated style is still relevant today, inspiring many to follow her lead. One can learn a lot from her wardrobe, which was full of simple yet chic dresses, statement necklaces, and the ever-reliable little black dress. Audrey always captured the perfect balance between elegance and simplicity, and her style continues to be a source of inspiration for the modern wardrobe.

James Dean

As an icon of the 1950s, James Dean had a distinctive, relaxed style that was all about comfort and casual cool. He popularized the classic ‘rebel look’ - a simple white T-shirt, dark-blue jeans, and a leather jacket. His style was the epitome of everyday elegance, and his easygoing attitude influenced many male fashion enthusiasts of the time onwards. Today, men emulate James Dean’s effortless style by opting for timeless pieces that exude confidence and ease.

David Bowie

David Bowie was a true fashion chameleon, constantly reinventing his look with each new era of his career. From the Ziggy Stardust days to the Thin White Duke, his outfits were a testament to his ever-evolving persona. Bold, daring, and boundary-pushing - just like Bowie’s music, his style remains a source of inspiration for those who dare to be different. His audience quickly followed suit, often emulating his eclectic and daring fashion choices.

Grace Jones

Grace Jones broke the mold of traditional fashion with her bold, avant-garde style. Her avant-garde approach to dressing transcended boundaries and played with the conventional norms of the fashion industry. Her unique sense of style, fusing elements of punk, new wave, and Jamaican flair, has undeniably had a lasting impact on modern fashion. To this day, fashion enthusiasts continue to be influenced by her daring and unconventional approach to fashion.

As we continue to follow the lead of these style icons, we can see that their influence has spanned many decades. Each of these individuals has brought something unique and powerful to the world of fashion. From Audrey Hepburn’s timeless elegance to James Dean’s casual cool, David Bowie’s bold experimentation, and Grace Jones’ avant-garde approach, these iconic figures have captivated the fashion world and left an indelible mark that we continue to see today. So, the next time you’re unsure about your outfit, just follow the lead of these incredible style icons, and you’ll surely look and feel your best.
