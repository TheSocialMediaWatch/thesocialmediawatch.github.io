The Funniest YouTube Channels That Will Make You Subscribe


YouTube, a platform filled with endless hours of entertainment, has given birth to a plethora of hilarious channels that will leave you in stitches. From comedy skits to parody videos, these channels are guaranteed to make you laugh out loud and crave more. In this article, we’ll take a look at some of the funniest YouTube channels that will make you subscribe and become a part of their loyal fan base.

1. Smosh

Smosh is one of the most popular comedy channels on YouTube, with over 25 million subscribers. The channel was created by Ian Hecox and Anthony Padilla in 2005 and has since become a household name. Smosh is known for its quirky humor, ridiculous skits, and hilarious parodies. From “Gangnam Style” to “Call of Duty,” Smosh has tackled almost every popular topic and made it funny. If you’re looking for a channel that will make you laugh, look no further than Smosh.

2. CollegeHumor

CollegeHumor is another popular comedy channel that has gained a massive following on YouTube. The channel features a range of comedic content, from sketches to music videos, and is known for its witty humor and clever writing. With over 10 million subscribers, CollegeHumor is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy. From “The Room” to “The Office,” CollegeHumor has parodied some of the most popular TV shows and movies, making them even funnier.

3. Epic Rap Battles of History

Epic Rap Battles of History is a unique comedy channel that pits historical figures against each other in epic rap battles. The channel, created by Peter Shukoff and George Watsky, features a range of hilarious battles, from Napoleon vs. Napoleon to Albert Einstein vs. Stephen Hawking. With over 10 million subscribers, Epic Rap Battles of History is a must-watch for anyone who loves history, comedy, or both.

4. Good Mythical Morning

Good Mythical Morning is a comedy channel created by Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal, also known as Rhett & Link. The channel features a range of comedic content, from sketches to challenges, and is known for its witty humor and entertaining hosts. With over 10 million subscribers, Good Mythical Morning is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy and is looking for something new and different.

5. The Slow Mo Guys

The Slow Mo Guys is a comedy channel that features a range of slow-motion videos, from explosions to slingshots. The channel, created by Gavin Free and Daniel Gruchy, is known for its hilarious commentary and clever editing. With over 5 million subscribers, The Slow Mo Guys is a must-watch for anyone who loves technology and comedy.

6. Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a comedy channel created by Daniel and Kelli Segars, a couple who share their fitness journey with their millions of subscribers. The channel features a range of workout videos, from strength training to yoga, and is known for its witty humor and entertaining hosts. With over 5 million subscribers, Fitness Blender is a must-watch for anyone who loves fitness and comedy.

7. The Key of Awesome

The Key of Awesome is a comedy channel created by Mark Douglas, a musician and comedian who shares his hilarious music videos with his millions of subscribers. The channel features a range of comedic songs, from parodies to original compositions, and is known for its witty humor and catchy melodies. With over 5 million subscribers, The Key of Awesome is a must-watch for anyone who loves music and comedy.

8. Ryan Higa

Ryan Higa is a comedian and actor who has gained a massive following on YouTube. The channel features a range of comedic content, from sketches to music videos, and is known for its witty humor and clever writing. With over 10 million subscribers, Ryan Higa is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy and is looking for something new and different.

9. The Try Guys

The Try Guys is a comedy channel created by Keith Habersberger, Ned Fulmer, Zach Kornfeld, and Eugene Lee Yang, a group of friends who share their hilarious experiences trying new things with their millions of subscribers. The channel features a range of comedic content, from food challenges to adventure activities, and is known for its witty humor and entertaining hosts. With over 5 million subscribers, The Try Guys is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy and is looking for something new and different.

10. Vsauce

Vsauce is a comedy channel created by Michael Stevens, a comedian and educator who shares his hilarious educational videos with his millions of subscribers. The channel features a range of comedic content, from science experiments to history lessons, and is known for its witty humor and clever writing. With over 5 million subscribers, Vsauce is a must-watch for anyone who loves comedy and education.

In conclusion, these 10 comedy channels are guaranteed to make you laugh out loud and crave more. From Smosh to Vsauce, each channel offers a unique brand of humor that is sure to entertain. So, if you’re looking for something new and different, be sure to check out these hilarious YouTube channels and become a part of their loyal fan base.
