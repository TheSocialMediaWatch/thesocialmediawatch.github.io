The Best Hotels for Music Festivals: Where to Stay


Attending a music festival is an exhilarating experience, but choosing the right hotel can make or break your adventure. With thousands of attendees flocking to popular festivals each year, finding the perfect accommodation close to the action is essential for a seamless experience. In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best hotels for music festivals across the globe, highlighting their unique features, amenities, and proximity to popular events.

Understanding the Importance of Location

When it comes to music festivals, location is everything. Staying near the festival venue not only saves time traveling but also allows you to soak in the entire experience. From late-night after-parties to early morning yoga sessions, being close to the action means you can maximize your fun. Moreover, a convenient location helps you avoid the hassle of parking or public transport, which can be overwhelming during peak festival times.

Top Festivals and Their Best Hotels

1. Coachella Valley Music and Arts Festival – Indio, California

Accommodations: The Ace Hotel & Swim Club

The Ace Hotel & Swim Club is a favorite among Coachella attendees. With its vintage aesthetic, vibrant atmosphere, and iconic pool parties, this hotel perfectly captures the essence of the festival. The Ace offers spacious rooms, a lively bar scene, and easy access to shuttle services that take you directly to the festival grounds.

What to Expect:

  • Vibrant Pool Parties: Enjoy live music and DJ sets by the pool during the festival weekends.
  • On-site Dining: Indulge in delicious meals at their restaurant, serving local and organic fare.
  • Community Vibe: Engage with fellow festival-goers in a relaxed and friendly environment.

2. Glastonbury Festival – Somerset, England

Accommodations: Glastonbury Hotel

Located just a short drive from the festival grounds, the Glastonbury Hotel offers comfort and convenience. This charming property features cozy rooms, an on-site pub, and a welcoming atmosphere. With easy access to public transport, getting to the festival is a breeze.

What to Expect:

  • Local Cuisine: Savor traditional British dishes at the hotel’s restaurant.
  • Charming Decor: Experience the quaint charm of the hotel, reflective of the local area.
  • Proximity to Attractions: Explore the beautiful Somerset countryside when you’re not at the festival.

3. Lollapalooza – Chicago, Illinois

Accommodations: The Chicago Athletic Association Hotel

The Chicago Athletic Association Hotel is a stunning historic building that combines luxury with a rich history. Located just a stone’s throw away from Grant Park, where Lollapalooza takes place, this hotel features stylish rooms and a rooftop bar offering breathtaking views of the skyline.

What to Expect:

  • Rooftop Views: Enjoy cocktails with an unparalleled view of the Chicago skyline.
  • Cultural Events: Participate in various cultural events hosted by the hotel, enhancing your festival experience.
  • Modern Amenities: Experience a blend of historical charm and modern comforts.

4. Tomorrowland – Boom, Belgium

Accommodations: Martin’s Patershof

For a truly unique experience, consider staying at Martin’s Patershof, a former church turned hotel. Located just a short drive from Boom, this stunning property offers a one-of-a-kind experience, complete with stained glass windows and a serene atmosphere.

What to Expect:

  • Unique Accommodations: Stay in rooms that were once chapels, each with its own character.
  • On-site Dining: Enjoy gourmet meals inspired by local Belgian cuisine.
  • Relaxing Ambiance: Unwind in a tranquil environment after a day of festival excitement.

5. Bonnaroo Music and Arts Festival – Manchester, Tennessee

Accommodations: The Lodge at the Bonnaroo Music Festival

A unique option for Bonnaroo attendees is The Lodge at the Bonnaroo Music Festival. This pop-up hotel offers luxury tents equipped with comfortable bedding and amenities, making it an ideal choice for festival-goers who want to experience the festival atmosphere without sacrificing comfort.

What to Expect:

  • Comfortable Tents: Enjoy the convenience of glamping in style.
  • Exclusive Amenities: Access to private showers and restrooms, ensuring a comfortable stay.
  • Community Atmosphere: Connect with fellow Bonnaroo fans right at the festival.

6. Austin City Limits Festival – Austin, Texas

Accommodations: The Driskill Hotel

The Driskill Hotel is an iconic Austin landmark that provides a luxurious stay just minutes from Zilker Park, the home of the Austin City Limits Festival. This historic hotel combines Southern charm with modern amenities, making it a perfect retreat after a day of music.

What to Expect:

  • Historic Charm: Experience the rich history of Austin while enjoying modern comforts.
  • On-site Dining: Savor delectable Southern cuisine at their acclaimed restaurant.
  • Close to the Action: Easy access to the festival grounds for a hassle-free experience.

7. Electric Daisy Carnival – Las Vegas, Nevada

Accommodations: The Venetian Resort

Experience the vibrant energy of Las Vegas by staying at The Venetian Resort during the Electric Daisy Carnival. This opulent resort features spacious suites, a world-class casino, and a variety of dining options, making it a luxurious choice for festival attendees.

What to Expect:

  • Luxurious Suites: Enjoy spacious and beautifully designed rooms with all the amenities.
  • Entertainment Options: Take advantage of the resort’s entertainment offerings, from shows to nightclubs.
  • Convenient Location: Close proximity to the Las Vegas Motor Speedway, where the festival is held.

8. Fuji Rock Festival – Niigata Prefecture, Japan

Accommodations: Naeba Prince Hotel

Set against the stunning backdrop of the mountains, the Naeba Prince Hotel is the perfect base for attending the Fuji Rock Festival. With comfortable accommodations and scenic views, this hotel offers a serene retreat after a day of music.

What to Expect:

  • Mountain Views: Enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • On-site Hot Springs: Relax in traditional Japanese onsen hot springs after a long day at the festival.
  • Easy Access to the Festival: Shuttle services are available to transport guests to the festival grounds.

9. Reading and Leeds Festivals – England

Accommodations: Crowne Plaza Reading East

The Crowne Plaza Reading East is a fantastic option for those attending the Reading and Leeds Festivals. This modern hotel offers comfortable accommodations and is conveniently located near transport links, making it easy to reach both festival sites.

What to Expect:

  • Modern Comforts: Enjoy well-appointed rooms with contemporary design.
  • On-site Bar and Restaurant: Relax with a drink and a meal after a day of music.
  • Convenient Transport Links: Quick access to trains and buses heading to the festival.

10. Isle of Wight Festival – Isle of Wight, England

Accommodations: The Priory Bay Hotel

For a picturesque stay during the Isle of Wight Festival, consider The Priory Bay Hotel. Nestled in beautiful gardens and just a short distance from the festival grounds, this hotel offers a tranquil escape with a touch of luxury.

What to Expect:

  • Stunning Grounds: Explore the beautifully landscaped gardens and private beach access.
  • Gourmet Dining: Indulge in exquisite meals prepared with locally sourced ingredients.
  • Relaxing Atmosphere: Enjoy a peaceful retreat after a day of festival fun.

Tips for Booking Your Festival Stay

1. Book Early

Music festivals often see a surge in accommodation bookings as the event date approaches. To secure the best rates and desired hotels, consider booking your stay as soon as tickets are purchased.

2. Check Cancellation Policies

Life can be unpredictable, so it’s wise to check the cancellation policies of your chosen hotel. Flexible booking options can save you stress if your plans change.

3. Consider Alternative Accommodations

In addition to hotels, consider alternative lodging options like Airbnb, hostels, or boutique guesthouses. These can sometimes offer unique experiences and more budget-friendly choices.

4. Read Reviews

Before making a reservation, read recent reviews from fellow festival-goers. Their experiences can provide valuable insight into the hotel’s atmosphere, cleanliness, and proximity to the festival.

5. Use Loyalty Points

If you frequently travel, consider using loyalty points from hotel chains to offset your costs. Many hotels offer rewards programs that can lead to significant savings.

Conclusion

Choosing the right hotel for a music festival can significantly enhance your experience, allowing you to focus on enjoying the music and the atmosphere. Whether you prefer luxury accommodations or unique lodging options, there are plenty of fantastic hotels near major festivals. By booking early and considering your options, you can ensure a memorable stay that complements your festival adventure. So pack your bags, grab your tickets, and get ready for an unforgettable music-filled getaway!
