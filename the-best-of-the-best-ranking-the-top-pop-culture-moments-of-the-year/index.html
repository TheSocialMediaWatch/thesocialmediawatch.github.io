The Best of the Best: Ranking the Top Pop Culture Moments of the Year


Welcome to our countdown of the most unforgettable pop culture moments you just couldn’t ignore in 2022. From shocking celebrity revelations to groundbreaking achievements in entertainment and sports, we’ve compiled a list of the top events that captured the attention and hearts of millions around the world. Prepare to be amazed and reminisce about the moments that had us talking all year long.

No. 10: Squid Game’s Global Phenomenon

In a year where streaming services dominated the entertainment scene, the South Korean series Squid Game captured viewers’ imaginations and became a global phenomenon. The dramatic and gripping narrative, combined with its unique premise, made it a must-watch for millions worldwide.

No. 9: Williams Sisters’ Historic Aus open Triumph

Serena and Venus Williams made history at the Australian Open, reaching the women’s doubles final and winning their record-equalling 14th Grand Slam as a team. The iconic pairing once again displayed their incredible talent and cemented their status as one of the greatest duos in tennis history.

No. 8: Harry and Meghan’s Tell-all Interview

In a 2-part CBS special, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle shared their emotional and revealing account of life within the British royal family. The interview brought a spotlight to the struggles they’ve faced, igniting conversations about mental health, the monarchy, and the media’s role in the disclosure.

No. 7: The Return of Live Events

2022 saw the return of live events, as restrictions lifted and people were eager to come together once again. Music festivals, concerts, sports games, and theatre performances enjoyed a renaissance, restoring a sense of normalcy in the world.

No. 6: Ridley Scott’s Comeback with House of Gucci

Acclaimed director Ridley Scott enjoyed a successful return to the big screen with House of Gucci, starring Lady Gaga and Adam Driver. The film showcased a stylish and gripping narrative of the Gucci family’s power struggle and maintained Scott’s legacy as a master storyteller.

No. 5: Succession’s Patriarch Wins Emmy

Patty LuPone took home a well-deserved Emmy for her gripping performance as ‘Dr. Nan Sarf’ in the hit series Succession. Known for her outstanding work in the acclaimed musical “Evita,” LuPone showcased her versatile acting skills in a dramatic departure from her previous roles.

No. 4: The Inclusion of Women’s Football in the Olympics

The inclusion of women’s football in the Olympics brought a new level of recognition to the women’s game. This historic moment showcased the talent and accomplishments of female athletes worldwide and paved the way for future growth in women’s football.

No. 3: Marvel’s Thor: Love and Thunder

Fans eagerly awaited the release of Marvel’s Thor: Love and Thunder, with many accolades going to the film’s surprising but exhilarating director, Taika Waititi. The film was a fun and entertaining addition to the Marvel Cinematic Universe, introducing new characters and delivering a fresh and unique take on a beloved superhero franchise.

No. 2: Harry Potter and the Cursed Child’s Success

In a phenomenal return to the stage, the enchanting world of J.K. Rowling’s wizarding realm rose again in Harry Potter and the Cursed Child. The play was a critical and commercial success, enchanting fans both young and old and proving once again that the magic of Hermione, Harry, and Ron will never fade.

No. 1: Steve Jobs’ Biographical Drama Pulled from Apple, then Returned

One of the year’s most-talked-about pop culture moments was Apple’s abrupt decision to remove the Steve Jobs biographical drama from their platform. In an unprecedented move, Apple then opted to bring the film back, reigniting conversations about creative control and streaming rights. This captivating story, told through the life and times of one of the world’s most iconic and influential figures, undeniably topped our list of the year’s most memorable pop culture moments.

From groundbreaking entertainment and celebrity revelations to the world-leading achievements of women in sports, these top pop culture moments of 2022 will forever be a part of our collective memory. As we look back on this whirlwind of a year, we can only wonder what 2023 has in store for us. Until then, keep celebrating the best of the best!
