Hotels with the Best Pool Areas for Families


When planning a family vacation, one of the top priorities is often finding a hotel with a fantastic pool area to keep everyone entertained while on break. A great pool area can be the highlight of a trip, offering relaxation, exercise, and plenty of opportunities for creating lasting memories. To help you make the most of your family getaway, we’ve chosen four hotels with outstanding pool areas that cater to families of all ages.

  1. The Walt Disney World Swan and Dolphin Resort, Florida, USA: Disney’s Swan and Dolphin resort offers two impressively sized pools, the Fantasia and the Ariel. Both provide children and adults with fun, Florida-style experiences. The Fantasia pool has a vibrant water feature that spurts jets of water, perfect for little ones to run around and get soaked. Ariel’s Grotto, part of the Ariel pool, has a hot tub, water slide, and underwater bench for a relaxing experience. With an array of activities available, from pool games to poolside menus, families can enjoy their time in this magical oasis.

  2. The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort, Maldives: Surrounded by the stunning Indian Ocean, the St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort is paradise for families. The resort offers a variety of pools, some with breathtaking views and others with exciting amenities. The main pool is a pristine, 50-meter-long oasis with intimate nooks, perfect for playing games or spending quality time. The zero-entry pool by the kids’ club, OW-DOH, allows young guests to enjoy a safe environment. The resort also offers the Fluttering Hearts Kids Club, where little ones can make donut races, cook their own pizzas, and have fun in an organized setting, while parents relax at the pool.

  3. The Grande Real Santa Luzia Resort & Spa, Portugal: The picturesque Grande Real Santa Luzia Resort & Spa not only boasts large and heated pools but also delivers a fantastic experience for the whole family. In addition to their main lagoon-style pool with a wading area, the resort offers a kids’ pool looking out to the ocean. With their unique Jungle Zoo and Dolphin and Seal show nearby, both children and adults can learn about fascinating marine life during their downtime. The family-friendly orientation makes it appealing for all ages, ensuring an unforgettable vacation.

  4. The Ritz-Carlton, Lake Tahoe, California, USA: Families seeking the ultimate outdoorsy experience can look no further than the Ritz-Carlton, Lake Tahoe. While the stunning pool area allows for relaxation and refueling, the adventure at this resort goes beyond. With the Sierra Nevada and beautiful nature all around you, soak up the vibe while the kids enjoy the Ritz Kids club, which offers arts and crafts, scavenger hunts, and more, surrounded by the splendor that is Lake Tahoe. When the weather turns colder, an outdoor heated pool keeps the fun going no matter the season.

Your pool experience shouldn’t end when the sun goes down, so be sure to inquire about the hotel’s night swimming facilities and activities. With diverse offerings ranging from poolside movies to night dives, each hotel is equipped to provide an unparalleled family experience.

These luxury hotel options prioritize entertainment, relaxation, and fun for families, going beyond the traditional hotel expectation to create memories that will last a lifetime. Take your pick, and prepare for a dream vacation with vibrant pool areas, leaving you feeling refreshed, entertained, and welcomed. Happy family vacation planning!
