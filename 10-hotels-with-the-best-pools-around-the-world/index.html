10 Hotels with the Best Pools Around the World


When it comes to planning a luxury getaway, the allure of an exceptional swimming pool often stands as a top consideration. Pools are more than just places to swim—they’re the centerpiece of relaxation, social interaction, and breathtaking views that elevate the entire hotel experience. From infinity edges blending into pristine ocean horizons to opulent rooftop oases overlooking vibrant cityscapes, the world’s finest hotels boast pools that redefine elegance and leisure. Let’s dive into ten of the most spectacular hotel pools around the globe, each offering something uniquely spectacular for travelers seeking memorable aquatic bliss.

1. Marina Bay Sands – Singapore

Arguably the most iconic hotel pool in the world, Marina Bay Sands’ infinity pool perches atop its three towers, 57 stories high. Spanning 150 meters, this rooftop marvel offers unparalleled views of Singapore’s skyline and the bustling Marina Bay below. It’s an unparalleled mix of architectural ingenuity and luxury, providing guests a chance to swim literally above the city lights. The pool’s design cleverly blurs the lines between water and sky, making it a favorite spot for photographers and travelers alike. Adjacent cabanas and loungers create an exclusive atmosphere, making it the perfect urban sanctuary.

2. Hanging Gardens of Bali – Indonesia

Set amid lush rainforests, the Hanging Gardens of Bali’s twin-level infinity pools offer a mesmerizing vantage of the Petanu River Valley. The larger main pool stretches 25 meters and is famous for its vanishing edges that appear to merge seamlessly with the surrounding jungle canopy. This resort’s focus on harmony with nature is evident in materials and design, promoting a sense of peace and balance. Swimming here is a meditative experience, with the gentle sounds of tropical fauna complementing the cool water and verdant views.

3. The Joule – Dallas, Texas, USA

The Joule Hotel’s rooftop pool in Dallas combines retro charm with contemporary design, boasting a striking 10-foot cantilevered edge that extends beyond the building’s façade. This daring feature creates a thrilling sensation of swimming over the cityscape with nothing but glass below. The pool’s urban setting is juxtaposed with luxurious amenities, such as a sauna and fire pits surrounding the deck. Whether sunbathing or taking a dip at sunset, guests enjoy a unique blend of bold architectural elements and metropolitan energy.

4. Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti – Tanzania

For those captivated by wildlife and wilderness, the Four Seasons Safari Lodge in Serengeti offers a pool that’s nothing short of extraordinary. Surrounded by the vast African plains, the infinity pool provides sweeping views of a watering hole frequented by elephants, zebras, and lions. Floating in the warm water while spotting wildlife in their natural habitat is an unforgettable experience. The lodge’s design incorporates natural materials, blending effortlessly into the environment and emphasizing eco-luxury as part of the adventure.

5. Hotel Caruso – Amalfi Coast, Italy

Perched on a cliff in Ravello, Hotel Caruso features a pool that looks as if it’s floating above the Mediterranean Sea. The historic hotel boasts a 17th-century, hand-painted majolica-tiled infinity pool, one of the most beautifully ornate pools worldwide. Surrounded by terraced lemon gardens and stunning coastal vistas, the pool area exudes timeless romance and luxury. Here, guests can savor Italian delicacies poolside while gazing out at the shimmering blue waters of the Amalfi Coast.

6. The Oberoi Udaivilas – Udaipur, India

Set alongside the serene Lake Pichola, The Oberoi Udaivilas is a palatial property inspired by traditional Rajput architecture. The centerpiece is an opulent pool carved out from marble, featuring intricate motifs and surrounded by lush gardens. By night, floating candles create a magical ambiance, marrying royal heritage with modern luxury. This pool isn’t just a swim spot—it’s a sensory journey steeped in rich cultural history and breathtaking aesthetics.

7. Belmond Hotel Caruso — Ravello, Italy

A distinctive gem on the Amalfi Coast, Belmond Hotel Caruso’s infinity pool offers guests a surreal swimming experience surrounded by towering cliffs and panoramic sea views. The pool, elevated hundreds of feet above the sea, is complemented by lush gardens and a romantic atmosphere that makes it a top choice for honeymooners and luxury travelers. The water’s edge seamlessly blends into the horizon, amplifying feelings of freedom and tranquility.

8. The Cambrian – Adelboden, Switzerland

In a snowy alpine setting, The Cambrian’s heated outdoor pool provides a stunning contrast of warmth against the crisp mountain air. Overlooking the Bernese Alps, the pool becomes a sanctuary for skiers and hikers alike seeking relaxation. The minimalist design enhances the natural beauty, with floor-to-ceiling windows inside offering views of the pool and mountains. It’s a sublime mix of adventure and indulgence wrapped in breathtaking scenery.

9. Jade Mountain – St. Lucia

Jade Mountain’s pools are extraordinarily private, with each suite featuring its own infinity pool overlooking the famous Pitons and Caribbean Sea. This architectural marvel uses open walls to dissolve boundaries between indoor and outdoor living. The pools are sunken into the sanctuaries, creating personal retreats that emphasize privacy and natural beauty. Organic materials and sustainable design highlight the eco-conscious ethos of the resort, making it a haven for serene luxury.

10. Amangiri – Canyon Point, Utah, USA

Nestled in a dramatic desert landscape, Amangiri’s pool is a sleek and minimalistic oasis designed to harmonize with the surrounding sandstone cliffs. The long, rectangular pool reflects the striking natural vistas and offers a tranquil spot for contemplation or cooling off after desert excursions. The pool’s calming hues and understated sophistication radiate an exclusive sense of calm. Here, luxury meets rugged wilderness, creating an unforgettable location for pool lovers seeking unique environments.

Final Thoughts

The art of designing hotel pools goes well beyond functionality nowadays. These pools serve as experiential focal points that tell stories of place, culture, and innovation. Whether suspended high above a megacity, integrated into natural landscapes, or adorned with historic tilework, the world’s most marvelous hotel pools promise far more than just a swim—they offer immersion into dreams.

Choosing a hotel with a remarkable pool can transform any vacation, amplifying relaxation while providing spectacular photo-worthy moments. From tropical jungles to alpine summits, these 10 pools stand as icons of hospitality luxury that travelers can seek out to make every journey unforgettable. Next time you book a trip, dive into one of these extraordinary experiences that redefine poolside living around the globe.
