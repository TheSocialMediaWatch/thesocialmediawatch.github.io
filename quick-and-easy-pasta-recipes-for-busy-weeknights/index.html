Quick and Easy Pasta Recipes for Busy Weeknights


Are you tired of spending hours in the kitchen after a long day at work? Do you crave a delicious and satisfying meal without the hassle? Look no further! In this blog post, we’ll share some mouthwatering pasta recipes that are quick and easy to prepare, perfect for those busy weeknights when time is of the essence.

Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of linguine
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the linguine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked linguine to the skillet and toss until the pasta is evenly coated with the creamy sauce.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

One-Pot Tomato Basil Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces of spaghetti
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cups of vegetable broth
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the spaghetti, diced tomatoes (with their juice), vegetable broth, onion, garlic, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bring the mixture to a boil over high heat, then reduce the heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer for 10-12 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked and most of the liquid has been absorbed.
  3. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. Serve the pasta hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces of fettuccine
  • 1 pound of large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add the shrimp to the skillet and cook until pink and opaque, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Stir in the lemon zest and juice, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss until the pasta is well coated with the lemon garlic sauce.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley and serve hot.

There you have it, three quick and easy pasta recipes that will save you time in the kitchen without compromising on flavor. Whether you’re craving a creamy and indulgent dish or a light and refreshing one, these recipes have got you covered. So, the next time you find yourself pressed for time on a busy weeknight, whip up one of these delicious pasta dishes and enjoy a hassle-free meal that will satisfy your taste buds. Happy cooking!
