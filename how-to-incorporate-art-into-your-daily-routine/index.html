How to Incorporate Art into Your Daily Routine


Life can get busy, and it’s easy to feel bogged down by the demands of our daily routines. Amidst work, family, and other commitments, finding ways to unwind and relax is crucial. Art, in all its various forms, offers an excellent way to accomplish that, but how can you make time for it amidst your packed schedule?

The beauty of art is that it can fit into any lifestyle. Here are a few ways to incorporate art into your daily routine and enjoy its therapeutic powers.

  1. Start the day on an artistic note

Art doesn’t have to be a luxury reserved for weekend painting sessions. Begin your day with art by setting an artistic screensaver or wallpaper on your phone or computer. Then, throughout the day, take a few moments to look at it and reflect on its colors, shapes, and textures.

  1. Embrace your work environment

Including art in your workplace doesn’t have to mean hanging paintings on the walls—though that’s always an option. Make use of the digital tools at your disposal. Set your screensaver to an inspiring piece, or choose an artistic background for your computer background. You can also snake a sketchbook into your routine, creating drawings during breaks or when the workday allows.

  1. Repurpose what you already have

You don’t need a large collection of art to enjoy it daily. Look at the items around you—the coffee mug, the clothing items, the towels, and the plates—and consider how you can transform them into artistic expressions. Mixing and matching your scarves can be an artistic expression, as can arranging your dishes in a visually pleasing manner.

  1. Dedicate time for creative expression

While not everyone can devote hours to painting or sculpting each day, small amounts of time can still be effective. Pick up a new skill like knitting, calligraphy, or cooking, which allows you to generate something beautiful and artistic while also engaging your creativity. Dedicate a few minutes each day to this activity, protecting the time like you would any other appointment.

  1. Create a home HQ for art

Your home doesn’t have to look like a modern art gallery to benefit from art. Ensure your living environment supports relaxation and artistic interests by creating a designated space in your home for artistic activities. This space needn’t be large—it can be a small corner filled with interesting books or an entire room devoted to creative expression. Ensure it offers a sense of refuge, some solitude, and above all, inspiration.

  1. Combine art with exercise

Art and physical exercise might not seem related, but they can complement each other beautifully. Consider walking in a botanical garden, practicing yoga in an art museum or park, or taking dance lessons infused with choreography from a famous ballet or modern dance troupe.

  1. Connect with others through art

Sharing creative experiences with others creates a sense of camaraderie and fun. Join a local club like a book club or a knitting circle, or explore classes that introduce you to new artistic techniques. These outings offer an opportunity to meet people with similar interests and form meaningful connections through art.

Embracing artistic experiences can truly enhance your daily routine. Incorporating art into your life might seem like a significant undertaking, but even the tiniest steps can make a significant difference. The world is filled with artistic expression, so find the one that resonates with you and makes you feel alive. In doing so, you’ll foster creativity, inspiration, and relaxation, ultimately leading to a more fulfilling daily life.
