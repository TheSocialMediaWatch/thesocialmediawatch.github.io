The Best Co-Op Games for PlayStation 5


When it comes to gaming, few experiences rival the thrill of teaming up with friends or family to tackle challenges, explore vast worlds, or simply enjoy shared adventures. The PlayStation 5 has taken cooperative gaming to new heights with its powerful hardware, faster loading times, and improved graphics. Whether you’re sitting next to each other or connecting online, the PS5 offers an impressive lineup of co-op games that cater to every taste, from intense shooters to strategic puzzles and sprawling RPGs.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll dive into the very best co-op games available on PS5, highlighting what makes each title stand out and why they deserve a place in your gaming library.

1. It Takes Two – A Masterpiece in Cooperative Gameplay

One of the most celebrated co-op games designed exclusively for two players, It Takes Two is a charming blend of heartwarming storytelling and inventive gameplay mechanics. Developed by Hazelight Studios and published by Electronic Arts, this title offers an extraordinary experience where players control a couple, Cody and May, who are navigating a miniaturized world filled with puzzles and challenges shaped around their relationship dynamics.

  • Gameplay Variety: Each level introduces unique mechanics—from platforming to rhythm games or physics-based puzzles—that require teamwork and creative problem solving.
  • Narrative Depth: The story strikes a perfect balance between humor, emotion, and whimsy, making the journey as captivating as the gameplay itself.
  • Split-Screen and Online Play: Whether you prefer couch co-op or online, the game supports both seamlessly.

It Takes Two isn’t just a game; it’s an emotional adventure that underscores the power of collaboration. It’s a must-play for anyone looking to experience co-op gaming beyond typical genres.

2. Returnal: Ascension Mode – Co-Op in a Roguelike Sci-Fi Setting

Returnal initially launched as a single-player roguelike shooter but later introduced Ascension Mode, an exciting addition allowing two-player cooperative gameplay. Set on a mysterious, hostile alien planet, Returnal challenges players with procedurally generated environments and relentless enemies.

  • Challenging Combat: The game’s fast-paced, bullet-hell shooting mechanics demand precise coordination between players.
  • Procedural Exploration: No two runs feel the same, promoting replayability and strategic cooperation.
  • PS5 Features: Utilizes the DualSense controller’s haptic feedback to amplify immersion.

Though demanding and intense, Returnal: Ascension Mode provides a fresh take on co-op gaming through its blend of sci-fi storytelling and roguelike difficulty.

3. Sackboy: A Big Adventure – Fun and Accessible Platforming for All

If you’re looking for a family-friendly co-op platformer bursting with creativity and charm, Sackboy: A Big Adventure is one of the best choices for the PS5. A spin-off from the LittleBigPlanet series, this title invites up to four players to join Sackboy on a quest filled with imaginative levels and delightful challenges.

  • Accessible Gameplay: Suitable for players of all ages and skill levels.
  • Vibrant Worlds: Each stage boasts colorful environments and clever level design.
  • Local and Online Co-Op: Seamlessly switch between couch play and online multiplayer.

Sackboy: A Big Adventure combines nostalgia with modern polish, making cooperative gameplay joyous and adventurous.

4. Borderlands 3 – Loot, Laughs, and Firepower Galore

The Borderlands series has long been a staple for cooperative shooters, and Borderlands 3 shines on the PS5 with enhanced visuals and performance. Featuring a sprawling open world, dozens of characters/classes, and a wealth of loot to discover, it’s perfect for those who love frantic gunfights coupled with humor and mayhem.

  • Four-Player Co-Op: Supports up to four players online, ideal for groups.
  • Diverse Playstyles: Plenty of character classes and weapon types to suit any strategy.
  • Engaging Story and Quests: The narrative and numerous side missions keep things lively.

Whether you’re a seasoned looter shooter veteran or new to the genre, Borderlands 3 delivers endlessly entertaining co-op gameplay.

5. Destiny 2 – A Persistent World of Cooperative Online Action

Destiny 2 is a powerhouse MMO shooter that emphasizes cooperative play through strikes, raids, and other PvE (player versus environment) activities. With its next-gen update tailored for the PS5, the game boasts richer graphics, quicker loading, and refined mechanics.

  • Varied Co-Op Modes: Explore story missions, challenging raids requiring tight teamwork, or weekly events with friends.
  • Cross-Play Support: Connect with players on other platforms, expanding your cooperative gaming circle.
  • Continuous Updates: Fresh content keeps the community engaged.

This game appeals to players who enjoy long-term engagement and strategic team-building in a sci-fi universe.

6. Diablo II: Resurrected – Classic Hack and Slash Returns

Blizzard’s remastered Diablo II brings back one of the most iconic cooperative RPG experiences, optimized for the PS5. The dark fantasy world, addictive loot system, and action-packed battles hold up perfectly even years later.

  • 4-Player Online Co-Op: Team up with friends to conquer dungeons and bosses.
  • Enhanced Visuals: Improved graphics without losing the classic feel.
  • Deep Customization: Character builds and skill trees offer endless replayability.

For fans of dungeon crawlers and loot-hunting, Diablo II: Resurrected offers timeless cooperative fun.

7. Ghost of Tsushima: Legends – Mythical Co-Op Adventures in Feudal Japan

The Ghost of Tsushima main game is a fantastic single-player experience, but its Legends expansion has transformed it into an engaging multiplayer cooperative mode. It combines action, stealth, and mythic elements that promote synergy among players.

  • Two to Four Player Co-Op: Engage in story missions, survival waves, and raid bosses.
  • Unique Classes: Each player picks from roles like Samurai or Assassin, each with special abilities.
  • Impressive Visuals: The PS5 elevates the game’s already stunning art style.

Ghost of Tsushima: Legends reinvents the samurai experience for co-op enthusiasts seeking a blend of history and fantasy.

8. Overcooked! All You Can Eat – Culinary Chaos in a Co-Op Party Game

For a more light-hearted take on co-op gaming, Overcooked! All You Can Eat combines Overcooked 1 and 2 with all their expansions into one definitive bundle optimized for the PS5.

  • Up to Four Players: Work together under hilarious, time-sensitive kitchen conditions.
  • Crazy Levels: Kitchens on moving trains, underwater, and more.
  • Local and Online Co-Op: Perfect for game nights.

It’s a recipe for laughter, coordination, and frantic fun, ideal for casual players and families.

9. Outriders – A Dark Sci-Fi Shooter-RPG with Cooperative Action

Combining intense third-person shooting with RPG elements, Outriders offers high-octane combat in an alien world. It supports a seamless drop-in-drop-out cooperative experience for up to three players.

  • Varied Classes and Builds: Customize your character’s skills and gear.
  • Fast-Paced Combat: Combines cover mechanics, abilities, and gunplay.
  • Strong Narrative: The story’s dark tone adds emotional weight.

If you enjoy blending RPG progression with shooter mechanics in co-op, Outriders is a compelling choice.

10. Minecraft – Endless Creativity with Friends

No co-op game list is complete without Minecraft. The PS5 version brings the perennial favorite pixelated sandbox with smooth performance and cross-platform multiplayer.

  • Create and Explore: Build incredible worlds with friends.
  • Multiple Modes: Survival, creative, adventure, and more.
  • Cross-Play: Join friends regardless of their platform.

Minecraft’s endless possibilities make it a timeless pick for cooperative fun.

Making the Most of Co-Op on PS5

While exploring these titles, a few tips can enhance your cooperative experience on the PlayStation 5:

  • Utilize DualSense Features: Many games offer unique haptic feedback and adaptive triggers that make teamwork feel tactile and immersive.
  • Explore Voice Chat: Coordinated communication often makes or breaks co-op success.
  • Experiment Across Genres: From shooters and RPGs to platformers and party games, stepping outside your comfort zone will uncover fresh cooperative thrills.

Conclusion

The PlayStation 5 has breathed new life into co-op gaming, supporting a diverse array of titles that emphasize teamwork, strategy, and shared adventure. Whether you crave the heartwarming journey of It Takes Two, the chaotic kitchen antics of Overcooked!, or the thrilling gunfights of Borderlands 3, there’s a cooperative game to suit every taste.

Dive into these standout PS5 co-op games and discover the joy of gaming together. After all, the best moments in gaming frequently come when we join forces.

Ready to start your next co-op adventure? Grab your controller, rally your friends, and let the gaming sessions begin!
