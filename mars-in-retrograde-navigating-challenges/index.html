Mars in Retrograde: Navigating Challenges


Have you noticed unusual challenges cropping up in your day-to-day life? Or have you been feeling a sudden surge of emotions? There is a celestial event happening that could be responsible for these occurrences: Mars in retrograde. This phenomenon might seem like a culinary recipe gone awry but in reality, it’s a significant astronomical occurrence.

Mars is the fourth planet from the sun and the second smallest planet in the solar system. It is the only other planet, besides Earth, known to support life. Mars goes into retrograde when it appears to move in the opposite direction of Earth’s rotation. This happens approximately every 26 months, but it’s a crucial period for introspection and self-reflection.

Retreating, Aspiring, Leaping Forward…

Mars retrograde signifies a time of retreat, introspection, and self-reflection. This period is crucial for personal and professional development. In the 19 weeks that Mars was retrograde in October 2020 to January 2021, life offered several challenges.

One of the biggest challenges during Mars retrograde is communication breakdown. There were moments where it was difficult to get a hold of someone or where miscommunication happened. Relationships felt strained, perhaps due to misunderstandings, leading to unnecessary conflicts.

During this time, people also struggled with time management. Delays were frequent, leading to frustrations and impatience. It was harder to stay focused, leading to procrastination or a tendency to scatter focus. Numerous projects fell through, businesses struggled, and cancellations became the norm.

Feeling overwhelmed or confused? That’s quite normal during Mars retrograde. Emotional instability was another common experience. Mood swings were evident, with people feeling more prone to irritability or melancholy. Emotional vulnerability was another characteristic. Picking up emotional cues from the environment became more challenging.

So how do we navigate these Mars retrograde challenges? Here’s how:

  1. Self-reflection: Use this time to take inventory of your life. Evaluate your relationships, career, and personal growth.

  2. Re-evaluation: Be open to reassessing your goals and expectations. Is this what you truly want? Or do you need to re-adjust your path?

  3. Restoration: This is the prime time to undo mistakes or fix what has gone wrong. Mars retrograde is a period to revitalize and renew.

  4. Recovery: Use this time to get back on track. Progress might slow down, but it is not stagnant. Recovery and revival are essential.

As we come out of this period, opportunities for new beginnings arise. Mars retrograde might seem like a challenging spell, but it’s a time to retreat and re-charge for advancement. Mars is the planet of action, and once it moves forward, action becomes productive, influential, and empowering.

Keep a positive attitude, and remember, you’re not alone in your journey. Embrace the retrograde’s challenges, because, in the cosmos, sometimes, moving backward is the only way to move forward.
