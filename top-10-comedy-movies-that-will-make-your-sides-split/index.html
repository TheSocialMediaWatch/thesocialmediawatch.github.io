Top 10 Comedy Movies That Will Make Your Sides Split


Are you in need of a good laugh? Look no further! We’ve compiled a list of the top 10 comedy movies that are guaranteed to make your sides split with laughter. From timeless classics to recent releases, these films are sure to leave you rolling on the floor with laughter. So, grab your popcorn, sit back, and get ready for a hilarious movie marathon!

1. “Airplane!” (1980)

“Airplane!” is a hilarious spoof on disaster movies that will have you laughing from start to finish. With its rapid-fire jokes and zany characters, this film has become a comedy classic. From iconic one-liners to slapstick humor, “Airplane!” is a must-watch for any comedy lover.

2. “Anchorman: The Legend of Ron Burgundy” (2004)

This comedy gem stars Will Ferrell as the hilarious news anchor Ron Burgundy. With its outrageous humor and unforgettable quotes, “Anchorman” has become a cult favorite. If you’re looking for a comedy that will make you laugh until you cry, this movie is a perfect choice.

3. “Bridesmaids” (2011)

“Bridesmaids” is a comedy that proves women can be just as funny as men. With its relatable characters and side-splitting situations, this film will have you laughing out loud. From the bridal shower disaster to the unforgettable dress fitting scene, “Bridesmaids” is a comedy that should not be missed.

4. “Dumb and Dumber” (1994)

Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels team up in this comedy classic that follows the hilarious misadventures of two dim-witted best friends. With its absurd humor and unforgettable gags, “Dumb and Dumber” will have you laughing until your stomach hurts.

5. “Superbad” (2007)

“Superbad” is a coming-of-age comedy that follows a group of high school friends on a wild night out. With its raunchy humor and quotable lines, this film has become a favorite among comedy enthusiasts. If you’re looking for a movie that captures the awkwardness and hilarity of teenage years, “Superbad” is a must-watch.

6. “The Hangover” (2009)

“The Hangover” is a comedy that takes the bachelor party concept to a whole new level. With its outrageous antics and unexpected twists, this film will keep you laughing until the very end. If you’re in the mood for a comedy that will keep you guessing, “The Hangover” is the perfect choice.

7. “Groundhog Day” (1993)

“Groundhog Day” is a comedy that follows the story of a man who is forced to relive the same day over and over again. With its clever writing and Bill Murray’s comedic brilliance, this film is a true comedy masterpiece. If you’re looking for a movie that combines humor with a touch of philosophy, “Groundhog Day” is a must-see.

8. “Shaun of the Dead” (2004)

“Shaun of the Dead” is a hilarious blend of comedy and horror that follows the misadventures of a group of friends during a zombie apocalypse. With its witty dialogue and clever satire, this film has become a cult favorite. If you’re a fan of zombie movies and comedy, “Shaun of the Dead” is a must-watch.

9. “Napoleon Dynamite” (2004)

“Napoleon Dynamite” is a quirky comedy that follows the life of the socially awkward teenager Napoleon. With its offbeat humor and memorable characters, this film has gained a dedicated fanbase over the years. If you’re looking for a comedy that celebrates the oddities of life, “Napoleon Dynamite” is the perfect choice.

10. “The Grand Budapest Hotel” (2014)

“The Grand Budapest Hotel” is a comedy that takes place in a fictional European hotel and follows the adventures of its eccentric staff. With its whimsical storytelling and impeccable comedic timing, this film is a visual delight. If you’re looking for a comedy that is as visually stunning as it is hilarious, “The Grand Budapest Hotel” is a must-see.

So there you have it, our list of the top 10 comedy movies that will have you splitting your sides with laughter. Whether you’re in the mood for slapstick humor, witty banter, or absurd situations, these films are sure to deliver. So, grab some popcorn, gather your friends, and get ready for a laughter-filled movie night!
