Voyage through a Venus Flytrap: The Survivalist's Guide to Blocking Procrastination


As we trek across the vast landscape of productivity, we face many formidable challenges. One of the most daunting adversaries on our journey is none other than the cunning, seductive collector of time otherwise known as procrastination. Much like a Venus Flytrap ensnaring unwitting insects for its sustenance, procrastination waits patiently in the shadows, waiting to ambush us at our weakest moments, throttling our dreams and gumming up the gears of progress. But fear not, fellow survivalists, for I’ve hatched a plan, a strategy to vanquish this nefarious foe and restore our rightful place among the ranks of the flourishing and fruitful.

Step 1: Embrace Your Inner Survivalist

The first step in our journey is understanding that you are indeed a survivalist. You are a warrior, a conqueror of inefficiencies and a guardian of your time. As with any seasoned adventurer, you train and arm yourself for battle, equipping yourself with the knowledge and tools necessary to vanquish the beast. Allow your spirit to grow strong and fearless, for your most potent weapon against procrastination is your indomitable will.

Step 2: Purify Your Environment

Much like the ancient mariner’s trusty compass, your environment can be an invaluable tool in navigating the tempestuous waters of procrastination. Through maintaining a clean, clutter-free space, you open the skies to the sun of productivity to irradiate the murky depths of your procrastination-trapped mind. As you rid your surroundings of unnecessary distractions, you situate yourself optimally to chart your course through the wilderness of work.

Step 3: Map Out Your Course

An adventurer without a map is a foolhardy wanderer destined to fall prey to the snares of procrastination. This preparation requires you to break down your tasks into manageable pieces, setting realistic goals and timelines for completion. By creating a list of specific, actionable steps that guide your work, you enable your mind to focus on the journey ahead, leaving no room for the tempting detours of procrastination. Plot your course, bolster your resolve, and march forth with the precision of a general poised to lead his troops to victory.

Step 4: Unchain Your Time

Perpetually trapping ourselves in the shackles of deadlines is counterproductive, as it spurs feelings of dread and anxiety, leaving our minds vulnerable to the seductive promises of procrastination. Instead, apply the powerful elixir of time chunking, divvying up your tasks into short, manageable bursts, with the option to pause and convene at designated intervals to reassess your progress. As the ancient fable reminds us, even the mightiest task becomes achievable when broken into bite-sized bits.

Step 5: Involve Your Senses

The adventuring survivalist knows that a muscle memory-trained warrior is an unstoppable force. The brain, a superior but often untamed and capricious organ, is no different. The senses are powerful allies in our great battle against procrastination, and we harness their potency by engaging in sensory-specific triggers designed to instigate our conscientious actions. Pair your most daunting tasks with the aroma of coffee, the feel of a comfortable blanket, or a burst of invigorating music, and you’ll transform your enemy into an unwitting friend, acting as a catalyst for work and realization.

Step 6: Prioritize Rest and Diversify

A monotonous lifestyle leaves one vulnerable and weak, easily swayed by the wiles of procrastination. Instead, engage in an array of activities that foster a sense of balance, diversion, and rejuvenation. Surmount tireless labor with joyous respite, and you’ll root out the roots of resistance, empowering your spirit to face anything that comes.

Step 7: Forge the Weapon of Vulnerability

Armed with knowledge and a strategy, our final step in the traversal of the Venus Flytrap involves unveiling our vulnerability, our anxieties, and fears, in unison. When we divulge our worries and doubts to a trusted confidante - a companion, a family member, or even an empathetic friend, we preemptively disarm the foe within. We lay the groundwork for support and camaraderie, fortifying our resolve and galvanizing our will to conquer procrastination.

With this guide in hand and the indomitable spirit of the survivalist burning inside us, we ready ourselves for the great journey across the wild terrain of our lives. A venue replete with the wondrous fruits of labor and the unbridled rewards of owning our time and unlocking the vast potential that lies within. Engage your senses, set sail for the uncharted seas of productivity, and conquer the Venus Flytrap of procrastination that lies before us.
