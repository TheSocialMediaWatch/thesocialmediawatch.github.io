The Art of Square Foot Gardening: Maximizing Small Spaces

Are you interested in gardening but don’t have a large yard or outdoor space? Square foot gardening might just be the perfect solution for you. This innovative gardening method allows you to maximize small spaces and grow a wide variety of plants, herbs, and vegetables in a compact and organized manner. What is Square Foot Gardening? Square foot gardening is a gardening technique developed by Mel Bartholomew in the 1980s. The concept is simple yet effective: instead of traditional row planting, the garden is divided into square foot sections, typically 4x4 feet, creating a grid. Each square is then planted with a different type of plant, depending on the plant’s size and its compatibility with neighboring plants. This method optimizes space and minimizes waste, making it ideal for small urban gardens, balconies, patios, and even indoor gardening. ...
