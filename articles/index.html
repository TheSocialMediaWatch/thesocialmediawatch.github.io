Chickpea Salad Wrap Ingredients: 1 can of chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1/2 red onion, finely chopped 1/2 red bell pepper, diced 1/4 cup of fresh parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons of olive oil 1 tablespoon of lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste 4 whole wheat tortillas Instructions: In a large bowl, mash the chickpeas with a fork until slightly chunky. Add the red onion, red bell pepper, and parsley to the bowl and mix well. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the chickpea mixture and toss to coat. Divide the chickpea salad evenly among the tortillas and roll them up tightly.
