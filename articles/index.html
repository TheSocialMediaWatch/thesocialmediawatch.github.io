The Pour-Over Method The pour-over method is a simple and classic way to brew coffee. All you need is a pour-over cone, a filter, and freshly ground coffee beans. Start by placing the filter in the cone and rinsing it with hot water to remove any paper taste. Then, add your desired amount of coffee grounds to the filter. Slowly pour hot water over the grounds in a circular motion, allowing the coffee to bloom and release its flavors. Continue pouring until you have your desired amount of coffee.
