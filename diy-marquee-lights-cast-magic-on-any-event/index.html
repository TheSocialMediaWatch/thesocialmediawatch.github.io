DIY Marquee Lights: Cast Magic on Any Event


Do you want to create a truly enchanting atmosphere for your upcoming event, but don’t have the funds for professional lighting? Don’t worry, because you can easily recreate that exquisite ambiance by making your own marquee lights! With just some simple materials, clever DIY hacks, and a dash of creativity, your event will shine brighter than any celebrity gala.

In this guide, we will walk you through the entire process of making your very own DIY marquee lights, from preparation and materials to setting up your creative masterpiece. With these magical lights casting ambient highlights wherever you need them, you can guarantee that your party, wedding, or any special event, will be a truly unforgettable experience!

Gather the Essential Materials and Tools

Before diving into the creative process, make sure you have everything you need:

  1. Clear, Tabletop Drink Dispensers
  2. String Lights
  3. Small LED Lights (Battery Operated or Plug-In)
  4. Spray Paint (Gold, Silver, or any color of your choice)
  5. Plastic Marbles or Gems
  6. Hot Glue Gun
  7. Jars or Büstal (for marble mixture)
  8. Double-sided Tape or Velcro Strips 9.Decorative gift bags or other translucent material for diffusing light, like tissue paper, lace, etc.

Create Your Light Covers

  1. Begin by carefully removing the clear plastic tops of the drink dispensers.

  2. Apply a layer of spray paint to the dispensers’ plastic tops in your chosen color. If you want a soft glow, apply several thin layers, allowing drying time between each one.

  3. Once the dispenser tops are dry, flip them over and place them onto the tabletop drink dispensers. You may need to make a small incision on the bottom of the dispenser top to make it fit securely on your tabletop dispenser. This will prevent any light leakage.

  4. Now, let’s prepare the LED lights. If you’re using battery-operated ones, install or replace the batteries. If using plug-in ones, screw them into the bulbs.

  5. Place the LED bulbs inside the painted glass dispenser top. If you want a more dramatic effect, you can arrange several bulbs together. Be sure not to obstruct the light diffusion with any metal components within the bulb itself.

DIY Marquee Lampshades

  1. Grab the gems, marbles, glass beads, or anything that shines to create a marquee-like effect. Mix them with some epoxy resin until you have a consistent, lump-free mixture.

  2. Pour this mixture into jars or Büstal, filling it halfway. Adding a double-sided tape or Velcro strip to the inside of the Büstal or jar’s rim with room to extend slightly above its rim will be a useful tip once the resin hardens.

  3. Place the glass dispenser top on the flat part of the Velcro, ensuring the light emitted from the bulbs could pass through the material without too much obstructions.

  4. Allow the resin to harden completely before removing any excess material from around the jar’s edge. Finally, place the Büstal or jar back on the marquee light – when switching it on, it will create a mesmerizing twinkling effect on the wall.

DIY Marquee String Lights

  1. To replace classic string lights with your DIY marquee style, simply tape or tie your hand-painted drink dispenser tops to the strings.

  2. For an even more magical touch, decorate the string lines connecting the lights at regular intervals with the translucent gift bags or tissue lace. Use hot glue to attach them securely to each string.

  3. Plug the string lights into an outdoor extension cord, ensuring they reach the desired location and cover the area with style.

Display Your Unique DIY Marquee Lights

Now, place your stunning marquee lights around the event space either by hanging them on the walls or setting them on tables. Your DIY marquee lights will create a warm, inviting atmosphere for your guests and make your event truly unforgettable.

Bear in mind that the beauty of these DIY marquee lights lies in their versatility. You can paint different colors, use different shapes, or even mix materials to create various lighting effects. Use this guide as inspiration and let your creativity flow.

DIY marquee lights will not only save you money on professional decorations but also allow you to customize the ambiance according to your taste and vision. Instead of settling for standard party lighting, create something uniquely yours that will put any hotel ballroom to shame.

So, gather your materials, get your hands sticky with glue, and let’s shine a spotlight on your creativity. With a bit of effort and imagination, your DIY marquee lights can be a triumph that’s sure to leave a lasting impression on your guests and remind them of the memorable event you’ve thrown.
