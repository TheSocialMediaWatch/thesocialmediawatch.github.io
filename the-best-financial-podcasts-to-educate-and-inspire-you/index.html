The Best Financial Podcasts to Educate and Inspire You


Discover the world of personal finance and investing through engaging conversations, insightful interviews, and expert advice. Whether you're a seasoned investor or just dipping your toes into the world of finance, these top financial podcasts are sure to educate and inspire you on your journey to financial freedom. The Dave Ramsey Show Hosted by Dave Ramsey, a well-known personal finance expert, this podcast offers practical advice on budgeting, saving, and debt reduction. With a unique blend of motivational segments and actionable tips, this podcast is perfect for those striving to achieve financial harmony. Dave Ramsey's expertise is matched by his infectious enthusiasm, making it easy to stay motivated and committed to your financial goals. Planet Money This podcast from NPR offers a revolutionary take on the world of finance, blending straightforward explanations with compelling storytelling. Regular contributors, such as Jacob Goldstein and Stacey Vanek Smith, explore the intricacies of personal finance, consumer culture, and economic trends. Planet Money is engaging, informative, and entertaining, covering topics from credit scores to international trade. The Radical Personal Finance Podcast Hosted by Joshua Sheats, a Certified Financial Planner, this podcast explores alternative financial strategies, questioning conventional wisdom and pushing listeners to think outside the box. With discussions on investing in real estate, retirement planning, and tax optimization, The Radical Personal Finance Podcast invites you to consider fresh perspectives on achieving financial independence. NerdWallet's Smart Money NerdWallet's podcast offers expert advice on navigating the complexities of personal finance. Hosted by Jill Cornfield, along with guests from various financial backgrounds, Smart Money covers topics such as investing in stocks and bonds, optimizing financial planning, and slamming high-interest debt. Both novices and experienced investors can gain valuable insights from this in-depth exploration of financial strategies. The Motley Fool's Money Podcast With Sean O'Reilly and Jonathan Bender leading the conversation, The Motley Fool's Money Podcast thinks critically about personal finance, entrepreneurship, and investing.
