10 Stunning Hotels with Infinity Pools


There’s something undeniably magical about infinity pools. These architectural marvels blur the lines between water and horizon, creating an illusion of endlessness that captivates every traveler’s imagination. When combined with the perfect setting, an infinity pool becomes more than just a swimming spot—it’s an immersive experience, a tranquil escape, or even a statement of luxury. Across the globe, a collection of exceptional hotels have embraced this concept, offering guests unforgettable views alongside serene waters. Let’s dive into ten stunning hotels where infinity pools take center stage.

1. Marina Bay Sands, Singapore

Marina Bay Sands is one of the most iconic hotels in the world, largely due to its breathtaking rooftop infinity pool that stretches over 150 meters. Perched 55 stories above the city, the pool offers unparalleled panoramic views of Singapore’s skyline, including landmarks like the Gardens by the Bay and the bustling Marina Bay. The sensation of swimming with the cityscape at your feet is surreal. The pool area’s minimalist design, alongside plush loungers and ambient lighting, makes it the perfect place to unwind after a day of sightseeing or business meetings.

2. Jade Mountain Resort, St. Lucia

Nestled in the heart of the Caribbean, Jade Mountain features dramatic infinity pools that open directly onto stunning views of the Pitons, a pair of volcanic spires and a UNESCO World Heritage site. Each room boasts its own private infinity pool, designed for complete privacy and immersion in nature. The interplay of turquoise waters with the lush green mountains and the ocean beyond creates an idyllic backdrop for romance and absolute relaxation. The resort’s architecture blends seamlessly with the environment, emphasizing open spaces and natural materials.

3. Hanging Gardens of Bali, Indonesia

Set amid the dense jungles of Ubud, the Hanging Gardens of Bali offers a truly unique infinity pool experience. The most famous feature is the multi-level, tiered infinity pool overlooking the Ayung River valley. The pool’s edges merge with the tropical forest below, creating an illusion of floating amidst the treetops. Guests often describe the feeling here as transcending the ordinary, with the serene environment fostering meditation and tranquility. The thoughtful integration of traditional Balinese design elements further enriches the sensory experience.

4. The Chedi Andermatt, Switzerland

At first glance, Switzerland might not be the first place you imagine for an infinity pool, but The Chedi Andermatt beautifully challenges this notion. Surrounded by majestic Alpine peaks and lush forests, this luxury hotel features an outdoor infinity pool with heated waters that remain inviting even in cold weather. The cold mountain air contrasts strikingly with the warm, clear water, offering breathtaking views of snow-capped summits. It’s a perfect combination of invigorating climate and soothing comfort, ideal for both winter and summer escapes.

5. Hotel Villa Mahal, Marrakech, Morocco

Stepping into the Hotel Villa Mahal’s infinity pool is like entering a dreamlike oasis. Situated amidst Marrakech’s vibrant and historically rich medina, the pool offers views over the city’s ancient rooftops leading to the Atlas Mountains in the distance. The design incorporates traditional Moroccan aesthetics with modern luxury, including intricate tilework, lush gardens, and ambient lantern lighting. Visitors find themselves enchanted not only by the stunning views but also by the blend of culture and relaxation that the hotel embodies.

6. Four Seasons Safari Lodge Serengeti, Tanzania

In the heart of the Serengeti National Park lies a unique infinity pool with a wild twist. The Four Seasons Safari Lodge features a large infinity pool overlooking a natural watering hole frequented by elephants, giraffes, and other wildlife. Imagine lounging in crystal-clear water while lions roam in the background or zebras graze nearby—this gives a whole new meaning to the phrase “wild luxury.” The pool’s setting within the savanna, combined with the lodge’s elegant safari-themed architecture, creates an unforgettable experience for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

7. Amangiri, Utah, USA

Located in the starkly beautiful desert landscape of the American Southwest, Amangiri offers an infinity pool that seemingly stretches out into the vast wilderness. The pool’s mineral-rich water complements the surrounding red-rock canyons and mesas, blurring the boundary between the built environment and the natural world. The minimalist design echoes the serenity and simplicity of the desert, making the infinity pool a meditative retreat. Sunsets here are particularly breathtaking, with the sky lighting up in hues of amber and crimson.

8. Six Senses Yao Noi, Thailand

Set on a small island in Phang Nga Bay, Six Senses Yao Noi features infinity pools that perfectly frame towering limestone cliffs and emerald-green waters. Each villa comes equipped with a private infinity pool that offers uninterrupted views of the bay’s dramatic seascape. The fusion of water, sky, and lush greenery creates an intoxicating sense of peace and exclusivity. The resort’s commitment to sustainable design and wellness experiences pairs beautifully with the natural environment, enhancing the pool experience with mindful luxury.

9. The Oberoi Udaivilas, Udaipur, India

Udaipur’s reputation as the “City of Lakes” is beautifully complemented by The Oberoi Udaivilas’ magnificent infinity pools overlooking Lake Pichola. The royal architecture and intricate carvings combine with the stunning reflection of the lake and nearby palaces in the water, producing a scene straight out of a fairy tale. The infinity pool here is perfect for leisurely dips or evening swims under the stars, with the gentle lapping of the lake and illuminated palaces surrounding you in a serene ambiance.

10. Hotel Caruso, Ravello, Italy

High above Italy’s Amalfi Coast, Hotel Caruso offers an infinity pool that seems to spill into the Mediterranean Sea. Sitting atop a cliff with views over pastel-colored villages and rugged coastline, the pool’s edge disappears into breathtaking vistas of the Tyrrhenian Sea. The historic palace-turned-hotel boasts beautifully manicured gardens and antique frescoes, enhancing the pool area’s romantic and timeless atmosphere. It’s an ideal spot for soaking in Italy’s natural beauty alongside the charm of southern Italian culture.

Final Thoughts

Infinity pools are more than just architectural feats; they hold the power to transform a vacation into a memory etched in time. These ten hotels showcase how the perfect blend of design, location, and atmosphere can turn a swimming experience into pure enchantment. Whether soaring above a futuristic cityscape, floating over tropical jungles, or gazing across endless desert horizons, an infinity pool has that special way of inspiring awe and serenity in equal measure.

From the bustling urban heights of Singapore to the tranquil wilds of Tanzania, the world’s best infinity pools invite you to immerse yourself in water that flows seamlessly into the horizon. If a trip with breathtaking views and soothing aquatic luxury is on your travel bucket list, these hotels offer a blueprint for the ultimate getaway. Whenever you’re ready, the water’s edge awaits.
