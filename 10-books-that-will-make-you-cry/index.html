10 Books That Will Make You Cry


We all know that books have the power to transport us to different worlds, evoke a range of emotions, and leave a lasting impact on our lives. Some books, however, have a unique ability to touch our hearts in such a profound way that they bring tears to our eyes. In this blog post, we will explore 10 books that will make you cry. Get ready to grab a box of tissues as we dive into these emotional rollercoasters of literature.

1. “The Fault in Our Stars” by John Green

This heart-wrenching tale follows the story of Hazel and Gus, two teenagers who meet and fall in love at a cancer support group. John Green’s beautifully written prose takes us on a journey filled with love, loss, and the fragility of life. Prepare to shed tears as you witness the struggles and triumphs of these unforgettable characters.

2. “A Little Life” by Hanya Yanagihara

“A Little Life” is a deeply moving novel that explores friendship, trauma, and the complexities of human relationships. Yanagihara’s exquisite storytelling will leave you emotionally devastated yet utterly captivated by the lives of the four main characters. Be prepared for a rollercoaster of emotions as you navigate through their heart-wrenching journeys.

3. “The Book Thief” by Markus Zusak

Set during World War II, “The Book Thief” tells the story of Liesel Meminger, a young girl living in Nazi Germany. Through the power of words, Liesel finds solace amidst the chaos and horrors of war. Zusak’s poetic writing and unforgettable characters will tug at your heartstrings and leave you with a profound sense of loss and hope.

4. “Me Before You” by Jojo Moyes

“Me Before You” is a poignant love story that explores themes of life, death, and the importance of living in the moment. Moyes expertly crafts a tale that will make you laugh, cry, and question what it truly means to live a fulfilling life. Brace yourself for an emotional rollercoaster that will leave you reaching for the tissues.

5. “The Kite Runner” by Khaled Hosseini

“The Kite Runner” takes us on a journey through friendship, betrayal, and redemption in war-torn Afghanistan. Hosseini’s powerful storytelling will tug at your heartstrings as you follow the lives of Amir and Hassan. Prepare to shed tears as you witness the devastating consequences of their choices and the enduring power of love and forgiveness.

6. “The Lovely Bones” by Alice Sebold

In “The Lovely Bones,” Sebold weaves a haunting tale of loss, grief, and the afterlife. Through the eyes of Susie Salmon, a young girl who was brutally murdered, we witness the impact of her death on her family and friends. Sebold’s vivid prose will leave you emotionally raw as you grapple with the complexities of grief and healing.

7. “A Man Called Ove” by Fredrik Backman

“A Man Called Ove” is a heartwarming yet melancholic story about an elderly man named Ove and his journey towards redemption. Backman’s witty and poignant writing will make you both laugh and cry as you follow Ove’s interactions with his neighbors and the unexpected friendships that blossom. Get ready for a bittersweet tale that will leave you reaching for the tissues.

8. “The Nightingale” by Kristin Hannah

Set in France during World War II, “The Nightingale” explores the bravery and resilience of two sisters as they navigate the horrors of war. Hannah’s powerful narrative will leave you emotionally invested in the characters’ struggles and sacrifices. Be prepared for tears as you witness the strength of the human spirit and the enduring power of love.

9. “Never Let Me Go” by Kazuo Ishiguro

“Never Let Me Go” is a thought-provoking dystopian novel that delves into themes of love, identity, and the fragility of life. Ishiguro’s hauntingly beautiful prose will tug at your heartstrings as you follow the lives of Kathy, Ruth, and Tommy. Prepare to shed tears as you contemplate the ethical implications of their existence.

10. “The Art of Racing in the Rain” by Garth Stein

Narrated by a wise and philosophical dog named Enzo, “The Art of Racing in the Rain” is a poignant exploration of life, love, and loss. Stein’s unique perspective and heartfelt storytelling will leave you emotionally moved as you witness the bond between Enzo and his human family. Get ready for a tear-jerking journey that will make you cherish every moment with your own furry friends.

These 10 books are guaranteed to evoke powerful emotions and leave you with tears streaming down your face. Whether you’re looking for a cathartic cry or a deeply moving reading experience, these novels will not disappoint. So grab a cozy blanket, a cup of tea, and immerse yourself in these emotional masterpieces. Just don’t forget the tissues!
