Handmade Soap Recipes for Clean and Soft Skin


Are you tired of commercial soaps that leave your skin feeling dry and rough? Do you crave a homemade solution that effectively cleanses and moisturizes your skin? Look no further - we’ve compiled some unique and effective handmade soap recipes that are perfect for clean and soft skin.

  1. Original three-step goat’s milk soap

Going back to basics, our first recipe is a classic with a modern twist. This simple yet effective soap is made with goat’s milk, which adds natural moisturizing properties to cleanse your skin softly.

Ingredients:

  • 2 parts distilled water
  • 1 part goat’s milk
  • 3 parts cold-pressed coconut oil
  • 2 parts olive oil
  • 1 part castor oil
  • 1 part lye
  • Fragrance or essential oils of your choice

Instructions:

  1. Mix lye and goat’s milk in a heat-safe container.

  2. Heat the solution with a heat diffuser or double boiler until it reaches a light trace.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine all oils and heat them until they reach the gel stage.

  4. Pour the oils into the lye/milk solution slowly and mix thoroughly.

  5. Add fragrance or essential oils and mix again.

  6. Pour mixture into a mold and let it sit for 24-48 hours.

  7. Unmold and allow to cure for four weeks before using.

  8. Rich shea butter and mango seed butter soap

This deeply moisturizing soap is perfect for those with dry skin. Shea butter and mango seed butter provide a rich, creamy lather that will leave your skin feeling soft and supple.

Ingredients:

  • 2 parts distilled water
  • 3 parts coconut oil
  • 1 part shea butter
  • 1 part mango seed butter
  • 1 part olive oil
  • 1 part castor oil
  • Lye
  • Fragrance or essential oils

Instructions:

  1. Mix lye and water in a heat-safe container.

  2. Heat the solution with a heat diffuser or double boiler until it reaches a medium trace.

  3. In a separate bowl, combine all oils and heat them until they reach the gel stage.

  4. Slowly pour the oils into the lye solution while mixing.

  5. Add fragrance or essential oils and mix again.

  6. Pour mixture into a mold and let it sit for 24-48 hours.

  7. Unmold and let cure for four weeks before using.

  8. Refreshing cucumber and calendula soap

This gentle soap is perfect for all skin types. Cucumber extract soothes and refreshes, while calendula petals add natural color and antioxidants.

Ingredients:

  • 2 parts distilled water
  • 2 parts olive oil
  • 1 part coconut oil
  • 1 part castor oil
  • 1 part lye
  • 1 tsp. dried calendula petals
  • 1/2 cucumber, processed to make 1/2 cup of extract
  • Fragrance or essential oils

Instructions:

  1. Mix lye and water in a heat-safe container.
  2. Heat the solution with a heat diffuser or double boiler until it reaches a light trace.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine all oils and heat them until they reach the gel stage.
  4. Pour the oils into the lye/milk solution and mix thoroughly.
  5. Add cucumber extract, dried calendula petals, and fragrance or essential oils.
  6. Pour mixture into a mold and let it sit for 24-48 hours.
  7. Unmold and let cure for four weeks before using.

Conclusion

With these unique handmade soap recipes, you can effectively cleanse and soften your skin naturally. No more dry and rough skin - enjoy the benefits of these homemade soaps and say goodbye to commercial soaps. Happy soaping!
