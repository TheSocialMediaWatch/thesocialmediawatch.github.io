The Disappearance of the Jamison Family: Lost in the Oklahoma Wilderness


The mysterious disappearance of the Jamison family in the rugged wilderness of Oklahoma continues to baffle and captivate the public imagination. The case, which remains unsolved to this day, has sparked numerous theories and speculations, adding to the enigma surrounding their fate.

The Enigmatic Disappearance

In October 2009, Bobby Dale Jamison, his wife Sherilynn, and their six-year-old daughter Madyson set out on a trip to the San Bois Mountains, located in southeastern Oklahoma. Their intention was to inspect a plot of land they were considering purchasing. However, as the days passed, the family failed to return home, prompting a widespread search and rescue effort.

What makes their disappearance even more perplexing is the discovery of their abandoned truck. The vehicle, found in a remote area, contained the family’s wallets, phones, and a significant amount of cash. Additionally, a dog that they had taken with them was found inside the truck, malnourished but alive. This peculiar set of circumstances has only deepened the mystery surrounding their vanishing.

Theories and Speculations

In the absence of concrete evidence, numerous theories and speculations have emerged regarding the fate of the Jamison family. Some suggest that they may have fallen victim to foul play, while others entertain the possibility of a deliberate disappearance. The remote location of their abandoned truck has also led to theories about the involvement of a third party in their disappearance.

Furthermore, there has been speculation about the family’s state of mind leading up to their disappearance. Reports from acquaintances indicated that the Jamisons may have been experiencing financial and emotional difficulties, which has led some to posit the theory of a tragic family suicide. However, without conclusive evidence, these theories remain mere conjecture.

The Search Efforts

Following the discovery of the Jamison family’s abandoned truck, extensive search efforts were launched in the rugged Oklahoma wilderness. A multitude of search and rescue teams, as well as volunteers, scoured the mountains and surrounding areas in the hope of finding any trace of the missing family. Despite these efforts, no substantial leads or evidence have surfaced, leaving their fate shrouded in uncertainty.

Unsolved Mystery

The case of the Jamison family’s disappearance stands as an enduring enigma, evoking both fascination and sadness. The lack of conclusive evidence and the mysterious circumstances surrounding their vanishing have left the public and authorities alike puzzled and intrigued. As time passes, the case continues to be a haunting reminder of the unresolved mysteries that persist in the vast and unforgiving wilderness.

In conclusion, the disappearance of the Jamison family remains one of the most mystifying unsolved cases in recent memory. The lack of closure and the multitude of unanswered questions only serve to deepen the intrigue surrounding their fate. As the years go by, the mystery of the Jamison family’s disappearance endures, leaving us to ponder the many possibilities and theories that continue to circulate.
