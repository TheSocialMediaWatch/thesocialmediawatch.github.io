Crafting on a Budget: DIY Projects Under $20


Crafter or not, everyone loves a good bargain. With a bit of creativity and a tight budget, you can bring life to your home, create beautiful keepsakes, and save money all at once. From wall art to jewelry, DIY projects under $20 can be a fun way to put your skills to the test. Gear up, grab your tools, and let’s dive into some budget-friendly crafts that will spruce up your space and make a statement.

Mason Jar Wall Planter

Add a touch of rustic charm to your space with a simple and cost-effective mason jar wall planter. Gather your materials: mason jars, a wooden board, screws, and hooks. Mount the board on your wall, hang the jars on hooks, and fill with succulents or air plants. This project will cost you no more than $10 and is perfect for a living room, bedroom, or kitchen accent.

Macrame Plant Hanger

Brighten up your living space by making a macrame plant hanger. With a supply of rope and a basic understanding of macrame knots, you can create a lovely hanging display for your plants. These plant holders not only add a boho touch to your home but also serve as a functional way to display your greenery. Costing around $5, this project is a must-try for any crafting enthusiast.

Fossil Stone Coasters

Fossil stone coasters are a unique and inexpensive addition to any living area. Gather stones from your local beach or craft store, pillar candles, and wooden skewers. With a bit of carving and varnish, these coasters become a beautiful conversation piece and functional accessories to protect your furniture. With a budget of under $15, carve your own stones and make your own coasters.

Yarn-Wrapped Candle Lanterns

Liven up an empty corner ormantle with these beautiful, easy-to-make candle lanterns wrapped in yarn. Using inexpensive lanterns, candles, and a ball of yarn, this project takes minimal effort and time. With a cozy, warm glow, these lanterns not only lighten up the room but also add a touch of rustic charm. A budget of $10 and simple steps can have these ready for display.

Upcycled Picture Frame Jewelry Board

Bring new life to old picture frames by turning them into a jewelry board. Use cork tiles, glue, and your old frames to create a stylish and functional storage solution. In under $10, create a personalized jewelry organizer that adds charm to your dresser or closet.

Cork Bulletin Board

Create a delightful storytelling space with a cork bulletin board. Gather a corkboard, decorative ribbon, and paper clips. Fasten the ribbon around the board, clip up photos, and hang progress updates for a vintage-style gathering space. With a budget of less than $15, design your own corkboard that reflects your personality and style.

DIY projects are not only a thrilling way to express creativity, but also help in saving money and adding character to your living space. Made with love and your own hands, each of these projects serves as a reminder of the creativity tucked deep within. Transform your homes, one budget-friendly project at a time, and watch as your space takes on a new, joyful atmosphere.

Remember, creativity knows no bounds. Be inspired by your surroundings, and never underestimate the power of a simple, inexpensive project. Whether you’re trying out one of these crafts or starting your own DIY masterpiece, have fun and enjoy the process!
