How to Find Hotels with the Best Views in Major Cities


When it comes to booking a hotel in a major city, one of the most sought-after features is a breathtaking view. Whether you’re gazing at a sparkling skyline, historic landmarks, or tranquil waters, the view from your hotel room can elevate your travel experience. Finding the best hotels with scenic vistas, however, can be a challenge with the vast number of options available in every destination. Here’s how to ensure you book a hotel with the best views in major cities around the world.

Understand What You Want to See

Before you even start looking for hotels, it’s crucial to define what kind of view you’re after. The definition of a “good view” can differ greatly from person to person. Some travelers love the energy of a bustling cityscape, while others prefer a serene natural landscape.

Urban Skylines

If you’re drawn to the pulse of the city, a view of the skyline can offer a striking backdrop. Cities like New York, Paris, and Tokyo are famous for their iconic skylines, with towering skyscrapers, historic landmarks, and the neon lights that make the nights come alive.

Landmarks and Monuments

Many travelers choose hotels that offer views of the city’s most famous landmarks. In cities like Paris, Rome, or Sydney, staying in a hotel with a view of the Eiffel Tower, the Colosseum, or the Sydney Opera House can turn an ordinary trip into an unforgettable one.

Waterfront Views

If you’re a fan of tranquil, scenic beauty, a hotel overlooking a river, lake, or ocean might be ideal. Cities like Vancouver, Hong Kong, and Cape Town are known for their stunning waterfronts, offering travelers a peaceful and picturesque view right from their rooms.

Nature Views

For those who prefer to wake up to lush greenery, rolling hills, or majestic mountains, opting for a hotel with a nature view is an excellent choice. Cities like Zurich, Vancouver, and Rio de Janeiro offer incredible natural landscapes that can make your hotel stay feel like a retreat.

Research Hotels with Scenic Locations

Once you’ve decided on the type of view you’re looking for, the next step is to research hotels that offer those vistas. Here are some strategies to help narrow down your options.

Use Travel Websites and Apps

There are plenty of online platforms that allow you to filter hotels based on the view. Websites like Booking.com, Expedia, and TripAdvisor provide comprehensive details about the views you can expect from a hotel room. Look for user reviews that specifically mention the view, as this will give you a better idea of what to expect.

Most hotel booking sites also allow you to filter based on location, which can help you find the ideal area in the city with the best vantage points. Many platforms now include detailed pictures of rooms and views, allowing you to see exactly what you’re booking.

Check Hotel Websites Directly

While third-party booking platforms are convenient, it’s always worth visiting the hotel’s official website. Often, the best rates and more accurate information about the view can be found here. Hotel websites may even provide 360-degree images or videos of the room and view, so you can get a better sense of what you’re booking.

Ask for Recommendations

Don’t hesitate to ask for recommendations from fellow travelers or locals. Social media platforms like Instagram or Facebook groups dedicated to travel can offer insights into the best hotels with views in specific cities. Travelers often share their personal experiences and post photos of the views from their rooms, giving you a firsthand look at what to expect.

Consider the Location of the Hotel

The location of your hotel plays a critical role in the type of view you’ll have. City planning and topography can influence the kinds of views available. Here are a few factors to consider:

Elevation and Building Height

In some cities, the best views can only be found at higher elevations. Look for hotels located on upper floors, as they typically provide more expansive vistas. In cities with dense urban areas, the higher you go, the better your view is likely to be.

Proximity to Landmarks

To get a good view of major landmarks, you’ll want to choose a hotel that is located near the landmark in question. For example, if you want to see the Eiffel Tower in Paris, it’s best to stay in hotels that are situated near the Seine River or around the Champs-Élysées. The same applies to other iconic sites like the Empire State Building in New York or the Sydney Opera House in Australia.

Waterfront Locations

Waterfront views are often among the most desirable, especially in cities surrounded by rivers, lakes, or oceans. If you’re visiting a city like Venice, Sydney, or San Francisco, look for hotels that are situated near the water’s edge. Many hotels with waterfront views also offer luxurious amenities, making them a prime choice for travelers seeking both comfort and scenic beauty.

Understand Room Categories and View Upgrades

Not all rooms in a hotel will offer the same view. In fact, the view you’re hoping for may require booking a more expensive or upgraded room category. Here’s what to keep in mind:

Room Categories

Hotels often have various room categories, and the room with the best view may be located in a higher-tier category. For instance, a deluxe suite on the upper floors might offer sweeping views of the city, while a standard room may have no view or a less desirable one. Check the room descriptions to ensure you’re booking a room that specifically mentions the view you desire.

View Upgrades

If you’re flexible with your budget, consider upgrading to a room that offers the best view. Many hotels offer an option to pay a bit more for a room with an enhanced view, such as an oceanfront room or a suite overlooking the city skyline. It’s often worth the extra cost to ensure you wake up to a beautiful and inspiring view every morning.

Confirm the View with the Hotel

If you’re booking a room online and the view isn’t clearly specified, don’t hesitate to contact the hotel directly to inquire about the room’s view. Hotel staff can often provide specific details about the rooms and floors with the best views. Some hotels even allow guests to request specific views, so don’t be afraid to ask for what you want.

Read Guest Reviews

Guest reviews are a powerful tool when it comes to ensuring you’re booking a hotel with the best views. When reading reviews, pay attention to comments that mention the room’s location, the view from the window, and the overall experience. Reviews from past guests often include photos of the view, giving you an authentic glimpse of what to expect.

Look for Photos of the View

Many travelers share their photos on review sites, allowing you to see the actual view from different rooms in the hotel. This can help you gauge whether the view matches what’s advertised. Keep in mind that photos may be taken from specific rooms or floors, so it’s always a good idea to confirm that the room you’re booking is similar.

Consider Seasonal Differences

Some views may look different depending on the time of year. For example, a hotel overlooking a park may offer lush greenery in the summer, while the same view in the winter could feature bare trees. Check guest reviews for comments on how the view changes with the seasons, and consider when you’ll be traveling to ensure the view meets your expectations.

Utilize Hotel Booking Sites with Detailed Filters

To make your search even more efficient, use hotel booking websites that offer detailed filters for views. Some platforms allow you to filter by specific types of views, such as:

  • City views
  • Ocean views
  • Mountain views
  • Riverfront views
  • Landmark views

Using these filters, you can easily sort through hotels and find the ones that match your preferences. Additionally, booking websites like Booking.com and Agoda often provide detailed photos and descriptions of each hotel room, including what kind of view it offers.

Final Thoughts

Finding hotels with the best views in major cities is all about knowing what you want and doing the necessary research. Whether you prefer a skyline view, a famous landmark, a waterfront scene, or a lush green landscape, there are countless hotels around the world that can provide you with the stunning vistas you’re looking for.

By understanding your preferences, researching hotel options, and reading reviews, you can ensure that your hotel stay becomes an unforgettable part of your travel experience. So, next time you’re planning a trip to a major city, take the time to find a hotel that not only offers comfort and convenience but also provides a view that will make your stay truly special.
