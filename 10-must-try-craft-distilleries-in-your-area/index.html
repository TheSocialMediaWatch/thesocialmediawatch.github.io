10 Must-Try Craft Distilleries in Your Area


Craft distilleries have been gaining popularity in recent years, offering unique spirits that reflect local flavors and artisanal methods. Whether you’re a seasoned connoisseur or a curious novice, visiting these distilleries can provide not only a taste of exceptional spirits but also a glimpse into the distillation process and the passion behind each bottle. Here’s a curated list of ten must-try craft distilleries that you absolutely should explore.

1. Local Grain Distillers

Nestled in the heart of your area, Local Grain Distillers is renowned for its commitment to using locally sourced ingredients. This distillery prides itself on crafting small-batch whiskey, gin, and vodka, each infused with the essence of the region. Their signature bourbon, aged in charred oak barrels, offers a complex flavor profile with notes of caramel, vanilla, and a hint of spice.

What to Try: Be sure to sample their flagship bourbon during the distillery tour, which includes a behind-the-scenes look at the distillation and aging processes.

2. Heritage Spirits Distillery

Heritage Spirits Distillery takes pride in preserving traditional distilling techniques while incorporating modern innovations. This family-owned distillery specializes in artisanal rum and flavored vodkas, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the area. Visitors will be captivated by their vibrant tasting room, where they can learn about the history of each spirit.

What to Try: The spiced rum is a crowd favorite, perfect for sipping neat or mixing in cocktails. Don’t miss their seasonal flavored vodkas, which change throughout the year.

3. Mountain View Distilling Co.

Set against a stunning mountainous backdrop, Mountain View Distilling Co. offers breathtaking views alongside exceptional craft spirits. They focus on creating organic products, from their gin infused with local botanicals to their smooth single malt whiskey. Their passion for sustainability is evident in every aspect of their operation.

What to Try: The organic gin, with its floral and herbal notes, is a must-try. Pair it with a tonic to enhance the fresh flavors.

4. Riverbend Spirits

Riverbend Spirits is known for its innovative approach to distilling, often experimenting with unique ingredients and techniques. Their craft cocktails, made with house-distilled spirits, are a highlight of their tasting room. The distillery also hosts regular cocktail classes, where visitors can learn to create their own mixes.

What to Try: Sample their signature cocktail made with their house-made gin and fresh local herbs, showcasing the best of both worlds.

5. Crafted Spirits Distillery

Crafted Spirits Distillery has quickly made a name for itself with its award-winning lineup of spirits. The emphasis here is on quality over quantity, with each batch meticulously crafted. Their selection includes everything from artisanal gins to aged rums, each telling a story of craftsmanship and dedication.

What to Try: Their barrel-aged gin is a standout, offering a unique twist on traditional gin with complex flavors developed during the aging process.

6. Artisan Distilling Co.

Artisan Distilling Co. places a strong emphasis on local partnerships, sourcing ingredients from nearby farms. Their spirits are crafted with a farm-to-bottle philosophy, ensuring freshness and quality. The distillery often hosts events and tastings, allowing visitors to experience the products in an engaging setting.

What to Try: Don’t miss their seasonal gin, which includes fresh botanicals handpicked from local gardens.

7. Copper Creek Distillers

Copper Creek Distillers is a family-run business that takes pride in its small-batch production. They specialize in whiskey and vodka, using traditional methods that have been passed down through generations. The cozy tasting room provides a warm atmosphere, perfect for enjoying their crafted spirits.

What to Try: Their signature copper pot still vodka is smooth and versatile, making it an excellent choice for cocktails or sipping neat.

8. Blue Sky Distillery

Blue Sky Distillery is well-known for its innovative spirit offerings, including infused spirits and liqueurs. Their commitment to experimentation means there’s always something new and exciting to try. The distillery offers guided tours, where visitors can see the distillation process firsthand.

What to Try: Their lavender-infused gin is a delightful choice, perfect for summer cocktails or even as a refreshing spritz.

9. Iron Oak Distillers

Iron Oak Distillers focuses on creating rich, flavorful spirits through meticulous attention to detail. Their barrel-aging process is designed to enhance the natural flavors of each spirit, leading to a unique tasting experience. Visitors are welcome to participate in tastings and learn about the aging process.

What to Try: Their aged whiskey, which boasts deep, complex flavors, is a must-try for whiskey lovers.

10. Sunny Side Distillery

Sunny Side Distillery offers a welcoming atmosphere and a diverse selection of spirits. Their approach to distilling is both traditional and innovative, making each visit a new adventure. The tasting room is vibrant and lively, often hosting local musicians and events, making it a great place to enjoy craft cocktails.

What to Try: Their fruit-infused vodkas are a refreshing choice, perfect for summer sipping or as a base for craft cocktails.

Conclusion

Exploring craft distilleries is not just about sampling spirits; it’s about experiencing the passion, creativity, and dedication that goes into every bottle. Each of the distilleries listed above offers something unique, whether it’s a commitment to local ingredients, innovative flavors, or a welcoming atmosphere. So, gather your friends and make a day of it—visit these must-try craft distilleries and discover the remarkable world of artisanal spirits in your area. Cheers to new adventures and unforgettable tastes!
