Crafting with Buttons: Cute and Quirky Projects


Buttons are not just functional fasteners; they can also be transformed into adorable and unique crafting materials. Whether you have a collection of vintage buttons or a jar full of colorful ones, there are countless creative projects you can undertake. In this blog post, we will explore some cute and quirky crafting ideas using buttons that will add a touch of whimsy to your creations.

Button Bouquet

Give your floral arrangements a playful twist by incorporating buttons into a button bouquet. Gather an assortment of buttons in different colors and sizes. Attach floral wire to each button by threading it through the buttonholes, then twist the wire to secure it. Arrange the button flowers in a vase, and voila! You have a vibrant and everlasting bouquet that will brighten up any room.

Button Bracelets

Create unique and eye-catching bracelets using an assortment of buttons. Start by selecting buttons of various sizes and colors. Using elastic cord or jewelry wire, string the buttons together, alternating between large and small buttons for added visual interest. Tie the ends securely and wear your one-of-a-kind button bracelet with pride.

Button Coasters

Protect your furniture in style with button coasters. For this project, you will need a set of cork coasters and an assortment of buttons. Using a strong adhesive, such as E6000, glue the buttons onto the coasters in a pattern or design of your choice. Once the glue has dried, you’ll have a set of quirky and functional coasters that will add a pop of color to your living space.

Button Wall Art

Create a captivating piece of art using buttons and a canvas or wooden frame. Start by sketching your desired design onto the canvas or frame. Then, using a strong adhesive, carefully glue the buttons onto the surface, following your sketch. Let your imagination run wild as you arrange the buttons to create a stunning and unique piece of wall art.

Button Bookmarks

Make reading even more enjoyable by crafting cute button bookmarks. Select a large button and attach a piece of ribbon or elastic to it. You can further personalize your bookmark by adding charms or beads. These adorable bookmarks will keep your place in style and make great gifts for fellow bookworms.

Button Hair Accessories

Add a touch of whimsy to your hairstyles with button hair accessories. Attach buttons to bobby pins, hair clips, or headbands using hot glue or a strong adhesive. Mix and match colors and sizes to create your own unique hair accessories that will make a statement wherever you go.

Button Keychains

Personalize your keychain with a touch of button charm. Select a large button and attach a keyring to it using a jump ring or strong adhesive. You can also add smaller buttons or charms to further embellish your keychain. This simple yet quirky accessory will make finding your keys a breeze.

Crafting with buttons opens up a world of possibilities for creating cute and quirky projects. From button bouquets to button keychains, the only limit is your imagination. So, gather your buttons, unleash your creativity, and start crafting!
