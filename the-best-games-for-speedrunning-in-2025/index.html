The Best Games for Speedrunning in 2025


Speedrunning has evolved from a niche pastime into a global phenomenon, captivating millions who thrive on the adrenaline rush of completing games as fast as possible. The year 2025 is shaping up to be one of the most exciting eras for speedrunners, with a burgeoning mix of new releases, enduring classics, and unexpected gems dominating leaderboards and Twitch streams alike. Whether you’re a seasoned speedrunner or just curious about the competitive scene, this comprehensive guide dives deep into the best games for speedrunning in 2025—highlighting what makes each title special, how community support fuels their speedrun culture, and why they are worth your time.

Why Speedrun Certain Games?

Choosing what to speedrun often depends on a few key elements: replayability, the depth of mechanics, glitch potential, and community involvement. Games that combine these factors spark interesting strategies, creative route planning, and lively competition. The most popular speedrunning games also enjoy regular updates, expansive forums, and frequent events like Games Done Quick, which bring visibility and fresh interest to the scene.

With these aspects in mind, let’s explore standout games for speedrunners this year.

1. Hollow Knight: Silksong

The much-anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed “Hollow Knight,” Silksong finally took center stage in 2024, and its momentum is only accelerating into 2025. Known for its rich Metroidvania gameplay, precise platforming, and atmospheric storytelling, Silksong presents a perfect storm of speedrunning potential.

  • Why It’s Great for Speedrunning: Tight mechanics allow for creative sequence breaks. Players can exploit enemy patterns and environmental nuances to shave seconds off their runs. Unlike its predecessor, Silksong introduces new moves and abilities that expand the skill ceiling.

  • Community & Categories: The game has spawned diverse categories, from Any% (completing the game by any means) to 100% completion runs. A dedicated Discord server and active leaderboards keep rivalry fierce but friendly.

  • What to Expect in 2025: Expect new route discoveries as the game matures and glitches surface. The upcoming DLCs will likely add fresh categories and challenges.

2. Celeste

Even years after its initial release, Celeste remains a darling of the speedrunning world. This platformer combines punishingly precise movement with emotionally resonant storytelling. Its handcrafted levels and consistent physics make it a playground for runners who enjoy perfecting pixel-perfect jumps.

  • Speedrun Highlights: The modularity of chapters means runners optimize both individual segments and full-game runs. Advanced techniques like “hyper-dashing” and “wave dashing” offer layers of depth.

  • Community Support: With one of the most welcoming communities, Celeste speedrunners regularly stream marathons and organize timed races.

  • Looking Ahead: In 2025, enhanced leaderboards and new community challenges keep Celeste thriving in the competitive scene.

3. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Nintendo’s newest major release leapt into the speedrunning spotlight almost immediately. Tears of the Kingdom rides on the open-world innovation that Breath of the Wild pioneered but adds new mechanics, tools, and environmental puzzles that players have quickly begun exploiting.

  • Speedrunning Appeal: The sandbox nature combined with physics-based puzzles enables out-of-the-box strategies and shortcuts. It’s not unusual to see completely new categories based on different speedrun rules popping up.

  • Community Dynamics: With a huge player base, numerous tutorials, and an active presence on social media, this title is becoming an esports contender in the speedrunning community.

  • Future Expectations: Modding communities and official patch updates might introduce surprises that shift current speedrun tactics.

4. DOOM Eternal

For runners who prefer aggressive gameplay and arcade-style pacing, DOOM Eternal stands out as a beast. The game rewards fast reflexes, memorization, and skillful weapon switching, all while maintaining non-stop, high-octane action.

  • Why It’s Speedrun Friendly: Each level’s layout lends itself to multiple routes, and players can chain movement combos and exploit enemy behavior for faster clear times.

  • Competition and Records: With a robust leaderboard and ongoing speedrunning tournaments, DOOM Eternal offers intense competition and regular record breaks.

  • 2025 Developments: Watching the meta evolve as players master new rocket jumps or skip boss phases is one of the most exciting aspects of DOOM’s speedrun scene.

5. Minecraft

It might seem unconventional to mention a sandbox survival game in the same breath as highly scripted titles, but Minecraft speedruns are wildly popular, especially for categories like Any% Glitchless and Set Seed. Each run is a blend of skill, luck, and strategy, which creates endless variation.

  • Speedrunning Challenges: Finding and defeating the Ender Dragon quickly requires mastery of resource gathering, combat, and portal mechanics.

  • Expanding Community: The phenomenally large Minecraft player base has cultivated countless sub-communities dedicated solely to speedrunning.

  • What’s New in 2025: Updates to game versions, new biomes, and mechanics keep the speedrun meta fresh. Watch for new seed manipulation strategies and tournament events.

6. Portal 2

Years after its release, Portal 2 retains a cult following for speedrunning thanks to its mind-bending puzzle design and physics-based glitches. Runners exploit portals, surface glitches, and advanced movement to leap through levels in astonishing times.

  • Speedrun Mechanics: Techniques like “edge boosts,” “portal bombs,” and “reverse portals” allow pathway shortcuts.

  • Dedicated Followers: Portal 2 speedrunners organize live streams and charity events, fostering a strong sense of community.

  • Continued Interest: Expect enthusiasts to keep uncovering new skips and strategies, ensuring this game stays relevant for years to come.

7. Metroid Dread

As one of the latest entries in the iconic Metroid series, Metroid Dread blends classic exploration with stunning new combat mechanics. Its design encourages intricate routing, precise platforming, and clever usage of abilities.

  • Speedrun Strategy: Players optimize damage boosts, sequence breaks, and boss skips to shave minutes off their times.

  • Community Engagement: Speedrunning competitions and leaderboards have grown steadily, with new runners joining regularly.

  • What’s Next: Continuous discovery of glitches and new timing categories keep the game fresh in 2025.

8. Cuphead

The notoriously difficult boss rush format of Cuphead has made it a darling for speedrunners looking to test reflexes and memorization. Its hand-drawn animation and jazzy soundtrack provide a unique backdrop for high-stakes speedrunning.

  • Key Speedrun Aspects: Perfecting boss patterns and attack cycles is essential, alongside flawless controller inputs.

  • Community Vibe: The Cuphead speedrun scene is small but fiercely passionate, organizing runs that showcase mastery and entertainment.

  • In 2025: Anticipate more routing discoveries and potential new categories involving DLC content.

Tips for Starting Out or Improving Your Speedrunning Game in 2025

If you’re inspired by these games, consider the following to jumpstart or refine your speedrunning journey:

  • Engage with Communities: Join Discord servers, Reddit forums, and Twitch channels dedicated to your game of choice. Learning from veterans can accelerate your improvement.

  • Practice Rigorously: Speedrunning is about muscle memory and strategy. Use tools like timers, split trackers, and gameplay recording to analyze performance.

  • Watch and Learn: Viewing top runners offers insight into tricks and techniques not immediately obvious.

  • Stay Updated: Games often receive patches or community-discovered glitches are revealed. Staying in the loop ensures you’re always on the cutting edge.

  • Participate in Events: Races, marathons, and charity streams are excellent ways to measure progress and contribute to the community.

Final Thoughts

Speedrunning in 2025 is a vibrant and continually evolving field, fueled by a variety of games that cater to different tastes and skills. Whether you prefer the exploration of Tears of the Kingdom, the precision platforming of Celeste, or the explosive chaos of DOOM Eternal, there’s a perfect speedrun waiting for you.

Each title mentioned here comes with a passionate community and a wealth of strategies that promise countless hours of engaging and rewarding gameplay. The best speedrunning games blend creativity, challenge, and community spirit, and 2025’s lineup showcases that balance beautifully. Jump in, pick your favorite, and experience the thrill of speeding through worlds faster than anyone else.

Happy running!
