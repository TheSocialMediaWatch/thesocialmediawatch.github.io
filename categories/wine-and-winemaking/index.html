What is Orange Wine? Orange wine, also known as skin-contact wine, is made from white wine grapes that are fermented with their skins, seeds, and sometimes even stems. This extended skin contact gives the wine its characteristic orange or amber hue, as well as a complex flavor profile that sets it apart from conventional white wines.
