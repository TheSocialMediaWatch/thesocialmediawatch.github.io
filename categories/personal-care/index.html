Practice Sun Safety The most fundamental aspect of summertime skincare is practicing sun safety. There are a few critical steps to follow: Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30 at least 30 minutes before going outside. Reapply after swimming or sweating, and every two hours. Seek shade, especially between 10 AM and 4 PM when the sun’s rays are strongest. Wear protective clothing, such as long-sleeved shirts, wide-brimmed hats, and sunglasses. Avoid indoor tanning, and protect others from overexposure by tanning only in a tanning bed with a certified operator.
