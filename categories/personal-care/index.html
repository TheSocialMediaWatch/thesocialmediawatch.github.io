As we soak up the warm rays and enjoy outdoor activities, it’s essential to know how to protect our skin from the harsh ultraviolet (UV) radiation that can lead to sunburns, premature aging, and skin cancer. In this post, we’ll explore some valuable tips for maintaining healthy and beautiful skin during the summer months. Practice Sun Safety The most fundamental aspect of summertime skincare is practicing sun safety. There are a few critical steps to follow: Apply broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30 at least 30 minutes before going outside. Reapply after swimming or sweating, and every two hours. Seek shade, especially between 10 AM and 4 PM when the sun’s rays are strongest. Wear protective clothing, such as long-sleeved shirts, wide-brimmed hats, and sunglasses. Avoid indoor tanning, and protect others from overexposure by tanning only in a tanning bed with a certified operator. Choose the Right Sunscreen Chemical-free sunscreens are growing in popularity, and for a good reason. They are less irritating and more effective than their chemical-based counterparts.
