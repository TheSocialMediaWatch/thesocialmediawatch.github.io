Hotels with the Best Outdoor Activities: Adventure Awaits


Hotels are no longer just a place to rest your head after a long day of exploring. They’ve become hubs for adventure and excitement, with plenty of opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts to pursue their passions. In this blog post, we’ll be taking a look at some of the best hotels offering a range of exhilarating outdoor activities, from thrilling water sports to serene hiking trails.

  1. The Lodge at Woodloch, Arizona:

Nestled in the Sonoran Desert, The Lodge at Woodloch offers a luxurious escape from the real world. Adventure seekers can revel in the diverse landscape surrounding the hotel, which boasts hiking trails, bird watching opportunities, and adrenaline-pumping off-road tours. In addition to those classic desert activities, The Lodge also offers an on-site guided medicated hike, which includes a gourmet picnic and a CBD-infused massage. Let’s just say that their relaxation experiences go above and beyond.

  1. Gion Sanjūroku, Japan:

Founded in 1876, Gion Sanjūroiku is a Kyoto institution with a rich history. While many visit this hotel to explore the city’s cultural treasures, those in the know flock to the dojo within the hotel for a true taste of samurai culture. Guests can participate in authentic samurai lessons, including sword fighting and archery, before experiencing the unparalleled hospitality of ancient Japan.

  1. Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea, Hawaii:

Imagine lounging on a white sandy beach, sipping refreshing cocktails, and taking in the breathtaking beauty of the Pacific Ocean. At the Four Seasons Resort Maui at Wailea, these idyllic daydreams become a reality. The resort offers unparalleled access to a wide variety of water activities, including snorkeling, paddleboarding, and whale watching. If relaxation is more your speed, indulge in a luxurious spa treatment or simply soak up the sun on one of Hawaii’s most beautiful beaches.

  1. The Mai Divina, Mallorca:

The Mai Divina is a hidden gem nestled in the picturesque mountains of Mallorca. This boutique hotel is the epitome of luxury, with a unique focus on pursuing natural adventure. Guests can participate in activities like hiking, cycling, horseback riding, or kayaking, all while surrounded by the stunning beauty of the island. For those who prefer a slower pace, the hotel also offers wine tastings and olive oil workshops, creating an unforgettable experience for even the most discerning traveler.

  1. ThePlainHearth, New Zealand:

For the adventurers who truly want to get back to nature, ThePlainHearth offers a unique and unforgettable experience. This sustainable guesthouse is nestled within a 200-acre working farm, providing up-close access to the magic of rural New Zealand. From horseback riding to cycling to guided stargazing tours, there’s no end to the variety of experiences that await. Best of all, the owners are enthusiastic about sharing their passion for sustainability, giving guests the chance to learn about eco-friendly practices and self-sufficiency while enjoying the peace and tranquility of the countryside.

As you can see, the possibilities for outdoor adventure are truly endless when it comes to these five hotels. So why not pack your bags and embark on your own adventure? Who knows, you might just find your sanctuary in one of these extraordinary locations.
