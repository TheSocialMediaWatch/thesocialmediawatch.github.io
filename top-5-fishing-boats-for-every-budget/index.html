Top 5 Fishing Boats for Every Budget


Fishing is not just a hobby for many; it’s a passion, a lifestyle, and for some, even a way of life. Whether you’re an experienced angler or just beginning, the right fishing boat can elevate your fishing experience. But with so many options available, finding the ideal boat that fits your needs and budget can be a daunting task. In this article, we’ll explore the top 5 fishing boats available across different price ranges, making it easier for you to pick the best one for your next fishing adventure.

1. Best Budget Fishing Boat: Sun Dolphin Journey 10 SS

If you’re looking for a fishing boat that doesn’t break the bank, the Sun Dolphin Journey 10 SS is one of the best choices. It’s an affordable, compact, and easy-to-maneuver boat that’s perfect for calm lakes or rivers.

Key Features:

  • Size: 10 feet long
  • Material: Durable polyethylene
  • Weight Capacity: 395 lbs
  • Seats: One comfortable padded seat, with an option to add more seats
  • Storage: Plenty of storage space for tackle boxes and gear

While it may not have all the bells and whistles of larger boats, the Sun Dolphin Journey 10 SS is perfect for solo fishing trips or for those who prefer a minimalistic approach. The boat’s lightweight design makes it easy to transport, and it’s extremely stable in calm waters, ensuring a smooth ride for anglers.

Why It’s a Good Fit:

  • Great for beginners or anglers on a budget
  • Ideal for fishing in small lakes or rivers
  • Easy to transport with a car or SUV

If you’re just starting and don’t want to make a huge investment, this boat offers excellent value for money.

2. Mid-Range Option: Tracker Grizzly 1648 SC

The Tracker Grizzly 1648 SC strikes a balance between price and performance, making it a fantastic choice for anglers who need a reliable, versatile boat without going for high-end luxury models. Known for its ruggedness and stability, this boat is designed to tackle more challenging waters, including rivers and lakes with a little more current.

Key Features:

  • Size: 16 feet long
  • Material: Aluminum hull for durability
  • Weight Capacity: 1,025 lbs
  • Seats: Two high-back seats for comfort
  • Storage: Ample storage for fishing gear, coolers, and more

One of the standout features of the Tracker Grizzly 1648 SC is its aluminum hull, which makes it both lightweight and durable. This boat can handle rough waters and can be used for a variety of fishing styles, from bass fishing to trolling.

Why It’s a Good Fit:

  • More spacious than smaller models
  • Perfect for those looking for a combination of budget and versatility
  • Great for families or group fishing trips

The Grizzly 1648 SC is excellent for anglers who want a tough, reliable boat that can be used in various types of water bodies, without stepping up to the high-end luxury boat category.

3. High-End Option: Boston Whaler 170 Montauk

If you’re willing to make a more significant investment and want a boat that’s built to last, the Boston Whaler 170 Montauk is an exceptional choice. Boston Whaler boats are renowned for their durability, and the 170 Montauk is no exception. This boat is known for its unsinkable design, which ensures safety and peace of mind while on the water.

Key Features:

  • Size: 17 feet long
  • Material: Unsinkable fiberglass hull
  • Weight Capacity: 2,000 lbs
  • Seats: Multiple cushioned seats, with ample space for additional seating
  • Storage: Large center console with plenty of storage compartments

The 170 Montauk is designed for anglers who require a boat that can handle larger waters and rougher conditions. Its well-balanced hull provides excellent stability even in choppy waters. Additionally, the boat’s design offers a lot of space for fishing gear and coolers, making it a perfect choice for long fishing trips.

Why It’s a Good Fit:

  • Excellent for both inshore and offshore fishing
  • The unsinkable hull provides added safety
  • Great for anglers who prioritize quality and durability

While the price is higher, the Boston Whaler 170 Montauk is an investment that will serve you well for years to come, offering superior performance and safety.

4. Best for Luxury and Comfort: Sea Hunt 211 Ultra

For those who demand luxury, comfort, and top-notch performance, the Sea Hunt 211 Ultra is a premium choice. This center console boat is packed with high-end features and designed for serious anglers who want both style and function on the water.

Key Features:

  • Size: 21 feet long
  • Material: Fiberglass construction
  • Weight Capacity: 2,500 lbs
  • Seats: Spacious seating for multiple passengers, with plush upholstery
  • Storage: Multiple storage compartments, including large fish boxes and live wells

The Sea Hunt 211 Ultra comes equipped with advanced features like a Garmin GPS and fishfinder, as well as a powerful engine that ensures top-notch performance in both calm and rough waters. Its versatility makes it ideal for a range of fishing styles, from deep-sea fishing to inshore trips.

Why It’s a Good Fit:

  • Offers luxurious comfort and advanced features
  • Great for serious anglers who need a boat for multiple fishing types
  • Ideal for long days out on the water with friends and family

This boat provides everything a serious angler needs, from storage to performance, and is perfect for anyone who wants to combine fishing with a little bit of luxury.

5. Best for Saltwater Fishing: Contender 39 ST

If you’re someone who enjoys venturing out into saltwater for some serious offshore fishing, the Contender 39 ST is one of the best fishing boats you can get. It’s built specifically for deep-sea fishing and is equipped to handle the toughest conditions.

Key Features:

  • Size: 39 feet long
  • Material: High-performance fiberglass construction
  • Weight Capacity: 8,000 lbs
  • Seats: Premium cushioned seating with ample space for multiple anglers
  • Storage: Large fish holds, live wells, and multiple storage compartments

The Contender 39 ST is known for its speed, stability, and high-performance capabilities. With multiple engines and advanced navigation systems, this boat can handle the roughest offshore conditions, ensuring that you have a smooth ride even in choppy seas.

Why It’s a Good Fit:

  • Designed for serious saltwater anglers
  • Excellent for deep-sea fishing and long-distance trips
  • Built to withstand tough conditions with superior durability

For anglers who are looking to take on big fish in open waters, the Contender 39 ST is the ultimate fishing vessel. Though it comes at a premium price, its capabilities make it well worth the investment for seasoned anglers.

Conclusion

Selecting the right fishing boat depends on several factors including your budget, the type of fishing you intend to do, and how much space you need. Whether you’re just getting started with a budget-friendly model like the Sun Dolphin Journey 10 SS, or you’re ready to take on the seas with a luxury boat like the Contender 39 ST, there is a fishing boat out there that’s perfect for you.

By considering the various features, sizes, and capabilities of these boats, you’ll be able to make an informed decision that will enhance your fishing experience for years to come. Whatever your budget, there’s a boat on this list that will make your time on the water both enjoyable and successful.
