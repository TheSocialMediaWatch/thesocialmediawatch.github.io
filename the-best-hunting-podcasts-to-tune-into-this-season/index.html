The Best Hunting Podcasts to Tune Into This Season


As hunting enthusiasts gear up for another season, there’s no better way to stay informed, entertained, and inspired than by tuning into the best hunting podcasts available. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter or a beginner looking to dive into the world of hunting, these podcasts offer valuable insights, expert advice, and stories from across the hunting community. From gear recommendations and hunting tactics to conservation efforts and hunting culture, there’s something for everyone.

In this blog post, we’ve rounded up some of the best hunting podcasts that you should tune into this season to enhance your skills, knowledge, and passion for the great outdoors. So grab your headphones, pack your gear, and let’s explore the top hunting podcasts that will elevate your hunting experience.

1. The Hunting Public Podcast

If you’re looking for a podcast that covers a wide range of topics related to hunting, from tactics and strategies to gear and hunting stories, The Hunting Public Podcast is a must-listen. Hosted by a group of passionate hunters, this podcast offers down-to-earth advice and tips for hunters of all skill levels.

The team behind The Hunting Public is known for their focus on public land hunting, which provides a unique perspective for hunters who enjoy the challenge of hunting on accessible land. The podcast dives into topics like scouting, hunting gear, bowhunting techniques, and tips for hunting in various terrains and conditions. The hosts also share personal stories from their hunting experiences, making each episode both informative and entertaining.

Why Listen:

  • In-depth hunting advice and tips
  • Focus on public land hunting and accessible hunting spots
  • Personal stories and experiences from seasoned hunters

2. MeatEater Podcast

For hunters who not only enjoy the hunt but also appreciate the art of preparing and cooking their game, the MeatEater Podcast is a great choice. Hosted by Steven Rinella, the founder of MeatEater, Inc., this podcast blends hunting, conservation, and food in a way that will leave you both inspired and hungry.

Steven Rinella is known for his deep knowledge of hunting and outdoor survival, as well as his passion for ethical hunting practices. In each episode, Rinella interviews experts in the field of hunting, conservation, and wildlife management, providing listeners with valuable insights into the importance of sustainable hunting and responsible harvesting. The podcast also delves into topics like cooking wild game, outdoor survival skills, and how to make the most of every part of the animal you harvest.

Why Listen:

  • Combines hunting with cooking and conservation
  • Focuses on sustainable and ethical hunting practices
  • Interviews with experts and thought leaders in the hunting and conservation world

3. Bowhunter Chronicles Podcast

For bowhunters, the Bowhunter Chronicles Podcast is an essential listen. Hosted by John and Brad, two passionate bowhunters, this podcast provides expert insights and advice specifically geared toward those who hunt with a bow. The hosts share their personal experiences, tips, and challenges they’ve faced while hunting with a bow, offering valuable lessons for hunters looking to improve their skills.

The podcast also features interviews with industry experts, from gear manufacturers to experienced bowhunters, providing listeners with up-to-date information on the latest hunting gear, trends, and techniques. If you’re a bowhunter looking to up your game, this podcast is full of actionable advice and stories that will keep you engaged from start to finish.

Why Listen:

  • Focused on bowhunting tactics, gear, and strategies
  • Personal stories and lessons from experienced bowhunters
  • Interviews with experts in the bowhunting community

4. The Gritty Bowman Podcast

The Gritty Bowman Podcast is a favorite among hunters who are serious about improving their skills and taking their hunting to the next level. Hosted by Brian Call, this podcast covers a wide range of topics, from fitness and endurance to gear reviews and hunting strategies. Call’s passion for bowhunting and fitness shines through in each episode, as he encourages listeners to push their limits and become better hunters.

In addition to hunting tactics, the podcast frequently covers topics related to physical fitness, as Call believes that being in top physical condition is crucial for success in the field. With interviews from top hunters, fitness experts, and gear manufacturers, The Gritty Bowman Podcast offers practical advice and inspiration to help hunters excel.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on fitness and endurance for hunters
  • In-depth gear reviews and hunting tips
  • Interviews with top hunters and experts in the field

5. The Western Hunter Podcast

The Western Hunter Podcast is a must-listen for those who are passionate about hunting in the western United States. This podcast focuses on big game hunting in rugged terrains, providing listeners with expert advice on everything from scouting and mapping to hunting strategies and gear recommendations.

The hosts of The Western Hunter Podcast are experienced hunters who specialize in hunting elk, mule deer, and other big game species in the western states. They offer in-depth discussions on the unique challenges of hunting in mountainous terrain, as well as tips on how to successfully plan and execute a western hunting trip. Whether you’re planning a hunt in the Rockies or the Pacific Northwest, this podcast will give you the knowledge and confidence to tackle your next big game hunt.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on hunting big game in western terrains
  • Expert advice on scouting, mapping, and hunting strategies
  • Insight into the unique challenges of western hunting

6. The Nock On Podcast

The Nock On Podcast is another fantastic option for bowhunters looking to improve their skills. Hosted by John Dudley, a world-class archer and bowhunting expert, this podcast offers a wealth of knowledge on everything related to bowhunting and archery. From shooting techniques and form to hunting gear and strategies, the Nock On Podcast covers it all.

Dudley’s expertise and passion for archery are evident in every episode, as he breaks down complex bowhunting topics in an accessible way. The podcast also features interviews with professional hunters, archers, and gear manufacturers, providing listeners with valuable insights into the latest trends and technologies in the world of bowhunting.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on archery and bowhunting techniques
  • Expert advice from world-class bowhunter John Dudley
  • Interviews with industry professionals and top hunters

7. Wired To Hunt Podcast

The Wired To Hunt Podcast is a favorite among whitetail hunters, offering in-depth advice and strategies for hunting one of the most popular big game animals in North America. Hosted by Mark Kenyon, the podcast dives into all things whitetail, from habitat management and food plots to hunting tactics and gear recommendations.

Kenyon brings on a variety of experts, from deer biologists to veteran hunters, to discuss the latest research and trends in whitetail hunting. Whether you’re hunting in the Midwest or the East Coast, Wired To Hunt offers practical tips and strategies that can help you up your whitetail game this season.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on whitetail hunting tactics and strategies
  • Expert advice from biologists and experienced hunters
  • Practical tips for hunters across North America

8. The Hunt Backcountry Podcast

The Hunt Backcountry Podcast is perfect for hunters who enjoy the challenge of backcountry hunting. Hosted by Adam and John, this podcast focuses on hunting in remote and rugged areas, providing valuable advice on everything from gear selection to survival skills.

The hosts are experienced backcountry hunters who share their personal experiences and lessons learned from years of hunting in the wild. The podcast also covers topics like physical conditioning, mental preparedness, and how to successfully plan and execute a backcountry hunting trip. If you’re looking to take your hunting to new, more challenging terrain, this podcast will provide the guidance you need.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on backcountry hunting and remote wilderness areas
  • Practical advice on gear, survival, and physical conditioning
  • Personal stories and lessons from experienced backcountry hunters

9. The Bowhunting.com Podcast

For bowhunters who want to stay up to date with the latest trends and gear in the industry, The Bowhunting.com Podcast is an excellent resource. Hosted by the team behind Bowhunting.com, this podcast features interviews with bowhunting experts, gear reviews, and discussions on hunting techniques and strategies.

The podcast covers a wide range of topics, from hunting tactics for different species to the latest advancements in bowhunting gear. Whether you’re a novice bowhunter or a seasoned veteran, the Bowhunting.com Podcast offers valuable insights that can help you improve your skills and increase your success in the field.

Why Listen:

  • Focus on the latest bowhunting gear and techniques
  • Interviews with bowhunting experts and gear manufacturers
  • Practical advice for hunters of all skill levels

Conclusion

Whether you’re preparing for your next hunting trip or simply looking to stay connected to the hunting community, these podcasts are the perfect companions for the season ahead. From expert advice and gear reviews to personal stories and hunting strategies, each podcast offers unique insights that will enhance your hunting experience.

So, whether you’re out in the field or relaxing at home, don’t forget to tune in to these top hunting podcasts. They’ll provide you with the knowledge, inspiration, and motivation to make this hunting season your best one yet. Happy hunting!
