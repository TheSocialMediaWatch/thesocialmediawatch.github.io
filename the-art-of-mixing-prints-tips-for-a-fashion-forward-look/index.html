The Art of Mixing Prints: Tips for a Fashion-Forward Look


Have you ever looked at someone who effortlessly mixes prints and wondered how they do it? Mixing prints is an art form in the fashion world that can elevate your style and make a bold statement. If you’re ready to step out of your fashion comfort zone and experiment with prints, then this blog post is for you. In this article, we’ll be discussing some key tips and tricks for mastering the art of mixing prints and creating a fashion-forward look that is uniquely yours.

1. Start with a Neutral Base

When it comes to mixing prints, it’s important to start with a neutral base. This can be a solid-colored top or bottom that will serve as the foundation for your print mixing. By keeping one piece neutral, it allows the prints to take center stage and avoids overwhelming your outfit. Neutral colors like black, white, navy, or beige are great options to start with.

2. Stick to a Color Palette

To create a cohesive and harmonious look, it’s essential to stick to a color palette when mixing prints. Choose prints that have at least one color in common to tie the pieces together. For example, if you’re mixing a floral top with a striped skirt, make sure they both have a similar shade of blue in them. This will create a visually pleasing and put-together look.

3. Vary the Scale of Prints

Mixing prints successfully involves playing with scale. Pairing prints of different scales adds depth and interest to your outfit. For example, if you’re wearing a small polka dot top, consider pairing it with a larger floral print skirt. This contrast in scale creates a visually appealing contrast and prevents the prints from competing with each other.

4. Pay Attention to Proportions

When mixing prints, it’s important to pay attention to proportions. If you’re wearing a bold and busy print on top, balance it out with a simpler and more subdued print on the bottom. This keeps the focus on the prints and prevents your outfit from looking too overwhelming. Similarly, if you’re mixing prints in a single garment, make sure the proportions of the prints are balanced and don’t overwhelm your figure.

5. Mix Different Types of Prints

Don’t be afraid to mix different types of prints to create a truly unique and fashion-forward look. Experiment with combining stripes with florals, polka dots with animal prints, or geometrics with abstract patterns. The key is to find a common element, such as color or scale, to tie the prints together and create a cohesive ensemble.

6. Use Accessories to Tie the Look Together

Accessories can be a powerful tool when it comes to pulling off a mixed print outfit. Use accessories like belts, scarves, or shoes to tie the different prints together and create a cohesive look. For example, if you’re wearing a floral top and a striped skirt, consider adding a belt in a color that appears in both prints. This will visually unify the prints and create a polished and put-together look.

7. Confidence is Key

Last but not least, confidence is the most important accessory when it comes to pulling off a mixed print outfit. Experimenting with prints and patterns can be intimidating at first, but remember that fashion is all about expressing yourself and having fun. Own your style choices, embrace the unexpected combinations, and wear your mixed print outfit with confidence. After all, fashion is a form of self-expression, and there are no rules when it comes to self-expression.

So, are you ready to take your fashion game to the next level? With these tips and tricks, you’ll be able to confidently mix prints and create fashion-forward looks that turn heads. Remember to start with a neutral base, stick to a color palette, vary the scale of prints, pay attention to proportions, mix different types of prints, use accessories to tie the look together, and most importantly, wear your outfit with confidence. Happy mixing!
