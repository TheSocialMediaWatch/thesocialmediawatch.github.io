How to Wear Denim on Denim in a Chic Way


Denim on denim, also known as the Canadian tuxedo, has been a fashion trend for decades. When done right, it exudes effortless style and a cool, laid-back vibe. Whether you’re looking to rock a casual daytime look or elevate your evening attire, mastering the art of wearing denim on denim can take your fashion game to the next level. Here are some tips on how to pull off this trend in a chic and sophisticated way.

1. Mix and Match Washes

The key to nailing the denim on denim look is to mix and match different washes. Pair a dark denim jacket with a lighter wash pair of jeans, or vice versa. The contrast between the two pieces adds visual interest and prevents the outfit from looking too monotonous.

2. Pay Attention to Proportions

When styling denim on denim, it’s crucial to pay attention to proportions. If you’re wearing a loose, oversized denim jacket, opt for a more fitted pair of jeans to balance out the silhouette. Conversely, if you’re sporting a tailored denim shirt, consider pairing it with a slouchy boyfriend jean for a chic, relaxed vibe.

3. Add Statement Accessories

Elevate your denim on denim ensemble by incorporating statement accessories. A bold belt, chunky jewelry, or a vibrant scarf can break up the denim and add a touch of personality to your look. By accessorizing thoughtfully, you can transform a simple denim outfit into a fashion statement.

4. Experiment with Textures

To add depth and complexity to your denim on denim outfit, experiment with different textures. Consider layering a denim jacket over a chambray shirt for a subtle contrast in fabric. Alternatively, mix in leather, suede, or knitwear to create a multi-dimensional look that’s both stylish and sophisticated.

5. Embrace Double Denim in Formal Settings

Contrary to popular belief, denim on denim can be worn in formal settings when executed with finesse. Opt for tailored denim pieces in dark, indigo hues and pair them with sleek, polished accessories. A structured blazer or a pair of elegant heels can instantly elevate your double denim look, making it suitable for a business casual environment or a sophisticated evening affair.

6. Play with Colors

While classic blue denim is timeless, don’t be afraid to play with different colors. White denim paired with a light-wash denim shirt exudes a fresh, summery vibe, while black denim on black creates a sleek, edgy aesthetic. Experimenting with colored denim opens up a world of possibilities and allows you to express your personal style in a unique and creative way.

7. Confidence is Key

Above all, the most crucial element in pulling off denim on denim is confidence. Own your look and wear it with self-assurance. When you feel good in what you’re wearing, it radiates through your demeanor, making any outfit, including double denim, undeniably chic.

In conclusion, wearing denim on denim in a chic way is all about balance, creativity, and confidence. By following these tips and experimenting with different combinations, you can effortlessly master this timeless trend and make it your own. So, go ahead, embrace the denim on denim look, and let your personal style shine through!
