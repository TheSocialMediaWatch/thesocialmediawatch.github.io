The Best Financial Podcasts to Follow


In today’s fast-paced world, staying updated on the latest financial trends and insights can be challenging. Fortunately, podcasts have emerged as a convenient and informative way to absorb valuable financial knowledge on the go. Whether you’re a seasoned investor, a budding entrepreneur, or simply interested in personal finance, there’s a wealth of financial podcasts available to cater to your interests. To help you navigate this sea of information, we’ve curated a list of some of the best financial podcasts that are worth adding to your playlist.

1. The Dave Ramsey Show

Hosted by personal finance guru Dave Ramsey, this podcast provides practical advice on getting out of debt, building wealth, and achieving financial freedom. With a focus on common-sense money management, Ramsey’s no-nonsense approach and real-life success stories make this podcast an essential listen for anyone looking to take control of their finances.

2. The Indicator from Planet Money

Produced by NPR, The Indicator offers a unique perspective on economic news and trends, breaking down complex financial concepts into digestible segments. Hosted by Cardiff Garcia and Stacey Vanek Smith, this podcast delivers insightful analysis while keeping the content engaging and accessible to listeners of all backgrounds.

3. The Tony Robbins Podcast

Renowned life and business strategist Tony Robbins hosts a podcast that delves into the world of finance, investing, and personal development. Featuring interviews with industry experts and leaders, Robbins shares valuable insights and strategies for achieving financial success and mastering the psychology of wealth.

4. Motley Fool Money

For those seeking investment advice and market analysis, Motley Fool Money serves as an excellent resource. Hosted by Chris Hill, this podcast provides in-depth discussions on stock market movements, company performance, and investment strategies, offering valuable perspectives for both novice and experienced investors.

5. BiggerPockets Money Podcast

Geared towards individuals interested in real estate investing and achieving financial independence, the BiggerPockets Money Podcast is hosted by Scott Trench and Mindy Jensen. Through candid interviews with successful entrepreneurs and investors, this podcast offers actionable advice and inspiration for building wealth through real estate and other investment opportunities.

6. So Money with Farnoosh Torabi

Hosted by award-winning financial correspondent Farnoosh Torabi, So Money features insightful interviews with leading financial experts, authors, and entrepreneurs. Covering a wide range of topics including entrepreneurship, career development, and personal finance, this podcast empowers listeners to make informed decisions about their financial futures.

7. Afford Anything

Hosted by Paula Pant, Afford Anything encourages listeners to think differently about money, lifestyle, and personal finance. With a focus on financial independence and smart investing, Pant’s podcast explores the concept of achieving financial freedom and designing a life without limits.

8. The Tim Ferriss Show

While not exclusively a finance podcast, The Tim Ferriss Show offers a wealth of knowledge on productivity, success, and financial freedom. Renowned author and entrepreneur Tim Ferriss interviews world-class performers from various fields, extracting practical tips and insights that can be applied to financial endeavors and personal growth.

9. The Money Guy Show

Hosts Brian Preston and Bo Hanson provide practical, down-to-earth advice on all things money-related in The Money Guy Show. Covering topics such as retirement planning, investment strategies, and wealth management, this podcast delivers valuable financial wisdom in an engaging and relatable manner.

10. The Clark Howard Podcast

Consumer expert Clark Howard hosts a podcast that focuses on helping listeners save more, spend less, and avoid costly financial mistakes. With a strong emphasis on consumer advocacy and smart money management, this podcast equips its audience with practical tips for making sound financial decisions in their everyday lives.

Whether you’re looking to expand your financial knowledge, gain insights from industry experts, or simply find motivation to take control of your financial future, these top financial podcasts offer a wealth of valuable content. With the convenience of on-demand listening, you can seamlessly integrate these podcasts into your daily routine, transforming your commute, workout, or downtime into valuable learning opportunities. So, grab your headphones and start exploring the world of finance through the power of podcasting!
