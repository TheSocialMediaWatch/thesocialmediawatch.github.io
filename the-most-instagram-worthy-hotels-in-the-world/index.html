The Most Instagram-Worthy Hotels in the World


Traveling is no longer just about experiencing a new place; it’s also about capturing those perfect moments for social media. With Instagram being such a powerful platform for sharing travel experiences, more and more hotels around the world are designed to be visually striking and Instagrammable. These hotels offer travelers a combination of stunning aesthetics, unique architecture, and luxurious amenities, making them perfect for snapping that enviable vacation shot. Here, we explore the most Instagram-worthy hotels around the world that will elevate your social media game.

1. The Soneva Jani – Maldives

The Maldives is a dream destination for many, and it’s easy to see why when you look at the Soneva Jani. This hotel is renowned for its overwater villas, which feature private pools, open-air bathrooms, and even waterslides that lead directly into the crystal-clear lagoon. The vast, transparent floors of these villas allow you to gaze directly into the ocean below. The stunning views of the turquoise waters, coupled with the sustainable architecture and luxurious interiors, make this resort an absolute Instagram gem.

The sunset views at Soneva Jani are extraordinary. Capture the vibrant colors of the sky from the deck of your villa or relax in your private infinity pool while the sun dips below the horizon. With plenty of opportunities for aerial shots from a drone, the Maldives’ stunning scenery combined with Soneva Jani’s design will make your Instagram feed sparkle.

2. The Plaza – New York City, USA

Located in the heart of Manhattan, The Plaza Hotel is an iconic symbol of luxury and opulence. This legendary hotel is famous for its historical significance, stunning architecture, and old-world charm. With its grandiose lobby, crystal chandeliers, and elegant rooms, The Plaza offers guests plenty of Instagram-worthy moments.

Don’t forget to take a picture in front of the hotel’s grand façade, or inside the ornate Palm Court, which serves as an Instagram-friendly backdrop with its lush greenery and sophisticated decor. The elegant Afternoon Tea experience is another perfect photo opportunity, with delicate pastries and fine china making for a picture-perfect shot.

3. Amangiri – Canyon Point, Utah, USA

Nestled within the dramatic landscape of southern Utah, Amangiri is a desert resort that seamlessly blends into its surroundings. The hotel’s minimalist design contrasts beautifully with the rugged desert scenery, featuring geometric structures that reflect the colors and textures of the landscape. From the pool, you can admire the panoramic views of the mesas and canyons, making it an ideal spot for breathtaking photos.

Whether you’re lounging by the pool, dining under the stars, or hiking through the nearby canyons, every corner of Amangiri offers a stunning backdrop. The natural light, combined with the earthy tones of the architecture, creates the perfect atmosphere for stunning photography.

4. Icehotel – Jukkasjärvi, Sweden

If you’re looking for a hotel that’s truly one of a kind, the Icehotel in Sweden offers an unforgettable, icy experience. Built entirely from snow and ice, this unique hotel is located in the small village of Jukkasjärvi in northern Sweden. The hotel is reconstructed each year with new designs, featuring intricate ice sculptures and an otherworldly atmosphere.

Guests can stay in rooms made entirely of ice, with ice sculptures, ice furniture, and even ice beds. The stark contrast between the frosty interiors and the warm, cozy areas will provide Instagram shots that are unlike anything you’ve ever seen before. It’s a place where creativity thrives, making it a perfect destination for photographers and those seeking a truly unique experience.

5. Nayara Springs – Costa Rica

Nayara Springs, located in the Arenal Volcano National Park in Costa Rica, is a luxurious retreat surrounded by lush tropical rainforests. The hotel is famous for its private villas with plunge pools, which overlook the pristine rainforest. It’s the perfect location for travelers who want to combine luxury with the beauty of nature.

The hotel’s unique blend of modern design and natural beauty makes it an Instagram hotspot. Capture the cascading waterfalls, wildlife, and vibrant plant life around you, or take a shot from your private villa’s plunge pool with the Arenal Volcano in the background. Whether you’re relaxing in the hot springs, enjoying the panoramic views, or exploring the rainforest, every moment at Nayara Springs offers a new opportunity for the perfect Instagram shot.

6. The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto – Japan

Kyoto is known for its traditional temples, beautiful gardens, and stunning natural surroundings, and The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto, perfectly captures this blend of old and new. The hotel is nestled along the Kamogawa River, offering picturesque views of the city’s natural beauty. The design of the hotel incorporates elements of Kyoto’s cultural heritage, with traditional Japanese decor fused with modern luxury.

Guests can enjoy serene moments in the hotel’s tranquil Japanese gardens, or in the stylishly designed interiors. The stunning views of the Higashiyama mountains in the distance, the traditional tea house, and the peaceful atmosphere make The Ritz-Carlton, Kyoto, a fantastic location for capturing the elegance of Japanese culture on Instagram.

7. Post Ranch Inn – Big Sur, California, USA

Set atop the cliffs of Big Sur, Post Ranch Inn offers dramatic views of the Pacific Ocean and the rugged California coastline. The resort’s organic architecture blends seamlessly with the surrounding environment, with rooms designed to maximize views of the ocean or the nearby mountains. The infinity pools appear to spill directly into the ocean, providing an ethereal backdrop for your Instagram photos.

With its unique design, including eco-friendly materials and a laid-back vibe, Post Ranch Inn is the perfect place for those looking to unwind while capturing the beauty of California’s coastline. Whether you’re watching the sunrise over the ocean or soaking in a hot tub with views of the mountains, Post Ranch Inn offers endless opportunities for social media-worthy moments.

8. Bora Bora Pearl Beach Resort & Spa – Bora Bora, French Polynesia

Bora Bora is a dream destination for many, and the Bora Bora Pearl Beach Resort & Spa offers a stunning base to explore the island’s turquoise waters and white sandy beaches. This luxury resort features overwater bungalows with direct access to the lagoon, and the panoramic views of Mount Otemanu provide an incredible backdrop for photos.

The clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and abundant marine life make the resort’s lagoon-side settings perfect for underwater shots, while the overwater bungalows make for postcard-worthy images. Whether you’re enjoying a cocktail on the terrace or diving into the lagoon, Bora Bora Pearl Beach Resort & Spa will give you the perfect Instagram moments throughout your stay.

9. The Langham – Sydney, Australia

Located in the heart of Sydney, The Langham is an iconic hotel that exudes elegance and charm. With its opulent interiors, including luxurious rooms and suites, this hotel offers the perfect backdrop for a sophisticated Instagram feed. The hotel’s rooftop pool, which offers stunning views of Sydney’s skyline, is one of the highlights, providing an excellent photo opportunity for those who love to capture city views.

The hotel’s renowned Afternoon Tea, served in the elegant Palm Court, is also a must-photograph moment. The chic decor, combined with high tea delicacies, makes for a highly Instagrammable experience. Whether you’re lounging by the pool or enjoying a luxurious meal, The Langham in Sydney will provide you with plenty of Instagram-worthy moments.

10. Six Senses Zighy Bay – Oman

Six Senses Zighy Bay in Oman offers a unique and secluded experience, where you can stay in traditional Omani-style villas nestled between the mountains and the sea. The resort’s dramatic setting, with its rugged mountains on one side and pristine beaches on the other, creates a magical atmosphere that is perfect for photography.

Take a picture while enjoying the private pool with panoramic views of the ocean or embark on an adventure by paragliding over the bay. The resort’s architecture, which blends traditional Omani design with modern luxury, along with its stunning natural surroundings, makes Six Senses Zighy Bay an ideal place to capture moments that will look incredible on Instagram.

Conclusion

The world is filled with stunning hotels that are not only luxurious but also perfect for sharing your travel moments on Instagram. From overwater villas in the Maldives to the icy sculptures of Sweden’s Icehotel, there is no shortage of breathtaking places to stay. Whether you’re looking for dramatic landscapes, opulent interiors, or serene natural beauty, these hotels offer the perfect backdrop for creating those unforgettable Instagram photos. So pack your bags, charge your phone, and get ready to experience the most Instagram-worthy hotels around the globe!
