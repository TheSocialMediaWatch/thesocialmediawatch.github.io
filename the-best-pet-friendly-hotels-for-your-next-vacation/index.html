The Best Pet-Friendly Hotels for Your Next Vacation


Traveling with your furry companion can be one of the most enjoyable experiences, offering companionship, comfort, and shared adventure along the way. However, finding accommodations that welcome pets without sacrificing comfort and convenience can be a challenge. Fortunately, many hotels have embraced pet lovers, offering tailored services that make vacations feel like a joy for both you and your four-legged friend. This guide explores some of the top pet-friendly hotels around the world, what they offer, and tips for ensuring a smooth and enjoyable stay.

Why Choose Pet-Friendly Hotels?

When planning a vacation with your pet, it’s not only about finding a place to rest but also about feeling welcomed and valued. Pet-friendly hotels understand this need and usually provide amenities such as pet beds, special treats, pet menus, and even on-site grooming and walking services. This can greatly reduce stress for both you and your pet, enabling a more relaxed and memorable experience.

Key Features of Top Pet-Friendly Hotels

Before diving into the list, it’s important to know what to look for when booking:

  • Space: Larger rooms or suites allow pets to move comfortably.
  • Pet Policies: Understand pet size limits, fees, and any restrictions.
  • Amenities: Look for pet beds, bowls, toys, and dedicated pet areas.
  • Nearby Pet Attractions: Proximity to parks, trails, or beaches where pets are welcome.
  • Safety and Cleanliness: Hotels that maintain hygienic environments and safe surroundings for pets.
  • Service: Friendly staff familiar with pet needs and responsive to requests.

With these criteria in mind, let’s explore some of the best pet-friendly hotel chains and unique properties around the globe.

Top Pet-Friendly Hotel Chains

1. Kimpton Hotels & Restaurants – Across the U.S.

Kimpton is well-known for its pet-friendly philosophy. They not only allow pets of any size but also waive additional fees. Pets receive a welcome kit with treats and toys, and many locations have designated pet-friendly suites. Plus, Kimpton hotels often have dog-friendly happy hours and direct access to nearby parks.

2. Loews Hotels – Nationwide in the U.S.

The Loews Loves Pets program goes beyond basic acceptance; it offers pet-specific room service menus, special pet amenities, and pet sitting or walking services. Loews also maintains pet guidebooks for each location with recommendations for local vet services, dog parks, and pet boutiques.

3. Best Western Hotels & Resorts

Best Western properties in many locations are proud to be pet-friendly, especially the Best Western Plus and Best Western Premier brands. They provide specific rooms for pet owners and supply pet bedding and bowls. Their website also makes it easy to filter pet-friendly locations.

4. Marriott Hotels

Many Marriott hotels include pet-friendly rooms in their portfolio. Brands like Residence Inn and TownePlace Suites are particularly pet welcoming, often featuring kitchens perfect for preparing your pet’s meals and ample outdoor spaces for walks and play.

Unique Pet-Friendly Hotels Worldwide

5. The Grove in Los Angeles, California

This luxury hotel in LA embraces pets with open arms. With sprawling gardens and a dedicated “dog zone,” your pet can stretch their legs within the premises. The Grove cozies pets with plush bedding and gourmet treats in-room. The nearby Runyon Canyon and Griffith Park are great for hiking together.

6. The Alexis Hotel in Seattle, Washington

A boutique property known for its sustainable practices, The Alexis warmly welcomes pets and offers special amenities such as pet blankets and water bowls. It’s located near many dog-friendly parks and walking trails, making it a fantastic spot for active pet parents.

7. The Kimpton Clocktower Hotel in Manchester, UK

One of the most iconic pet-friendly hotels in the UK, the Kimpton Clocktower offers pet beds, bowls, and even a pet spa menu with grooming services. Plus, Manchester boasts many parks and pet-friendly cafes just a short walk away.

8. The Fife Arms in Braemar, Scotland

Luxury meets tradition at this historic hotel. It warmly welcomes pets, providing welcome baskets with local treats for your furry friend. The surrounding Scottish Highlands offer expansive trails and lochs perfect for dog walking and exploration.

9. The Fairmont Banff Springs in Alberta, Canada

Nestled within Banff National Park, this stunning hotel accommodates pets with attentive services and access to the breathtaking natural landscape. Hiking trails, dog-friendly outdoor dining, and pet sitting are among the perks here.

Tips for Traveling and Staying at Pet-Friendly Hotels

Successfully traveling with pets requires some planning and preparation. Here are essential tips:

Make Reservations Early

Pet-friendly rooms can be limited, especially in popular destinations. Book early to ensure availability.

Understand the Pet Policy

Each hotel will have Rules About Pets, including weight limits, fee structures, and restrictions on where pets can go on the property. Clarify these details before you arrive.

Pack a Pet Travel Kit

Include your pet’s favorite toys, bedding, food, water bowls, medications, and cleanup bags to keep comfortable and prepared.

Respect Hotel Rules and Other Guests

Keep pets on a leash in public areas, clean up after them, and ensure they do not disturb other guests.

Explore Local Pet-Friendly Activities

Look for hiking trails, dog parks, pet-friendly restaurants, and events near your accommodations.

Health and Identification

Ensure your pet’s vaccinations are up to date and carry any required health certificates. Have your pet wear identification tags or microchips as a safety precaution.

Conclusion: Traveling Together Is the Ultimate Bonding Experience

Choosing the right pet-friendly hotel can transform your vacation into an unforgettable adventure shared with your companion. Whether you’re seeking luxury, rustic charm, or convenience, there’s a perfect pet-friendly hotel ready to welcome you and your furry friend. From iconic chains with comprehensive services to charming boutique stays rich with local character, the options are abundant. With a little planning and the right hotel, your next trip can be a harmonious blend of relaxation, discovery, and quality time with your pet.

Travel well, travel together, and enjoy every moment of your journey!
