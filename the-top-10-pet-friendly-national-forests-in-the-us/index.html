The Top 10 Pet-Friendly National Forests in the US


When it comes to exploring the great outdoors, there’s nothing quite like taking your furry friend along for the adventure. Luckily, the United States is home to a plethora of pet-friendly national forests that offer stunning scenery, exciting trails, and unforgettable experiences for both you and your beloved pet. Whether you have a four-legged friend who loves to hike, swim, or simply bask in nature’s beauty, these top 10 pet-friendly national forests in the US are sure to provide the perfect backdrop for your next outdoor escapade.

1. Olympic National Forest, Washington

Located in the beautiful state of Washington, Olympic National Forest offers a diverse range of landscapes, including lush rainforests, pristine lakes, and rugged mountain peaks. With over 600 miles of trails to explore, you and your pet will have plenty of opportunities to embark on memorable adventures together. Don’t miss the breathtaking views from Hurricane Ridge or the refreshing dip in Lake Quinault.

2. Pisgah National Forest, North Carolina

Nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains, Pisgah National Forest is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and their furry companions. From scenic waterfalls to challenging hiking trails, this pet-friendly forest has it all. Be sure to check out the iconic Looking Glass Rock and the picturesque Blue Ridge Parkway, both of which offer incredible views that will leave you in awe.

3. Angeles National Forest, California

For those seeking a pet-friendly escape near the bustling city of Los Angeles, Angeles National Forest is the perfect choice. With its sprawling wilderness, towering mountains, and abundant wildlife, this forest provides a welcome respite from the urban jungle. Take your pup for a stroll along the scenic Gabrielino Trail or enjoy a picnic by the tranquil San Gabriel River.

4. Chattahoochee-Oconee National Forest, Georgia

Located in the southern part of the Appalachian Mountains, Chattahoochee-Oconee National Forest offers a wide range of outdoor activities for both you and your furry friend. From camping and fishing to hiking and swimming, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Don’t forget to explore the picturesque Blood Mountain Wilderness and the enchanting Anna Ruby Falls.

5. Mount Hood National Forest, Oregon

Nestled in the Cascade Range, Mount Hood National Forest is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and their pets. With its towering peaks, pristine lakes, and lush forests, this pet-friendly forest offers endless opportunities for adventure. Be sure to take your furry friend on a hike to the iconic Mirror Lake or challenge yourselves with a climb up Mount Hood itself.

6. White Mountain National Forest, New Hampshire

If you’re seeking a pet-friendly national forest on the East Coast, look no further than White Mountain National Forest in New Hampshire. Known for its stunning fall foliage and breathtaking alpine scenery, this forest is a must-visit for nature lovers. Take your pup for a walk along the picturesque Franconia Notch State Park or explore the popular Kancamagus Highway.

7. Coconino National Forest, Arizona

With its towering red rock formations and awe-inspiring canyons, Coconino National Forest in Arizona is a pet-friendly destination like no other. Explore the breathtaking beauty of Sedona’s red rocks or take your furry friend for a swim in the cool waters of Oak Creek. Don’t miss the opportunity to hike the famous West Fork Trail, known for its stunning scenery and peaceful ambiance.

8. Nantahala National Forest, North Carolina

Nantahala National Forest, located in the western part of North Carolina, is a hidden gem that offers a wide range of outdoor activities for you and your pet. From whitewater rafting and kayaking to hiking and fishing, this pet-friendly forest has it all. Be sure to check out the scenic Nantahala River Gorge and the breathtaking views from Wayah Bald.

9. Uinta-Wasatch-Cache National Forest, Utah

For those seeking a pet-friendly national forest in the western United States, Uinta-Wasatch-Cache National Forest in Utah is an excellent choice. With its majestic peaks, crystal-clear lakes, and abundant wildlife, this forest is a dream come true for outdoor enthusiasts. Take your furry friend for a hike in the picturesque Big Cottonwood Canyon or enjoy a scenic drive along the Mirror Lake Highway.

10. Green Mountain National Forest, Vermont

Last but certainly not least, Green Mountain National Forest in Vermont offers a charming and pet-friendly escape for nature lovers. With its rolling hills, dense forests, and picturesque lakes, this forest provides the perfect setting for outdoor adventures with your furry companion. Don’t miss the opportunity to hike the iconic Long Trail or take a dip in the refreshing waters of Lake George.

In conclusion, the United States is home to a vast array of pet-friendly national forests that cater to outdoor enthusiasts and their furry friends. Whether you’re seeking breathtaking mountain vistas, tranquil lakeside retreats, or lush green forests, these top 10 pet-friendly national forests offer the ideal setting for unforgettable adventures with your beloved pet. So pack your bags, leash up your furry friend, and embark on a journey of a lifetime in one of these incredible destinations. Happy exploring!
