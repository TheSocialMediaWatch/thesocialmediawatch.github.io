Top 5 Rum Brands You Need to Try This Year


As the world's most iconic spirit, rum has a rich history and a wide range of flavors to offer. With its origins in the Caribbean and its spread across the globe, rum has become an integral part of many cultures and is enjoyed by people from all walks of life. So, if you're a rum enthusiast or just looking to explore the world of rum, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll take a journey through the top 5 rum brands you need to try this year.

  1. Myers's Original Dark Rum For over 150 years, Myers's has been the gold standard of dark rums. With its rich, full-bodied flavor and smooth finish, it's no wonder why this Jamaican rum has gained cult status among rum enthusiasts. Myers's Original Dark Rum is a true representation of the island's spirit, with its robust flavor profile featuring notes of oak, vanilla, and a hint of smokiness. Perfectly balanced, this rum is a must-have for any serious rum aficionado. Tasting Notes: Oak, vanilla, smoky, spicy Recipe: Try pairing Myers's Dark Rum with a cola or ginger beer for a refreshing twist on the classic Cuba Libre.
  2. El Dorado Special Reserve 15 Year Old Rum Aged for 15 years in oak barrels, this rum from Demerara Distillers Limited in Guyana is a masterpiece. The El Dorado Special Reserve 15 Year Old Rum boasts a rich, complex flavor profile with notes of honey, apricot, and a hint of citrus. With its smooth, velvety texture, this rum is perfect for sipping on the rocks or enjoying as a base for craft cocktails. Tasting Notes: Honey, apricot, citrus, oak Recipe: Try pairing El Dorado Special Reserve 15 with a splash of orange liqueur and a dash of bitters for a sophisticated rum cocktail.
  3. Appleton Estate Signature Blend Appleton Estate, the oldest rum distillery in Jamaica, has been crafting some of the world's finest rums for over 265 years. Their Signature Blend is a masterclass in balance and complexity, featuring a rich, full-bodied flavor profile with notes of oak, vanilla, and a hint of tropical fruit. This rum is perfect for those who enjoy a smooth, approachable drinking experience. Tasting Notes: Vanilla, oak, tropical fruit, smooth Recipe: Try pairing Appleton Estate Signature Blend with a splash of simple syrup and a squeeze of fresh lime for a refreshing Collins-style cocktail.
  4. Mount Gay Black Barrel Rum A true pioneer in the world of rum, Mount Gay has been producing exceptional rums for over 300 years. Their signature Black Barrel Rum is a masterfully balanced blend of rums aged in hand-chosen black bourbon barrels, giving it a rich, complex flavor profile. With notes of vanilla, oak, and a hint of spice, this rum is perfect for sipping or mixing. Tasting Notes: Vanilla, oak, spice, molasses Recipe: Try pairing Mount Gay Black Barrel Rum with a splash of coconut cream and a dash of nutmeg for a decadent rum cocktail.
  5. Foursquare Doorly's 12 Year Old Extrasrhobados Rum From the esteemed Foursquare Distillery in Barbados comes this exceptional rum. Doorly's 12 Year Old Extra is a rich, full-bodied rum with a complex flavor profile featuring notes of oak, vanilla, and a hint of smoky spice. Perfectly balanced and with a smooth finish, this rum is a true testament to the craftsmanship of the Foursquare Distillery. Tasting Notes: Oak, vanilla, smoky, spice Recipe: Try pairing Doorly's 12 Year Old Extra with a splash of cacao and a dash of bitters for a rich, complex rum cocktail. Conclusion: If you're a rum enthusiast or new to the world of rum, the top 5 brands mentioned in this article are a must-try. From Myers's Original Dark Rum to Foursquare's Doorly's 12 Year Old Extra, each of these brands offers a unique and exceptional tasting experience. With their rich flavor profiles and smooth finishes, they're sure to delight even the most discerning palates. Whether you prefer to sip or mix, these top 5 rum brands are sure to become a staple in your rum collection.
