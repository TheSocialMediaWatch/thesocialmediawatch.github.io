The Best Fantasy Novels for Fans of Romance


Fantasy novels offer a gateway to rich, imaginative worlds filled with magic, quests, and epic stories. When blended with romance, they create an irresistible concoction of passion and adventure, conjuring tales that transport readers to far-off realms while tugging on their heartstrings. For fans of both genres, the integration of romance into fantasy stories adds complexity, emotional depth, and stakes that go beyond the protagonist’s heroism. Whether you’re looking for epic tales of love in magical kingdoms, forbidden romances between warriors and sorcerers, or slow-burn relationships that carry the weight of entire worlds, there’s something to ignite your imagination. Below are some of the most enchanting fantasy novels perfect for readers who crave stories steeped in romance.

1. A Court of Thorns and Roses by Sarah J. Maas

Sarah J. Maas has a reputation for blending spellbinding fantasy with heart-stopping romance. A Court of Thorns and Roses, the first in its namesake series, follows Feyre Archeron, a fiercely independent huntress who is swept into the dangerous, magical land of the Fae after an ill-fated decision.

What makes this book stand out is its ability to weave fairy tale-inspired themes into an exhilarating story of love and survival. Feyre’s fiery personality clashes and harmonizes with Tamlin, the enigmatic Fae lord who holds her captive. The romance between Feyre and Tamlin unfolds against the backdrop of an enchanting world where ancient curses and simmering tensions make every moment feel charged with urgency.

For readers who crave intricate love stories set in mystical realms, Sarah J. Maas’ ability to blend sizzling chemistry with breathtaking fantasy elements guarantees that you won’t be able to put this book down.

2. From Blood and Ash by Jennifer L. Armentrout

From Blood and Ash catapulted Jennifer L. Armentrout into the upper echelon of fantasy romance authors. The story centers around Poppy, a maiden whose very existence is riddled with obligation and secrecy. As one destined to usher in the future of her kingdom, her role is to remain untouched and sheltered from the world.

But enter Hawke, a mysterious and fiercely protective guard who challenges everything Poppy knows — including the boundaries of her heart. The sizzling chemistry and forbidden nature of their relationship give this book its romantic edge, while the expansive world-building and unfolding mysteries make it impossible not to keep turning the pages. Lovers of slow-burn romance will appreciate the tension between Poppy and Hawke as they navigate danger, betrayal, and emotion.

3. Serpent & Dove by Shelby Mahurin

Magic and romance collide in Shelby Mahurin’s Serpent & Dove, a dazzling enemies-to-lovers fantasy novel. The story follows Lou, a crafty witch determined to escape her enemies, and Reid, a devout witch-hunting Chasseur. When fate binds them together in an unexpected marriage, sparks fly as they clash on every conceivable level.

What sets Serpent & Dove apart is its dynamic exploration of love blooming amidst opposition. Lou and Reid’s differences—perceived morality, beliefs, and personal histories—provide a fascinating framework for their relationship to thrive. As the characters grow closer, their romance is as tantalizing as the magical and political intrigue surrounding them. Mahurin’s sharp writing and wit enhance the chemistry between her protagonists, ensuring every moment feels as vibrant as the story’s magical undercurrents.

4. The Cruel Prince by Holly Black

For readers who delight in intricate power plays and morally complex characters, Holly Black’s The Cruel Prince offers an irresistible mix of court intrigue, dark temptations, and simmering romance. The story introduces Jude Duarte, a mortal girl thrust into the ruthless world of the Fae. Determined to carve out a space for herself, Jude finds herself entangled with Cardan, the cruel and captivating prince of the Faerie realm.

The romance in The Cruel Prince is anything but straightforward. Jude and Cardan share a complicated dynamic filled with tension, veiled emotions, and occasional cruelty that keeps readers guessing. Their relationship evolves slowly, layered amidst themes of ambition, betrayal, and survival in a treacherous world. Black’s deft writing ensures the story remains enthralling, creating a unique intersection of romance and brutal fantasy.

5. The Wrath & The Dawn by Renée Ahdieh

Renée Ahdieh’s The Wrath & The Dawn takes inspiration from One Thousand and One Nights, presenting a lush fantasy world rich in romance, intrigue, and suspense. The story focuses on Shahrzad, who volunteers to marry Khalid, the Caliph known for murdering his brides before dawn. Determined to survive and avenge her friend, Shahrzad spins captivating stories to win Khalid’s favor.

As their relationship deepens, Shahrzad discovers there is more to Khalid than his ruthless reputation. Secrets unravel, hearts are tested, and emotions come to the forefront as a romance blooms within the confines of lies and danger. Ahdieh’s exquisite prose makes this book a treasure for readers who love sensuous storytelling and slow-burning love set against a backdrop of mystery.

6. Daughter of Smoke and Bone by Laini Taylor

Few fantasy novels can match the lyrical beauty and emotional depth of Laini Taylor’s Daughter of Smoke and Bone. Karou, an art student with striking blue hair, leads a seemingly ordinary life in Prague — except for the secrets she keeps. Raised by a chimera named Brimstone who trades in teeth, Karou’s world is anything but mundane.

When Karou encounters Akiva, an angel battling against Brimstone’s kind, their connection is instant. But their romance is far from simple — it’s layered with tragedy, betrayal, and the histories of their people. Taylor’s evocative writing and lush world-building create a story brimming with passion and heartbreak, striking a balance between romance and the broader fantasy narrative.

7. The Night Circus by Erin Morgenstern

Erin Morgenstern’s The Night Circus is more than a novel — it’s an experience. Set in an ethereal, magical circus that appears without warning, the story centers around Celia and Marco, two illusionists locked in a competition orchestrated by their mentors.

As their rivalry deepens, so does their connection, weaving an intoxicating romance amidst a setting of wonder and enchantment. The love story isn’t the book’s sole focus; instead, it’s seamlessly interwoven into the mesmerizing world of the circus, where magic and mystery abound. Morgenstern’s poetic prose makes the relationship between Celia and Marco unforgettable and hauntingly beautiful, appealing to readers who adore subtle yet powerful romance.

8. Kingdom of the Wicked by Kerri Maniscalco

For fans of darker themes blended with romance, Kingdom of the Wicked offers an enthralling read. Emilia, a witch seeking answers after the murder of her twin sister, summons Wrath, a wicked prince from Hell. With the two clashing over secrets, magic, and motives, their chemistry crackles with intensity.

Kerri Maniscalco’s tale incorporates Gothic undertones and shadowy intrigue, creating a lush atmosphere where desires and dangers coexist. Wrath’s brooding nature and Emilia’s fiery resolve form the backbone of a romance that evolves in unexpected ways. Beyond the romance, the book’s vivid world-building immerses readers in a sinister yet captivating story with plenty of twists.

9. The Song of Achilles by Madeline Miller

While technically a reimagining of Greek mythology, The Song of Achilles deserves a place on this list due to its stunning exploration of love in a fantastical setting. The story follows the life of Patroclus, as he is drawn to Achilles, the legendary hero destined for greatness. Their bond deepens, turning into a touching and deeply emotional romance that endures trials of war, destiny, and sacrifice.

Madeline Miller’s writing is achingly beautiful, capturing the tenderness and tragedy of their love against the backdrop of mythological intrigue. Readers seeking soul-stirring romance will find themselves transfixed by this poignant tale that feels timeless yet uniquely intimate.

Conclusion: Fantasy and Romance, a Perfect Blend

Fantasy novels enriched with romance succeed because they add layers of emotion to otherwise epic tales of magic, adventure, and heroism. From forbidden love and enemies-to-lovers dynamics to smashing stereotypes and exploring profound emotional connections, these books demonstrate why romance makes fantasy stories even more engaging.

Whether you’re looking for lush prose, deep character dynamics, or magical worlds with an undercurrent of passion, the novels listed above promise unforgettable journeys — both through fantastical realms and the mysteries of the heart. Ready to dive into your next enchanting read? These stories await, each offering a unique blend of love and magic to sweep you off your feet.
