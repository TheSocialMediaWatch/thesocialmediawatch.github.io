The Best SUVs for Families in 2023


When it comes to choosing the perfect family vehicle, SUVs have cemented their place as the go-to option for many households. Their combination of space, comfort, safety, and versatility makes them ideal for families looking to tackle everything from school runs to long road trips. With manufacturers constantly improving design, technology, and functionality, the SUV market in 2023 is rich with options that cater to various family needs. In this guide, we take an in-depth look at some of the best SUVs for families this year, highlighting what makes them standout choices and why they could be the perfect addition to your driveway.

What Makes an SUV Ideal for Families?

Before diving into specific models, it’s important to understand the essential qualities that make an SUV a great family vehicle. For most families, the following factors matter the most:

  • Safety: Advanced safety features, crash test ratings, and reliable build quality are top priorities.
  • Space: Roomy interiors with ample seating and cargo capacity.
  • Comfort: Adjustable seating, climate control, and user-friendly infotainment systems contribute to an enjoyable ride.
  • Efficiency: Fuel economy and hybrid or electric options help families save money while reducing environmental impact.
  • Technology: Connectivity features, navigation systems, and entertainment options for kids can improve road trips and daily commutes.

In 2023, automotive manufacturers have refined these aspects across their offerings, resulting in a highly competitive lineup. Let’s explore some of the best SUVs that check all the boxes for family-friendly transportation.

Top Contenders for Family SUVs in 2023

1. Kia Telluride

The Kia Telluride continues to dominate the family SUV market due to its balance of affordability, luxury, and practicality. For 2023, this three-row SUV remains highly regarded for its spacious cabin that comfortably seats up to eight passengers. The Telluride offers premium materials and thoughtful design touches throughout the interior, making long drives more enjoyable for everyone.

Key highlights:

  • Standard suite of safety features, including forward collision warning and lane-keeping assist.
  • 12.3-inch infotainment display with seamless smartphone integration.
  • Smooth and quiet ride, perfect for commuting with kids.
  • A competitive price point with upscale trims available for added luxury.

Families who prize reliability and comfort in an affordable package will find the Kia Telluride hard to beat. It also boasts an impressive towing capacity, ideal for vacations with trailers or boats.

2. Toyota Highlander Hybrid

Efficiency is becoming increasingly important for families, and the Toyota Highlander Hybrid delivers both practicality and eco-friendly performance. This midsize SUV offers seating for up to eight passengers and shines with exceptional fuel economy.

What sets it apart:

  • Hybrid powertrain achieves up to 36 MPG combined, reducing costs over time.
  • Strong emphasis on advanced safety technology, including adaptive cruise control and pedestrian detection.
  • Quiet ride with a well-insulated cabin designed for family comfort.
  • Generous cargo capacity that can accommodate luggage, groceries, and sports equipment.

For families seeking an SUV that aligns with environmental values while keeping routine expenses down, the Highlander Hybrid is a solid choice.

3. Honda CR-V

Honda’s redesigned CR-V for 2023 has cemented its place as one of the most versatile compact SUVs on the market. While slightly smaller than midsize competitors, the CR-V boasts excellent interior space—more than enough to fit an average family with two or three kids. The new design also includes a hybrid variant for those interested in fuel efficiency.

Why families love it:

  • Intuitive controls and family-friendly technology like wireless phone charging and USB ports.
  • Smooth handling with reliable performance on city streets and highways.
  • Fantastic resale value and proven durability, making it an investment in the long term.
  • Advanced safety systems, including lane departure warning and collision mitigation.

Its manageable size allows easier parking and maneuvering, ideal for urban families that split their time between city life and weekend getaways.

4. Hyundai Palisade

The Hyundai Palisade has solidified itself as a favorite among family SUVs, thanks to its upscale design and thoughtful features. For 2023, Hyundai has introduced updates to the Palisade’s styling and technology, making it an even more compelling choice.

Standout features:

  • Luxurious interior materials that rival some luxury-brand SUVs.
  • A spacious third-row seating area that comfortably accommodates adults, offering true practicality for large families.
  • Cutting-edge connectivity with features like an integrated Wi-Fi hotspot and voice command functionality.
  • Comprehensive driver assistance features, including blind-spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert.

The Palisade pairs premium comfort with affordability, making it a strong contender for families looking to balance class and cost.

5. Subaru Ascent

Known for rugged reliability and standard all-wheel drive, the Subaru Ascent caters to families with adventurous lifestyles. Whether you’re dealing with snowy winters or enjoying weekend camping trips, the Ascent provides peace of mind through its stability and capability.

What makes it adventure-ready:

  • All-wheel drive comes standard, ideal for tackling tough terrains or inclement weather.
  • Ample cargo storage with flexible configurations for transporting gear.
  • Subaru’s EyeSight system delivers advanced safety features like adaptive cruise control and pre-collision braking.
  • Stellar crash test ratings and a reputation for durability.

If your family enjoys outdoor activities while staying comfortable on the road, the Subaru Ascent is tailor-made for the task.

How to Choose the Right SUV for Your Family

With so many excellent choices available in 2023, selecting the right SUV for your family might feel overwhelming. Here are a few tips to make the process smoother:

  • Assess Your Needs: Consider how many passengers you need to accommodate and how much cargo space is essential for your lifestyle.
  • Evaluate Your Budget: While some SUVs offer luxury features, many affordable options deliver high value without breaking the bank.
  • Test Drive: Always take prospective SUVs for a test drive to gauge comfort, handling, and ease of operations for features like infotainment and safety systems.
  • Explore Long-Term Costs: Research fuel efficiency, maintenance expenses, and resale values to determine overall affordability.

Closing Thoughts

The best SUVs for families in 2023 offer a rich mix of comfort, technology, and versatility. Whether you prioritize fuel efficiency, luxurious interiors, or rugged dependability, there is undoubtedly an option tailored to your needs. For busy households, these vehicles provide a reliable and enjoyable way to navigate daily routines, school drop-offs, and weekend road trips.

As the automotive industry continues to innovate, SUVs will remain pivotal in improving family transportation experiences. Remember to weigh all aspects carefully, from safety to space, before making your decision—and enjoy the journey ahead in your new family vehicle.
