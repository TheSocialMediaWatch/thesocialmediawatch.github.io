Top 10 Upcoming Video Games to Watch in 2024


As we dive into 2024, gamers around the world are eagerly anticipating a plethora of exciting new titles set to release this year. The gaming industry continues to evolve, bringing innovative gameplay mechanics, stunning graphics, and captivating storytelling. Whether you’re a fan of action, adventure, RPGs, or indie gems, there’s something for everyone on the horizon. Here’s a detailed look at the top 10 upcoming video games to keep an eye on in 2024.

1. Star Wars: Eclipse

Set in the High Republic era of the Star Wars universe, Star Wars: Eclipse is shaping up to be a groundbreaking narrative-driven action-adventure game. Developed by Quantic Dream, known for their interactive storytelling, this title promises players a rich, immersive experience filled with moral dilemmas and branching choices that affect the storyline. Players will navigate a galaxy teeming with new characters, planets, and the iconic conflict between the Jedi and the Sith. With stunning visuals and a gripping story, Star Wars: Eclipse is poised to be a must-play for fans of the franchise.

2. Final Fantasy VII Rebirth

Following the critically acclaimed Final Fantasy VII Remake, Final Fantasy VII Rebirth continues the epic saga of Cloud Strife and his companions. This installment expands upon the beloved classic, introducing new gameplay mechanics while retaining the charm of the original. Players can expect an expansive world filled with side quests, character development, and breathtaking visuals that bring the story to life. With its release set for early 2024, fans are eagerly awaiting the chance to explore more of Midgar and beyond.

3. The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Continuing the legacy of one of the most beloved franchises in gaming history, The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom promises to elevate the open-world experience established in Breath of the Wild. Players will once again step into the shoes of Link as he embarks on a new quest to save Hyrule. With new mechanics, environments, and an even more intricate storyline, this game looks to push the boundaries of exploration and puzzle-solving. The combination of nostalgia and innovation makes it one of the most anticipated releases of 2024.

4. Hogwarts Legacy: The Forbidden Forest

After the success of Hogwarts Legacy, fans are excited for the upcoming expansion, The Forbidden Forest. This title will delve deeper into the magical world of Harry Potter, allowing players to explore the dark and mysterious Forbidden Forest. Expect new spells, magical creatures, and quests that expand upon the already rich narrative. The expansion promises to enhance the gameplay experience while allowing players to uncover secrets that lie within the forest’s depths.

5. Avowed

From the creators of Obsidian Entertainment, known for their rich storytelling in RPGs, Avowed is an ambitious new title set in the world of Eora, the same universe as Pillars of Eternity. This first-person RPG aims to combine classic fantasy elements with modern gameplay mechanics. Players can expect a deep narrative, customizable characters, and an expansive world to explore. With its release anticipated for mid-2024, Avowed is sure to attract both new players and longtime fans of Obsidian’s work.

6. Fable

The beloved Fable series is making a comeback with a new installment that promises to capture the charm and humor that fans loved from the original games. Developed by Playground Games, this reboot is set in the picturesque world of Albion, where players can expect to experience a blend of whimsical storytelling and engaging gameplay. With the return of choices that affect the game world and characters, Fable is looking to enchant a new generation while satisfying longtime fans.

7. Dragon Age: Dreadwolf

As the next chapter in the iconic Dragon Age series, Dreadwolf is highly anticipated by RPG enthusiasts. This installment promises to deliver an engaging narrative filled with political intrigue, complex characters, and rich lore. Players will once again dive into the world of Thedas, facing new challenges and making choices that will affect the outcome of the story. With its emphasis on character development and moral choices, Dragon Age: Dreadwolf is sure to be a standout title in 2024.

8. Spider-Man 2

Following the success of the first Spider-Man game, Insomniac Games is set to release Spider-Man 2, which will feature both Peter Parker and Miles Morales. Players can expect an even larger open-world experience, new abilities, and exciting storylines that delve deeper into the Spider-Verse. With the introduction of new villains and dynamic gameplay mechanics, Spider-Man 2 is shaping up to be one of the most thrilling superhero games to date.

9. Stalker 2: Heart of Chernobyl

The highly anticipated sequel to the cult classic Stalker, Stalker 2: Heart of Chernobyl promises to bring players back to the eerie and atmospheric world surrounding the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone. With its mix of survival horror, first-person shooting, and immersive storytelling, this title is set to elevate the franchise to new heights. Players will explore a vast open world filled with mutants, anomalies, and moral choices that will influence their journey. The game’s detailed graphics and chilling atmosphere make it a title to watch for horror fans in 2024.

10. Hollow Knight: Silksong

Fans of indie gaming are buzzing with excitement for Hollow Knight: Silksong, the sequel to the critically acclaimed Hollow Knight. This title shifts the focus to Hornet, a character who has captivated players since the first game. With new abilities, environments, and an expanded storyline, Silksong aims to deliver the same challenging gameplay and beautiful art style that made its predecessor a hit. As players navigate a new world filled with danger and intrigue, the anticipation for this release continues to grow.

Conclusion

2024 is shaping up to be an exciting year for gamers, with a diverse lineup of titles that cater to various tastes and preferences. From expansive RPGs to enchanting adventures and gripping narratives, these upcoming releases promise to captivate and entertain. As we await their arrival, the gaming community remains buzzing with anticipation, ready to dive into new worlds and stories. Whether you’re a seasoned gamer or just starting your journey, these top 10 upcoming video games are definitely worth keeping on your radar. Mark your calendars and prepare for an unforgettable gaming year ahead!
