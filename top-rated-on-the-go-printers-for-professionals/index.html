Top Rated On-the-Go Printers for Professionals


In today’s fast-paced, digital world, business professionals are constantly on the move. Whether you’re traveling for meetings, working remotely, or managing a business from various locations, having reliable tools that can keep up with your demands is crucial. One such tool is a portable printer. On-the-go printers provide professionals with the ability to print documents, reports, and presentations anytime and anywhere, offering convenience and flexibility in various work environments.

Choosing the right portable printer can make all the difference in maintaining productivity on the go. However, with so many options available, it can be difficult to select the best one that aligns with your specific needs. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore some of the top-rated portable printers for professionals, taking into consideration factors such as print quality, speed, battery life, size, and connectivity options.

What to Look for in a Portable Printer

When selecting a portable printer, it’s essential to focus on a few key features that can impact the overall performance and user experience:

  • Portability: A compact and lightweight design is essential for professionals who are often on the move. The printer should be easy to carry and not take up too much space in your bag or briefcase.

  • Print Quality: Professional-quality prints are crucial, especially for presentations, contracts, and important documents. Look for printers that offer high-resolution prints with sharp text and clear images.

  • Connectivity: Wireless connectivity options, such as Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, allow for seamless printing from smartphones, laptops, and tablets. Having multiple connection options adds flexibility, enabling printing from a variety of devices.

  • Battery Life: A long-lasting battery is essential for ensuring that your printer remains functional throughout the day. Look for printers with rechargeable batteries that offer several hours of printing capability.

  • Speed: Efficiency is important for professionals who need to print multiple pages quickly. Choose a printer that balances speed with print quality, ensuring you don’t have to wait too long for your documents.

1. HP OfficeJet 200 Portable Printer

HP’s OfficeJet 200 is one of the most popular choices for professionals in need of a reliable on-the-go printer. This compact inkjet printer is known for its high-quality prints and portability. It weighs just 4.18 pounds and features a slim design that makes it easy to carry around in a bag or backpack.

The HP OfficeJet 200 delivers excellent print quality with a resolution of up to 4800 x 1200 optimized dpi, ensuring sharp text and vibrant color prints. It supports both Wi-Fi and USB connections, allowing users to print from a variety of devices, including smartphones, laptops, and tablets. The rechargeable battery offers up to 500 pages of printing per charge, providing ample power for daily use.

For professionals who need to print documents quickly, the OfficeJet 200 offers a print speed of up to 10 pages per minute for black-and-white prints and up to 7 pages per minute for color prints.

Key Features:

  • Compact and lightweight design
  • High-quality printing with sharp text and images
  • Wi-Fi and USB connectivity options
  • Long-lasting rechargeable battery
  • Print speed of up to 10 pages per minute

2. Canon PIXMA TR150 Wireless Printer

The Canon PIXMA TR150 is a highly versatile portable printer, perfect for business professionals who need a high-quality printer that’s easy to take on the go. Weighing just 4.5 pounds, it’s light enough to be carried around in a small briefcase or backpack, and its small size makes it a great choice for travelers.

This printer offers a high print resolution of up to 4800 x 1200 dpi, making it suitable for both sharp text printing and vibrant color images. It supports Wi-Fi, AirPrint, and Google Cloud Print, allowing seamless printing from mobile devices, tablets, and laptops. For those who prefer direct connections, it also features a USB interface.

The Canon PIXMA TR150 features an optional battery that provides flexibility for on-the-go printing without the need for a nearby power source. The printer also has a fast print speed of up to 9 pages per minute for black-and-white documents and 5.5 pages per minute for color prints.

Key Features:

  • High print resolution of up to 4800 x 1200 dpi
  • Wireless printing via Wi-Fi, AirPrint, and Google Cloud Print
  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Optional rechargeable battery for increased portability
  • Fast printing speeds

3. Epson WorkForce WF-100 Wireless Mobile Printer

For those who require a compact and lightweight option without sacrificing print quality, the Epson WorkForce WF-100 is an excellent choice. Weighing just 3.5 pounds, this ultra-portable printer is easy to carry around and can fit into most bags.

The WorkForce WF-100 is equipped with a built-in rechargeable battery, which allows professionals to print documents without the need for an electrical outlet. It offers wireless printing via Wi-Fi Direct, which enables users to print from smartphones and tablets without a network connection. The printer also supports AirPrint for seamless printing from Apple devices.

When it comes to print quality, the Epson WorkForce WF-100 delivers sharp black-and-white prints at up to 600 x 600 dpi, ensuring that your documents look professional. The print speed is approximately 6.7 pages per minute for black-and-white documents and 3.8 pages per minute for color prints.

Key Features:

  • Compact and lightweight, weighing just 3.5 pounds
  • Rechargeable battery for portable printing
  • Wireless printing with Wi-Fi Direct and AirPrint support
  • High-quality black-and-white prints
  • Print speed of up to 6.7 pages per minute

4. Brother PocketJet 773

The Brother PocketJet 773 is a rugged and reliable portable thermal printer designed for professionals in industries such as field service, logistics, and transportation. Unlike inkjet printers, the PocketJet 773 uses direct thermal printing technology, meaning it doesn’t require ink cartridges, making it a cost-effective and low-maintenance option.

Despite its small size, the Brother PocketJet 773 offers full-page printing and can produce high-quality black-and-white prints at a resolution of 300 x 300 dpi. It also boasts a fast printing speed of up to 8 pages per minute, which is ideal for those who need to print forms, receipts, and other documents quickly.

The PocketJet 773 offers a variety of connectivity options, including Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and USB, and it’s compatible with both Windows and Mac devices. The printer’s rechargeable lithium-ion battery provides up to 600 pages of printing per charge, making it perfect for professionals who need to work remotely for extended periods.

Key Features:

  • Compact design with full-page thermal printing
  • No ink cartridges required, reducing maintenance costs
  • Multiple connectivity options, including Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and USB
  • Print speed of up to 8 pages per minute
  • Long-lasting battery with up to 600 pages per charge

5. Zebra ZQ520 Mobile Printer

For professionals who require a rugged and durable mobile printer, the Zebra ZQ520 is a top choice. Designed for use in harsh environments, this thermal printer is built to withstand drops, water, and dust, making it ideal for industries such as logistics, retail, and warehousing.

The ZQ520 offers high-quality thermal printing with a resolution of 203 dpi, which is suitable for printing receipts, invoices, and shipping labels. It supports wireless printing via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and cellular connectivity, enabling professionals to print from a variety of devices, including smartphones, tablets, and laptops.

The printer’s long-lasting battery can handle a full day of printing, and the compact design makes it easy to carry around without weighing you down. The Zebra ZQ520 is also equipped with advanced features like mobile device management and remote monitoring, which make it ideal for managing large fleets of printers.

Key Features:

  • Rugged design built for harsh environments
  • High-quality thermal printing for receipts, invoices, and labels
  • Wireless printing via Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and cellular connectivity
  • Long-lasting battery with all-day printing capacity
  • Advanced mobile device management features

Conclusion

Portable printers are essential tools for professionals who need to stay productive while on the move. The HP OfficeJet 200, Canon PIXMA TR150, Epson WorkForce WF-100, Brother PocketJet 773, and Zebra ZQ520 are among the best options available, offering a range of features to suit different professional needs. Whether you require high-quality prints, fast speeds, or rugged durability, there is a portable printer that can meet your demands.

When choosing the best portable printer for your work, consider factors such as portability, print quality, battery life, connectivity, and speed. By selecting the right printer, you can ensure that you remain productive, efficient, and ready to tackle any task that comes your way, no matter where you are.
