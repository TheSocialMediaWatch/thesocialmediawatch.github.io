Laughs for Days: The Best Comedy Podcasts


Are you in need of a good laugh? Look no further! In this blog post, we have compiled a list of the best comedy podcasts that will keep you entertained for hours. From hilarious storytelling to witty banter, these podcasts are guaranteed to have you laughing out loud.

1. “The Comedy Store Podcast”

Hosted by some of the biggest names in comedy, “The Comedy Store Podcast” takes you behind the scenes of one of the most iconic comedy clubs in the world. Join the hosts as they share outrageous stories, interviews with legendary comedians, and dive into the rich history of The Comedy Store. Get ready for some side-splitting laughter!

2. “Conan O’Brien Needs A Friend”

If you’re a fan of Conan O’Brien, you can’t miss this podcast. Join Conan and his co-hosts as they invite some of the funniest and most interesting people in the entertainment industry for candid conversations. From comedians to actors, each episode is filled with hilarious anecdotes, personal stories, and of course, plenty of laughs.

3. “My Dad Wrote A Porno”

Prepare yourself for a unique and hilarious experience with “My Dad Wrote A Porno.” In this podcast, the hosts read and dissect a chapter from an erotic novel written by one of their dads. With their witty commentary and sheer disbelief, this podcast will have you laughing uncontrollably and questioning how it ever got published.

4. “The Dollop”

If you’re a history buff with a sense of humor, “The Dollop” is the perfect podcast for you. Each episode explores a bizarre and often obscure event from history, presented in a humorous and entertaining way. With the hosts’ witty banter and comedic storytelling, you’ll find yourself learning and laughing simultaneously.

5. “Comedy Bang! Bang!”

“Comedy Bang! Bang!” is the ultimate podcast for improvisational comedy. Hosted by Scott Aukerman, this show features a mix of celebrity guests, character cameos, and hilarious games. With its fast-paced and unpredictable format, you never know what outrageous moment or hilarious character will appear next.

6. “How Did This Get Made?”

Calling all movie lovers! “How Did This Get Made?” is a podcast that delves into the world of bad movies. Join the hosts as they dissect and mock some of the most perplexing and poorly made films ever created. With their humorous commentary and sharp wit, this podcast will have you laughing at the absurdity of it all.

7. “The Joe Rogan Experience”

Known for its long-form conversations, “The Joe Rogan Experience” covers a wide range of topics, including comedy. Joe Rogan invites some of the funniest comedians to discuss their careers, share hilarious stories, and provide unique perspectives on the world of comedy. With its relaxed and conversational style, this podcast is perfect for those looking for a good laugh and thought-provoking discussions.

8. “The Hilarious World of Depression”

While depression may not seem like a laughing matter, this podcast takes a unique approach to mental health. Comedians share their personal experiences with depression and how humor has helped them cope. With its blend of laughter and vulnerability, “The Hilarious World of Depression” offers a refreshing and uplifting take on a serious topic.

So, there you have it - a list of the best comedy podcasts to brighten your day and keep you laughing. Whether you’re a fan of stand-up, improv, or storytelling, these podcasts offer a diverse range of comedic styles and will surely have you chuckling in no time.

So, grab your headphones, find a cozy spot, and get ready to laugh your way through the day with these hilarious comedy podcasts. Comedy has never been more accessible, and with these podcasts, the laughs are just a click away!
