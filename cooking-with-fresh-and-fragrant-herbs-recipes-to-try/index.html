Cooking with Fresh and Fragrant Herbs: Recipes to Try


Herbs are a wonderful addition to any dish, providing a burst of freshness and flavor that can take your cooking to the next level. Whether you have a flourishing herb garden or just a few pots on your windowsill, incorporating these fragrant plants into your recipes can elevate your culinary creations. In this blog post, we will explore some delicious recipes that showcase the beauty and versatility of cooking with fresh herbs.

1. Lemon-Herb Roasted Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 lemons, sliced
  • Fresh thyme sprigs
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs
  • Fresh sage leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Rinse the chicken under cold water and pat it dry with paper towels.
  3. Rub the chicken with olive oil, salt, and pepper, ensuring it is evenly coated.
  4. Stuff the cavity of the chicken with lemon slices, thyme sprigs, rosemary sprigs, and sage leaves.
  5. Place the chicken on a roasting pan and roast for approximately 45 minutes to an hour, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  6. Allow the chicken to rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve with roasted vegetables for a complete meal.

2. Fresh Herb Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts, toasted
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces pasta of your choice

Instructions:

  1. In a food processor, combine the basil, parsley, mint, pine nuts, garlic, and Parmesan cheese. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
  2. With the food processor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture is smooth and well combined.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and reserve a small amount of pasta water.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta with the fresh herb pesto, adding a splash of pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  6. Serve the pasta warm, garnished with additional Parmesan cheese and a sprinkle of fresh herbs.

3. Herbed Feta and Tomato Salad

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the cherry tomatoes, crumbled feta cheese, basil leaves, parsley, dill, and chives.
  2. Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Toss gently to combine all the ingredients.
  5. Allow the salad to sit for 10 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve as a refreshing side dish or as a topping for grilled meats.

Conclusion

Cooking with fresh and fragrant herbs can transform your dishes from ordinary to extraordinary. Whether you’re adding them to a roasted chicken, tossing them with pasta, or incorporating them into a vibrant salad, herbs bring a burst of flavor and aroma that will delight your taste buds. So, get creative in the kitchen, experiment with different herb combinations, and enjoy the wonderful world of cooking with fresh herbs!
