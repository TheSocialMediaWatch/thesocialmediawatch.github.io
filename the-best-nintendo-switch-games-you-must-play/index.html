The Best Nintendo Switch Games You Must Play


Nintendo Switch has become a must-have console for gamers of all ages, offering a unique blend of portability and high-quality gameplay. Whether you’re playing at home or on the go, the Switch provides an immersive experience with a library of games that spans genres from action-adventure to puzzle-solving, role-playing games (RPGs), and more. In this post, we’ll take a closer look at the best Nintendo Switch games you simply cannot miss. If you’re searching for titles that will keep you entertained for hours, here’s your ultimate guide.

1. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

No list of the best Nintendo Switch games would be complete without The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. This masterpiece redefines what open-world gaming can be, offering an experience that is vast, visually stunning, and filled with endless possibilities. Set in a beautifully designed world, you step into the shoes of Link, who awakens to find the land of Hyrule in ruins. Your mission? To defeat Calamity Ganon and restore peace to the kingdom.

Breath of the Wild offers a unique approach to gameplay, with no rigid path forward. You’re free to explore the world in any way you choose. From climbing mountains to solving puzzles or cooking meals, the game allows for a level of freedom that is rare in modern gaming. If you haven’t experienced the world of Hyrule yet, there’s no better time to dive in.

2. Animal Crossing: New Horizons

For something a bit more relaxed but equally engaging, Animal Crossing: New Horizons is a fan favorite. Released in March 2020, this title quickly became a cultural phenomenon. The premise is simple: you live on a deserted island and build your dream home while cultivating relationships with the local villagers. The open-ended nature of the game makes it endlessly replayable.

What sets this game apart is the attention to detail and the peaceful atmosphere. Whether you’re fishing, gardening, or hosting a summer barbecue, Animal Crossing is all about creating your little slice of paradise. It’s a perfect escape from the fast-paced world, and the seasonal events and updates keep things fresh and exciting throughout the year.

3. Super Mario Odyssey

Super Mario Odyssey takes Mario to new heights, offering a fully three-dimensional world for players to explore. In this action-packed platformer, Mario embarks on a journey across various kingdoms to rescue Princess Peach from Bowser’s clutches. But unlike traditional Mario games, Odyssey introduces Cappy, a sentient hat that Mario can use to possess enemies and objects.

This unique mechanic adds a layer of depth to the gameplay, encouraging you to think creatively as you solve puzzles and defeat foes. With vibrant worlds, an unforgettable soundtrack, and challenging levels, Super Mario Odyssey is one of the Switch’s standout titles and a must-have for Mario fans.

4. Mario Kart 8 Deluxe

For those who love racing games, Mario Kart 8 Deluxe is the definitive kart racer on the Switch. This game offers fast-paced, thrilling racing action, where you can choose from a wide range of iconic characters from the Mario universe. The gameplay is easy to pick up, but mastering each course requires skill, strategy, and timing.

The inclusion of both local and online multiplayer modes means you can race against friends in your living room or compete against players from around the world. The fun, chaotic nature of Mario Kart 8 Deluxe makes it perfect for both casual and competitive players alike.

5. Splatoon 2

Splatoon 2 is an action-packed third-person shooter that offers a refreshing take on the genre. Instead of guns, you use ink-based weapons to battle opponents in vibrant, fast-paced multiplayer matches. The main objective is to cover as much of the map as possible with your team’s color ink while trying to prevent the opposing team from doing the same.

What makes Splatoon 2 so unique is its emphasis on teamwork and strategy. Each match is a race to control the environment, requiring coordination between players to secure victory. With regular updates, new weapons, and stages, Splatoon 2 remains one of the most engaging multiplayer experiences available on the Nintendo Switch.

6. Fire Emblem: Three Houses

If you’re a fan of strategy games, Fire Emblem: Three Houses is a must-play. Set in a richly detailed world filled with political intrigue and complex characters, this tactical RPG lets you take on the role of a professor at a prestigious academy. You must guide a group of students, each with their own motivations, skills, and backstory, through various challenges while shaping the future of their kingdom.

The game’s turn-based combat is deep and rewarding, and the narrative is engaging enough to keep you invested for hours. The multiple story routes add replayability, as each choice you make influences the outcome of the game. With its combination of strategic gameplay and story-driven elements, Fire Emblem: Three Houses is one of the most acclaimed titles in the Fire Emblem franchise.

7. Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

For fans of crossover brawlers, Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is the definitive fighting game. This title brings together characters from across the Nintendo universe—and beyond—allowing you to pit Mario against Pikachu, Link against Samus, or any combination of your favorite characters in fast-paced, chaotic battles.

The sheer amount of content in Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is staggering. With over 70 playable characters, hundreds of stages, and a variety of modes, this game offers something for everyone, whether you’re a casual player or a competitive fighter. The game’s accessible controls and deep mechanics make it perfect for both newcomers and seasoned veterans.

8. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt – Complete Edition

For those who crave a dark, immersive RPG experience, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt – Complete Edition is an absolute gem. Geralt of Rivia, the monster hunter, is tasked with finding his adopted daughter while navigating a world filled with political intrigue, dangerous creatures, and moral choices.

The Nintendo Switch version of The Witcher 3 is a technical marvel, delivering a visually stunning and highly detailed world in a portable format. With its rich narrative, deep combat system, and vast open world, The Witcher 3 stands out as one of the best RPGs of all time, and the Switch version holds its own, offering an excellent experience on the go.

9. Xenoblade Chronicles 2

For fans of expansive RPGs, Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is an absolute must-play. Set in a world where massive titans roam the land, this game follows Rex, a young man who embarks on an epic journey with the sentient Blade named Pyra. The game offers a deep, tactical combat system and a story filled with twists, all set against the backdrop of a beautifully realized world.

The world of Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is massive, with countless side quests, hidden secrets, and characters to discover. The game’s intricate battle system, which blends real-time combat with strategy, offers depth and complexity that fans of the genre will appreciate. Whether you’re new to the series or a long-time fan, Xenoblade Chronicles 2 is a must-have for your Switch library.

10. Luigi’s Mansion 3

If you’re looking for a spooky, yet charming, adventure, Luigi’s Mansion 3 delivers a delightful experience. Luigi must navigate a haunted hotel to rescue his friends, all while battling ghosts and solving puzzles. The game mixes lighthearted humor with eerie, atmospheric environments, making it both fun and slightly spooky.

The core gameplay revolves around using Luigi’s vacuum to capture ghosts and interact with the environment. It’s a perfect blend of exploration, puzzle-solving, and action, and the quirky humor ensures that the game never takes itself too seriously. If you enjoy platformers or have a soft spot for the Mario universe, Luigi’s Mansion 3 is a great addition to your Switch collection.

11. Hollow Knight

For fans of challenging, exploration-driven games, Hollow Knight is a true masterpiece. This Metroidvania-style game features a beautifully hand-drawn world filled with secrets, hidden bosses, and difficult platforming challenges. As the mysterious knight, you’ll explore the vast, ruined kingdom of Hallownest, battling enemies, upgrading abilities, and uncovering the dark history of the world.

Hollow Knight offers tight, responsive controls and a rich, atmospheric world that encourages players to dive deep into its many layers. With a rewarding difficulty curve and a hauntingly beautiful soundtrack, Hollow Knight is a must-play for anyone who loves a good challenge and a captivating story.

Conclusion

The Nintendo Switch has quickly become one of the most versatile gaming consoles on the market, thanks to its extensive library of top-notch games. Whether you’re a fan of action, adventure, puzzles, or strategy, there’s something for everyone on the platform. From the expansive worlds of The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild to the lighthearted fun of Animal Crossing: New Horizons, each game on this list offers a unique and unforgettable experience.

With so many great options to choose from, it’s no wonder the Nintendo Switch continues to be a fan favorite. If you’re looking for a new game to add to your collection, any one of these titles will offer hours of fun, and they are just the beginning of what’s available on the console. Happy gaming!
