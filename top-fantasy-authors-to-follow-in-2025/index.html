Top Fantasy Authors to Follow in 2025


The world of fantasy literature has continued to grow in complexity and creativity, with new authors constantly pushing the boundaries of imagination. As we approach 2025, the genre is thriving with new voices, fresh ideas, and captivating stories. Whether you’re a long-time fantasy fan or new to the genre, there are some incredible authors you should keep an eye on in the coming year. These authors are shaping the future of fantasy and offering works that will undoubtedly become classics.

In this blog post, we highlight some of the top fantasy authors to follow in 2025. From established names to emerging talents, these writers are creating unforgettable worlds and intricate plots that will keep readers hooked for years to come.

1. Brandon Sanderson

Brandon Sanderson is no stranger to fantasy fans. Having already built an empire with his Cosmere universe, Sanderson continues to dominate the genre with his expansive world-building and complex magic systems. In 2025, his influence is expected to grow even further with the release of new works and continued expansions of the Cosmere universe.

His Stormlight Archive series remains one of the most popular high fantasy epics of the 21st century, and with each new installment, he only enhances his reputation as one of the most talented and ambitious writers in the genre. With multiple projects in the works, including a secret project announced in 2024, Sanderson’s fans are eagerly awaiting what he will do next.

If you’re new to Sanderson’s work, start with The Way of Kings, the first book in The Stormlight Archive, and prepare for an intricate narrative filled with unforgettable characters and a masterful magic system. The upcoming releases in 2025 are sure to be worth the wait.

2. N.K. Jemisin

N.K. Jemisin is an author who continues to make waves in the fantasy genre, both in terms of her storytelling and the themes she explores. Her Broken Earth trilogy, which won the Hugo Award for Best Novel three times, has cemented her place as one of the most innovative and thought-provoking fantasy authors of her generation.

In 2025, Jemisin is expected to release new works that further explore complex themes such as environmentalism, power dynamics, and the human experience. Her ability to weave together intricate world-building with meaningful social commentary makes her a must-follow author for any fantasy fan.

Her latest work, The City We Became, set in a world where cities become living, breathing entities, is another example of her remarkable talent for combining speculative fiction with insightful critiques of society. Jemisin’s unique perspective and boundary-pushing narratives ensure that her influence on the genre will only grow in 2025.

3. S.A. Chakraborty

S.A. Chakraborty is an author whose rise in the fantasy world has been swift and undeniable. Her Daevabad Trilogy is a lush and vibrant exploration of Middle Eastern mythology, politics, and power. With The City of Brass as the starting point, Chakraborty created a world filled with djinn, magic, and deep-rooted conflicts.

In 2025, Chakraborty is poised to continue making an impact on the genre. Her ability to infuse rich cultural and historical references into her storytelling provides a unique perspective that stands out among the typical Western fantasy narratives. Chakraborty’s works are known for their immersive settings, multifaceted characters, and intricate plots.

If you’re looking for an author who can offer a fresh, multicultural take on fantasy, S.A. Chakraborty is a writer you won’t want to miss in 2025.

4. Tasha Suri

Tasha Suri is another rising star in the fantasy world, known for her exploration of complex relationships, political intrigue, and epic fantasy landscapes. Her debut novel, Empire of Sand, launched her into the limelight with its unique blend of Indian-inspired mythology, magic, and a focus on the intersection of power, politics, and personal agency.

Suri’s writing is known for its rich world-building and the depth of her characters, particularly her female protagonists who navigate complex societal expectations. With her upcoming releases, Suri is set to continue capturing the imaginations of readers in 2025. Expect to see more of her signature style in future works that tackle themes of power, legacy, and the impact of colonialism.

If you enjoy epic fantasy with cultural richness and deep character studies, Tasha Suri is a must-follow in the coming year.

5. R.F. Kuang

R.F. Kuang has quickly become a favorite among fantasy readers thanks to her powerful storytelling, complex characters, and thoughtful exploration of history and politics. Her Poppy War trilogy, which blends Chinese history with dark fantasy elements, has garnered critical acclaim and established her as one of the most important voices in the genre.

In 2025, Kuang’s presence in the fantasy world is expected to expand with her highly anticipated new works. Her talent for exploring the intersections of war, trauma, and power dynamics in her writing ensures that readers can expect even more thought-provoking narratives.

Kuang’s writing is not for the faint of heart—her work often delves into dark and uncomfortable topics—but it is precisely this boldness that has made her one of the most important voices in modern fantasy.

6. Rory Power

Rory Power is a writer whose work straddles the line between fantasy, horror, and speculative fiction. Her debut novel, Wilder Girls, captivated readers with its eerie atmosphere, compelling characters, and exploration of survival in a mysterious, dangerous environment. Power’s unique blend of genres creates a reading experience that is both unsettling and utterly engrossing.

As 2025 approaches, Power is expected to release new works that continue to explore the intersection of horror and fantasy. If you enjoy dark, atmospheric fantasy with complex character dynamics, Rory Power is an author you won’t want to miss.

Power’s ability to create tension and craft compelling, often morally ambiguous characters sets her apart as one of the most exciting voices in contemporary fantasy.

7. L.L. McKinney

L.L. McKinney is an author who brings a fresh, contemporary voice to fantasy, particularly with her A Blade So Black series, which reimagines the world of Alice in Wonderland with a bold, modern twist. McKinney’s works are known for their diverse characters, sharp dialogue, and exploration of identity and belonging.

With new projects on the horizon, McKinney is expected to continue breaking barriers in fantasy writing in 2025. Her unique blend of fantasy, adventure, and real-world issues makes her an author to follow for anyone interested in diverse, dynamic narratives.

Whether you’re a fan of reimagined fairy tales or looking for fresh voices in the genre, L.L. McKinney is a writer whose works should be on your radar in 2025.

8. Richard Swan

Richard Swan’s The Empire of a Thousand Worlds series has garnered significant attention in the fantasy community for its compelling combination of political intrigue, military strategy, and immersive world-building. Swan’s work blends hard-hitting action with deep philosophical themes about power, control, and personal agency.

As we move into 2025, Swan is expected to continue building on the success of his debut series, offering more complex narratives filled with high stakes and unforgettable characters. His blend of military fantasy and traditional epic storytelling makes him an author to watch in the coming year.

If you enjoy hard-hitting political fantasy with a focus on military tactics and complex characters, Richard Swan is an author who should be at the top of your list.

9. Miranda Asebedo

Miranda Asebedo is an author whose work combines elements of fantasy and contemporary fiction, often featuring characters who navigate both magical worlds and real-life challenges. Her debut novel, The Deepest Blue, is a beautiful exploration of sisterhood, family, and the magic that exists within us all.

Asebedo’s storytelling is often marked by emotional depth and a keen understanding of character development. In 2025, she is expected to continue expanding her unique voice within the fantasy genre, offering fresh perspectives on fantasy’s intersection with the real world.

If you’re looking for magical realism with heartfelt characters and emotionally resonant stories, Miranda Asebedo is an author whose work will resonate with you.

10. Anna Stephens

Anna Stephens is an author whose works are known for their brutal, no-holds-barred approach to fantasy. Her The Shattered Sea trilogy is a grimdark fantasy series that explores themes of survival, loyalty, and the harsh realities of war. Stephens’s writing is gritty, raw, and unflinchingly honest, and she has earned a devoted following among readers who enjoy darker, more complex stories.

In 2025, Anna Stephens is set to release new works that promise to continue her exploration of grimdark fantasy, with themes of moral ambiguity, political maneuvering, and the human condition. If you’re a fan of epic, brutal fantasy with deep, morally complex characters, Anna Stephens is an author to watch closely.

Conclusion

The fantasy genre is in an exciting phase of growth and transformation, with new voices and perspectives reshaping the landscape. From epic, high fantasy to dark, grimdark narratives and everything in between, the authors on this list are at the forefront of the genre’s evolution. Whether you’re a fan of intricate world-building, rich cultural narratives, or thought-provoking social commentary, there’s something for everyone in the upcoming works of these talented authors.

As we move into 2025, these writers are poised to make even greater contributions to the fantasy genre, offering stories that will captivate readers for years to come. So, be sure to follow them and dive into their imaginative worlds when their new books hit the shelves.
