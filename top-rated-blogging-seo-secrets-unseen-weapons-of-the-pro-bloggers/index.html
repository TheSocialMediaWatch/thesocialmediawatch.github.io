Top Rated Blogging SEO Secrets: Unseen Weapons of the Pro Bloggers


There’s an old saying that goes, “Give a man a fish, and you feed him for a day. Teach a man to fish, and you feed him for a lifetime.” The same applies to blogging—it’s not enough to simply write a blog post and expect success. True mastery comes from understanding SEO (Search Engine Optimization) and utilizing it to your blog’s advantage.

The difference between a decent and an extraordinary blogger often comes down to their unorthodox SEO techniques. As cliche as it may sound, their “secrets” are a mixture of hard work and clever strategies that you could adopt to skyrocket your blog to the top. In this article, we’ll dive into these unseen weapons of pro bloggers – guaranteed to put your blog on the map.

Keyword Research And Competitor Analysis

A crucial step in blogging is identifying the keywords that will lead your target audience to your blog. While some bloggers find success by following SEO trends and jumping on the latest bandwagon, the truly savvy bloggers understand the importance of keyword research.

Utilizing tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ubersuggest, or SEMrush, you can find optimal keywords to target. Dig deep into the keywords your competition is using, but be sure to choose longtail keywords that have less competition but high search volumes.

That said, you don’t just want to focus on competitor analysis. Overrelying on what your competition’s doing may result in imitation rather than innovation. Stand out through your unique perspectives and ideas.

Content Quality And Originality

Google’s main goal is to provide users with the best, most relevant results for their search queries. Understand that quality content triumphs over content created purely for SEO.

Original content – well-written, informative, and engaging – is surefire to catch attention. Building a reputation for providing high-quality, well-researched content will allow your blog to become both a resource for your audience and a magnet for search engines.

However, don’t let your guard down when it comes to originality. Plagiarism is a no-go in the blogging world, and search engines have algorithms that sniff out copied content. Always double-check your work and cite any sources you use, especially when quoting.

Technical SEO Practices

Don’t overlook the importance of technical SEO – your website’s infrastructure should be tailor-made for search engines. Ensure your blog loads quickly, as speed is a considerable ranking factor. Use a content delivery network (CDN) and optimize images to improve load times.

Another vital technical aspect of SEO is updating your site’s schema markup. Though often overlooked, schema markup helps search engines better understand your website’s content and significantly improves your site’s search presence.

Backlinks and Social Media

Backlinks – links from other websites directing to your blog – are incredibly important for building your blog’s credibility and increasing its search ranking. Guest blogging, social media mentions, and collaboration with influencers are common ways to attain backlinks, but do not overdo it. The quality of the backlinks is more vital than the quantity.

With an ever-expanding digital world, social media plays an undeniable role. Share your content and engage with your audience. Build a following on various social media platforms and veer towards blogs or influencers partnered with your niche. This not only broadens your reach but also brings in organic backlinks and more site visitors.

In conclusion, there’s no shortcut to achieving a high-ranking blog. SEO is a marathon, not a sprint, and getting there takes time, persistence, and consistency. Incorporate these seemingly “under-the-radar” blogging SEO secrets to give your blog a well-rounded push in the right direction. Remember, even though we may give you the tools and secrets in this article, the rest is up to you.

Now that you know what the pros do, roll up your sleeves and get started on making your blog the best it can be. Happy blogging!
