Best Hotels with Rooftop Bars for Your Next Night Out


When it comes to planning an unforgettable evening, choosing the right venue can make all the difference. Whether you’re traveling to a vibrant city or just looking for a place nearby to escape the monotony of the everyday grind, hotels with rooftop bars are the ultimate destination for a night out. Combining breathtaking views, expertly crafted cocktails, and elegant ambiance, these places redefine the art of lounging under the stars. From sophisticated urban settings to coastal hideaways, the allure of rooftop bars serves as the perfect companion to any celebration, casual meet-up, or romantic evening. Here, we’ve rounded up some of the best hotels with rooftop experiences that promise to elevate your next night out.

Sophistication in the City

Cities often serve as epicenters of nightlife and innovation, and the rooftop bars within hotel properties are no exception. Offering stunning skylines and the vibrancy of city life, they create the perfect backdrop for evenings filled with laughter and discovery.

1. The Refinery Hotel - New York City

Nestled in Manhattan, The Refinery Hotel boasts a rooftop bar that effortlessly blends industrial chic character with a cosmopolitan vibe. The Refinery Rooftop offers unobstructed views of the Empire State Building and an expansive indoor-outdoor space perfect for enjoying year-round. Its curated cocktail menu features signature drinks like tropical-inspired creations and classic Manhattan twists, making it an ideal spot for both first-time visitors and New Yorkers alike.

2. The Hoxton, Southwark - London

Overlooking the iconic London skyline, The Hoxton’s rooftop bar serves up spectacular views mixed with a laid-back atmosphere. The chic outdoor seating area is complemented by warm lighting and contemporary decor, allowing guests to savor hand-selected wines and cocktails while soaking in the city ambiance. Ideal for groups or solo travelers, this rooftop gem provides endless opportunities for relaxation and socializing.

Coastal Escapes with a View

For those craving the sea breeze paired with panoramic views, many beachfront hotels offer rooftop bars designed to create memorable seaside moments. Whether you’re traveling for a vacation or planning a quick getaway, these venues are perfect for unwinding.

3. The Rooftop at Pier 77 - San Diego

Located atop one of San Diego’s most stylish boutique hotels, The Rooftop at Pier 77 manages to combine coastal charm with urban sophistication. Guests are treated to premium cocktails alongside fresh seafood bites, making it a favorite for those who appreciate the finer aspects of waterfront dining experiences. The bar’s location offers striking views of both the Pacific Ocean and the dynamic cityscape.

4. W Barcelona - Barcelona

The W Barcelona effortlessly blends Mediterranean charm with cutting-edge design, making its rooftop bar one of the hottest destinations in Europe. Known as “Eclipse,” this venue exudes a playful yet luxurious vibe with its innovative cocktail menu and party-like atmosphere that beckons nightlife enthusiasts from across the globe. Marvel at the sunset where sky meets sea before diving into the next chapter of your evening escapade.

Elevated Luxury in Exotic Locations

Sometimes, the best night out calls for an adventure in far-flung destinations. There’s no better way to soak in a postcard-perfect landscape than from a rooftop bar perched at the heart of paradise.

5. ME Dubai by Meliá - Dubai

When in Dubai, an evening at ME Dubai’s rooftop bar is nothing short of magical. With views of the city’s towering skyscrapers and the dazzling Burj Khalifa, it is easy to see why this rooftop lounge has become a sought-after spot for locals and travelers alike. The sleek design, coupled with mixology masterpieces, makes it the quintessential destination for sophistication.

6. The Nai Harn Resort - Phuket

The island of Phuket is synonymous with indulgence, and The Nai Harn Resort takes it to new heights (quite literally). Its luxury rooftop bar provides an intimate yet lively setting that overlooks the pristine beaches and lush mountains of southern Thailand. Whether sipping on tropical cocktails or savoring fusion dishes, guests leave with a sense of serenity matched with delightful memories.

Feeling the Pulse in buzzy hotspots

Some hotels embrace their urban environments wholeheartedly by offering rooftop bars that pulse with energy, creativity, and entertainment, making them favorite meeting spots for trendsetters and locals alike.

7. Ace Hotel Downtown - Los Angeles

The Ace Hotel Rooftop in LA carries its reputation for being one of the coolest hangout spots in the heart of this bustling city. With eclectic events like live music performances and open-air movie nights under the stars, this rooftop transforms nightlife into an art form. Pair that with incredible skyline views and expertly concocted cocktails—it’s impossible not to feel the vibrant energy.

8. Mama Shelter - Paris

Quirky, colorful, and unapologetically fun, Mama Shelter’s rooftop bar in Paris offers an entirely fresh perspective on the traditional Parisian aesthetic. Featuring a space that’s both cozy and eclectic, the bar invites patrons to let loose with board games, DJ sets, and casual mingling amidst sweeping views of the city. If you want to see Paris differently, this lively rooftop scene is worth exploring.

Rooftop Experiences Closer to Nature

Hotels leaning into their natural surroundings often find that their rooftop bars invite a special kind of tranquility. If you’re looking for a natural retreat, these venues can provide a calming yet luxurious experience.

9. Amangiri - Canyon Point, Utah

The Amangiri resort delivers serenity in spades—its open-air rooftop bar is no exception. Nestled amidst scenic desert landscapes, the bar provides unobstructed views of surrounding canyons, offering patrons a front-row seat to breathtaking sunsets. Here, celebrating the quiet beauty of nature feels seamless, complemented by hand-crafted cocktails and serene silence.

10. Andaz Maui at Wailea Resort - Hawaii

A rooftop lounge in paradise is always a good idea, and Andaz Maui takes that concept to heart. Its bar exudes tropical vibes with intimate spaces for relaxation or casual gatherings. Overlooking the Pacific Ocean, its live music nights and fresh cocktail offerings create an unmissable opportunity to revel in Hawaii’s signature laid-back atmosphere.

Why Rooftop Bars Are Worth It

Rooftop bars encapsulate the very essence of modern indulgence: the climb to elevation offers a symbolic escape from the ordinary. Hotels featuring rooftop bars do more than just provide cocktails—they cultivate meaningful experiences that celebrate everything from local culture to breathtaking nature or cosmopolitan luxury.

The perfect night out is always within reach when perched above the world around you. Whether seeking romance, excitement, or simple relaxation, the ambiance of a rooftop escape is a choice you’ll never regret. While each venue offers its own distinct charm, they share a common thread: the ability to transform any evening into something extraordinary.
