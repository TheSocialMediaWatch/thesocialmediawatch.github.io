Time is Money? Investing in the 10 Hidden Gems of Time Management Stocks


The saying “time is money” wonderfully encapsulates the valuable nature of time in our busy lives, especially in the day-to-day world of investing. In today’s fast-paced economy, being efficient with our time could make all the difference in investing success, be it individual stocks or an entire portfolio. Time management, then, is a precious commodity in and of itself, and investing in companies specializing in time saving tech, practices, and services could yield significant returns, both financially and personally.

In this post, we’ll explore a selection of lesser-known, yet promising “time management stocks” that have the potential to become investment winners. Before we jump into these hidden gems, let’s first understand what we mean by time management stocks.

Time management stocks are companies that provide products or services aimed at optimizing time consumption and increasing productivity. These companies operate in various sectors, including software, tech, consulting, and more, all contributing towards saving you time across various aspects of your personal and professional life.

So, without further ado, here are some intriguing time management stocks to consider for your portfolio:

  1. Atlassian Corporation PLC (TEAM): Atlassian develops products such as Jira, Confluence, and Trello, widely used for project management, collaboration, and productivity. This Australian software company is a great investment option for anyone interested in enhancing their team’s performance.

  2. Asana, Inc. (ASAN): An emerging star in the project management universe, Asana offers work management platforms designed to help teams organize, track, and manage their work more efficiently. Investing in Asana could potentially generate substantial returns as its user-base grows.

  3. Twilio Inc. (TWLO): Communications platform Twilio provides an array of cloud communications tools, enabling businesses to effectively manage their customer relationships. Time saved in customer communication is invaluable in today’s fast-moving market, making Twilio an intelligent time management investment.

  4. CoStar Group, Inc. (CSGP): This real estate technology company has developed a suite of software tools focusing on optimizing real estate data, which can significantly save time for commercial real estate professionals. CoStar Group is not only a smart time management investment but also a potential beneficiary of the worldwide real estate boom.

  5. Voxtronix SA (VXSN.SW): While Voxtronix is less known outside of Europe, this Swiss company offers a forward-thinking approach to time management tools through its product “SLV” - a Time and Leave Management Software which simplifies and streamlines employee-related procedures, saving businesses significant time and overhead cost.

  6. Slack Technologies, Inc. (WORK): A prominent name in the realm of time management, Slack is a collaboration software that helps teams work efficiently and streamline communication. Its ability to reduce time spent on emails and meetings makes Slack an essential tool for modern productivity and a prime candidate for potential returns.

  7. HubSpot, Inc. (HUBS): HubSpot is an all-in-one inbound marketing software that offers an entire suite of tools, including social media publishing, email marketing, lead generation tools, and a powerful CRM. With the ability to optimize marketing efforts and save time, HubSpot is a worthwhile investment for anyone looking to improve their marketing tactics.

  8. LogMeOnce, Inc. (LMLX): A perfect example of a time-saving tech for today’s fast-paced world, LogMeOnce offers a digital vault for securely organizing and managing a wide range of account login details, which can be a substantial time-saver for individuals as well as corporations.

  9. Okta, Inc. (OKTA): Okta is a leading independent provider of identity solutions for enterprises. By streamlining access to various cloud-based services, Okta helps users save time through their secure and efficient services, making it an attractive option to consider in your investments.

  10. Proofpoint, Inc. (PFPT): With a focus on email security and compliance software solutions, Proofpoint aims to reduce the time and resources businesses invest in dealing with cyber-security threats. In an increasingly digital world, protecting business information and infrastructure is a top priority, making Proofpoint an essential investment for security-focused businesses.

While investing in lesser-known stocks can be risky, the potential benefits of a diversified portfolio can be significant. Each of these companies holds promising growth potential, and by investigating their operations and histories, you can make more educated decisions about maximizing your investments.

Remember that diligent time management can also greatly enhance your financial success in investing. Given that you’re reading this, congratulations on making time to allocate and optimize your resources, including the time spent selecting stocks. Keep in mind that not all investments will yield the expected return, especially the hidden gems. Do your homework, monitor your investments, and always have an exit strategy in place.

As Benjamin Franklin once said, “Dost thou love life? Then do not squander time, for that’s the stuff life is made of.” With these time management stocks on your radar, you’ve taken the first steps to not only optimize your financial portfolio but also to appreciate and negate the negative effects of time squandering. Here’s to time well-spent, both on and off the stock market – good luck!
