The Best Hunting Shows to Watch for Inspiration


Hunting is not just a pastime for many—it’s a passion, a way of life, and for some, a spiritual journey. Whether you’re an experienced hunter or just getting started, there’s always something to learn, appreciate, and discover. One great way to fuel your passion and enhance your skills is by watching hunting shows that offer a mix of education, entertainment, and inspiration. From breathtaking landscapes to incredible wildlife encounters, these shows provide a unique window into the world of hunting, offering tips, tricks, and unforgettable experiences.

In this blog post, we’ll take a look at some of the best hunting shows to watch, each of which provides a unique perspective on the world of hunting, from educational tips to thrilling hunting expeditions. So, grab your gear, sit back, and get ready to explore some of the most inspiring and entertaining hunting shows available today.

1. MeatEater

One of the most well-known hunting shows in recent years, MeatEater is the brainchild of renowned hunter and conservationist Steven Rinella. With a strong focus on the ethical aspects of hunting, the show not only showcases hunting trips but also educates viewers on the importance of conservation and sustainable practices.

Rinella, an experienced outdoorsman, takes audiences on hunts across the United States and around the globe, exploring different wildlife species and the habitats they call home. The show is packed with useful tips on everything from proper rifle use to butchering techniques, and it includes culinary segments where Rinella prepares meals using the game he’s harvested.

What sets MeatEater apart is its emphasis on ethical hunting and conservation efforts. Rinella goes beyond the hunt, discussing the ecological importance of wildlife and the positive impacts hunting can have on local ecosystems. For those looking for a show that blends hunting with education and sustainability, MeatEater is a must-watch.

2. The Hunting Public

The Hunting Public is a show that celebrates the real, down-to-earth aspect of hunting. Hosted by a group of passionate hunters, this show is perfect for those who appreciate the raw and unfiltered side of hunting. The show’s cast members are not only skilled hunters but also down-to-earth, relatable individuals who offer valuable insight into the challenges and rewards of hunting in various environments.

What makes The Hunting Public unique is its focus on public land hunting. The hosts take on the challenge of hunting in accessible public lands, offering viewers practical advice on how to approach hunting in these areas. With episodes dedicated to everything from scouting techniques to tree stand setups, The Hunting Public is perfect for those who want to hunt like a pro, without the luxury of private land or high-end equipment.

If you’re looking for a realistic, hands-on hunting show that focuses on skill-building and the hunting process itself, The Hunting Public is an excellent source of inspiration.

3. Wild Bill’s Hunting Adventures

For those who prefer a more adventurous take on hunting, Wild Bill’s Hunting Adventures offers a thrilling perspective. Hosted by Wild Bill, a larger-than-life character who is as much about adventure as he is about hunting, this show takes viewers on exciting hunts across the globe. Whether it’s chasing big game in Africa or pursuing exotic species in remote regions, Wild Bill’s show is filled with heart-pounding moments and jaw-dropping scenery.

What makes Wild Bill’s Hunting Adventures special is its focus on the thrill and excitement of the hunt. The show emphasizes the pursuit of adventure and highlights the beauty of nature, from dense jungles to vast savannas. Wild Bill also brings a sense of humor to the show, making it not only an exciting experience but also a fun one. For those who love adventure and adrenaline, this show is an inspiring watch.

4. Bowhunter TV

Bow hunting has a special place in the hearts of many hunters, and Bowhunter TV is dedicated to showcasing this unique form of hunting. The show features a variety of bowhunting techniques, from the basics to advanced strategies, making it perfect for both beginners and seasoned hunters. The show’s hosts are expert archers who demonstrate how to master the craft of bow hunting, providing valuable lessons on shooting accuracy, stalking techniques, and hunting in different terrains.

What makes Bowhunter TV stand out is its focus on the equipment and gear needed for successful bow hunting. Viewers will gain a deep understanding of bow selection, arrow setup, and hunting strategies tailored specifically to archery. In addition to practical advice, the show also features some stunning hunts, with hunters taking down impressive game using nothing but their bows.

If you’re passionate about bow hunting or interested in giving it a try, Bowhunter TV is a fantastic resource to enhance your skills and deepen your appreciation for this unique hunting style.

5. North Woods Law

For a different kind of perspective on hunting, North Woods Law offers a behind-the-scenes look at the lives of wildlife officers in the state of Maine. While not strictly a hunting show, North Woods Law provides invaluable insight into the legal and ethical aspects of hunting. It follows the law enforcement officers tasked with protecting wildlife and ensuring that hunters follow the regulations designed to preserve natural resources.

The show highlights the challenges these officers face in their work, such as dealing with poaching, illegal hunting methods, and animal conservation efforts. It also offers a glimpse into the lives of hunters who are abiding by the rules and highlights the importance of responsible hunting practices. If you want to understand the broader impact of hunting laws and the conservation efforts that keep wildlife populations healthy, North Woods Law is a must-watch.

6. Chasing Giants

If you’re passionate about big game hunting, Chasing Giants is a show you can’t miss. This show focuses on hunting large game, particularly giant whitetail deer, in the Midwest. The hosts are experienced hunters who share tips and tactics for hunting some of the largest and most elusive deer in North America.

What makes Chasing Giants so compelling is the in-depth approach to big game hunting. The hosts break down the science behind tracking giant bucks, including how to understand deer behavior, how to read sign, and how to use specialized gear for hunting trophy-sized animals. Whether you’re a seasoned hunter looking to up your game or a newcomer hoping to learn from the pros, Chasing Giants offers plenty of inspiration and expertise.

7. Rugged Expeditions

For viewers looking for a combination of rugged terrain and exhilarating hunts, Rugged Expeditions offers exactly that. This show focuses on hunting in extreme conditions, whether it’s the mountains of Alaska or the wilds of Canada. The show’s hosts are seasoned hunters who take on some of the most challenging environments in the world.

What sets Rugged Expeditions apart is its focus on survival and adaptability. The hunters often have to contend with harsh weather, dangerous terrain, and unpredictable wildlife. This makes the hunts more thrilling and the lessons learned more applicable to real-life situations. Whether it’s learning how to track game in deep snow or how to hunt in dense forests, Rugged Expeditions offers plenty of inspiration for hunters looking to test their skills and push their limits.

8. Field & Stream Total Outdoorsman Challenge

For those who love a good competition, Field & Stream Total Outdoorsman Challenge is the ultimate show to watch. This reality competition series pits talented outdoor enthusiasts against each other in a series of challenges that test their skills in hunting, fishing, survival, and other outdoor activities.

While the show features a competitive edge, it’s also a great way to learn new skills and techniques from some of the best in the business. Contestants face off in a variety of challenges that require them to demonstrate expertise in multiple areas of outdoor survival. For hunters who are looking to expand their outdoor knowledge and improve their overall skills, Field & Stream Total Outdoorsman Challenge provides a great platform to learn while being entertained.

9. Deadliest Hunts

As the name suggests, Deadliest Hunts focuses on the world’s most dangerous and extreme hunting experiences. From hunting large predators like lions and bears to chasing big game in the most challenging environments, this show offers a high-stakes look at some of the most intense hunts in the world.

Each episode of Deadliest Hunts delves into the technical aspects of hunting dangerous game, with tips on strategy, weaponry, and safety. While the stakes are high, the show also provides viewers with a better understanding of the risks and rewards of hunting predators and big game. For those looking to watch thrilling hunts with a focus on danger and strategy, Deadliest Hunts is an exciting and inspiring choice.

10. Heartland Bowhunter

For bowhunting enthusiasts who want to see hunting in its most heartwarming form, Heartland Bowhunter offers an inspiring journey. The show follows a group of skilled bowhunters as they embark on their hunts in the Midwest, with a strong emphasis on family, tradition, and the values of hunting.

What makes Heartland Bowhunter so appealing is its personal touch. The hosts share their love for hunting with viewers, focusing on the connections they share with their families and the traditions passed down through generations. It’s a reminder that hunting is not just about the kill but about the bonds formed and the memories created. This show offers a deeper, more emotional connection to the outdoors and is a perfect choice for anyone who values the personal and spiritual side of hunting.

Conclusion

Hunting is more than just a sport—it’s an experience that connects us to nature, teaches us valuable skills, and challenges us to grow. The shows listed above offer a wide range of perspectives, from exciting adventure hunts to educational insights into ethical hunting and conservation efforts. Whether you’re looking for inspiration, practical advice, or simply an entertaining experience, these hunting shows will provide you with everything you need to fuel your passion for the outdoors.

So, grab your gear, prepare your hunting lodge, and settle in for a binge-watch session that’ll inspire you to get out into the wild and experience the thrill of the hunt for yourself.
