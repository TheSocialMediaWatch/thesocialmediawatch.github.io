The 12 Best Flowers for Your Garden This Summer


When it comes to designing your garden, one of the most exciting parts is selecting the perfect flowers to enhance its beauty and vibrancy. Summer not only brings us plenty of sunshine but also offers the perfect growing conditions for a wide array of colorful and fragrant flowers. This season, transform your outdoor space with these twelve best flowers for your garden this summer.

1. Sunflowers

Not only do sunflowers provide a sunshiney boost to any garden, but they also attract bees and birds, making them a perfect choice for wildlife enthusiasts. They require full sun to thrive and offer a fantastic display of yellow petals and dark centers throughout the summer.

2. Roses

Roses are a classic garden staple. Select a variety of climbing roses, bush roses, or shrub roses to bring an elegant, timeless charm to your garden. Keep them well-fed and watered for the most vibrant blooms.

3. Lavender

This calming, fragrant flower is not only beautiful but also highly-prized in the culinary world. Grow different varieties together to enjoy the bee-attracting blooms of various shades of purple and lavendar throughout your garden.

4. Marigolds

These cheerful flowers offer a bright pop of color in your garden, and are also well-known as natural pest deterrents. They’re low-maintenance, rustic, and perfect for adding a burst of color to your garden bed.

5. Petunias

An all-time favorite, petunias are available in an array of bright, bold colors and easy-to-maintain options. Plant them in hanging baskets, containers, or garden beds for a sensational summer display that will impress friends and neighbors alike.

6. Penstemon

Attract hummingbirds and butterflies to your garden with the red, pink, or whitish blue trumpet-shaped flowers of penstemon. They are native to North America, making them an excellent choice for those looking for drought-resistant, low-maintenance flowers.

7. Zinnias

A cut-and-come-again favorite, zinnias bring a dazzling range of bright colors to your garden. Deer and rabbit-resistant, they are a wonderful choice for those looking to keep the garden animals at bay.

8. Snapdragons

These unique flowering plants not only have a fascinating appearance but also attract bees, butterflies, and hummingbirds. They are ideal for adding height and layers to a flower bed, and in the summer, they’ll provide your garden with striking color combinations.

9. Cosmos

The elegant and delicate nature of cosmos flowers paired with their Late summer to fall bloom times make them a popular choice. They attract pollinators effortlessly and provide a wide range of captivating colors for every garden.

10. Geraniums

These are a great option for adding color and texture to your summer garden. They are versatile plants, thriving in both pots or garden beds, and will reward you with a captivating display of bright, vibrant blooms all season long.

11. Coneflowers

Coneflowers are a perfect fit for any cottage-style garden, with their rustic charm and mounded, bushy growth habit. They attract butterflies and other pollinators as well as birds, and their unique cone-shaped flowers offer a great addition to any bouquet.

12. Lilies

These majestic blooms bring an exotic flair to any summer garden. Plant these tall, elegant flowers at the back of your flower bed for a dramatic effect, and take advantage of their intoxicating fragrance up close. Choose from an array of colorful varieties for endless possibilities.

Now that you have the list of 12 best flowers to adorn your garden this summer, you can start by selecting the ones that best suit your taste and style. Design and enhance your garden by incorporating these captivating blooms and enjoy the beauty and allure they bring to your outdoor oasis. Have fun planting and enjoy a vibrant, flourishing garden full of life and color!
