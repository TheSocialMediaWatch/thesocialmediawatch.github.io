10 Quick and Easy Weeknight Dinner Recipes


After a long day, the last thing anyone wants to do is spend hours in the kitchen preparing dinner. That’s why quick and easy weeknight dinner recipes are a lifesaver. Whether you’re a busy parent, a student, or simply someone who wants to enjoy a delicious meal without the fuss, these ten recipes will help you get dinner on the table in no time. Each recipe is designed to be simple, requiring minimal ingredients and effort, while still being packed with flavor.

1. One-Pan Lemon Garlic Chicken and Vegetables

This one-pan dish is perfect for a weeknight dinner. Not only is it simple to make, but the cleanup is a breeze.

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (like bell peppers, zucchini, and carrots)
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the chicken breasts, mixed vegetables, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture evenly on a baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This dish is not only quick to prepare but also provides a balanced meal, making it an excellent choice for busy weeknights.

2. 15-Minute Shrimp Tacos

Tacos are a favorite for many, and these shrimp tacos are quick, easy, and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • Lime wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet over medium heat, add the shrimp and taco seasoning. Cook for 3-5 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and cooked through.
  2. Warm the corn tortillas in another pan or microwave.
  3. Assemble the tacos by placing shrimp, cabbage, and avocado on each tortilla.
  4. Serve with lime wedges for a fresh squeeze of flavor.

These shrimp tacos are not only quick to make, but they also offer a fun and interactive dining experience.

3. Quick Beef Stir-Fry

Beef stir-fry is a classic weeknight meal that can be customized with your favorite vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, sliced thin
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (like broccoli, bell peppers, and snap peas)
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Cooked rice for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced beef and cook for 3-4 minutes until browned.
  3. Add the garlic and mixed vegetables, stir-frying for another 5 minutes.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and cook for an additional minute.
  5. Serve over cooked rice.

This quick beef stir-fry is not only delicious but also a great way to use up leftover vegetables.

4. Creamy Tomato Basil Pasta

Pasta is always a winner for weeknight dinners, and this creamy tomato basil version is both comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces pasta of your choice
  • 1 can (14.5 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a skillet, combine crushed tomatoes, heavy cream, garlic, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the fresh basil and cooked pasta, mixing until well combined.
  4. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese.

This pasta dish is luxurious yet simple, perfect for a comforting weeknight meal.

5. Sheet Pan Sausage and Potatoes

This sheet pan dinner is a fantastic way to get a hearty meal with minimal effort.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound smoked sausage, sliced
  • 1 pound baby potatoes, halved
  • 2 cups green beans, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. On a sheet pan, combine sliced sausage, potatoes, and green beans.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper, and toss to coat.
  4. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and sausage is browned.

This recipe is not only easy to make but also allows for the use of whatever vegetables you have on hand.

6. Veggie Quesadillas

These quesadillas are perfect for a quick and healthy dinner option. They are packed with vegetables and cheese, making them a hit with both kids and adults.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (like cheddar or Monterey Jack)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (like spinach, mushrooms, and bell peppers)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salsa for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Place one tortilla in the skillet, sprinkle with cheese and vegetables, then top with another tortilla.
  3. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden and cheese is melted.
  4. Cut into wedges and serve with salsa.

These veggie quesadillas are not only quick to prepare but also an excellent way to sneak in some extra vegetables.

7. Easy Chicken Fajitas

Fajitas are a fun and flavorful meal that can be made quickly with just a few ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons fajita seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Flour tortillas for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the chicken, bell pepper, onion, and fajita seasoning. Cook for 7-10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  3. Serve with warm flour tortillas.

Chicken fajitas are vibrant and packed with flavor, making them a perfect quick dinner option.

8. Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

This refreshing salad is not only quick to prepare but also healthy and filling.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) chickpeas, rinsed and drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.

This Mediterranean chickpea salad is not only nutritious but also perfect for those warm evenings when you want something light.

9. Quick Baked Salmon

Salmon is a healthy choice that cooks quickly and pairs well with many sides.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, and season with salt and pepper. Top with lemon slices.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is flaky.
  5. Garnish with fresh dill before serving.

This quick baked salmon is not only healthy but also elegant enough for special occasions.

10. Instant Pot Chili

For a hearty and satisfying meal, this Instant Pot chili can be made in no time.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef or turkey
  • 1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can (15 ounces) black beans, drained
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 packet chili seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Set the Instant Pot to sauté mode and brown the ground meat.
  2. Add kidney beans, black beans, crushed tomatoes, chili seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  3. Close the lid and set to cook on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Allow the pressure to release naturally for 5 minutes, then quick release any remaining pressure.

This Instant Pot chili is a perfect one-pot meal that can be served on its own or with cornbread.

Conclusion

These ten quick and easy weeknight dinner recipes are designed to simplify your cooking routine while still delivering delicious and satisfying meals. From one-pan dishes to hearty salads, these recipes cater to a variety of tastes and dietary preferences, making it easy to keep your dinner menu exciting. With these recipes in your arsenal, you can spend less time in the kitchen and more time enjoying your evenings with family and friends. Happy cooking!
