Fantasy Book Club: Discussion Questions and Recommendations


Have you ever wished that you could escape the monotony of everyday life and lose yourself in the realms of fantasy books? If the answer is yes, then you’re in luck! Starting a fantasy book club can be the perfect catalyst to unite like-minded individuals, make new connections, and explore fantastical worlds together. To help jumpstart this literary adventure, we’ve compiled a list of essential discussion questions and recommendations for your consideration. As we traverse through mythical lands, face fierce creatures, and ally with unlikely companions, let’s dive into the heart of these magical narratives.

What Makes a Great Fantasy Book

Before we delve into the questions and recommendations, it’s essential to understand the qualities that define an excellent fantasy book. Notable aspects include imaginative world-building, rich character development, intricate plots, and thought-provoking themes. The perfect fantasy tale not only transports readers to a new realm but also resonates on a deeper level, leaving them with unforgettable characters and life lessons gleaned from their adventures.

Fantasy Book Club Discussion Questions

  1. Setting and World-building: How did the author create the lore and geography of their world? Did you feel they were successful in building a fully-rounded universe? Would you have liked more or less detail?
  2. Character Development: Which character did you like or dislike the most? What traits did they possess, and how did their story impact the narrative’s progression?
  3. Plot and Narrative Structure: How did the story build to its climax? Did the plot seem logical and coherent? If not, which aspects did you find lacking or confusing?
  4. Themes: What did you think were the underlying messages or themes of the book? Did these align with your expectations of a fantasy story, or were they unexpected and thought-provoking?
  5. Representation: Did the book feature diverse representation in terms of character representation, cultures, or perspectives? How did this impact your reading experience?
  6. Moments of Suspense and Action: Which scenes gave you goosebumps or left you on the edge of your seat? Why did these moments resonate with you?
  7. Closing Reflections: In your opinion, how did the book conclude? Would you have preferred a different ending, and why?

Fantasy Book Club Recommendations

Embarking on your fantasy book club journey filled with epic battles, enchanting love stories, majestic creatures, and powerful magic? Here are some enticing novels to consider:

  1. ‘A Song of Ice and Fire’ series by George R.R. Martin – Popularly known as the ‘Game of Thrones’ books, this epic tale features a vast cast of characters and intricate political machinations within the richly designed world of Westeros.
  2. ‘The Wheel of Time’ series by Robert Jordan and Brandon Sanderson – This high fantasy series follows an epic quest to save the world from an evil force, immersing readers in a deeply-realized, magical landscape.
  3. ‘The Kingkiller Chronicle’ by Patrick Rothfuss – This beautifully crafted narrative follows Kvothe, a legendary wizard and musician, as he shares his extraordinary life story to a chronicler. It’s a must-read for aficionados of character-driven stories.
  4. ‘The Magic of Fantasy’ by Crystal Ardton – While exploring her own fictional world of Verebia, Crystal skillfully weaves together captivating characters, intricate world-building, and a riveting plot that fans of all ages will adore.
  5. ‘The Mistborn Trilogy’ by Brandon Sanderson – This captivating tale takes place in a post-apocalyptic world where magic-fueled rebellion unfolds. It’s a thrilling and entertaining adventure.

Conclusion

Embarking on this fantasy voyage with a book club not only provides endless enjoyment, but it also cultivates a community of like-minded kindred spirits who share a love for mesmerizing alternate realities. As you dive into these unforgettable worlds, these discussion questions and recommendations will serve as a reliable compass and a guide to enrich your expeditions. So, gear up for the magical journey that awaits, and let the enchanting realm of fantasy sweep you off your feet.
