The Most Anticipated RPG Games of 2025


The role-playing game (RPG) genre has long been a cornerstone of the gaming industry, captivating audiences with expansive worlds, deep narratives, and complex character development. As we look ahead to 2025, the landscape of RPGs is set to evolve in exciting ways, bringing forth a blend of traditional elements and innovative gameplay mechanics. Whether you’re a fan of open-world exploration, tactical combat, or emotionally driven stories, the RPG titles coming in 2025 promise to deliver unforgettable experiences. In this post, we’ll dive into some of the most anticipated RPGs of 2025, examining their features, storylines, and what makes them stand out.

1. The Elder Scrolls VI

It’s no surprise that “The Elder Scrolls VI” is among the most anticipated RPGs of 2025. Bethesda has kept details about the game under wraps, but the legacy of the Elder Scrolls series speaks for itself. Known for its massive open-world environments, intricate lore, and player-driven stories, the new installment is expected to raise the bar once again.

While Bethesda has been tight-lipped about specifics, fans are hoping to return to the epic world of Tamriel. Rumors suggest that the game may take place in Hammerfell, a region known for its diverse landscapes and complex political tensions. Players can expect to explore vast deserts, bustling cities, and treacherous mountains while interacting with a host of new factions and characters. Given the success of “Skyrim,” which has been a mainstay in gaming for over a decade, expectations for “The Elder Scrolls VI” are sky-high.

Key Features:

  • Massive open-world exploration with seamless transitions between environments.
  • Advanced AI systems that make NPC interactions more dynamic.
  • New combat mechanics, possibly incorporating more fluid and reactive systems.
  • Expansive lore that builds on the rich history of Tamriel.

2. Starfield: The RPG in Space

Bethesda’s “Starfield” has already created waves in the gaming community, but the upcoming expansion and potential sequels in 2025 are expected to redefine space exploration RPGs. “Starfield” promises an unprecedented level of freedom, allowing players to explore an expansive universe, craft their own spaceships, and engage in deep, immersive quests. Players will take on the role of a space explorer, navigating through planets, space stations, and ancient alien ruins in search of the unknown.

What makes “Starfield” particularly exciting is Bethesda’s commitment to blending traditional RPG mechanics with the thrill of space exploration. The game’s universe is vast and offers a high degree of interactivity, from customizable space combat to faction alliances that will impact the progression of your journey.

Key Features:

  • Endless space exploration with procedurally generated planets.
  • Rich faction systems that affect gameplay choices and story outcomes.
  • Ship building and customization, allowing players to create their ideal vessels.
  • Complex narrative threads that intertwine personal stories with the mysteries of the universe.

3. Final Fantasy VII Rebirth

The “Final Fantasy VII Remake” was a smash hit, revamping the classic 1997 RPG for modern audiences. With “Final Fantasy VII Rebirth,” Square Enix promises to continue the story and offer even more expansive gameplay mechanics. Building on the success of the first part, “Rebirth” will take players deeper into the world of Gaia as Cloud Strife and his companions battle against the Shinra Electric Power Company, the mysterious Sephiroth, and the impending threat to the planet.

What sets this game apart is its blend of real-time combat with traditional RPG elements, such as materia-based magic and summons. The world of Gaia is brimming with life, from bustling cities to dark, ominous dungeons. With improvements in the graphics engine and more open-world exploration, “Final Fantasy VII Rebirth” will undoubtedly captivate fans of the series while attracting new players.

Key Features:

  • Real-time combat combined with strategic elements.
  • Expansive world-building that allows for greater exploration.
  • Classic characters and summons from the original game, reimagined for a new generation.
  • Engaging narrative that expands the story and provides new perspectives.

4. The Witcher 4: A New Saga Begins

After the success of “The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt,” CD Projekt Red is set to return to the world of Geralt of Rivia in 2025 with “The Witcher 4.” While Geralt’s story may be concluded, the upcoming game will feature a new protagonist, set in the same rich and detailed universe that fans have come to love. It’s likely that the new protagonist will inherit the legacy of Geralt, facing new monsters, political intrigue, and moral dilemmas in a land torn apart by war and chaos.

“The Witcher 4” is expected to build upon the open-world elements of its predecessor while introducing new gameplay systems, such as improved combat mechanics and even deeper narrative choices. With its dark tone, memorable characters, and deep lore, “The Witcher 4” will undoubtedly be one of the standout RPGs of 2025.

Key Features:

  • A new protagonist with unique abilities and storylines.
  • A larger, more interactive world to explore, filled with monsters, magic, and politics.
  • Improved combat mechanics, refining the mix of swordplay and magic.
  • Morally complex choices that impact the course of the story.

5. Fable (Reboot)

The “Fable” series has been a fan favorite for years, known for its whimsical world, humor, and choice-driven narrative. In 2025, the much-anticipated reboot of “Fable” will bring the series back to life with a fresh vision. While details about the plot are scarce, we can expect to return to the land of Albion, a world filled with magic, mythical creatures, and morally ambiguous choices.

The new “Fable” will likely retain its humor and lighthearted tone while offering deeper RPG mechanics. Players will once again shape the fate of their character based on their decisions, which will impact the world around them. The game will also feature an improved combat system and modernized visuals, all while preserving the core elements that made the original games so beloved.

Key Features:

  • A dynamic world that changes based on player choices.
  • Improved combat and magic systems that feel fresh but familiar.
  • A humor-filled narrative with engaging characters and quests.
  • Enhanced visuals that bring Albion to life like never before.

6. Avowed: The Pillars of Eternity Successor

Obsidian Entertainment is known for its narrative-driven RPGs, and “Avowed” is shaping up to be one of the studio’s most exciting titles yet. Set in the same world as “Pillars of Eternity,” “Avowed” promises to expand upon the deep lore and intricate world-building that made the first game a critical success. Players will take on the role of a character navigating a world filled with ancient magic, political intrigue, and dangerous monsters.

With an emphasis on choice and consequence, “Avowed” is expected to feature branching storylines, character customization, and tactical combat. The game will also incorporate elements of first-person exploration, providing a more immersive experience than previous Obsidian titles.

Key Features:

  • Expansive world-building rooted in the Pillars of Eternity universe.
  • Tactical combat that allows for deep strategic planning.
  • Rich character customization and story choices.
  • First-person exploration to further immerse players in the world.

7. Dragon Age: Dreadwolf

“Dragon Age” has long been a beloved series, and “Dragon Age: Dreadwolf” promises to be the next chapter in the epic fantasy RPG saga. Set in the world of Thedas, the game will focus on the return of the Dreadwolf, a powerful elven figure with ties to the game’s overarching mythology. Players will once again step into the boots of a hero in a world on the brink of destruction, faced with choices that will define the fate of entire nations.

With its deep character development, tactical combat, and branching storylines, “Dragon Age: Dreadwolf” will be a must-play for fans of BioWare’s storytelling and RPG mechanics. Expect a large, open world to explore, with multiple factions, rich dialogue options, and a combat system that combines real-time action with strategic elements.

Key Features:

  • Return to Thedas, with an even more expansive world.
  • Choice-driven narrative that impacts alliances and the story.
  • Tactical and action-oriented combat that can be customized.
  • Complex characters and moral dilemmas that test the player’s values.

Conclusion

2025 is shaping up to be a year full of incredible RPG releases, each bringing its own unique blend of storytelling, combat, and world-building. Whether you’re looking forward to revisiting old favorites like “The Elder Scrolls VI” and “Dragon Age: Dreadwolf,” or excited to explore new realms in “Starfield” and “Fable,” there’s something for everyone. These titles promise to offer engaging narratives, expansive worlds, and immersive gameplay, cementing RPGs as one of the most exciting genres in the gaming industry. As the year approaches, keep an eye out for more updates on these upcoming releases, and prepare to dive into the adventures that await in 2025.
