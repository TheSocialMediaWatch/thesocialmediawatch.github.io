Top 10 USB-C Hubs for MacBooks and PCs


As technology continues to evolve and USB-C increasingly becomes the standard connection port on modern laptops, finding the right USB-C hub has become essential. Whether you’re a creative professional juggling multiple peripherals or a casual user needing extra functionality, a USB-C hub can transform your MacBook or PC into a powerful workstation. In this article, we’ll break down the top 10 USB-C hubs currently available, offering the ideal blend of functionality, portability, and reliability.

1. Anker PowerExpand 8-in-1 USB-C Hub

Anker is a trusted name in the tech accessories space, and the PowerExpand 8-in-1 USB-C Hub lives up to the reputation. This hub is perfect for those who need a mix of ports for productivity and entertainment. It features an HDMI port capable of delivering 4K resolution at 30Hz, multiple USB-A ports for legacy devices, an Ethernet port for stable internet connectivity, and SD/microSD card slots for photographers and videographers. The slim design makes it easy to slip into your laptop bag, and its aluminum exterior complements modern devices like MacBooks.

Main Features:

  • 4K HDMI support
  • High-speed data transfer with USB 3.0 ports
  • Gigabit Ethernet functionality
  • SD and microSD card slots

2. Satechi Aluminum Multi-Port Adapter V2

Satechi has cemented its position as a premium accessory provider, and this hub demonstrates why. The sleek design matches the minimalist aesthetic of MacBooks and ultrabooks alike. With three USB 3.0 ports, 4K HDMI support, and a USB-C pass-through for power delivery, this hub is ideal for professionals who demand versatility without sacrificing style. Its aluminum build ensures durability while maintaining lightweight portability.

Main Features:

  • USB-C Power Delivery pass-through
  • Ethernet port for wired internet connections
  • Dual card readers suitable for photographers
  • 4K HDMI port for external monitors

3. HyperDrive Duo 7-in-1 USB-C Hub

The HyperDrive Duo is tailored specifically for MacBook users but works seamlessly across other USB-C laptops as well. This compact hub connects directly to two side-by-side USB-C ports, adding seven additional ports to your machine, including USB-A, HDMI, microSD, and Thunderbolt 3 for ultra-fast data transfer. It’s small enough to carry daily and capable of handling both office and creative workflows.

Main Features:

  • Thunderbolt 3 port with 40Gbps speeds
  • 4K HDMI support
  • Compact and MacBook-friendly design
  • High-speed USB-A 3.0 ports

4. CalDigit TS3 Plus Thunderbolt 3 Dock

For professionals who require the best connection speeds and expanded functionality, the CalDigit TS3 Plus Thunderbolt 3 Dock is a standout option. Although slightly bulkier than typical USB-C hubs, its feature set adds unparalleled convenience. With up to 15 ports, including multiple USB 3.1, audio in/out, dual card readers, and support for dual 4K displays, this hub is ideal for users wanting to convert their laptops into full-scale workstations.

Main Features:

  • 15 versatile ports
  • Dual 4K display support at 60Hz
  • Daisy-chain compatibility for Thunderbolt devices
  • Durable aluminum design

5. Belkin USB-C Multimedia Hub

Belkin’s USB-C Multimedia Hub is a robust, all-in-one solution that works perfectly for both MacBooks and PCs. Equipped with an HDMI port for video output, a USB-C pass-through port for charging, USB-A connections, and SD/microSD card slots, it covers almost all bases. Its lightweight design is especially suited for travelers who need connectivity on the go.

Main Features:

  • HDMI support for 4K video
  • USB-C charging pass-through
  • SD & microSD capability for media professionals
  • High-speed USB-A connectivity

6. VAVA USB-C Hub 8-in-1 Adapter

The VAVA USB-C Hub is a top contender for budget-conscious users. Its 8-in-1 configuration offers everything you need to transform your MacBook or PC setup, including USB 3.0 ports, a 4K HDMI port, SD/microSD card reader, an Ethernet port for stable connections, and Power Delivery support. The compact design ensures you can carry it with ease, while the robust build holds up to daily use.

Main Features:

  • Affordable price point
  • Power Delivery capable USB-C port
  • Gigabit Ethernet port
  • Sleek and space-saving design

7. Twelve South StayGo USB-C Hub

If you’re in need of a USB-C hub that blends portability and desktop convenience, the Twelve South StayGo USB-C Hub is worth your attention. It comes with an extra-long cable for desktop setups, while the compact hub itself is perfect for travel. You’ll find multiple USB-A ports, an HDMI port, SD/microSD slots, and a USB-C Power Delivery port for seamless usage.

Main Features:

  • Supports 85W Power Delivery
  • Long cable for versatile setups
  • 4K HDMI compatibility
  • Durable and lightweight structure

8. Uni USB-C Hub Adapter

Uni’s USB-C Hub Adapter is a straightforward and budget-friendly solution for everyday use. Offering USB-A ports, a 4K HDMI port, and SD/microSD card readers, this hub delivers in terms of functionality and ease of use. Its sleek aluminum body not only ensures durability but also matches the aesthetic of most modern laptops.

Main Features:

  • Affordable and reliable performance
  • Lightweight and compact design
  • Supports 4K HDMI
  • Plug-and-play functionality

9. Plugable USB-C Triple Display Docking Station

For users requiring advanced multi-tasking and increased screen real estate, the Plugable USB-C Triple Display Docking Station is an exceptional choice. Perfect for business professionals or gamers, this hub supports up to three displays at once, all while providing USB-A, USB-C, ethernet, and audio ports. Though bulkier than portable hubs, it’s ideal for dedicated workstations.

Main Features:

  • Triple display support
  • Integrated Ethernet connection
  • Multiple high-speed USB ports
  • Sturdy design for desk setups

10. Baseus USB-C Hub Adapter 6-in-1

The Baseus USB-C Hub is a compact, affordable hub that doesn’t compromise on features. Sporting an HDMI port, USB-A ports, a USB-C Power Delivery port, and SD/microSD card slots, it’s the perfect all-around hub for both MacBook and PC users. Its ultra-thin design makes it convenient to carry, while its multi-functional interface meets almost all productivity needs.

Main Features:

  • Compact design for portability
  • High-speed data transfer capabilities
  • Power Delivery support for charging devices
  • Fully plug-and-play functionality

Final Thoughts

Choosing the best USB-C hub depends on your specific needs, whether it’s portability, multimedia functionality, or professional-level connectivity. From the sleek designs of Satechi and HyperDrive to the powerful versatility of CalDigit and Plugable, the options are vast and cater to diverse requirements. Remember that compatibility with your MacBook or PC, number of ports, and overall build quality are key factors to consider when investing in a USB-C hub.

By selecting a hub that complements your workflow, you can expand your laptop’s functionality and unleash its full potential—whether for productivity, entertainment, or creative endeavors.
