The Best Multiplayer Games to Play with Friends


Playing multiplayer games with friends is one of the most enjoyable experiences in gaming. Whether you’re teaming up for co-op missions, battling in competitive arenas, or exploring vast open worlds, multiplayer games offer something for everyone. In this guide, we’ll explore the best multiplayer games to play with friends, providing a wide range of options for different tastes and gaming platforms.

1. Fortnite

Fortnite is a household name in the world of multiplayer gaming. Known for its unique battle royale mode, Fortnite has captured the attention of players worldwide with its fast-paced action and vibrant art style. The game allows players to build structures, gather resources, and fight to be the last person or team standing.

What sets Fortnite apart is its frequent updates, introducing new skins, events, and challenges. Whether you’re jumping into a solo match or teaming up with friends in squads, Fortnite’s ever-changing nature keeps the game fresh and exciting. With its cross-platform play feature, you and your friends can join forces on different consoles and devices.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • The cooperative modes offer great teamwork dynamics.
  • Regular content updates keep the game engaging.
  • Cross-platform play allows for seamless gaming between different devices.

2. Minecraft

If you’re looking for a more creative experience, Minecraft is the perfect multiplayer game to play with friends. Minecraft allows players to build and explore vast worlds made entirely of blocks. Whether you’re constructing complex structures, going on adventures in survival mode, or playing custom mini-games created by the community, Minecraft offers limitless possibilities.

The multiplayer aspect of Minecraft shines through in its collaborative building and survival gameplay. Whether you’re digging deep underground to mine resources or building your dream castle, there’s always something fun to do with friends.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Offers both creative and survival modes for different play styles.
  • The ability to join custom servers with friends and play user-generated content.
  • Endless opportunities for exploration and building.

3. Among Us

Among Us became a global sensation thanks to its simple yet captivating gameplay. Set in space, players are tasked with completing various tasks while trying to identify who among them is the Impostor. The Impostor’s job is to sabotage the crew’s efforts and eliminate everyone without getting caught.

What makes Among Us especially fun with friends is the social aspect. You’ll need to use deception, persuasion, and strategy to figure out who’s lying and who’s telling the truth. The game creates hilarious moments, intense discussions, and even moments of betrayal among friends.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Fun and engaging social deduction mechanics.
  • Easy to learn but hard to master.
  • Perfect for small groups of friends looking for a quick gaming session.

4. Call of Duty: Warzone

If you and your friends are fans of first-person shooters, Call of Duty: Warzone is a must-try. This free-to-play battle royale game combines fast-paced action with strategic elements. The game features a large open world and a variety of weapons and equipment to help you secure the victory.

Warzone’s emphasis on teamwork and communication makes it ideal for playing with friends. You can form squads, communicate through voice chat, and strategize to outwit other teams. The game’s constantly evolving map and new content updates keep the experience fresh.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • High-intensity battles require good communication and coordination.
  • Free-to-play model makes it accessible to everyone.
  • Large-scale maps with intense firefights keep gameplay exciting.

5. Rocket League

Rocket League takes soccer and combines it with rocket-powered cars, creating a unique and exciting multiplayer experience. Players control cars with the goal of hitting a giant ball into the opposing team’s net. While the concept sounds simple, the gameplay is anything but easy.

Rocket League is perfect for those who want a competitive multiplayer game that isn’t too complex to pick up. It’s easy to get into, but mastering the mechanics can take time. Playing with friends adds an extra layer of fun, as you can work together to pull off insane goals and plays.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Quick, competitive gameplay that’s easy to learn but difficult to master.
  • Encourages teamwork and coordination.
  • Available on multiple platforms with cross-play functionality.

6. League of Legends

League of Legends is one of the most popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) games in the world. In this game, two teams of five players each compete to destroy the enemy’s Nexus, located at the heart of their base. Players select champions, each with unique abilities, and work together to secure victory by pushing lanes, farming minions, and taking down objectives.

What makes League of Legends particularly enjoyable with friends is the teamwork and coordination required. You’ll need to communicate effectively, make strategic decisions, and support your teammates to succeed. If you and your friends are into competitive gaming, League of Legends offers endless depth and challenge.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • A deep and rewarding game with tons of strategic depth.
  • Perfect for friends who enjoy competitive multiplayer.
  • Free-to-play with constant updates and new champions.

7. Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout

Fall Guys: Ultimate Knockout is a fun, chaotic battle royale game where up to 60 players compete in a series of wacky obstacle courses. The goal is to be the last player standing after several rounds of challenges. The game’s colorful and whimsical art style, combined with its physics-based gameplay, makes it a lighthearted and entertaining experience.

What sets Fall Guys apart from other battle royales is its non-violent nature and emphasis on silly fun. It’s perfect for friends looking for a more relaxed and humorous game to play together. Plus, the random nature of the obstacle courses ensures no two rounds are ever the same.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Fun and unpredictable gameplay keeps things exciting.
  • The light-hearted, non-violent nature is suitable for all ages.
  • Easy to pick up and play, making it perfect for casual sessions.

8. Apex Legends

Apex Legends is another free-to-play battle royale game, but with a twist. It combines elements of hero shooters with traditional battle royale mechanics. Players choose from a roster of unique characters, each with special abilities, and work together to be the last team standing in a constantly shrinking battlefield.

What makes Apex Legends stand out is its emphasis on squad-based gameplay. It’s not just about getting the best loot; it’s about using each Legend’s abilities strategically and working as a team. Apex Legends also features a ping system that makes communication easier, even if you’re not using voice chat.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Unique characters with individual abilities add a layer of strategy to the game.
  • Squad-based mechanics make teamwork essential.
  • Free-to-play with regular updates and new Legends.

9. The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt (With Mods)

While The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is primarily a single-player RPG, there are mods available that allow players to enjoy multiplayer co-op gameplay. The Witcher 3 is a deeply immersive open-world game with rich storylines, detailed characters, and stunning visuals. By adding mods, you can explore the world of The Witcher together with friends.

This game offers a unique blend of action, exploration, and storytelling that few multiplayer games can match. Whether you’re hunting monsters, exploring the vast open world, or participating in intense combat, The Witcher 3 offers an unforgettable experience.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Rich, immersive world with compelling storytelling.
  • Mods bring multiplayer functionality to an already great game.
  • Engaging combat and exploration provide a unique experience.

10. Sea of Thieves

Sea of Thieves is a multiplayer pirate adventure game where you and your friends can become pirates and explore a vast, open world full of treasure, mythical creatures, and other players. You’ll sail the seas, battle enemies, discover hidden secrets, and seek treasure chests in a quest for fortune and glory.

The best part about Sea of Thieves is the sense of adventure. Every time you set sail, you’re embarking on a unique journey with your friends. Whether you’re engaging in epic naval battles or simply exploring the islands, Sea of Thieves offers an exciting and dynamic multiplayer experience.

Why It’s Great for Friends:

  • Adventure and exploration create memorable moments.
  • Fun, cooperative gameplay with a focus on teamwork.
  • A variety of activities to enjoy, from treasure hunting to combat.

Conclusion

Finding the best multiplayer game to play with friends can be a daunting task, given the wide variety of options available. Whether you’re looking for a competitive experience, a cooperative journey, or a game that focuses on creativity, there’s something for everyone. From high-stakes battle royales to relaxing sandbox games, these top picks offer exciting, engaging experiences that will make your gaming sessions with friends unforgettable. Choose one of these games, gather your friends, and dive into the fun!
