The Best Hunting Events and Expos to Attend


For any hunting enthusiast, attending a well-organized hunting event or expo can be a fantastic way to gain insights into the latest gear, meet fellow hunters, and expand knowledge on various techniques and conservation efforts. Whether you are a seasoned hunter or a beginner eager to immerse yourself in the world of hunting, these events are invaluable for networking, learning, and staying updated on the latest trends in the hunting community.

In this post, we will explore some of the best hunting events and expos that should be on every hunter’s radar. From grand conventions to intimate gatherings, there is an event for every type of hunter.

1. The National Hunting and Fishing Day Expo (USA)

Held annually in various states across the country, the National Hunting and Fishing Day Expo is one of the largest and most celebrated hunting events in the United States. This expo is particularly special as it serves as a celebration of both hunting and fishing, with various exhibitors showcasing the newest and most innovative equipment, including firearms, fishing rods, and outdoor gear.

Why Attend:

  • Educational seminars: Attendees can learn from experts in the field, covering topics such as wildlife conservation, hunting safety, and tips for successful hunting.
  • Hands-on demonstrations: You can try out the latest gear and test new products on-site.
  • Networking opportunities: Meet other hunters, fishing enthusiasts, and conservationists, sharing stories and tips.

Key Takeaway:

This expo is perfect for anyone interested in both hunting and fishing, offering a well-rounded experience in both fields.

2. SCI Convention (Safari Club International Convention)

If you’re into big game hunting, the SCI Convention in Reno, Nevada is an event that should not be missed. This annual gathering is one of the premier events for hunters interested in international hunting trips, big game conservation, and the latest hunting technology. The convention brings together top-tier outfitters, guides, and industry leaders from around the world.

Why Attend:

  • International focus: If you’re looking for information on hunting in other countries, this is the place to be. Meet outfitters who specialize in hunting trips to places like Africa, New Zealand, and Argentina.
  • High-quality vendors: The SCI Convention attracts a variety of vendors offering everything from exotic hunting gear to luxurious safari packages.
  • Exclusive auctions: Attendees have the chance to bid on exclusive hunting trips, collectible hunting items, and more.

Key Takeaway:

The SCI Convention is a must-visit for serious hunters seeking big game experiences and a wealth of knowledge from experts in the field.

3. The Great American Outdoor Show (USA)

Held annually in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, the Great American Outdoor Show is the largest event of its kind, with over 1,000 exhibitors representing all aspects of the outdoor industry, including hunting, fishing, and camping. This massive expo is a hub for hunters to discover the latest products, equipment, and innovations in the hunting world.

Why Attend:

  • Wide range of products: Whether you’re interested in firearms, hunting apparel, or even ATV accessories, you’ll find everything you need at this event.
  • Celebrity appearances: Famous hunters and outdoor personalities often make appearances, offering advice, demonstrations, and signings.
  • Activities for the whole family: This event is not just for hunters but also for outdoor enthusiasts of all kinds. Kids can enjoy archery ranges and other interactive activities.

Key Takeaway:

The Great American Outdoor Show is perfect for those looking for variety, with something for everyone in the family to enjoy while also offering exclusive products for hunters.

4. The British Shooting Show (UK)

For hunters in the United Kingdom, the British Shooting Show is an essential event. Held in Birmingham, this show is the largest of its kind in the UK and focuses heavily on shooting sports, hunting, and gun-related events. The expo features a wide range of exhibitors, from top gun manufacturers to clothing brands that cater to hunters and shooters.

Why Attend:

  • Shooting sports focus: The British Shooting Show offers a deep dive into the world of shooting sports, including clay pigeon shooting and practical shooting.
  • Top-tier exhibitors: Get a closer look at the best hunting rifles, shotguns, and accessories on the market.
  • Seminars and talks: Learn from experts about shooting techniques, hunting laws, and the best practices for hunters in the UK.

Key Takeaway:

This event is perfect for those in the UK looking for hunting gear, shooting sports insights, and education from seasoned professionals.

5. The Western Hunting and Conservation Expo (USA)

For those passionate about conservation and hunting, the Western Hunting and Conservation Expo in Salt Lake City, Utah, is an outstanding event. This expo highlights the importance of wildlife conservation while offering attendees the chance to purchase permits for hunting and bid on exclusive tags for some of the most sought-after game species in North America.

Why Attend:

  • Conservation efforts: The expo promotes responsible hunting practices and emphasizes the importance of preserving wildlife habitats.
  • Exclusive hunting permits: Attendees have the opportunity to bid on hunting permits for various species, including elk, mule deer, and bighorn sheep.
  • Workshops on conservation and hunting ethics: Learn about sustainable hunting practices and how to contribute to the conservation of wildlife.

Key Takeaway:

The Western Hunting and Conservation Expo is perfect for hunters who are also deeply passionate about wildlife conservation and sustainable hunting practices.

6. Outdoor Adventure Show (Canada)

The Outdoor Adventure Show in Toronto is one of Canada’s largest outdoor expos, with a strong focus on hunting and outdoor adventure. This event is a great place for hunters to explore the latest hunting gear, as well as other outdoor equipment such as camping gear, fishing supplies, and hiking tools.

Why Attend:

  • Wide variety of outdoor activities: While the show focuses on hunting, attendees can also explore gear for camping, hiking, and fishing.
  • Live demonstrations: Get a first-hand look at how various outdoor equipment works, including new products like high-tech hunting gear.
  • Expert advice: Meet professionals who offer advice on outdoor survival, hunting techniques, and best practices for Canadian hunters.

Key Takeaway:

This event offers a comprehensive outdoor experience, ideal for hunters who enjoy exploring other outdoor pursuits alongside their passion for hunting.

7. The Hunter’s Extravaganza (Texas, USA)

Known as one of the most exciting hunting events in the United States, The Hunter’s Extravaganza in Texas brings together hunters from across the country for a weekend full of hunting products, seminars, and competitions. This event is known for its fun atmosphere and is ideal for families and individuals who want to enjoy a weekend immersed in the hunting world.

Why Attend:

  • Fun, festive atmosphere: With live music, games, and contests, this event is perfect for hunters looking to have a great time while learning about hunting and outdoor activities.
  • A variety of products: Vendors showcase hunting rifles, scopes, hunting apparel, and even off-road vehicles.
  • Competitions and giveaways: Visitors have a chance to win prizes through raffles and contests, adding an element of excitement to the event.

Key Takeaway:

If you want a laid-back, fun, and engaging hunting event, The Hunter’s Extravaganza is a great choice.

8. The International Sportsmen’s Exposition (ISE)

The International Sportsmen’s Exposition (ISE) is a series of expos held in various cities across the United States, including Denver, Salt Lake City, and Sacramento. These expos are designed for both hunting and fishing enthusiasts, offering a diverse range of vendors and outdoor experts.

Why Attend:

  • Diverse range of products: From hunting gear to fishing equipment and off-roading tools, this expo covers a wide range of outdoor sports.
  • Hands-on workshops: Learn new skills through workshops focused on hunting techniques, survival tactics, and safety practices.
  • Engagement with industry professionals: Meet industry leaders, guides, and outfitters to plan your next hunting adventure.

Key Takeaway:

The ISE expo is ideal for those who enjoy a mix of hunting, fishing, and other outdoor sports, providing an opportunity to expand knowledge and skills in all areas.

9. The Deer and Turkey Expo (USA)

For hunters with a particular interest in deer and turkey hunting, the Deer and Turkey Expo is the perfect event. This expo is held annually in various cities and focuses on hunting these two species, providing specialized seminars, gear, and advice.

Why Attend:

  • Species-specific focus: If you’re passionate about deer or turkey hunting, this event provides specialized insights into tactics and gear tailored to these animals.
  • Expert speakers: Attend seminars by top hunters and wildlife experts who can provide valuable tips on how to improve your hunting techniques.
  • Product deals: The expo features discounts on hunting gear specifically designed for deer and turkey hunting.

Key Takeaway:

Perfect for those who hunt deer and turkeys, this expo offers highly specialized content and a dedicated community of like-minded enthusiasts.

Conclusion

Whether you are an experienced hunter or just beginning your hunting journey, attending a hunting event or expo can offer invaluable benefits. From gaining access to the latest gear and learning from experts to making connections with other hunters, these events offer a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the hunting community.

The events listed above represent just a small selection of the best hunting expos and conventions available, but there are many more across the world. No matter where you are, there is a hunting event that can help you enhance your skills, learn new techniques, and discover exciting new products. So, mark your calendars and prepare for an enriching hunting adventure at one of these top expos!
