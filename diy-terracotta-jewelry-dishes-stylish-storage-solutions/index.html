DIY Terracotta Jewelry Dishes: Stylish Storage Solutions


Are you tired of your jewelry getting tangled up or lost in the depths of your jewelry box? If so, we have the perfect solution for you - DIY terracotta jewelry dishes! These stylish and functional storage solutions will not only keep your precious jewelry organized but also add a touch of elegance to your dressing table.

Materials You’ll Need

Before we dive into the step-by-step process, let’s gather all the materials you’ll need for this project:

  • Terracotta saucers (in various sizes)
  • Acrylic paints (in your preferred colors)
  • Paintbrushes
  • Clear sealant spray
  • Painter’s tape
  • Fine-grit sandpaper
  • Pencil
  • Paper towels

Step 1: Preparation

Start by cleaning the terracotta saucers with a damp cloth to remove any dust or dirt. This will ensure that the paint adheres properly to the surface. Once cleaned, use the fine-grit sandpaper to lightly roughen the surface of the saucers. This step will help the paint to adhere better and prevent chipping in the future.

Step 2: Design and Paint

Now comes the fun part - designing and painting your jewelry dishes! Use a pencil to lightly sketch your desired design onto the saucers. If you’re not confident in your drawing skills, you can also use painter’s tape to create geometric patterns or mask off specific areas for a clean and modern look.

Once you’re happy with your design, it’s time to bring it to life with acrylic paints. Choose colors that complement your personal style and the overall aesthetic of your dressing table. Remember to use thin layers of paint and let each layer dry completely before applying the next one. This will ensure a smooth and even finish.

Step 3: Finishing Touches

Once the paint has dried, it’s time to add some finishing touches to your jewelry dishes. Use a fine brush to add details or highlights to your design if desired. Once you’re satisfied with the look, apply a clear sealant spray to protect the paint and make the dishes water-resistant. This step is essential to ensure the longevity of your DIY creations.

Step 4: Enjoy Your Stylish Storage Solutions

Congratulations! You’ve successfully created your own terracotta jewelry dishes. Now it’s time to put them to use. Place them on your dressing table or vanity and start organizing your jewelry. The different sizes of the saucers will allow you to separate your rings, earrings, bracelets, and necklaces, making it easier to find and access your favorite pieces.

Conclusion

DIY terracotta jewelry dishes are not only functional storage solutions but also stylish additions to your dressing table. By following these simple steps, you can create unique and personalized dishes that reflect your personal style. Say goodbye to tangled necklaces and missing earrings - with these DIY jewelry dishes, your jewelry will always have a stylish and organized home.

So why wait? Grab some terracotta saucers and let your creativity shine. Happy crafting!
