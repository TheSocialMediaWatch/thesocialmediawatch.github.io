The Best Hunting Blogs to Follow for Tips and Inspiration


Hunting is more than just a hobby or a sport; it’s a lifestyle. Whether you’re an experienced hunter or someone new to the outdoors, having access to quality information can significantly enhance your hunting experiences. In the age of digital media, there’s no shortage of hunting blogs that provide valuable insights, expert advice, gear reviews, and inspiration for hunters of all skill levels. Here’s a comprehensive guide to some of the best hunting blogs you should follow for tips, tricks, and plenty of motivation.

1. The Hunting Public

Why Follow It?

The Hunting Public has quickly become one of the most popular and respected hunting blogs in the industry. Their team of passionate hunters travels across the country, sharing their hunting adventures, strategies, and gear reviews. Their approach to hunting is relatable and practical, making it ideal for hunters who prefer to hunt in public land, which is often overlooked by other hunters.

What You Can Expect:

  • In-depth Hunting Strategies: From scouting to tracking and positioning, The Hunting Public offers expert advice on all aspects of hunting.
  • Real-World Tips: The team provides actionable insights based on their own experiences in the field, helping you improve your own skills.
  • Gear Reviews: They test and review the latest hunting gear, offering detailed insights into performance, value, and reliability.
  • Video Content: The blog features video content that showcases real hunting scenarios, making it easy to understand techniques and tactics.

Great for: Beginners and seasoned hunters alike, especially those who prefer hunting on public lands.

2. Outdoor Life

Why Follow It?

Outdoor Life has been a staple in the outdoor and hunting community for years. They cover a broad range of topics related to hunting, fishing, and survival. The blog is particularly renowned for its detailed and informative articles on hunting tactics, hunting gear, and outdoor gear reviews.

What You Can Expect:

  • Expert Hunting Advice: Outdoor Life has a team of seasoned hunters and outdoor experts who provide tips on everything from stalking techniques to tracking and field dressing.
  • Product Reviews: If you’re in the market for hunting gear, Outdoor Life provides comprehensive reviews on a wide range of products, helping you make an informed purchase.
  • Survival and Safety Tips: Learn the ins and outs of outdoor survival, including safety tips that can save your life in the wilderness.
  • Event Coverage: Outdoor Life regularly covers hunting events, tournaments, and conventions, providing you with updates on the latest in the hunting world.

Great for: Those looking for a well-rounded hunting blog with a mix of tips, gear reviews, and expert advice.

3. Field & Stream

Why Follow It?

Field & Stream has long been one of the most trusted sources of outdoor and hunting knowledge. Their blog covers everything from hunting tips and tactics to stories of adventure and survival. They focus on providing readers with the information needed to excel in the field.

What You Can Expect:

  • Hunting Tips for Every Species: Whether you’re hunting deer, elk, turkey, or waterfowl, Field & Stream offers targeted advice tailored to different species.
  • Gear and Product Reviews: They provide in-depth reviews of everything from rifles to hunting boots, giving you a reliable look at what’s worth investing in.
  • How-to Guides: If you’re looking to learn new skills or improve existing ones, Field & Stream’s guides cover everything from field dressing to preparing a hunting camp.
  • Hunting Culture and Stories: Enjoy reading personal hunting stories, adventures, and the culture surrounding the sport.

Great for: Hunters looking for detailed species-specific advice and comprehensive gear guides.

4. MeatEater

Why Follow It?

MeatEater has established itself as one of the most influential hunting brands, blending the art of hunting with culinary adventure. Their blog, like their broader brand, emphasizes not only the hunt but also how to process and cook the animals you harvest.

What You Can Expect:

  • Hunting and Cooking: MeatEater excels at combining the hunt with the preparation of wild game. Their blog offers excellent recipes, cooking tips, and advice on preparing wild meat.
  • Conservation and Ethics: They emphasize sustainable hunting practices and promote a deep respect for the environment and wildlife.
  • Interviews and Stories: The blog features interviews with experts, experienced hunters, and conservationists, sharing their thoughts on hunting and wildlife management.
  • Hunting and Adventure: Whether it’s a backcountry hunt or a casual day in the field, MeatEater covers a wide range of hunting experiences.

Great for: Hunters who love the culinary side of hunting and want to learn more about the ethical aspects of the sport.

5. Bowhunting.com

Why Follow It?

Bowhunting.com is dedicated entirely to bowhunting, which is a niche but growing segment of the hunting community. If you’re interested in improving your skills with a bow and arrow or want to learn more about bowhunting gear, this is the blog for you.

What You Can Expect:

  • Bowhunting Tips: Detailed advice on everything from perfecting your shot to choosing the right bow for your needs.
  • Gear Reviews: Bowhunting.com provides extensive reviews of bows, arrows, and accessories, helping you make the best decisions for your bowhunting needs.
  • Techniques and Tactics: They offer a range of tactics specifically designed for bowhunters, whether you’re chasing big game or small game.
  • Community and Forums: Engage with fellow bowhunters through their community forums where you can discuss hunting strategies, gear, and experiences.

Great for: Bowhunters looking for focused advice and reviews on all things related to bowhunting.

6. Hunt Arizona

Why Follow It?

Hunt Arizona is a regional hunting blog focusing primarily on hunting opportunities in Arizona. However, the advice shared on this blog is valuable for hunters across the nation, especially those interested in Western hunting.

What You Can Expect:

  • Arizona-Specific Tips: If you’re hunting in Arizona or the surrounding regions, this blog offers highly specialized advice about hunting in the deserts, mountains, and forests of the Southwest.
  • Tag Draw Strategies: Arizona is famous for its complex game management and tag draw system. Hunt Arizona provides readers with essential tips on how to secure tags for hunts.
  • Gear and Tactics: From high desert hunts to mountain stalking, Hunt Arizona shares detailed strategies and gear recommendations.
  • Hunter Stories: Personal hunting tales from the wilds of Arizona, providing inspiration and practical advice from real-life hunters.

Great for: Hunters interested in pursuing hunts in Arizona or Western states and those looking for specialized advice on desert and mountain hunting.

7. Backcountry Hunters & Anglers

Why Follow It?

Backcountry Hunters & Anglers (BHA) focuses on public land access and conservation. Their blog emphasizes not only hunting techniques but also advocacy for preserving public lands, which are critical for hunters across the country.

What You Can Expect:

  • Conservation Advocacy: Learn about the challenges facing public lands and what you can do to help preserve these vital spaces.
  • Hunting and Gear Tips: Practical advice for hunters who frequent backcountry terrain, including survival gear, navigation tools, and hunting tactics.
  • Wildlife Management: In-depth discussions on the importance of sustainable wildlife management and how hunters can contribute to conservation efforts.
  • Stories and Events: Enjoy stories from passionate hunters, conservationists, and outdoor enthusiasts, as well as updates on BHA events.

Great for: Hunters who are passionate about conservation and want to stay informed about public land access issues.

8. The Ready Hunter

Why Follow It?

The Ready Hunter is a blog that stands out due to its focus on readiness and preparation, two essential elements for successful hunting. The blog covers everything from physical fitness to mental preparedness, ensuring hunters are equipped both physically and mentally for their hunts.

What You Can Expect:

  • Fitness and Conditioning: Articles focusing on how to stay in shape for hunting, including strength training and endurance exercises to improve your performance.
  • Hunting Prep Tips: Learn how to prepare for different types of hunts, from selecting your hunting spot to packing the right gear.
  • Gear and Equipment: Reviews and recommendations on the best hunting gear, from optics to clothing and footwear.
  • Survival Skills: Learn essential survival skills that will help you in emergency situations during a hunt.

Great for: Hunters who want to be fully prepared, both mentally and physically, for every hunting adventure.

Conclusion

No matter your experience level, following the right hunting blogs can provide valuable information, gear insights, and much-needed inspiration. The blogs listed here cover a wide range of topics, from bowhunting tips to backcountry survival and gear reviews, ensuring that you have the tools and knowledge to succeed in the field. Make sure to follow these blogs regularly to stay updated on the latest hunting trends, techniques, and gear advancements. Whether you’re planning your next big hunt or just want to learn something new, these blogs will help guide you on your hunting journey.
