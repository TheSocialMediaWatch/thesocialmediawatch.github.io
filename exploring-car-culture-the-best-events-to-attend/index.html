Exploring Car Culture: The Best Events to Attend


If you're a car enthusiast, there's no shortage of exciting events to attend throughout the year. From classic car shows to high-performance racing events, there's something for everyone to enjoy. In this article, we'll explore some of the best car events to add to your calendar, covering a range of interests and geographic locations. Top Events for Classic Car Enthusiasts For those who appreciate the nostalgia and charm of vintage vehicles, there are numerous events celebrating the world of classic cars. One of the most iconic events is the Mecum Auctions' Monterey Classic Car Auction, held annually in August during the iconic Monterey Car Week in California. This four-day auction features some of the rarest and most valuable classic cars in the world, with notable highlights including the 1968 Shelby Cobra 427 Competition Roadster and the 1962 Ferrari 250 GTO. Another highly-anticipated event for classic car enthusiasts is the Goodwood Revival Meeting, held in September at the Goodwood Motor Circuit in West Sussex, England. This vintage racing event showcases historic racing cars, with demonstrations of iconic racing cars, as well as thrilling on-track action. Visitors can also explore the arcades, stalls, and exhibits featuring the finest classic cars from around the world. High-Performance Road Trips For those who crave adrenaline and speed, there are numerous events that promise a thrilling experience. The Goodguys Trans Am Nationals in Ohio is one of the largest classic and performance car events in the country, with over 3,500 classic cars, hot rods, mopars, and 'fleet finesse show cars, along with drag racing, auto crosses, and product exhibits. Take a road trip to Talladega Nights: A Go Retro Days Celebration, in Alabama, for a dash of high-octane action. Head-to-head racing, a Strawberry Festival with art and collectibles on display, and high-performance camping make this event a thrilling combination of competition and fun. Jeepers Jamboree takes place in Sacramento, CA, with heavily contested 4x4 on-course action. Off-the-beaten-path automotive events like RiverRun in Utah, a 200-mile journey along rivers, runs the gamut from enthusiasts' lines and fine tuned engines.
