Sustainable Spirits: Eco-Friendly Liquor Choices


In recent years, there has been a considerable surge in the demand for eco-friendly products across various industries. The liquor industry has not been left behind. More and more distilleries are adopting sustainable practices in the production of their spirits. This move is not only benefiting the environment but also providing consumers with an array of choices for responsible drinking. In this article, we will explore five sustainable spirits that every environmental-conscious drinker should consider.

  1. Absente Vodka

Absente Vodka, founded in 2007, has been dedicated to producing eco-friendly vodka by utilizing 100% renewable energy. Their dedication to sustainability does not stop at energy consumption. They also use 100% sustainable ingredients, and their bottles are made from lightweight, recycled materials. By choosing Absente Vodka, you are not only sipping on a smooth drink but also supporting a company that genuinely cares about the environment.

  1. Greenbar Collective

Greenbar Collective is a craft distillery that operates on a mission to create the world’s cleanest spirits. Terms like “clean” and “sustainable” only scratch the surface of what they do in their distillery. For instance, Greenbar uses organic grains sourced from local farmers, solar energy, and landfill-free packaging. The result? An environmentally-friendly and delicious gin and other spirits.

  1. Green Road Distillery

Green Road Distillery takes a unique approach to sustainability by using hemp to create their spirits. Is hemp the answer to the world’s environmental problems? Not entirely. Nonetheless, distilling with hemp is a unique way of making use of this widely misunderstood plant in a different industry.

  1. Terreuner Rums

Terreuner Rums, based in France, has been working on practical ways to reduce the environmental impact of spirits production for years. One of the most notable things the distillery has done is called reverse osmosis filtration. Essentially, this filtration process eliminates the need for water and energy-intensive distillation methods. Terreuner Rums also use natural packaging to minimize waste.

  1. Astor Wines & Spirits

Astor Wines & Spirits carries an extensive collection of sustainable wines and spirits, making it a go-to destination for any environmentally-conscious drinker. Whether it’s organic or biodynamic wines or spirits made using renewable energy, Astor Wines & Spirits have you covered. As a result, this store serves as a one-stop-shop for conscious liquor choices.

In conclusion, the fact that the liquor industry has embraced sustainability is a significant step towards protecting our environment. As consumers, we all have a duty to ensure that the products we consume are eco-friendly wherever possible. So, the next time you go shopping for liquor, consider these sustainable spirits and support environmentally-friendly distilleries.

Lastly, remember that every tip of the bottle is a step towards making the world a better place. Cheers to that!
