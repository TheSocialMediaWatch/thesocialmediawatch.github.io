Top 10 Luxury Hotels You Must Visit This Year


Luxury travel continues to be one of the most sought-after experiences for those who want to combine comfort, style, and exceptional service. Whether you’re planning a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, the world’s top luxury hotels offer an unparalleled level of sophistication and relaxation. In this post, we highlight the top 10 luxury hotels that should be on your travel radar this year. Each one of these iconic hotels promises unforgettable experiences, unique features, and world-class amenities. Let’s explore what makes these hotels stand out in the luxury hospitality industry.

1. The Ritz-Carlton, Paris

If you’re in search of a hotel that combines elegance with rich history, The Ritz-Carlton in Paris is the epitome of Parisian luxury. Situated in the heart of the French capital, this iconic hotel offers stunning views of the Eiffel Tower and is a short walk from the city’s renowned art museums and cultural landmarks.

The Ritz-Carlton is known for its opulent interiors, including grand chandeliers, marble floors, and ornate furniture. It offers spacious rooms and suites with high ceilings, antique-style furnishings, and plush bedding. The hotel’s Michelin-starred restaurant, L’Espadon, offers exquisite French cuisine, while the spa provides the ultimate relaxation experience with world-class treatments and a luxurious pool. Whether you’re indulging in a signature cocktail in the glamorous bar or enjoying an afternoon tea, the Ritz-Carlton Paris is the definition of refined luxury.

Key Features:

  • Exquisite dining at L’Espadon
  • A historic location near the Louvre and the Eiffel Tower
  • Lavish rooms and suites with custom décor
  • High-end spa with signature treatments

2. Burj Al Arab Jumeirah, Dubai

When it comes to the epitome of luxury, few places come close to matching the grandeur of Burj Al Arab in Dubai. Known for its sail-shaped structure that towers over the Persian Gulf, this seven-star hotel offers unrivaled opulence. Situated on its own island, the hotel boasts 202 suites with panoramic views of the Dubai coastline.

Guests at the Burj Al Arab can expect world-class service, including personal butlers, chauffeur-driven Rolls-Royce cars, and exclusive access to private beaches. Dining options include high-end restaurants like Al Muntaha, offering European cuisine, and Al Mahara, where seafood is served in an underwater setting. The hotel’s spa provides a range of treatments using exclusive products, making it a sanctuary for ultimate relaxation.

Key Features:

  • The world’s only 7-star hotel with exceptional architecture
  • Private butlers and Rolls-Royce chauffeur service
  • Exclusive underwater restaurant for seafood lovers
  • Lavish spa with luxurious treatments

3. Aman Tokyo, Japan

For those looking to immerse themselves in a serene and minimalist environment, Aman Tokyo offers a tranquil escape that blends traditional Japanese design with contemporary luxury. Located in the heart of Tokyo, the hotel’s sleek and modern architecture provides a stunning contrast to the natural beauty of its surroundings, which include expansive gardens and breathtaking views of the Tokyo skyline.

The hotel’s spacious rooms are designed with natural materials like wood, stone, and glass, creating a peaceful and soothing atmosphere. Guests can enjoy a variety of wellness services, including massages, yoga sessions, and an indoor swimming pool. The hotel also features a range of fine dining options, from traditional Japanese dishes to modern international cuisine. Aman Tokyo is a true retreat for those seeking calm amidst the hustle and bustle of one of the world’s busiest cities.

Key Features:

  • Zen-inspired design and tranquil atmosphere
  • Wellness and spa offerings for total relaxation
  • Stunning views of the Tokyo skyline
  • A mix of traditional Japanese and modern dining experiences

4. The Langham, London

The Langham is one of London’s oldest and most prestigious luxury hotels, offering an exceptional combination of historic charm and modern amenities. Located in the West End, the hotel is just steps away from London’s most famous landmarks, including the British Museum, Buckingham Palace, and Oxford Street.

The Langham offers a selection of beautifully designed rooms, each equipped with the latest technology, lavish furnishings, and high-end toiletries. The hotel’s Michelin-starred restaurant, Roux at The Landau, offers a world-class culinary experience. After dining, guests can unwind in the elegant Artesian Bar or indulge in rejuvenating treatments at the Chuan Spa. Whether you’re here for business or leisure, The Langham guarantees an unforgettable stay.

Key Features:

  • Prime location in the heart of London
  • Michelin-starred dining at Roux at The Landau
  • Beautifully appointed rooms with modern amenities
  • Luxury spa and wellness services

5. Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan, Indonesia

For a tropical paradise that combines natural beauty with world-class luxury, the Four Seasons Resort Bali at Sayan is the ideal destination. Located in the lush Ubud rainforest, this stunning resort offers guests the chance to immerse themselves in the island’s natural beauty while enjoying exceptional service.

The resort’s spacious villas and suites are designed with traditional Balinese aesthetics, complete with thatched roofs and private plunge pools. Guests can enjoy fine dining at the resort’s Ayung Terrace restaurant, which offers breathtaking views of the surrounding rice paddies. For relaxation, the resort offers holistic spa treatments that incorporate local healing traditions and organic ingredients. A stay at the Four Seasons Resort Bali is the perfect way to unwind in paradise.

Key Features:

  • Stunning villas with private plunge pools and lush rainforest views
  • Authentic Balinese design and aesthetics
  • Wellness offerings that combine traditional healing methods with modern luxury
  • Exceptional fine dining at Ayung Terrace

6. The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort

Set on a secluded private island in the Maldives, The St. Regis Maldives Vommuli Resort is an idyllic tropical retreat where luxury meets the natural beauty of the Indian Ocean. With overwater villas and beachfront suites, guests can enjoy complete privacy while still indulging in world-class amenities.

Each villa features modern comforts, including plunge pools, outdoor showers, and stunning ocean views. Dining is a treat at the resort’s various gourmet restaurants, including the overwater restaurant, which specializes in seafood. The Iridium Spa offers bespoke treatments tailored to each guest’s needs, while water sports and activities like snorkeling, diving, and sailing allow guests to fully explore the surrounding coral reefs.

Key Features:

  • Overwater villas with private pools and stunning ocean views
  • Multiple world-class dining options with a focus on seafood
  • Exclusive spa and wellness services
  • A range of water-based activities for adventure seekers

7. Palazzo di Varignana, Italy

Nestled in the rolling hills of the Emilia-Romagna region in northern Italy, Palazzo di Varignana is a true hidden gem. This historic hotel is located in a former 18th-century villa surrounded by vineyards, olive groves, and lush gardens, offering guests a peaceful, picturesque setting.

The hotel’s rooms are elegantly designed with a combination of traditional Italian furnishings and modern touches. Palazzo di Varignana boasts a luxurious spa that utilizes local products, offering treatments such as grape-based facials and massages using olive oil. The hotel’s fine dining options include exquisite regional Italian cuisine, and guests can enjoy a glass of wine while overlooking the beautiful countryside. Whether you are visiting for the food, the views, or the serenity, this hotel offers a true Italian luxury experience.

Key Features:

  • Historic villa with stunning views of the Emilia-Romagna countryside
  • A range of treatments incorporating local ingredients like olive oil and grapes
  • Fine Italian dining and wine-tasting opportunities
  • Tranquil spa and wellness offerings

8. The Biltmore, Coral Gables, Florida

The Biltmore in Coral Gables is a historic hotel that has been a symbol of luxury since its opening in 1926. Located in the charming city of Coral Gables, just a short drive from Miami, The Biltmore offers a perfect blend of old-world elegance and modern comforts.

The hotel’s Spanish-style architecture, coupled with its lush gardens and sprawling golf course, creates an atmosphere of refined relaxation. Guests can unwind by the beautiful outdoor pool or indulge in a range of spa treatments. The Biltmore’s dining options are equally impressive, with restaurants serving a variety of gourmet dishes. Whether you’re in town for business or leisure, this hotel offers an unforgettable experience.

Key Features:

  • Iconic Spanish-style architecture with expansive grounds
  • Historic charm combined with modern luxury
  • World-class dining options and a renowned golf course
  • Relaxing spa treatments and a stunning outdoor pool

9. Hotel de Crillon, Paris

Hotel de Crillon, another jewel in the luxury hotel landscape of Paris, exudes historic charm and modern opulence. Located on the Place de la Concorde, this hotel offers unparalleled views of the Seine River and the iconic landmarks of Paris.

Following a multi-year renovation, the hotel’s interiors are a perfect blend of classic French design and contemporary elegance. The rooms are exquisitely decorated with French antiques and luxurious textiles, offering a truly regal experience. The hotel’s gastronomic offerings include a Michelin-starred restaurant, L’Ecrin, as well as refined cocktails at the bar. Whether you’re enjoying the lavish spa or taking in the views of the city, Hotel de Crillon promises an unforgettable Parisian getaway.

Key Features:

  • Historic hotel with modern luxury and sophistication
  • Michelin-starred dining at L’Ecrin
  • Stunning views of the Seine and major Paris landmarks
  • Renowned spa and wellness center

10. The Peninsula, Hong Kong

The Peninsula Hong Kong is a symbol of luxury in one of the world’s most dynamic cities. Offering a unique blend of classic elegance and modern sophistication, this hotel provides guests with an exceptional experience in the heart of Kowloon.

The Peninsula’s rooms are spacious and elegantly decorated, featuring the latest technology and stunning views of Victoria Harbour. The hotel boasts several fine dining options, including a Michelin-starred Cantonese restaurant, as well as a rooftop bar offering spectacular views of the Hong Kong skyline. Guests can also indulge in a variety of spa treatments or take a classic afternoon tea in one of the hotel’s charming lounges.

Key Features:

  • Iconic hotel with a blend of classic and modern luxury
  • Michelin-starred Cantonese restaurant
  • Panoramic views of Victoria Harbour
  • Elegant afternoon tea and indulgent spa experiences

These 10 luxury hotels offer not just accommodation, but an experience—whether you’re looking for the serenity of a tropical island, the romance of a Parisian landmark, or the sophistication of a historic villa. From Michelin-starred restaurants to world-class spas and breathtaking views, these hotels are the perfect destination for travelers who expect the very best. If you’re planning your next luxury escape, make sure to include these iconic hotels on your list.
