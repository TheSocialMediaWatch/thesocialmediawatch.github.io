The Best Fashion Podcasts to Tune Into for Style Tips


Fashion has always been a medium of self-expression, a way to tell the world who you are, without speaking a single word. As trends evolve, so do the ways we consume fashion, and podcasts have become one of the most popular platforms for discovering new style inspiration, insights, and expert opinions. If you’re a fashion enthusiast or just someone looking to enhance your style, tuning into the right podcasts can help you stay up to date with the latest trends, discover timeless style secrets, and learn how to refine your personal fashion sense.

In this post, we’ve rounded up the best fashion podcasts that deliver an abundance of knowledge, tips, and entertainment for listeners. From style tips and expert interviews to deep dives into the history of fashion, these podcasts offer something for everyone.

1. The Fashion Show Podcast

The Fashion Show Podcast is a must-listen for anyone interested in the fashion industry’s inner workings. Hosted by two seasoned fashion insiders, the show explores a wide range of topics, from emerging designers to fashion week recaps and everything in between. What sets this podcast apart is its focus on the nuances of the fashion business, including how to break into the industry and the challenges designers face.

Whether you’re a budding fashion entrepreneur or simply a lover of high fashion, The Fashion Show Podcast will give you a behind-the-scenes look into what makes the fashion world tick. You’ll also enjoy in-depth discussions with fashion designers, stylists, and industry experts, making it an excellent resource for anyone looking to understand the industry’s complexities.

2. Fashion No Filter

For listeners who want to dive deep into the glitzy world of fashion, but with a bit of humor and flair, Fashion No Filter is the perfect choice. Hosted by Parisian fashion journalist Lucia and British fashion editor Tamara, this podcast is packed with candid discussions on everything fashion-related, from runway shows to Instagram influencers and celebrity style.

The beauty of this podcast lies in its down-to-earth yet glamorous approach. Lucia and Tamara bring their years of experience in the fashion world to the table, providing listeners with the inside scoop on the fashion scene. Whether it’s breaking down viral trends or giving listeners tips on how to incorporate runway looks into everyday outfits, Fashion No Filter is the go-to podcast for a fun, informative, and relatable look at the fashion industry.

3. Dressed: The History of Fashion

If you’ve ever wondered about the origins of your favorite fashion trends or the stories behind iconic pieces, Dressed: The History of Fashion is the podcast you need. Hosted by fashion historians April Calahan and Cassidy Zachary, this show delves into the rich history of fashion, exploring how clothing has evolved over centuries and shaped society in the process.

From the opulent styles of the French court to the rise of streetwear, Dressed offers a comprehensive and entertaining look at the cultural significance of fashion. Each episode features fascinating insights into the history of fashion, with topics that range from the development of haute couture to the influence of war and politics on fashion trends. Listening to Dressed not only enriches your knowledge of fashion history but also provides a deeper understanding of how past trends influence present-day fashion choices.

4. The Business of Fashion Podcast

For fashion enthusiasts who are also interested in the business side of the industry, The Business of Fashion Podcast is an essential listen. Hosted by BoF founder Imran Amed, this podcast focuses on the intersection of fashion and business, offering listeners insights into the strategies, challenges, and innovations that shape the global fashion industry.

Imran interviews industry leaders, from CEOs of fashion houses to influential designers, providing an insider’s look at how major fashion companies navigate challenges in a rapidly changing market. Whether you’re interested in how technology is transforming fashion, the impact of sustainability on the industry, or the business strategies behind global fashion brands, The Business of Fashion Podcast will provide you with valuable information that can enhance your understanding of the fashion world beyond just the trends.

5. The Cutting Room Floor

The Cutting Room Floor is a podcast that takes a deep dive into the world of fashion design and the creative process. Hosted by a team of designers and stylists, the show explores the creative journey from concept to finished product. Through interviews with designers, stylists, and other industry professionals, listeners get a peek into the challenges and triumphs behind the scenes of fashion design.

This podcast is a perfect listen for anyone interested in fashion design, whether you’re an aspiring designer, a seasoned industry veteran, or simply someone who appreciates the artistry behind fashion. The Cutting Room Floor sheds light on the intricacies of the design process, including sketching, fabric selection, and the technical aspects of garment construction. By tuning in, you’ll learn how designers bring their vision to life and the effort it takes to create the clothes that ultimately define the latest trends.

6. Vogue Podcast

No list of fashion podcasts would be complete without including Vogue Podcast. As one of the most iconic fashion publications in the world, Vogue has long been a trusted source for fashion insights, celebrity style, and trend forecasts. Their podcast brings all of that and more, offering listeners exclusive interviews with some of the biggest names in fashion.

The Vogue Podcast covers a wide range of topics, from in-depth interviews with designers and models to discussions about cultural movements in fashion. In addition to style tips and fashion news, this podcast also explores the intersection of fashion with art, music, and politics. Whether you’re looking for high-end fashion advice or curious about the latest trends in the fashion industry, the Vogue Podcast delivers expert insights that are both informative and inspiring.

7. The Style Files Podcast

For those seeking personal style advice and a deeper understanding of how to craft a wardrobe that truly reflects their personality, The Style Files Podcast is the ideal choice. Hosted by fashion expert and stylist, Lauren Messiah, this podcast offers practical style tips, wardrobe advice, and insights into building a timeless, versatile wardrobe.

Each episode focuses on a different aspect of personal style, such as how to dress for your body type, create a capsule wardrobe, or master the art of accessorizing. Lauren’s expertise and approachable tone make this podcast an invaluable resource for listeners who want to upgrade their wardrobe and develop a signature style that stands the test of time.

8. Unstyled

If you love hearing from fashion insiders who share real stories about how fashion shapes their lives, Unstyled is the podcast for you. Hosted by fashion stylist and editor, Christine de Lassus, Unstyled brings in a range of influential guests to discuss everything from personal style to the role fashion plays in their careers.

What sets Unstyled apart is its focus on authenticity. The podcast encourages guests to share their personal journeys and experiences in the fashion industry, providing a unique perspective on how fashion intersects with identity, culture, and lifestyle. Whether you’re tuning in for the personal anecdotes or the fashion tips, Unstyled is an engaging and insightful podcast that gives you a glimpse into the lives of fashion’s most influential figures.

9. Clothes Horse

Clothes Horse is a fantastic podcast for fashion lovers who are passionate about sustainable fashion and conscious consumption. Hosted by fashion writer and sustainability advocate, Amanda, the podcast explores the world of slow fashion, ethical production, and the environmental impact of the fashion industry.

Listeners are treated to interviews with sustainable fashion designers, ethical brands, and industry experts who are working to reshape the way we think about fashion. Clothes Horse delves into important topics such as how to shop more responsibly, the benefits of buying secondhand, and how to care for clothes in a way that extends their lifespan. If you’re interested in making more conscious choices when it comes to fashion, this podcast provides a wealth of knowledge and resources to help guide you on your journey toward a more sustainable wardrobe.

10. The Style School Podcast

For those who want to elevate their fashion sense and feel more confident in their style choices, The Style School Podcast is an excellent resource. Hosted by stylist and image consultant, Jenny Brandt, the podcast focuses on practical style advice that helps listeners define and express their personal style.

Each episode covers a specific fashion topic, from wardrobe essentials and styling tips to how to embrace your body type and develop a fashion identity that feels true to who you are. Jenny’s down-to-earth and approachable style makes The Style School Podcast a fun and informative resource for anyone looking to boost their fashion confidence.

Conclusion

The world of fashion is ever-evolving, and podcasts offer an accessible way to stay connected, informed, and inspired. Whether you’re looking for expert style advice, insights into the fashion industry, or just a fun and engaging way to learn about trends, the podcasts listed above are all excellent resources for fashion lovers.

By tuning into these shows, you can expand your fashion knowledge, discover new trends, and learn how to incorporate style tips into your everyday life. No matter where you are in your fashion journey, there’s a podcast out there that will resonate with you and help you take your style to the next level. So, grab your headphones, hit play, and get ready to dive into the world of fashion!
